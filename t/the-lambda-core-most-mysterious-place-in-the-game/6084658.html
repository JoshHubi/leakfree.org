Hard to say my favourite chapter. When I first played it was Questionable Ethics, I loved being in this lab where they studied the aliens. And you got to fight soldiers and had first encounter with alien grunts. Also loved the puzzle with bouncing the lasers, and it was great getting the tau cannon. I’m less keen on that chapter now though, because I now realise how absurd the layout of those labs is, they aren’t convincing labs at all.

You’ve got to walk THROUGH a dangerous biological lab to get to another dangerous biological lab? So if the doors are sealed at the first one everyone in the second one is trapped too? Crazy!
