I Went to Valve!

Half-Life Series
Skaevola November 25, 2018, 12:35am #1

For this high school dealy, I had to check out a prospective job. I live in the Seattle area, and obviously any excuse to get into the Valve offices is a good excuse, so I called 'em up. I got a tour of the offices.
They didn’t make me sign anything, so I guess I’m allowed to tell anyone I want about the stuff I saw? There’s no such thing as an implied NDA, is there? They never even said, like, “Now don’t tell anyone about this!” So whatever.

The point is… And the reason I’m posting this in the Half-Life Series forum… They were in the middle of remodeling when I visited the office, and there was a lot of concept art just kind of… spread around. In, like, big stacks and piles. Most of it was for Dota 2, and there was a little bit for some weird Team Fortress 2 stuff that I didn’t understand because I don’t really play Team Fortress 2 (and one big ol’ picture that JUST had the word MAGICKA on it, but I dunno what that means), but there were also four or five that I’m reasonably certain were Ep3/HL3 concept art.

There were a couple other ones like the one we’ve already seen, with the sort of jagged metal edges, but they were darker, with a blue tint instead of a green one, and were kind of… more cramped. Like they were of smaller areas? These ones were pretty nondescript and I was kind of being rushed through those parts of the office.

The one that really caught my eye, though, was a wide-shot picture of the ocean with a ship floating in it. I mean, it obviously wasn’t TF2 or Dota, so I’m pretty sure it was the Borealis, but what circumstance would put the Borealis back in the water?

I guess concept art isn’t really that concrete of a source, plus the fact that you’re hearing it from a teenager on the internet. And clearly my recollection really isn’t all that helpful. But it’s nice to know that the next Half Life game is being actively worked on!

Skaevola November 25, 2018, 12:35am #17
Medevilae:

Skeptical that Valve has large, intermixed, concept art piles, let alone lying about during remodeling of their new offices.

They weren’t intermixed. The Half Life ones were the only ones that seemed to be, like, stacked around, and I thought that was weird too. The Dota ones were hung up on corkboards, but I couldn’t actually tell what those were. I think they were just for the UI. And they wouldn’t let me near the Team Fortress 2 ones, and I think that they were just for animation, not for the game, because the guy leading me around said they’d just finished Meet the Medic.

And I’m not sure what you mean about the remodeling, but the offices were on floor five, six, and seven of the building it was in, and the guide-guy said they’d just acquired floors eight and nine (I think) and they expected to be up to floor 12 by 2013. So maybe they were remodeling them to match their new floors?

Also, I totally would have taken pictures, but he wouldn’t even let me take my backpack out of the lobby. I had to leave it behind the counter. Which is another reason I thought it was odd that I didn’t have to sign an NDA…

And no, didn’t see the gravity gun or tau cannon, but there was the motion-sensing sentry gun (I asked the guy if it ever got annoying and he said they tune it out) and a big ol’ rocket with miniature soldiers all over it. I don’t even know what game that was from. Also a cool trophy of a golden crowbar!

I hope Valve doesn’t hunt me down! I’m not trying to leak anything, and they didn’t tell me not to tell anyone. There was just a lot of cool stuff around that I want to tell people about!
They weren’t even, like, shy about letting me in. I sent a pretty grovelling email and the lady was like, “Sure, just talk to this guy and come down whenever.” I posted the email on my tumblr a couple months ago (xtaliesin.tumblr.com). You can read it if you want.
