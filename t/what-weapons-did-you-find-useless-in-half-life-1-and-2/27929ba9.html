Mangled November 24, 2018, 7:46pm #51

None of the weapons are absolutely useless, most serve as backups if you run out of ammo on your preferred weapon. They all have their individual uses, some are more useful than others.

The crowbar is your last backup weapon, you can’t classify it as useless. If you do then it means you’re doing it wrong. It consumes no ammo and is great for killing headcrabs and zombies, which in turn saves ammo. It should also be used to break crates and boxes to find more ammo. It’s fundamental to the game.

I found the Snarks, Grenades and Tripmines to be the least useful weapons in the game.

Snarks aren’t very reliable, and at the point in the game in which you get them, you’re already at a stage where you’re encountering the much harder enemies in the game like Agrunts and Soldiers very frequently, using Snarks leaves you quite vulnerable and defenseless, when you do attack with them you have to wait for the Snark(s) to reach the enemy and do their job whilst also preparing for the possibility of them turning around and coming back at you.

Grenades in HL1 do not handle intuitively like in HL2, so on the rare occasion where it’s appropriate to use (for indirect attack) them you’re likely to waste one or two using trial and error. That plus the fact that you can only carry a limited number makes them fairly useless

Tripmines are obviously very useless because there’s rarely an opportunity to use them, and if you do there’s no certainty (unless the enemy is part of a scripted event) that an enemy will set them off. You could plaster a corridor with them expecting a bunch of enemies to come charging down it and them not come. Generally they’re just more of a hindrance to you. In multiplayer they’re a treat though, easy kills.
