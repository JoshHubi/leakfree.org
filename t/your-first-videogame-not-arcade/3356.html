So, I’m interested in knowing what everyone’s FIRST video game was…and how old you were. Like I mentioned in the title however, arcade machines do not count…unless it was a port to a home console.

IDK but I may be unique, my first video game was the classic Sierra action/adventure game Space Quest. I was 1 year old when it was released and I remember sitting at my father’s “swan” computer playing the series with my older bother. When I was finally old enough to actually use the keyboard and play on my own, I was so young he had to make me cheat sheets with all the specific commands to get through the game (If you ever played those classic sierra games, you know the commands can be a little picky). I grew up with the entire series…I was 10 when SQ6 was released and was devastated when they canceled SQ7. I feel I was lucky to have grown up during the adventure gaming era, they are perfect for that age.

In addition the the SQ series, I also played The Colonel’s Bequest, another sierra adventure game…I still remember getting “super sleuth” for the first time. I also had an NES, but my roots are on the PC.

So…what about you?
