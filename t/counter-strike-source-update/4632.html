Yea, I see why some of you would be upset. Most of these bugs people are saying, messed up achievements screen and what not, are only for some people. I must have gotten a clean update because I’m getting none of these bugs at all. As I said, Valve will be releasing a few minor but helpful updates in the next month or two that will make the game and engine more stable.

I have found a fix for the so called “surrender animation” people have been getting. Type these commands in console to be rid of those horrid animations.
violence_agibs 1
violence_ablood 1
violence_hgibs 1
violence_hblood 1
There’s the fix.

IF YOU DO NOT LIKE THE FREEZE CAM: (Which I did not.)
Go to Options> Multiplayer> Advanced> and Check Off the “Do Not Show Freeze Cam” or something along those lines. It will get rid of the freeze cam or kill cam as some are saying.

As for frame-rate drops, that is just because of the new engine supporting better graphical advances. Advances like better HDR Lighting, better texture render quality, Multi-Core Rendering, Better AA and AS Quality, and the list goes on.

Why are people complaining about achievements? I find the achievements give the game a more fun experience because it gives you something to do if your bored. Go achievement hunting; it’s fun.

One more thing. People who complain about the forced 66 tick servers, there’s a logical reason for it and I shall tell you now.
The reason for 66 tick servers and not 100 tick anymore is because of the Orange Box Engine. A 66 Tick server on the OB engine is the equivalent of a 100 tick server on the EP1 Engine. 66 Tick on the OB Engine runs better and smoother and is more compatible with the engine and game play then a 100 tick would be. The 100 tick servers on OB were messed up and Valve forced 66 tick so the game play would be more stable and flow better.
