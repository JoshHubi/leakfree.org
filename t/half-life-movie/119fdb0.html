Why do so many people feel the need to try to make games into movies?

Most games tell a story, and if it’s a good game then it’s likely that the story was told well. Why can’t we leave it at that? Why can’t the game be a story’s delivery system? If the game’s so great, just play the damn game. You don’t need a movie to retell it again.

Books are one thing, games are another. Games are already a visual entertainment source, we don’t need to re-present each great game in a less interactive way.

The problem may be for the movie <> game relationship is that both movies and games get inspiration from books and other sources, so when the transition takes place the ideas behind them are distilled too much.
