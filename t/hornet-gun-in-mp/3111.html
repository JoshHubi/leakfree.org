Hornet gun in MP

Black Mesa Archive Suggestion Box
OneManArmy November 24, 2018, 8:07pm #1

One day I was playing HL1 multiplayer and suddenly this idea came in to my head. To me hornet gun isn’t a particularily interesting weapon to use in MP because of its randomness: you just spray hornets in all directions without actual aiming and then you can score a cheap kill. What I propose is the mechanism reminiscent of target locking system in guided rockets - before unleashing the swarm of merciless stinging insects on the head of your opponent you will need to hold a crosshair on the enemy for a second or two to lock on.
