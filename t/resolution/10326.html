pissed:

my resolution is 1366x768

1366x768 is ~16:9 (16 pixels wide for every 9 pixels tall). The aspect ratio option is only really there to “filter” the correct resolution options.

Select the aspect ratio of 16:9 FIRST, then you should see 1366x768 in your resolution drop-down. Save the setting after you’ve changed both.
