Flavrans November 25, 2018, 4:45am #46
JakSpoon:

How can i oper the Developer Console with another character from my Italian Keyboard??

It is possible to change the console key to something else in the game’s options:

2016-01-28_00003.jpg1920×1080 136 KB

From the main menu, click “Options”, then “Keyboard”. Scroll down the list and locate “Toggle developer console” under “Miscellaneous”. Click it and then click the “Edit key” button. Now press the key you would like to use for the console.

Note that the developer console key doesn’t work when you are in the menu screen (at least it doesn’t for me), only when you are in-game.

Also, it might be worth noting that the Inbound Tram Vox voice and the overhead announcements have not been translated in the Italian translation (nor in the Norwegian translation). So you will see English closed captions during the entire tram ride until you meet the guard at the lobby entrance.
