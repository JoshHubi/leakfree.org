pRopaaNS November 25, 2018, 2:52am #26

Lack of atmosphere is one of BMs flaws. Thanks for pointing out one of reasons why this atmosphere was lacking.
This is what I wrote in my early review:

…although game is great, but it lacks atmoshpere and anticipation, as I could quit game any moment without feeling any particular motivation to keep playing.

So, in other words, levels weren`t organized in chronological order gameplay wise, by correctly introducing new weapons and giving environment to try out and experiment with those weapons, which players would normallly want to do after picking up a weapon for the first time.

So, in that sense, we picked up magnum instead of shotgun, but after leaving area bunch of Vortigaunts spawned and mangnums slow fire rate and inablility to drop them with a single shot unless its a headshot made a bad first impression for this newly picked up gun. It wasnt an appropiate situation to use this weapon and, honestly, magnum isnt even suited against xen creatures, but against hecus, which is why it originally was indroduced when we were leaving blast pit. Shotgun itself was introduced right before running into literally horde of Houndeyes, whose numbers were too much to handle if youre not already familiar with this gun and if you werent already expecting such a big pack of enemies there. Could analyze all other guns as well, but I dont remember that much and I don`t really have time to write all this neither.

p.s. and yeah, it isnt just about weapons, but can be refered to other things as well. For example, as I mentioned meeting this horde of houndeyes after picking up shotgun, which was completely unexcepted and didnt follow any flow.
