Game is looking fucking amazing. How I’ve longed for unsubtle abrasive stealth.
My body couldn’t be more ready.

The thing I always hated about open-ended-choice type games is that they all seem to sacrifice ergonomics for gameplay potential. They just seem to jam a whole bunch of features into a game and leave things like weapon handling and (in most cases) AI being bottom rung material. Pretty much every recent game like this has excelled at failing at every aspect beyond interactivity imo. The weapons and AI in Human Revolution were terrible despite the good things about the game, and because everything relies on those two factors the experience felt pretty damn flat to me. Firing a weapon didn’t feel right with tiny ass gun sounds and fucked up aim acceleration that sucked with both the controller and the mouse. The enemies that don’t react at all to bullets and slump into a ragdoll in a dull fashion (ragdull) when their health goes kaput. Even the game’s melee combat boiled down to walking up to the enemy and holding the “win fight” button. There’s no impact to anything. Bioshock had similar issues to a lesser extent, with overly decorated flashy and obstructive crosshairs that get in between me and my target and convoluted menus that get in between me and my powers. The weapon handling and AI was ok, but it still has weird ass acceleration on the controller. It’s like the gameplay choice aspect has been an excuse to skimp out on the individual facets of the gameplay leaving everything dull. Kinda like how humans can run, swim, and jump, but fucking suck at it compared to a cheetah, a shark, or a frog, who specialize in that shit. Jack of all trade and master of none. All that shit.

I’m really thrilled to see that Dishonored has a real immediate and visceral feel about it. When he dropped the body I expected a slow careful body dropping animation that would take 5 seconds and feel like an eternity, but he just slammed it on the floor and went about the game. When he engaged in combat, it felt like branching well thought out COMBAT and not the phoned in holding down a trigger or mashing a button that you’d see in other titles that claim to be a “do anything” game. BULLETS MAKE PEOPLE DIE AND NOT FAINT. THEY TEAR THROUGH CLOTH AND FLESH LEAVING A CONE OF BLOOD COMING OUT OF THE ENEMY INSTEAD OF SPAWNING A RED PUFF AT THE END OF A RAY DRAWN FROM THE PLAYER. God damn it it’s just so CORRECT! It’s everything I ever wanted from this type of game! There is ZERO CLUNK. These games are usually ONE HUNDRED PERCENT CLUNK.

Fucking hell I can’t wait.

I can’t fucking wait.
