Maybe I was alone with this, maybe there are thousands more.

So, after more than a year I reinstalled Half-Life (steam version, on my Win8 64bit) to play it again. However, the moment I entered the main menu the screen went black.

To fix this :

  • launch Steam, and right click on the game. -> go to Properties -> select ‘set launch options’ and type this: “-nofbo”
