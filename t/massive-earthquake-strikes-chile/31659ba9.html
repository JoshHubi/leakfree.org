Well, things aren’t looking so good now. Since my aunt, my dad’s sister, who lived near the epicenter of the quake, got squished when her house collapsed, my dad and mom went there to check on things and bury her.

We live in the capital, and Chanco, the epicenter, is very far away, and the roads are pretty much fucked. My grandmother was in a different town at the moment of the quake so she doesn’t know her daughter is dead; besides, as far as we know my grandmother may be dead too, since she’s old.

My parents left on the car at noon yesterday with other of my dad’s sisters, and we haven’t heard of them since. It’s noon again now, so that’s 24 hours. The trip usually takes about 4 hours, now it’s supposed to be taking up to 10, so I am heavily worried right now. I’m hoping they’re okay, and that they’re not answering the phone because there is no signal, and not because they’re… dead or something.

Around the country around 7 cities got wiped my tsunamis, and looting is starting to be a real problem. I mean, seriously, I kinda can understand it if people steal food, but how can people be such dicks that they steal LCD televisions from stores? Makes me rage >.<
