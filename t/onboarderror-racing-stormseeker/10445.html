Not really racing, but the developer behind the last overhaul of Inbound and Anomalous Materials (probably more sorry) is trying to beat Stormseeker’s (the developer behind Surface Tension notably) Black Mesa speedrun record of 3hrs and 22mins.

Go here to see the TwitchTV feed.

It’s also a BMS Steam Group Community event. Go here to see it.

(Sorry this post is late so those who didn’t already know have missed a lot.)

Edit: I guess OBE did some of Surface Tension too so IDK.

Edit2: It’s over, he’s answering questions now, but that’ll be over in a few minutes. He got 2hrs and 27 minutes.

Some answered questions:
1.There was originally gonna be a part at the end where the Controllers try to dismantle the pod the scientist is in.
2.Surface Tension had some missing parts because the developer working on some parts quit mid development.
3.The OaR maps that filled more of the original chapter were apparently cut because they were developed by a sub-par team member who left as well.

There were definately more answered questions, I just don’t remember all. Please reply and add what you remember as well!

Good job OBE! It was interesting listening to your development commentary, please work on a commentary add-on! It’s not boring .
