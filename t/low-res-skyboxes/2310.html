Most of the textures are 512x512 and 256x256 (often used for the BSP) - the engine allows for the usual maps (diffuse, normal, specular etc.)
CoD is a fast-paced game, you are always on the move thus highly detailed textures would be a waste of resources.
The engines limitations are very tight when it comes to materials and such.
