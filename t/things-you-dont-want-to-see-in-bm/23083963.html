They are going to work on Deathmatch after the single player experience, afaik. All of their resources are going into Single Player right now.

What I don’t want to see…Killstreaks. I’ve seen people request these for a bunch of different games, the latest one being America’s Army. Seriously, they don’t really add anything to the game. It just makes the good gamers better, and the bad ones worse. Half of your gamerbase is going to be constantly murdered. It’s pointless.

Deathmatch-only multiplay. I like Domination, Capture the Flag, Juggernaught, Last Man Standing, Objective, and other gamemodes. It would be refreshing to see new gamemodes in the remade maps, and it would be better than just boring, bland old deathmatch. Probably not going to happen, but still…

Quick-time events. These frustrate me, especially if I can’t get them no matter what I do, or how fast or slow I do it. Plus, they don’t fit in well.

Enemies coming out of locked rooms with only one door. I’ve seen a few mods do this, and it happens in L4D, too. It doesn’t make sense, unless an alien teleported into the room.

A lack of direction. If the player spends fifteen to twenty minutes just trying to figure out where the hell to go next, that gets infuriating. I don’t want the game spoon-fed, but I also want to know where it expects me to go next. If I spend ten minutes lost, that’s the longest it should take. Five minutes lost should be the average, I think.

Torrent-only downloads. I’ve downloaded torrents that took a week to DL a 3 gig file. I’ve also had torrents take one minute for a file the same size. Their speeds are too unreliable. I’m not saying don’t use torrents, but I would like to see plain .rar downloads as well, for people that either can’t, or won’t, use torrents.
