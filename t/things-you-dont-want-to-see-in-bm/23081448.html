spicyHam November 24, 2018, 7:22pm #184

I don’t want to see the old style HL1 barnacles. It always irritated me how every barnacle drops 3 body parts when killed, usually a skull, leg/arm or chest gib. Is it really possible that every single barnacle manages to kill one person, even if it’s in an empty air vent maintenance shaft that no-one goes into?
and would scientists educated in theoretical physics really be dumb enough to run right into a barnacle’s tongue in an open space? they need to act more like hidden traps.
And if a barnacle manages to swallow someone, it shouldn’t deposit gibs if you kill it immediately afterwards… it would take time to digest a person. This would be a perfect opportunity for some of the gore lovers out there to see a corpse with the outer flesh stripped off fall down…

tbh i think barnacles are dumb altogether, like how did they even get there and grow so quickly after the resonance cascade? but they are a part of half-life i guess…

other things:

  • Get rid of or fix the pointless floating crate room!
  • remove the instant death punishment for touching a spinning vent fan. Either that or make the fans spin faster, so it’s more believeable.
  • slow down the crowbar attack speed and up the damage, at least to HL2 speeds. Those things are damn heavy, theres no way u could swing one as fast as u can in HL1, even with an HEV suit…
  • remove the interchangeability of mp5 and pistol ammo. Makes the pistol pointless, there are barely any underwater areas where you need it.
  • rifle grenades need timed fuses, like real ones, so they dont blow up in your face.
  • no annoying door squeak noise… why does every door in Black mesa sqeak the same?

And in general make the tech. more believable and interactive. I don’t want to see that dome generator with 2 buttons on the side of it,hidden up ladders, that leave you trapped when strange electricity prongs come out of the walls and enter it… doesn’t make any sense, and surely instead of a button push something more interactive could be used here?

Im sure i,ll think of some more later, i think ive said enough…
