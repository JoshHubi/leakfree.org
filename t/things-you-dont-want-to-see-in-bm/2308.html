Things you DON'T want to see in BM.

Black Mesa Archive Suggestion Box
Tei August 11, 2020, 2:23am #1

Yeah yeah yeah, we’ve read 'em all; things we WANT in Black Mesa. Helmets, mirrors, retcon character references, etc. in the last several months i don’t think there’s been any suggestions posted that haven’t been posted before.

So i propose we start a new fantasy list : Things you DON’T want in Black Mesa!

  • in the opening chapters if the game, if i hear any scientist ask, “Having fun yet, Gordon?” i’m going to lose it. Everybody says this to the newest hire in EVERY job. i don’t care if you’re stocking freight at your local Wal-Mart or managing a small team of production assistants, at every job there’s always one jackoff that asks, “Having fun yet”, every. freaking. day.

  • Fourth-wall breaking wordage regarding “health”, “guns”, “pressing USE keys” or any other jargon commonly read in videogame manuals, tutorials or out-of-game discussion. Real world references like Metal Gear Solid ARE FiNE! i just don’t want the 'wall broken by accident a la Episode:Two.

  • Every GoldSource game had the same stock sound effects for PC movement. Footstep sounds were terrible in the original Half-Life and thanks to the popularity of CounterStrike, i came to HATE the sound of boots on gravel. No matter what surface Gordon’s boots touch, it either crumbles beneath the weight of his feet ( CRUNCH crunch CRUNCH crunch ) or -oddly enough- squeaks and creeks. Either the tiles of Black Mesa’s floors are affixed to their foundation with springs or their maintenance staff needs to switch to a thicker floor wax. A quick sprint across the hall always sounds like a college basketball game. if Gordon’s feet are going to squeak that much, i want to turn around to see some black sole marks! Thank God this is the Source Engine.

  • if guards and HECU are supposed to be weapons experts, make them SOUND like it. There’s a clear and obvious difference between, “magazines” and “clips”. That being said, i AM NOT A WEAPONS EXPERT, nut, fanatic, enthusiast or collector . i don’t even own a real gun and i know the difference. Hollywood ALWAYS screws this up. Please please PUH-LEASE get it right in Black Mesa.

Obviously the game’s been in production for a while, it should be out soon and it’s way too late to start removing features and the like from the game. Nobody should expect any of this to be taken seriously. it’s a list of items to post, discuss and pass the time. A list of realistic expectations. A list of off-the-wall annoyances. A list for the hopeful, the impatient and the totally bored.

Have fun and feel free to post anything.

-Kawai Tei-

Neon Impulse:

wait; what are 1st,2nd and 3rd walls?

Technically speaking, the phrase “fourth wall” comes from the days of stage plays. You’d have the left wall, the back wall, the right wall, and the invisible “fourth wall” that the audience observes the goings-on through. The actors, however, are to treat that “fourth wall” as merely another wall. Breaking the fourth wall acknowledges the audience, often for humorous effect (“This is reality, Gordon, not some first person shooter.”).

Some feel references to gameplay mechanics by in-game characters breaks this fourth wall. I don’t know how the word “gun” does, or how “health” did in Ep2, considering it’s a decade or two in the future from now/in an alternative timeline where the slang for “walk-over-and-get-healed-packs” have been shortened to “health”.
