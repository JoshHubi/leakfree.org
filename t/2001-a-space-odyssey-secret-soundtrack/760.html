2001, A Space Odyssey Secret Soundtrack.

Imonfire August 11, 2020, 1:08am #1

Apparently, Pink Floyd made Ummagumma, Meddle and Atom Heart Mother in a way that they could play during the entire movie and fit perfectly. Here is how it should be:

"Start the sync right at the beginning of the film’s Overture. Play the songs in the exact order listed below until the end of the movie. Some versions of the movie, such as the DVD version, have an Intermission in them. If it does, pause the music during the Intermission. (On the DVD version, the word Intermission appears on the screen for a short time, then the screen goes blank. Resume the music at this moment because the actual movie has restarted. On the VHS version, there is no Intermission, so there is no need to pause the music.) “Entr’ acte” appears on the screen in some versions just before the next scene. Music is playing in the movie at this time.

Song order:

Meddle

  1. One of These Days

Ummagumma (1973 Cassette Side 2)

  1. Several Species of Small Furry Animals Gathered Together in a Cave and Grooving with a Pict

  2. Sysyphus: Parts I, II, III, and IV

  3. The Grand Vizier’s Garden Party: Part I - Entrance, Part II - Entertainment, and Part III - Exit

Ummagumma (1973 Cassette Side 1)

  1. Astronomy Domine

  2. The Narrow Way: Parts I, II, and III

  3. Grandchester Meadows

Atom Heart Mother

  1. Atom Heart Mother

Ummagumma - Live Album

  1. Careful with That Axe, Eugene

  2. Set the Controls for the Heart of the Sun

  3. A Saucerful of Secrets: a. Something Else, b. Syncopated Pandemonium, c. Storm Signal, and d. Celestial Voices

Meddle

  1. Echoes"

Source.
