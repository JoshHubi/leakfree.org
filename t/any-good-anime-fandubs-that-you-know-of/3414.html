I don’t understand why someone would want to do a fandub. I mean, maybe if its a bad dub or never got one, but could you really get enough people? Then, can you get enough talented people? And then, can you get enough talented people who sound older than 14? If you can, are you gonna pay them? Otherwise, are they all really bored, really good friends, or owe you tremendous debts? Supposing you had all this - why are you doing a fandub and not the official dub?!

Seriously though, when you consider the logistics, any hope of finding a good one kind of fades away.
