lexa2 November 25, 2018, 2:33am #4

Could you please post your hardware and software specs so we would be able to provide you with a more useful advices?

As for your problem: try reading sticky FAQ thread, especially parts about dxlevel and white line crossing the screen, there are no doubts that information applies to your case. As for “Failed to create D3D Device” - it is yet another known problem mostly affecting people having laptops with “Optimus” technology and people who use Intel GPUs to play the game (Intel HD 2000/3000/4000). Working workaround for this problem is to add “-windowed” to the startup command line of the Black Mesa and swith the game into desired resolution in fullscreen mode afterwards.
