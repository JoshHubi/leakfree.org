[it’s mean to be an easter egg, and a reference to one of the first teleporting tests in Half Life 2, a cat that has met some sort of… ‘problems’ during the test itself. In-game it could be an hidden crate that, once opened, leaves free a cat and maybe a small amount of non-lethal radiations]

[Headcrabs shouldn’t count, it would make this achievement harder and fair. Also triggering explosives with the gun should be avoided, the meaning is 1 kill per shot]

Ricochet: Kill an enemy with an arrow that has been deviated from a wall or similar
NOTE: I don’t know how is supposed to word the crossbow, but in HL2 the crossbow shots can bounce from wall IF the incident angle is almost parallel to the surface. In HL 1 they don’t explode if shot while aiming. This achievement was originally thought for the Tau cannon, but the incident angle should be too low to make sense. It could be changed with a Tau cannon achievement if the refraction angle is sufficient high, or simply higher than the crossbow one

BSOD : Destroy “X” turrets in the game
[this is a reference to the Blue Screen Of Death, relative to Windows system crashes. It could be referred as RROD, for the XBOX, but I prefer the first one. NOTE: it could be added the way how the turret has been destroyed]

Guglielmo Tell: Remove the headcrab of X zombies with the crossbow
[set the amount of kills and the class of zombies if it can make this achievement more challenging]

…And if that don’t work, use MORE guns!: Unload three clips of three different weapons on the same enemy
[the percentage of bullet that reach the target must be set, and so must be the number of weapons that have to be unloaded on the enemy. The target could be the Gargantuar, but it must be verified if other enemies can whitstand such a firepower. If yes, the Gargantuar could be excluded to make the achievement harder]

Can I introduce to you my sister Sasha?: Kill X enemies with the Gluon gun without removing the finger from the trigger

And yet it moves!: Survive to a fall with 1 hp
