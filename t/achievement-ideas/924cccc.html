I will agree that I’m not fond of grind-type achievements. Anything that could take the form “[Verb] [nouns]” is just some arbitrary number pulled out of the air, and I fucking-HATE magic numbers.

What separates doing it once and doing it times? It won’t be much different the next few iterations. I’d feel better if I didn’t have to look up what things to repeat, and instead got rewarded for doing weird things on accident (the first time).

  • Offer You Can’t Refuse: Kill a grunt by crowbarring his legs.
  • How Are You, GI Joe?: Whack a grunt’s hand.

See how much better?
