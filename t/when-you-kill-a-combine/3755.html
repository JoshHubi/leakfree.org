Pettifogg November 24, 2018, 8:49pm #7

It picks randomly from this selection of phrases:

“Unit down at [location] [location number].”
“Unit down at [location] [location number]. All teams respond code three.”
“Lost bio-signal for Protection Team unit [unit name] [unit number].”
“[Unit name] [unit number] unit deserviced. Remaining units contain.”
“[Unit name] [unit number] unit down at [location] [sector number].”

[Location] is the name of the area that you’re in, gathered from key words used in the map name.

[Location number] is a random number applied to the map.

[Unit name] is a random name given to the squad from a selection, and [unit number] is a random number given to the cop himself.

Everything the Metropolice and Combine Soldiers say are contained within “sentences.txt” in the scripts folder of “source engine.gcf” and suchlike. This includes the death sounds, etc.

Also, if you want to make them give a certain location when they’re talking you have to include one of these keywords in the name of the map:

trainstation, canals, eli, town, coast, prison, c17, citadel
