Wark November 24, 2018, 9:46pm #10

I’ve made a few maps that will never see the light of day, so what the hell.

de_greenhills was a hospital map with bombsites in the waiting and OR areas. Had lots of custom assets and teams would spawn at opposing parking lots. Had some pretty nifty features, one was a revolving door which in the end didn’t work well with any sort of ping.

de_mist was a Denver freeway overpass and irrigation system with bombsites in the control room and pump areas. Pine trees and cliffsides.

de_edge I’m still working on, but it’s a mall in the style of mirrors edge. Did some custom modern furniture and very clean bright colorful textures and storefront signs. Looking pretty good.
