lexa2 November 25, 2018, 2:31am #17

BM is a userland app, it can’t cause kernelspace hangs unless you have something wrong with drivers or hardware.

In case you have your GPU and sound card drivers up to date (Logitech USB thingy you have attached to your PC included) you have to suspect hardware problem.

Yes, BM uses Source engine but don’t expect it to be that easy on hardware - it doesn’t.

What I’d tried to do if I were experiencing the same problems you have:
a) Reverted CPU/GPU and system RAM to the default clocks in case I had overclocked any of these.
b) Installed nvProfile tool allowing to overclock GPU and used it to (A) force GPU FAN into maximum speed and (b) downclocked - yes, reduce freq - GPU and VRAM ~15-20% just to be sure it’s not overheating or power supply shortage problem.
c) Cleaned up all the fans inside the PC case from dust, replaced thermal glue under CPU cooler and under GPU heatsink with a fresh compound.
d) Performed memtest86+ test for at least 30 minutes to check if there are no errors related to system RAM.
e) Replaced PSU with brand new and more powerful one just to be on a safe side.
f) Started to replace internal parts of the PC one by one checking that the problem persists after each change.
g) Quit using computers.
