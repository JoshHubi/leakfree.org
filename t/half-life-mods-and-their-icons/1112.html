I had the distinguished pleasure of gaining assistance from another gamer that likes his steam list nice and tidy WITH icons next to the HL Mods. Marc Schröder; the Project Lead of Poke 646: Vendetta was happy to help me.

  1. Open the “liblist.gam” file for each mod (found in the root
    of the mod’s folder) with a text editor (such as Notepad)
  2. Add the following line to the file and save it.

icon “your_icon_name”

  1. Place a 16x16px TGA named “your_icon_name.tga” in the rood mod folder.
    (the name can be really anything you desire as long as it matches the
    given name in the liblist.gam)
  2. Launch steam to see your new icon showing.

This was a feature added with Steam so most pre-Steam mods don’t use it, even if they released a later Steam compatibility patch.
