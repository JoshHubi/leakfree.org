Which monitor are you replacing?

Depending on the quality you are used to from the old monitor, if you are buying cheap you might end up disaspointed.
For me, i am using the AOC F22+. Cheap 1080p, 60 FPS monitor with no special features (or hdmi input, but it is old though).

However, 7+ years in the running on settings:
Contrast: 50/100
Brightness: 80/100

Still running perfectly with no dead pixels or other faults.

Cons: The monitor isn’t placed on a stand, rather it’s designed to always be at an angle.
Bit annoying, but not that bad.

I don’t remember what i paid for it.

Conclusion:

Just buy any 1080p 60fps monitor from a respectable brand.
