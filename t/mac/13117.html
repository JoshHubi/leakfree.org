You can install Windows on your Mac through Bootcamp. I use my Mac for work and switch it over to Windows 8 periodically to access most all Steam games off my external hard drive (even if they have Mac support, they most often ALWAYS run better on Windows because of better support for graphics card drivers).

Black Mesa looked and played beautifully on my Macbook Pro 2010 (Gforce 320m, Intel i5 2.4Ghz). I’d say its worth going through the trouble of getting Windows on your machine!
