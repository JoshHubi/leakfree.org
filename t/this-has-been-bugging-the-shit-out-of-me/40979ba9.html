Crysis had some really great ideas. I love the idea of open-ended gameplay. I love the nanosuit - power armor with stealth camo. I also love how it looks great even if you can’t run it on maxed-out settings, and winding through that alien zero-G cave only to emerge on a flash frozen tropical Island was amazing and epic.

But Crysis also has some serious shortcomings. The voice acting is shit. At heart, it’s another goddamn military shooter (I hate space marines, I hate normal marines, I hate any scenario that puts you as a mindless grunt taking orders from some superior officer. It’s LAZY, HACKNEYED writing that gives us this endless barrage of military scenarios in shooters. It’s done, it’s played out, it’s rarely any fun). You have to manually pick up a weapon every time you need ammo, which is just annoying (apparently they fixed that with warhead, but I can’t be bothered to play it). And you usually have to run and hide if there are more than 3-4 enemies, even on EASY (you could call that combat realism, but considering you are wearing a nanosuit that allows you to jump like 25 feet high and run at 50 MPH, I say where the fuck does realism enter the picture?).

I’d give the game 2.5/5
