EvilGoodGuy November 25, 2018, 1:39am #22
Blushot69:

As for GD and OBM, will all the guns and the screen layout (i.e. position, font etc for HP, armour, ammo etc) be identical?

The weapons will all be shared for consistency.
The HUDs, however, will be fairly different. We are still experimenting with some ideas for the OBM HUD. Each mod has its own style to present, so we really want to cater to that.

Blushot69:

Is there a plan to produce a multiplayer free-for-all from these projects?

There is a plan to re-create Opposing Forces multiplayer for OBM, and some maps have already had quite a bit of work done on them.
Currently it’s taking a back seat to SP. But we plan to have the standard Deathmatch and Team Deathmatch for its release.
There has not been any real consensus of whether or not we will produce other game modes for it though.

I personally, would like to see us add other gamemodes for it such as Capture The Flag and something more original… maybe like a VIP sort of gamemode where security guard players have to escort scientists to a particular destination or to do some sort of objective while HECU players have to kill them. I also have a couple other ideas for more original game modes, but we have yet to discuss much, because MP is still very early in development and not the main focus right now.
I would also like to see the powerups that were present in opposing forces MP.

The way I see it is… why should we even bother working on a MP when its just gonna be the same old game modes which you see in every other game and mod? I feel like it should have something original which keeps people playing and attracts new players.
