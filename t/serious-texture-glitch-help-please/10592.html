ningen, had you tried to read first before posting answers?

2OP: Glitches looks like messed up triangles which 90% of the times means overheated or underpowered GPU with rest 10% being a driver errors. Knowing that this problem “auto-heals” for a brief moment after Alt-Tab-bing from the game most probably it is really an overheating GPU rather than a driver bug: you alt-tab from the game -> load on GPU lowers down -> GPU cools down a bit -> you get back into the game -> GPU can’t heat up to a “faults starting temperature” immediately thus it work OK for some time it takes to heat up.
If this is a laptop - you should service it (either yourself or bring it to a service company offering this kind of works) by “refreshing” the thermointerface between CPU/GPU cooler/radiator plate and chips themseleves. And another typical cause of the laptop’s overheating is a lot of garbage (cats/dogs hair, dust and alike) accumulated on the radiator plate inside the laptop making normal cooling air flow impossible. As a temporarily measure you could try to downclock your GPU though but having cooling system cleaned would be a better idea.
