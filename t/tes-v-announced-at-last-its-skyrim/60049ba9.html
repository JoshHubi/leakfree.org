New TES game? Awesome.
Dragons? Sweet.
New graphics engine? Sure, I’ll take that.

But honestly, use the engine you have correctly first. I remember being SO excited about Oblivion’s tech demo. Did anyone else geek out when they grabbed that manacles attached to the chain and moved it around for the first time?

Turns out that the physics in that game were not only useless and not integrated into gameplay, but cool little wow moments like that movable chain were few and far between in Oblivion. Not even the barrels were movable. Oblivion could have been a better game if they took advantage of the engine they had.

Now I’m honestly skeptical. Like… Fable skeptical. Will I even want to level? Is player jumping going to be fixed? Is combat going to be so bland that even the most idiotic modder can make it better? Is making friends going to consist of forcing you to insult them over and over? Will physics actually play a role in gameplay?

I mean, don’t get me wrong. Yay, pretties. But… I more want to throw a rock across the room to distract attention. I more want movement to be fluid. I more want fighting to make me feel awesome.
