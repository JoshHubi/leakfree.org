My FPS bounces up and down like a rubberband making the game move freakishly fast, then slow, then fast, then slow. i had the exact same stuttering problem with the original Half-Life and with Minecraft. The only way to fix it is to specify only one core by setting TESV.exe’s affinity to 1. …which sucks because my FPS drop quite a bit.

Ever so often my FPS drops to 0 for a few seconds. The game itself seems to play and advance just fine, audio keeps going and things keep happening but my screen is completely locked in place for about 6 seconds, effectively damning me if i’m in a fight.

in Morrowind, Oblivion, Fallout3 and Vegas, i use the vanity came A LOT. in Skyrim the vanity camera doesn’t work most of the time, and even when it does, it seems to take on different functions!. Half the time i press and hold the “change FOV” key to move the camera, it only moves in and out. Then the other half the time it lets me swivel, but NOT move up and down. Plus, it’s also impossible to swivel or move the camera to look around while moving, which is the whole reason i use the vanity cam!

And for some reason, the default FOV is set to “tunnel vision mode”. i managed to modify my FOV using the same old console commands from Oblivion but i still feel like i’m seeing the world through a keyhole. Plus, i’ll bet the using the console disabled half of the achievements in the game too.

On another note, i was hoping to make me a smaller, younger character. Unfortunately the only face options i have for Nords makes me look at least 40 years old. Nobody out there has put together any mods yet or shared how to make younger characters so i might have to wait a little while to play the game how i wanted.
