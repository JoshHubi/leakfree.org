Ryλn:

Some people have mentioned mouse lag…if anyone had a simple remedy for this problem with mouse lag and sensitivty, then do help a bro out.

I fixed my mouse lag first by editing a couple .ini files thus disabling vsync (I also used my nVidia drivers to force vsync off, but that’s optional):

…\steamapps\common\skyrim\Skyrim\SkyrimPrefs.ini
-and-
…\my documents\my games\Skyrim\Skyrim.ini

Add: iPresentInterval=0

to the bottom of the [Display] sections in both files.

Then I raised my graphics settings from Medium to High, which improved performance drastically for me. If your sensitivity won’t change, you can always try mouse control panel settings in Windows.
