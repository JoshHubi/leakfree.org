mankyman6 November 25, 2018, 1:06am #1952

Here’s my .ini I got from some website. It works pretty well, although you’ll need to change your resolution back.

Also change these if they aren’t already changed:

bTreesReceiveShadows=1
bDrawLandShadows=1

This makes everything cast shadows and makes casting shadows on trees possible.

EDIT: And if you wish to disable quest markers and the compass:

bShowFloatingQuestMarkers=0
bShowQuestMarkers=0
bShowCompass=0
