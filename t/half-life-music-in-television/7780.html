Orpheon November 25, 2018, 12:48am #8

Stock sounds and music. I remember not too long ago someone heard an instrument from the Half Life track Nepal Monastery in another song - we tracked it down for him and it turned out that it was used in the song Snakefood by Safri Duo, and originally came from a CD of stock music called ‘Ali Baba and the 40 Ravers’. This sort of thing happens all the time.

Examples: Check out the beginning of Daft Punk’s Da Funk and the ending of the ending of Gorillaz’s Dare. They both use the same stock crowd/background noise. The car honk at the beginning of Da Funk is also used in an traffic scene from the anime FLCL. The White Forest Base Alarm danielsangeo mentioned is also the tactical nuke alarm from Modern Warfare 2. The standard metal/metal hit sound used in most Source/Valve games was also used in an episode of MLP. And so on and so forth.
