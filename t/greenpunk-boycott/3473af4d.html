Steampunk: Machinery and inventions created after the age of steam-powered machines made to look like or made to be steam-powered, having extra elements such as wooden or brass fixtures, large gears, dials, or valves. Hence, the word “steam” in “steampunk”. For example: A steampunk computer:

Steampunk is the “alternate history” if 19th-century technology had been built upon.

It CAN be Victorian-inspired, but it doesn’t HAVE to be. A movie that is classic steampunk is “Wild Wild West”.
