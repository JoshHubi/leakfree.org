Here are some things you can change to improve performance on your laptop.
*

  • Make sure your game is not defaulting to the integrated video card.
  • In the Black Mesa options turn off dynamic muzzle flashes and god rays.
  • Decrease the resolution.
  • Turn off Anti-Aliasing, MSAA etc…
  • Try low or medium settings.
  • Turn off or turn down CSM.
