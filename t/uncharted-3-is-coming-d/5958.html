Turns out Marky Mark isn’t the only Uncharted newsmaker this week. Citing an “informed source”, Kotaku reported today that Nathan Drake’s next journey will take him to the desert, possibly in northern Africa. The source also stated that Uncharted 3 was set to be revealed “on or around” the Spike TV Video Game Awards.

Sony themselves have stated that a big announcement was on the way for December 12 in Hollywood, the day after the VGAs, so this rumor would seem lineup with an Uncharted 3 reveal in the coming weeks.
