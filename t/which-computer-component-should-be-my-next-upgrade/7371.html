I would upgrade your video card, 9400GT is about as powerful as the 4350 if I’m not mistaken, which is an HTPC classic. Video card first in your case. The CPU will hold at lower resolutions with newer games, the RAM will be needing upgrading if you want good gaming capability on games like battlefield 3 that at min take 2gb (which you’re fine with that) but recommended take 4gb ram and quad core processors. I have a dual core too. But the CPU should hold up for a little while. Sorry for being so long-winded but in short…the video card first. I upgraded from a 4350 1gb ddr2 to a 5670 1gb gddr5. but if you upgrade…i recommend the best memory type. currently that’s gddr5 and etc.

Me personally. I’d (if possible) upgrade your CPU first. The Core 2 Duo, is kind of a weak processor now-a-days. I’d upgrade to either an i7, or a Xeon.
