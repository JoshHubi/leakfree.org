Bonzai Bounce, my first Iphone game

Show Off
Spitfire August 11, 2020, 4:32am #1

Me and 2 friends finally finished our first iPhone and iPod touch game!
The game is called bonzai bounce and is a simple ball game with styleized graphics, i was responsible for creating and painting all the graphics while my friends worked on the technical aspecs of the game.

The game atm only works on the Iphone 3GS and Ipod Touch because of time and technical limitations but we are working on a patch to make it less heavy for older platforms.
