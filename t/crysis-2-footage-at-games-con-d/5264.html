It wasn’t so much the aliens, themselves, that ruined the gameplay, but rather is was how they were used.

They didn’t really have an exploitable weakness or some kind of special tactics you could develop to take them down, except maybe grabbing them since that would mean they automatically are dead when you throw them, but the grabbing mechanic was flaky for them, and they could one-shot melee you to death even on the easier difficulties.

Before, you were a little more free to approach things from various angles (though not EVERY angle, but it often had open camps and stuff to take out in the way you wanted), whereas the aliens were put along a much more narrow and linear path. The brittle frozen plants also couldn’t really provide cover anymore before they shattered, which made sense, but also made things less variable. Your enemies also didn’t necessarily operate in an organized way that could be analyzed. They just kinda meandered around.

Despite their flaws, they actually could have been fine if it wasn’t just a switch to ALIEN BATTLE NOW mode for the rest of the whole game. Crysis Warhead fixed this to a degree, mixing up the various combat situations throughout the game, as well as trying to improve ones people didn’t like before, and the game benefitted greatly from this.

AS FOR THE VIDEO, it actually raised my hopes. Gaining new suit abilities, customizing it and leveling up sounds delightfully addicting and Deus-Exy. Sure, it still looks like it’s taking a bit from Halo with anthropomorphized aliens and whatnot, but what they said sounded cool, and rather unexpected. Also, the graphics look better, but I don’t really care about the graphics much. It’s the candy on the icing on the cake.
