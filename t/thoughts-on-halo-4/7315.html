Ramirezoid November 25, 2018, 12:24am #10

reports say they’ve reworked the library somewhat for the reissue. Still giddy that you can press the back button and it reverts to the original graphics.

Anyway 343 has this quirk where every so often, they’ll gruseomely fuck the art style of Halo. Take the sleek cosmetic and aesthetic charms you’d find in the bungie-designed content and turn it into thick, chunky roundedness that shares nothing with the original design but overall silhouette and color scheme. See the CG segments of Halo Legends.
I don’t hate their new take on what I assume is still supposed to be Mk. 6 armor from the end of Halo 3, the one Chief would be wearing in the teaser trailer. It’s just so drastically different from what I know Mk. 6 is supposed to look like.
My hypothesis is that maybe 343 is using the model as a placeholder for the Halo 2/3 armor, and that the one featured in the trailer is actually Mark 7 or something that you’d receive at some point in the game.

Don’t honestly care all that much, It doesn’t look all that ugly when you analyze it, (I like the upper body and visor shape, myself) and I know I’ll probably grow to prefer it. Nothing looks as good as the original 2001 design, IMO.

I don’t know what they’re gonna do with multiplayer, Reach still has map packs coming out, especially with the release of Anniversary. I’m sure H4 will have its own component. Depending on what kind of gimmick it has I may or may not move out of Reach.
Halo 2 had online matchmaking and customization, Halo 3 had armor permutations, and Reach has superpowers, extensive backstabbing sequences, and outrageous customization.

if H4 includes all that, they’ll have to add something more, and that something more may or may not suck.

Halo is still going places and it’s still fun and enjoyable, and that’s just great. Reason not to die.
