sersoft November 24, 2018, 7:00pm #4

Make sure your windows disc is in the PC when you see the picture of the motherboard on the screen.

Press F8 or F10 or F12 to open the boot menu.

If that doesn’t work, try DEL to enter setup and change the boot order manually.

sersoft November 24, 2018, 7:00pm #6

Not really, the BIOS should list all your devices and tell you which keys you need to press to make the adjustment.

If it failed to boot upon selecting CD from the boot menu, I think there might be a problem with the disc or the disc drive. Check that all the sata/power cables are properly connected to the device.
