I realise that I’m guilty of necroposting here, but I guess I’m just a rebel.

Simply put, if you want small collections of society that make do with the bare minimum and don’t progress, anarchy is fine. It’s a life, and a good one.

However, if you want progress, knowledge, medicine and all those yummy things you’re going to have to leave anarchy behind. Think of two of the most progressive eras in history, for example: ancient Greece and the industrial revolution (bear in mind that I’m not an expert about ancient Greece, so if I make a mistake, please do point it out).

Ancient Greece had council systems and then democracy, and it was responsible the birth of natural philosophy, a.k.a. science, and developed a mind numbing plethora of theories, natural laws and inventions.

The industrial revolution was basically down to capitalism: if it weren’t for a few entrepreneurial cunts, we simply wouldn’t have modern technology. These were awful, greedy people, but, fuck, it was worth it for the simply astounding leaps in technology it all led to.
