Twitch Plays Pokemon.

So this is a thing that has been going on for about a week now (and I myself have discovered about a couple days ago). It’s a livestream that involves tens of thousands of people trying to play the same game of Pokemon Red at once.

It’s just as crazy as it sounds.

The stream uses an IRC bot that takes what’s entered into the stream chat and translates them into in-game commands. The bot has two states: “anarchy” is where it accepts all commands, even if there are more than the game can execute, and there is “democracy” where everyone votes on a button-push over a period of twenty seconds.

Already it’s spawned a few memes, such as the aforementioned Helix Fossil, streams of commands and randomness, and this almost irrational fear of the PC, as well as at least a dozen more.

You can see it for yourself here: https://www.twitch.tv/twitchplayspokemon
