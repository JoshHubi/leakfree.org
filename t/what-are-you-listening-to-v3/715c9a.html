speaking of mixes!

Thought I would give a mega (in this case giga) mix a try. A hair under 5 hour live to tape mix of 84 electronica, electrorama, electronique, house, mashups, dance, D&B, some Alternative-techno and even a video game remix thrown in for flavor.

Note that this is still a beta mix. Some of the transitions need some work also my system wasn’t properly configured so there are a few blips, buzzes, and skips but nothing that will blow your ear drums. ALSO there were a few songs that in hindsight I would have rather replaces with better tracks. Grab a copy here and let me know what you think. Also seeing as this is unfinished work, if there a song that you think would make a perfect addition to the collection or a song on there that kills the mix for you let me know! Hopefully by next week I’ll be ready to give the live mix to tape another go, hell I might even stream while making it!
