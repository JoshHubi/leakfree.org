Malidictus November 25, 2018, 2:18am #4

There seems to be an AI quirk where the Gargantua will ignore the player if the player is outside of its designated area, which is the large railway crossing chamber. In the original, the Gargantua followed the player regardless of where he went, leading to a moment where he’d stand right next to the “power up” button, making it hard to press. This one appears intentionally programmed to ignore anything that isn’t in the large open space.

He’ll actually do the same when you go into the generator room to kill him. He’ll chase you and shoot at you while you’re downstairs, but as soon as you climb up the ladder, he ignores you. You can walk up to the railing and stick your tongue out to him - nothing. You can stand on top of the console in full view and in line of fire - nothing. He’ll only shoot at you if you inch ahead far enough to be in his “chamber,” technically.

This does make the encounter SIGNIFICANTLY easier since the thing will ignore you most of the time, but it also makes it very non-threatening.
