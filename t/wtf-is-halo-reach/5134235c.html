Halo has generic gameplay that actually manages to be great regardless. It really is the best game I can think of for introducing people to the fps genre… If you forget that it is a console game, I mean.

Halo has a generic story: War for the universe, and the enemies will try to destroy the universe, unintentional, and only one guy can save everything. Sounds a bit like Star Wars, tbh. The only part that makes it any better is the fact it’s a religious war, which is uncommon in scifi.

The art style seems nothing better than the average scifi stuff. The brutes reminded me of PoA though, so that’s a plus.

Graphics are quite good for a console game. So good, in fact, that I dare call them amazing. It’s still no GoW III though. It’s not that good.

Overall, I give Halo an 9, with only downsides being the fact it’s a console game and the art style being (mostly) generic. The only reason it’s not a 7 though, is that I dont care that much for story. If I want story I’d watch a good movie (even though those are rare) or a good book.

Just my 2 cents.
