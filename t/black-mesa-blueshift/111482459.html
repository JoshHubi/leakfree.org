TehBoxNoob:

If you tell me where to get those, I love you for eternity.

I made them in Hammer, out of brushes. The red and green one are HL2 textures normally used for brush shipping containers, but they are used once in d1_trainstation_06 as (small and unusually designed) box cars. (See hereandhere). They look okay, not amazing. My favourite is the third one, which takes a door texture used once (afaik) in HL2 for a broken shipping container puzzle in Water Hazard. The wall I’m not 100% sure where it’s from, but it’s a Black Mesa texture. The two seemed similar, so I put them together, and they blended wonderfully!

The structure itself is very simple, I just joined 2 HL2 train005.mdl’s together, and hid the extra set of wheels in the middle by having the green metal ‘skirt’ extend down low enough to block them almost entirely. Hammer picture:

I use 2 because 1 is short, much too short for an American train imo. It worked fine for HL2’s trains, but I don’t think it fits modern American standards, where box cars are much longer and wider than HL2’s examples.
