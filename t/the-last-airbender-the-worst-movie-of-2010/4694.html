I just saw it, and I think it’s probably the worst movie I’ve ever seen. I went in expecting it to be terrible and was not disappointed. It’s only book one, but the ending suggests that they are planning for sequels (it even says “book one” in the opening).

Orpheon:

I hear they changed everything from cool to mega-retarded

Yeah. They jump around the story way too much. They tell a lot of the back-story through flashbacks that really don’t hold any relevance to the current scene.
The characters aren’t well developed at all. There’s barely any casual dialog between the trio. Everyone is serious almost all of the time. I think Aang laughs once, and that’s for about two seconds during a flashback.
A few of the lines are references to specific episodes in the series (the puppetmaster was one). The way they use them, it seems like they’re trying to provide fan service. In reality, it sounds like “geddit its liek form teh cratoon lolol <33333”
Then there’s the race issue. I don’t consider myself a racist person, but it’s really really distracting. They try to keep everything else in the movie looking and sounding asian, but almost all of the actors are Latinos. The fire nation are all darker-skinned Latinos. Even Ozai. It makes him look way less sinister.
In the movie, firebenders can’t create their own fire. They have to bend fire that already exists. During lots of fights they use swords and arrows instead of firebending. I guess this was to save money on cgi. There are a few scenes where it shows them apparently bending, but you can’t see anything happening.
Also Aang’s arrows are dumb.
