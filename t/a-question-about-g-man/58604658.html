Valve is notorious for being intentionally vague about canonicity. I could go on for a while about things Marc Laidlaw has said in that regard, but what it basically comes down to is this: Op4 and BS were created by Gearbox, with permission from Valve. Thus, Valve has the right to reuse or contradict elements from their games.

So yes, the Gearbox expansions can be considered canon, but if there are ever any conflicts between them and Valve’s games, Valve’s side is the correct one.

Google “Marc Laidlaw Vault” if you want to read up on Marc’s answers to fan questions like this.
