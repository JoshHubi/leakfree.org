Penny Arcade Report (I had no idea they had such a thing) just posted their interview with VALVe cofounder and managing director Gabe Newell.

https://penny-arcade.com/report/editorial-article/valves-gabe-newell-talks-wearable-computers-rewarding-players-and-whether-w/all

As I read through it, it is clear the parallels between VALVe and our friendly neighborhood BM development team. Gabe Newell explains that he mostly does odd jobs fixing up areas that need it, “Whether it’s game design, or business issues, or writing, or programming, or anything” which seems very similar to the recent rumblings of developers filling in areas others have been slow together to. He also talks about how they are working on HL Ep 3, but in a reaction to fans getting upset by delays they don’t want to show anything as not to get people’s hopes up before they are ready. I wonder if this philosophy is actually influenced by the state of BM or the other way around. Please, discuss. The link I have left below is a rough summary of the interview from GameTrailers.

https://www.gametrailers.com/side-mission/2012/02/20/gabe-newell-on-annoying-fans-terminal-sequelitis-wearable-computers/
