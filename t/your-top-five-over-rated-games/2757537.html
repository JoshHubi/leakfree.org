Stuntz:

Super Mario 64 was the first 3D game I played, back when I was… 4 maybe? 5? Im suprised no one else has mentioned Guitar Hero for being overrated yet, including the other hero game. DJ Hero. They SUCK.

I think you’re confusing ‘overrated’ with ‘bad by personal opinion’. An ‘overrated’ game is one which, despite being marketed as good, is, in fact, substantially less than what it was built up to be. A game which, prior to its release, is advertised as one which will cure cancer and make kittens pour from its bountiful awesomeness but which, upon playing, causes one to gauge one’s eyes out in pain is an overrated game.

Guitar Hero, on the other hand, is nothing more or less than what it is advertised as; its a music rhythm game where you play along to well known songs using a variety of instruments. Some people find it good, others do not, but I wouldn’t classify it at all as ‘overrated’ at all. True, some people believe that its the greatest gift to humanity since your mother, but they do so from their own opinion.

For a game to be ‘overrated’ means that, in the lead up to release, the populace has been misled to its actual quality for the purposes of boosting sales. If someone thinks a game is legitimately good, then that’s not making a game sound overrated, the person in question is just voicing his opinion. You may disagree as much as you want, but that doesn’t qualify as classifying the game was ‘overrated’.
