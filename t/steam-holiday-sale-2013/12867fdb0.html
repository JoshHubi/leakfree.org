geekofalltrades November 25, 2018, 3:51am #65

L4D’s story isn’t really relevant to the gameplay. That said, it’s fun to follow and piece together, because Valve are genius storytellers. Just dive in and start playing campaigns. Strictly speaking, I think the proper order for the L4D1 campaigns in No Mercy, Crash Course, Dead Air, Death Toll, then Blood Harvest, then The Sacrifice. The L4D2 campaigns go Dead Center, The Passing, Dark Carnival, Swamp Fever, Hard Rain, The Parish, and then Cold Stream is officially supported but not really considered canon.

There are also comics out that fill in some of the gaps between the campaigns. If you’re adamant that you must have the story, then be sure to read the comic that comes between Blood Harvest and The Sacrifice, because it fills in a lot of blanks that might otherwise make first few conversations of The Sacrifice kind of confusing.

Also, now that L4D2 is up for free, I’m guessing that means that people are going to be playing it again for a while. Finding people to play with was getting to be slim pickings. And now I’ve got a mic for the first time… time to dive in.
