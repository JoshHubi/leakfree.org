I’m just losing patience with Valve when now they’re announcing games that are just kinda, “Meh”.

I know there was a Gordon model in HL, but that’s a little different than having a model of him for a co-op game. Half-Life is not Left 4 Dead, I enjoyed the variety in their game types.

Half-Life was their ‘main’ series of a single player experience.

TF2, CS, DoD and HL: DM were their different varieties of MP games.

Portal series is their more puzzle-centric series with user created maps.

Left 4 Dead was the team based zombie shooter that actually had a story.

And even DOTA 2 could establish a new RTS series for them potentially, not necessarily under the same name.

But making HL co-op is just kinda like taking away the portal gun in Portal, especially if you’re playing as Freeman. (Decay does NOT count) He was never meant to be just another character for you and your buddies to play as while you fought the Combine, you were supposed to BE him. If you were playing as random rebels through a story, that might work, but it wouldn’t be a proper sequel to Gordon’s story.

I don’t know what Valves gonna do with HL3, but I seriously hope it’s not a co-op like L4D. I don’t know what their deal is with the single player ‘forever alone’ type now anyway. It’s worked well so far.
