higgsboson November 24, 2018, 11:05pm #27

It would indeed make sense, and the other thing is that the developers don’t have to care that much about what we think. If they give us media, a release date, or progress updates, people on the forum will start speculating, complaining and moaning, probably, but so what? No one said they have to please us, or care about what we think, or even read what we say on the forums. So saying that they don’t want to give us media because they think we will never be satisfied is just like saying that they will give us media but not care about our reaction.

Anyway, the thing is that big companies like Valve generally give a release date but make no attempt to reach it. Then they give us a more precise release date fairly close to the actual release, just as we expect from the Black Mesa team. So it makes sense to say that giving a release date is pointless, as it is almost entirely random and will only probably cause disappointment that could have been avoided by not saying anything.

Does Valve release media? Do they say how much more work they have to do? No. They probably have a straightforward reason for that. The only thing they do is give us release dates, and they keep missing them, which only leads to people complaining and making the company look stupid.

For a comparison, we have no idea whether Episode 3 is almost done, half-done, or not even started. Then if they do end up doing it, they will announce it one day and say that it will be released in 2011 or whatever. And then there will be a delay until 2012 and then eventually after missing 2-3 more deadlines, they will release it at some point. But Valve is professional and a big company and they have loads of experience with this stuff, and even they cannot predict anything accurately at all.

So it makes sense that the Black Mesa team can’t say anything for certain. I’m sure they could tell us that it won’t be released within the next 3 weeks or whatever, but maybe it’s not worth it for them. Maybe they’re really close to the release, and they have been really close for the past 2 months, but still cannot predict whether it will be done in 1 week or 1 months. This kind of stuff is very complicated and impossible to predict.
