CygNuS November 24, 2018, 11:05pm #1

If it’s not possible for the devs to give us a release date or progress update, so be it.

The way it is now is that for us, the mod could be ready tomorrow, next week, next month, or 6 months from now, so we check back every day. Crazy theories arise and everyone checks everywhere for hints, and that’s perfectly normal.

So why not just tell us in what period we definitely DON’T need to expect the mod to be released?

For example:
“Guys, don’t expect the mod to release in the following 6 months. That doesn’t mean that after those 6 months it will be ready though, but at least you know you don’t have to check here every day. After those 6 months we can tell you what’s up then (perhaps the same)”

After initial bitching and hating, it gets us off your backs for 6 months and we’ll see then. Everyone happy. Nobody lied. No more crazy speculation till then.

Answer from developer CatzEyes (page 3; 24/12/2010):

Breaking news folks…

I am sorry but do not expect the game to come out within the next two months.

That is all. :wipes the tears from her eyes:
