CatzEyes93 August 11, 2020, 3:18am #42

Aussie Fries (apologies to those that would slap me for that) A true fav in my house.

Ingredients

  • Deep Fryer (I use a fry daddy)
  • 24 Fl. Oz. Peanut Oil for the fry daddy
  • Frozen French Fries
  • 1 Cup shredded Colby Jack cheese
  • 6 pieces of Bacon, cooked

Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 C. Sour Cream
  • 1 Tbsp. Prepared Horseraddish
  • dash Cayenne Pepper
  • dash Salt
  • dash black pepper

Directions
Heat fry daddy oil to 350 degrees.

  1. Fry the potatoes in small batches, they are done when they are golden brown, and float to the top of the skillet.
  2. Drain the potatoes on paper towels. You can keep them warm in the oven while the other fries are done.
  3. When all french fries are done cooking and drained, place them onto a platter that can be baked in the oven.
  4. Salt the french fries as you like, sprinkle the cheese on the top, then sprinkle the cooked bacon on top.
  5. Put the fries back into a warm oven until the cheese begins to melt.
  6. If you want to add thin sliced green onions… do it after oven.

DIPPING SAUCE:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
