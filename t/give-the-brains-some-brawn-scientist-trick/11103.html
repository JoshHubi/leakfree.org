To save myself, some typing, here’s my post from Facepunch:

So I was messing around in Black Mesa making NPC battles when I discovered that I could arm the scientists with weapons. Turns out that if you give them something to fight with, the normally meek scientists become effective fighters. Guess there’s a Gordon Freeman in everybody.




For best results, equip them with the MP5. It works with other weapons, but I notice they’re a bit more hesitant to use them. I’m not sure if you can give them firepower with “npc_create_equipment”, but what I did was:

ent_fire !picker giveweapon weapon_weaponnamehere

And when using “ent_fire !picker”, make sure your crosshair is aimed on the NPC you wish to give the weapon to.

Enjoy your squad of super geniuses.

Hope you guys have fun with this!
