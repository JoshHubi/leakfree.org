GenericPlayerX November 25, 2018, 12:55am #47

Crowbar. No game gave me a bigger rush than the time I found out by accident that HL1 was cool enough to let you actually beat the end-boss, even as he is floating hundreds of feet above the ground, to death with a f***ing crowbar. I was playing my first time and I thought you had to do something complicated like shoot rockets into the brain from above. So I was jumping over and missing every time. Eventually, I jumped over and landed on the SOB when I mistimed the leap. Biggest surprise of my life. So I started to unload my SMG, only to find it empty and so I whipped out the crowbar and whaled on it.

  What other games give you that chance? Few, if any! They all make it a puzzle where you power-up a super-cannon, shoot the left eye three times and the right eye two times to open up its belly, or just unload your most powerful gun, reload, and repeat 100 times. But Valve said, "Let they who have the guts to climb and jump onto the monster be given the right to smack the SOB's brain with a small, bent piece of metal with some nicks in the paint-job." Beautiful...
