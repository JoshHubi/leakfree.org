Jeannotvb November 24, 2018, 9:49pm #8

Never go for dual-GPU if you’re in the low-mid range. Why? Microstuttering.
Unless your computer has the power to pump out more frames per second than your monitors refresh rate you should stay away from dual-GPU’s. Many people including myself have had experience with microstuttering and it makes playing with 50 fps worse than 25 fps on a single-GPU videocard.

And yes, the problem still exists with the newer line of ATI and NVIDIA cards.
