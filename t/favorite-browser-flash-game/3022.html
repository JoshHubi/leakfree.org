Here you can post your most favorite browser- or flash game. It could be anything from a mmorpg to a tetris clone or something in between

Anyway, here is my current favorite flash game:

https://www.kongregate.com/games/Ivory/6-differences

It is called 6 differences and is probably the best “Spot the Difference” game i have ever played. It portraits Night life through pictures with a couple of small differences in each of them. These differences are not small pixels or somethings like that (like some other games), but things that could be what the picture is focused on. Things you may only notice subconsiously if you only take a quick look at the pictures. The atmosphere in the game is also very special, thanks to excellent music and programming. I would recommend you to play through this one, if you manage.
(The hint button is “H”)

Orpheon August 11, 2020, 2:58am #4

Nanaca Crash.

Hit a dude with your bike and get him to fly as far as you can. Once you hit him he enters a track with a randomly repeating set of charcters that have different effects on him (e.g. some hit him to keep him going, some slow him down, and one just ends the round out right). Well-timed hits from your bike can guide him to or away from the characters you want. It’s a little difficult to explain, but once you start playing it’s simple and addictive. It’s based on a hentai PS2 game but the flash game is completely SFW/family friendly. Oldie but goodie.
