Dias Mansion - Price & Practicality Be Damned

Dias November 25, 2018, 1:23am #1

Recently I started coming up with ideas for designing a house out of boredom. Since it was something I knew I’d never actually build, so I started making it bigger & adding more & more over-the-top expensive, impractical stuff to it. Sadly I’ve pretty much run out of junk to add to it asside from currently-impossible technology like teleporters & room-specific gravity control. I was thinking that if I do ever finish designing it I could make it a playable multiplayer map in hammer just for the hell of it. Although it seems I keep starting over from scratch any time I add a lot of things so it might never get done.

Some of the things I might add:

  • lazy river moat
  • inset porch behind drawbridge
  • intercom system
  • sauna
  • guest bedrooms
  • movie theatre
  • fallout shelter
  • no library, but a server room
  • rooftop terrace
  • balconies
  • castle-style battlements
  • helicopter landing pad
  • hot tubs in the bathrooms
  • olympic-sized swimming pool outdoor/indoor convertable with a retractable awning & curtian walls
  • automated double pocket doors with movement prediction
  • elevators & escalators instead of stairs
  • waterfall connects from roof to pool or moat
  • electrochromic smart windows
  • hydronic radiant underfloor heating
  • moving walkways

I’d love to hear some more ideas for things to add, althought I’d like to keep the house/castle/mansion/thing somewhat realistic structurally. Fictional things like portals could be added so long as they aren’t there solely to access otherwise inaccessable areas.
