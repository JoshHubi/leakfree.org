I don’t know how old news this is, but I couldn’t find it in here! Tim Shafer (Psychonauts, Grim Fandango, Day of the Tentacle) and Ron Gilbert (Monkey Island) are working on a new adventure game, but can’t get a publisher to fund it, so they got the fans to do it instead!

Kickstarter project

You can still donate of course, and $15 for a pre-order, beta access, video series of them making the game, and private forum access doesn’t seem bad to me.
