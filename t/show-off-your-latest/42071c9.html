Someonerandm:

Aren’t procedurals textures, requiring something for them to be placed on?

A couple pages old, but still, bit of a stretch there…

Something procedural is generated randomly, or generated through some sort of algorithm…terrain in Terragen is procedural, perhaps the input mesh for the terrain model was generated procedurally…but if it was truly procedural it would be different every time. (L4D/2 incorporate ‘procedural’ gameplay by randomly placing things, some levels have procedural level generation where they assemble a level from various tiles or pieces, etc…) A procedural texture would be a texture map that’s generated by some sort of algorithm or filter, if you will, as opposed to just a handpainted bitmap.

If we’re going to look at it that way, I suppose you could call the ST vista scene ‘procedural’…
