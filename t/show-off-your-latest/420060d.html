Bolteh August 11, 2020, 2:40am #924

Ye indeed. You always have to keep in mind that a logo has to fit on any type of background and in any size. If you really want to smooth it out, you could add a faded shadow around the edges (with no distance nor direction) so it can remain strong on lighter background.

Also, I’d opt for slightly less popping colors. A slightly more orange yellow, and a slightly faded orange. The black could also do with a dark dark gray, so it would benefit from the gloss you added. My main problem is the gear-bit in the middle. All the other stuff in the logo has this shadow/gloss thing going on, but the yellow-black bits are as flat as Fats Domino’s face.

I did a similar logo last summer for a WoW guild I was in (not saying it’s any better, just showing what I meant with what I said above)
