Jeannotvb November 24, 2018, 9:56pm #2272

I advise anyone never to use chamfers when trying to maintain the topology of your model when you’re using smoothing techniques. Why? Because they cannot be undone, a simple edge ring or loop will do the same trick. An edge ring/loop can be removed at any time because it does not modify the shape of the model unlike a chamfer.
