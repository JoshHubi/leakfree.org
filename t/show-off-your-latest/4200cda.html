Actually surprisingly little of that is displacements. I felt like texture blending was a little limiting on something of this size, so most of it is a static prop with a 2k texture map.

If you’re having problems with displacements, you’d be surprised how much looking at reference photos helps. Most people tend to subdivide and pull out some verts, but if you know what you’re trying to create before you start, it ends up looking a lot more natural, especially when you start putting textures on it.
