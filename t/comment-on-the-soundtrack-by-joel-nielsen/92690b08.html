FrontLineAssembly November 25, 2018, 2:13am #162

HL1 and Black Mesa’s soundtrack are = Equally good. I see what Joel did, a hybrid between Kelly Bailey, 90s Metal, and Orchestral music. It fits good together.

I liked that he didn’t remix the original tracks, they were already perfect and couldn’t be made better, instead he tried something different. I like that. It’d be boring to only have one kind of music for so long, changes in textures has to be made often to make it interesting.

I fail to see why all the complainers call this “Typical orchestral music” It is not typical at all, there are huge differences between this soundtrack and the million generic CoD/Modern-military type soundtracks, its also more electronic and less orchestral than that. Most of it was sampled electronically with additional live-instrument samples put in. Good job with the soundtrack Joel! This was worth it.

Congrats.

Joel_Nielsen November 25, 2018, 2:13am #168
sps96:

Quick question…was Joel allowed to play the mod and get a feel of the levels to properly add the corresponding music sequences?

Yes, I was actually quite involved in the development. I did all the sound effects in the mod, (including the ambient sound scripting for each level), provided game play ideas / feedback (some of it actually made it into the game ), and tested the game alongside all the other devs.

Other than the Vox work, everything else you hear in the game is my fault.
