TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:27am #2

To fix the language thing, try adding -language english to the launch options. Our font currently does not support other character sets.

The fullbright occurs because mat_hdr_level is not 2, or dxlevel is below 9. Try starting up the game with -dxlevel 95, and then setting mat_hdr_level 2 in the console. We are looking into both issues as we speak.
