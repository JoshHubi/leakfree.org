I found a funny glitch with the Bullsquid :

If you throw a grenade to a Bullsquid, but the explosion doesn’t kill him, he’ll get angry and hit you if you’re in range, no matter what’s between him and you (it can be a wall, a box etc.).

This is a bit annoying, especially if it makes you drop 100ft into Radioactive Water xD.
