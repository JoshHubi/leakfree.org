ok, a while ago I noticed that when you pick up the assassins glocks they replace yours but do nothing different.

this got me thinking why not have it so that when the player picks up the assassins glock they get an improved pistol with new stats.

this idea is pretty basic as tehre would only be two chances to get it and one of them you will lose it right afterwords. (but might be good for xen).

concept

tactical glock
pick up animation: Gordon looks at the glock he has then the one he just picked up and tosses the one he had away.
looks: teh glock with an extended mag and extended barrel/suppessor
random google image to show: https://www.thefirearmblog.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/IMG_1043.jpg

differences

loaded ammo: 28
recoil: reduced by 25%(not really noticable)
automatic fire: accuracy increased by 50%.

and that would be it, no real improvements but it makes teh automatic fire mode more useful at longer range and reduces need to reload.

yeah likely a really bad idea, but eh posting do to beign bored.
