Alright, let’s do this.

It’s incredibly awesome how much dialogue is in Anomalous materials. I actually didn’t hear all of it until my third or fourth playthrough, when I finally decided to backtrack a bit after getting the suit. There were at least three conversations that I hadn’t noticed before.

It’s nice to hear them express themselves a bit more before I start laying tripmines everywhere and watching the gibs fly.

I also really, really love the gargantua. It may not be as bulky as the original, but it’s just as terrifying, maybe even more so. That big glowing eye it has is just awesome. And its death scene was really satisfying.

Oh, also I like the pools of blood under dead NPCs. It makes the aftermath of a big fight look just that much messier.
