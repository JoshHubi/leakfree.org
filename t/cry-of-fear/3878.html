Has anyone here ever played Afraid of Monsters? It was a small little SP mod for Half-Life that was good at being fucking scary while screwing with your head. Heres the MODDB page for the directors cut:

https://www.moddb.com/mods/afraid-of-monsters-dc

Anyways, the guy who made this has been working on another mod, called Cry of Fear. It uses a modified version of the GoldSource Paranoia engine…meaning that you’re still playing a Half Life mod but with visuals that don’t shit all over your eyeballs.

https://www.moddb.com/mods/cry-of-fear/videos/review-of-the-cof-demo#imagebox

So yeah, I have been following this mod for a while. Besides BMS, this is my other most anticipated mod…
