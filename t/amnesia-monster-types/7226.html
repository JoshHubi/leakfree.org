So I’ve been noticing that many people post in forums asking what type of monsters and how MANY Amnesia has.

For what I’ve seen, I believe there are 5 types:

First monster – Servants/Grunts: They’re the main monster so as well the more common one. They have a severed/tortured jaw, a demented left arm with claws and ‘reptile’ boogy eyes;
Pic: https://i1224.photobucket.com/albums/ee367/Ch1kara/800px-AmnesiaServent.jpg

Second monster – Brutes: They’re the second monster that you normally encounter and they’re faster than the Grunts. They have a ‘smashed’ abnormal face, a bloody blade IN their left arm and lots of nails in their legs. (which actually makes me think about Frankeinstein monsters but whatever);
Pic: https://fc04.deviantart.net/fs70/i/2010/276/e/4/amnesia_is_scary_by_redroses19-d300asr.jpg

Third monster – The Water Monster (I call him “Splashy”): He’s an invisible monster that can kill you only when you’re in water. He’s attracted by sound and movement. (I’d suggest something to throw and distract him from your path. Smart way. );

Fourth monster – The Shadow: An unseen force. Its sole purpose is to guard the Orb that Daniel took from the burial chamber and has killed numerous people to achieve that end. It is not seen as a physical being, it’s described as a huge sluggish mass of darkness and leaves behind a fleshy damaging substance over anything it touches. Pic of the fleshy thing: https://images3.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20101217222138/amnesia/images/b/b1/ShadowResidue.jpg (It is disgusting, I know);

NOW the FIFTH monster is actually found in the game “Amnesia: Justine DLC” and is more seemed as a tortured prisioner.
They’re skinny and have a bloody wheel around their neck. Their face is actually very seemed at Agripa’s Lol and they drag with them a prison chain iron ball if I’m not mistaken. Their sounds are like… seems like they’re being strangled or even like they’re in need of air to breath. (In my opinion sounds more like an old man having a heart attack after a “good time”…).

Actually for what I’ve understood, they’re not really monsters. They’re 3 of the characters that are mentioned in the game (Not gonna spoil for you saying who) that were tortured by Justine – the character we play with -. Justine for what it seems and as the story explains? It’s a woman that was born with mental problems, so she sees who she tortured (that are important people or were…) as horrible monsters that want to kill her for vengeance. (So yeah she sees them in a distorted way, making them seem like monsters because she’s frightened).
I haven’t found many pics of it yet, but I’ve been able to find one where it can actually be rather visible: Pic: https://fc08.deviantart.net/fs70/i/2011/106/7/4/amnesia__justine__ohshit_by_deejaycannibal-d3e3k4o.jpg

So yeah, that’s pretty much like it, hope I helped for who wanted to know. :>
