Move the spawn point entities slightly up into the air, about 4-8 units of height. That should allow you to spawn.
Your map is dark because you’ve made a gigantic skybox box on top of it.
You have to make the sky out of walls and a ceiling, the skybox works just like any other regular block.
Skybox Tutorial

And make sure you have texture lock checked, the icon for it looks like ‘tl[/size]’ , without having it checked you get streched textures.

  • The .vmf is your mapfile, not the .vtf thats a texture file
