sersoft November 24, 2018, 8:00pm #2

The ipod only plays mp4 encoded video, if you input avi files into iTunes it will just convert them into mp4 and transfer that to the iPod.

Anyway AVI is a container, not a codec, with iPodlinux you can only play 1 avi format at about 15fps max and with Rockbox you can play 30fps mpeg2 files.
