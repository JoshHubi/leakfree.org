Orpheon November 25, 2018, 12:48am #27

Half Life, for the most part, took place in an American lab with alien creatures invading and messing everything up. Half Life 2 took place years later in Eastern Europe with the Combine invading and messing everything up. The shift in environments, enemies, and situation logically dictated a new art style. If the next installment changes that drastically as well, then it should also have a new art style. Personally, I’m betting it will look a lot different from both Half Life 1 and 2. Valve doesn’t like to retread old ground in their games, and I agree with them. Why keep going back to the same place, remaking the same places, using the same style? Of course, this doesn’t please everyone but it’s what they want to do. I don’t expect the next Half Life to be any more similar to 1 or 2 than 1 and 2 were to each other, but I do expect it to be up to Valve’s high standards of quality.

Disturbulator November 25, 2018, 12:48am #30

It’s funny how you’re all using Portal and Portal 2 as an analogy to how (Half-Life 2: Episode 3/ Half-Life 3) will differ from the episodic models.

Portal was just a test-game VALVe threw into the Orange Box to see how the community would like it. That’s why Portal 1 was bland in comparison to Portal 2, VALVe didn’t want to spend time, effort, and money into something the community might reject.
When everyone exploded over Portal, VALVe went all-out on Portal 2. This time around, they redesigned characters, models, and soundtracks to the style and feel that they had originally intended.

Half-Life 2 was not a test, and everything that’s in the game is the way they want it to look, so the episodes did not change much at all in comparison to Half-Life 2, because there are no restrictions depending on whether or not the community will accept or reject it. And I’m willing to bet that yes, episode 3 will be run on the same updated engine as Portal 2, but no, Episode 3 will not be very stylistically different from its episodic counterparts.

AS for Half-Life 3…nobody knows yet.
So why are we arguing over this?
