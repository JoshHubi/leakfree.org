JamesKane November 24, 2018, 8:57pm #8

The “Beam locking” is a bug where the fx was prevented from being destroyed.

The end point of the beam is computed only when the primary fire button is down. So, when you trigger the bug not only does the beam still show up but the end point does not update. Hence it looks like it is targeting something, but it is not.
