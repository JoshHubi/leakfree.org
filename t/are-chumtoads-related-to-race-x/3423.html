what do you think

I think you’re putting way too much thought into this.

Seriously though - nice theory! But I don’t think it’s correct. Chumtoads also appear in the game files of Half-Life itself. When Half-Life was released Race-X hadn’t been “invented” yet. Race-X first appeared in the Gearbox expansion “Half-Life Opposing Force”, which came out later.

There’s more information on the Chumtoads in the book “Raising the bar”. I believe it says that Chumtoads were initially designed to be some kind of weapon like the Snarks, but were dropped somewhere in the development cycle of Half-Life. I can recommend reading the book. It gives lots of information on the thought-process behind and development of the Half-Life & Half-Life 2 games. It’s a good read if you are a fan of the series.

Update: The Combine Overwiki also has some information on the Chumtoads. Interesting part is that apparently, at one point, the Chumtoads were even designed to have six eyes.
