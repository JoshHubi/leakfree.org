3.- Loading Times take way too long.
At the beginning, It had to take 45 seconds to more than a whole minute to load the next screen. Settings were on high (and recommended for my system by the game) on Model, Texture and Shadow Details. After reducing them to Medium the Loading times now take 20-30 seconds.
One could say “Your system sucks”
But my Loading times at Half Life 2 games take less than 10 seconds and their settings are on High by default. So it’s something with this particular game.

4.- I’ve got this one today after the 0.1.0 update.
I left my character at certain part of Residue Processing. The 1st person view displayed Gordon’s HEV suit gloves and suit (obviously)
But when i Loaded my game AFTER the update… Gordon became a HEADCRAB ZOMBIE! :

The ONLY things i modified AFTER the update was selecting the G-man on multiplayer and i played my first match on it. (It’s awesome)
EDIT I just had to start a new save file. No worries!
