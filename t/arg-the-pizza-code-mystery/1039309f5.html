Old Black Mesa joke. Basically, some guy by the name of ‘Toma Basarab’ applied to the team at some point and this was his example work. He called this one a ‘pingun’ and a very similar work in which he ‘quaffed to maek dog’. Basically it’s been a joke ever since, as there’s been a number of… derivative works… done by the team:


https://soundcloud.com/bacon333/tomabasarabcallsin

Then, when someone asked who “-TB” was, the signature on the letter from the main page, pre-launch. Ram answered saying it was Toma Basarab.
