Brute forcing is a method which you generate every combination of key that could decrypt the encrypted data. Cryptanalysis is a method used for analyzing encrypted data (who would have known) and attempt to learn information about the data.

“Brute Force Attacks. Unlike hacks that focus on vulnerabilities in software, a Brute Force Attack aims at being the simplest kind of method to gain access to a site: it tries usernames and passwords, over and over again, until it gets in.”

“Cryptanalysis refers to the study of ciphers, ciphertext, or cryptosystems (that is, to secret code systems) with a view to finding weaknesses in them that will permit retrieval of the plaintext from the ciphertext, without necessarily knowing the key or the algorithm.”

If you make the encrypted data readable, then you have decrypted it.
