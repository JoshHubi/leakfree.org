You can know for sure if pizza is in a map by using this command in the console:

r_drawmodelstatsoverlay 1

Then filter on pizza. Click on the found textures and it will give you the option to make it blink in game.

The Philosopher’s Stone is a mythical alchemy item that could turn normal metal into gold. The story has been around a lot longer than Harry Potter. In the context of the game, the scientists are researching nuclear alchemy. By adding or removing protons from an atom it can be changed to a completely different element, like gold.

And yah, radioactive decay and half life are closely related. heh
