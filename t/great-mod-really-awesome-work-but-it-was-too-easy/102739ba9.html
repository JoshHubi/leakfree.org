Some aliens however seem disproportionately weak even on hard, especially compared to HL1:
-I like the fact that now Houndeyes can be a threat if they catch you with your pants down.
Great change here.

-The new Bullsquid spit is great, but their health nerf makes them too quick to dispatch.
In HL1 you needed 2 grenades/3 magnum/4 shotgun hits to kill one.
In BMS you need 1 grenade/2 magnum/2 shotgun hits, even on hard.
This makes the improved spit a non-issue since you don’t need to dodge more than 1 salvo before you kill it.

-The Vortigaunt charges and fires much faster but the damage nerf makes them dangerous only in 3+ groups. Even then, they’re kinds squishy.
They seem work better as lower damage with a spammy attack, just add more of them on hard.

-The Ichthyosaur is incredibly weak compared to HL1, I mean 2 crossbow hits?!

-Alien Grunts seem ok as they are, they didn’t feel nerfed.
