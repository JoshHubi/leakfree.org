Are BS and OF considered spin-offs? Or just (non)canonical expansions?

Maxey November 25, 2018, 1:38am #77

Those are expansion packs that happen to run in parallel to the HL1 plot.

A spin-off is when you take a familiar element from a property’s universe and make a new property based around it that does its own thing.

Basically, like spin-offs of TV shows, where they take a character from a popular show and make a new show around that character with new stories that are independent from the show of origin.

Portal does not do any of that and OF/BS are simply retellings of the HL1 plot but from the point of view of different characters.
