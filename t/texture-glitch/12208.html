That bright magenta means the textures are missing. This can be caused by many things, usually because said textures are corrupted, have missing/erroneous VMT values, or simply haven’t been included in your installation.

If a clean install downloaded from a different mirror doesn’t fix this, it’s probably a porting thing. Try your luck with bootcamp.
