d41m40u November 25, 2018, 2:19am #8

I got around this issue by doing some testing.

I set all the video options to the lowest settings. At this point the game did not crash if I turned on the flashlight. Then I turn each settings on one at a time. I tested between each change and left any settings that didn’t seem to affect the game. In the end, I managed to turn everything back on to my original settings and that cleared the crashing issue up. It no longer crashes any more even though my settings are back to what they were before I set them all to low. I don’t know what’s happening here, but I suggest you try the same.
