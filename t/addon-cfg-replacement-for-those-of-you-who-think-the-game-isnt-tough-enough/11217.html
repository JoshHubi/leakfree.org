[ADDON/CFG Replacement] For those of you who think the game isn't tough enough

General Development Black Mesa
Chickenprotector August 11, 2020, 9:39am #1

Think Black Mesa’s too easy? I’m gonna post my skill.cfg so those of you who want more out of it can play it with this customized .cfg file. On a further note, this is meant for players who are extremely experienced at FPS, if you aren’t good with fps DO NOT plug this in.

[color=RED]WARNING[/size] I’ve played through this several times and tweeked it to just how I want it, if you don’t like it, you can tweek the ammunition values, etc. to your own specifications. I’m not responsible for any rage you may harbor to the NPCs or otherwise. You WILL get your ass handed to you in several instances, I can promise you that.

While this is really tailored to myself, I’m sure the rest of you can enjoy it as much as I have. Like I stated many times, the difficulty is insane now. If you want to play this on hard, then good luck to you, you’ll be just as easy to kill as the entities are when you swap it to easy.

The list below is simply a change log of everything I did to the .cfg file. If you wish to download it already and skip reading, just get it here. I would highly recommend reading it though, so you can know what you’re getting into. The NPCs are not bullet sponges unless you aim somewhere else besides the head.

General:

  • Damage modifiers for body parts are universally as follows:

Head: 3
Ricochet: 0.6
Everything else: 1

  • Flashlight power is now no longer infinite… you can still “sprint” (run) forever.
  • Weapon Damage, they are as follows, the damage (where valid) applies to NPC’s weapons as well – in addition, their damage also matches default values, where valid or otherwise noted. The slash is just a divider between the entity getting hit with a single round from said weapon.

Crowbar: 40/whack
9mm (Glock/MP5): 8/round
Buckshot pellet: 9/pellet
.357: 75/round
Crossbow: 100/bolt
Snarks: 8/bite
Hornet (hivehand): 10/round; precision matches the players
Tripmine/Satchel: 150 (per half-life)
Gulon: 20/energy spent (consumption rate cut in half – ammo’s cut to 60 ffs. Had to be done)
Tau: 35/shot; 350/max charge
RPG: Precision & speed matches the players
TOW: 225/missile

  • Weapon Ammunition amount changes are as follows:

RPG: Reduced to 3
Buckshot: Increased to what’s actually in the box; 20 per pickup (holding values are the exact same)
Bolt: Capacity doubled (20); ammunition pickup reduced to 5
Energy (Uranium): reduced to 60
Frag Grenade: Halved (5)
Satchel: reduced to 3
Tripmine: reduced to 2
Snark: reduced to 10
Hornet: Increased capacity to 24

NPC’s:

  • Security

Hitpoints raised to 120

  • Scientists

Hitpoints raised to 100

  • Marines

Hitpoints raised as follows:
Commander: 150
Regular/Grenadier: 140
Medic: 120
Melee damage increased to 40/whack
Medic heals 70 hitpoints rather than the base value (to compensate)
Crouch dist increased to 1024 units (about 4 times the original value); min health reduced to 50 for when they can’t crouch anymore

  • Zombie

Hitpoints increased to 80, melee range, etc. Remains the same (zombie security get a 0.2 damage reduction from bullets, etc.)
One melee is 25, double-handed is 50

  • Headcrab

Hitpoints raised to 30
Melee does 10/hit

  • Vort/Alien Slave:

Hitpoints raised to 90
Ranged attack damage is 50/zap, get into cover quick or keep your ass moving… they can be lethal in an engagement
Melee is 15/hit

  • Bullsquid

Hitpoints raised to 160
Bite damage raised to 35, whip raised to 65
Spit increased to 6 per glob (9 globs in total)
Damage take scale is 0.65 on all weapons (2 shotgun shells to the face is usually enough to take care of one if you get most of them on the head)

  • Ichthyosaur

Hitpoints raised to 600 (4-6 shells on the head is enough to take one out)
Their melee will instantly kill you… so be cautious (it’s supposed to be 75/hit)

  • Gargantua

Fire damage raised to 10 per frame (or whatever the rate is, they’ll chew through you pretty quickly if they get close)
Melee is an instant kill

  • Alien Grunt – these guys are the bullet sponges…

They’ll use their hivehand at any range (even in melee, so if you plan on crowbarring them to death, make sure you have enough health/armor to absorb some damage)
Melee damage increased to 70/hit, range is increased to 94
Charge range slightly increased (256 from 166)
“rage threshold” – AkA how many failed charges before it gives up and just aims for a few melees… the amount is 2
Anything to the chest area from the front gets halved. cough aim for the fucking head or back… if you can cough

  • Alien Controller

Hitpoints raised to 160
Movment speed increased to 800 on all axis (400 for those appropriate); in other words, doubled

  • Human Assassins – while quite challenging to deal with before, they’re a HELL of a lot tougher and smarter.

Health increased to 120
Pistol damage increased to 8 per round as per the glock the player uses; Range is 2048 (basically when they spot you)
