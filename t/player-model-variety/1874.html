Hello everyone!

I have a suggestion for Black Mesa (for the multiplayer part):

I read that Black Mesa uses a face creation system for having a wide range of different looking heads for NPCs.

I think it would be great also to take use of this feature for Black Mesa deathmatch and co-op, so that you can choose or lets say “design” your own individual player model.

As in HL1, where you had the option to color the clothes of a player model, you could now have options (list boxes and controllers) where you can select a head, change the facial flexes, select a skin and if wanted, also subobjects.

I think this would be a much better solution, than just having one form of a scientist, guard and soldier.
