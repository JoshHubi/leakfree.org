Technology and Gaming
Medevilae November 24, 2018, 10:16pm #1
USA News:

PlayStation 3 owners will finally get the chance to experience the adventures of Commander Shepard and the crew of the Normandy.

During Electronic Arts’ press conference at Gamescom 2010 in Germany, BioWare revealed Mass Effect 2 will be available on PS3 in January 2011.

The second chapter in the intergalactic sci-fi series features Shepard working for the organization Cerberus to wipe out a potential threat to making. To tackle this threat, Shepard must assemble a crew of assassins, soldiers, and other characters for a potential suicide mission.

Mass Effect 2, which released in January for PC and Xbox 360 is one of the year’s best titles, weaving stellar storytelling with an overhauled combat system. Head here for more details on Mass Effect 2.

For those wondering whether the first Mass Effect will appear on PS3, keep in mind the first game was published by Microsoft, home to the rival Xbox 360
