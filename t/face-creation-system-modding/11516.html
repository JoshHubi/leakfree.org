JeffMOD November 25, 2018, 2:47am #10

What I recommend is opening the model you’re working on in modelviewer, then moving the flex sliders for the face and selecting your submodels and skins (remember to subtract 1 for use in the manifest) as wanted. You’ll have to eyeball where the sliders are, but it makes it easier when you can see roughly what you’re doing.

Don’t forget to use the [npc]_02 models for additional skins, as well!
