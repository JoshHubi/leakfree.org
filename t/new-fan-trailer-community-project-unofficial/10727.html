General Development Black Mesa
Deathmonkey7 November 25, 2018, 2:28am #1

So here’s the thing. If you visit ModDB, Steam greenlight and the main page of this very website what do you see? The old, outdated 2009 trailer that has almost no resemblance of what the mod is now. It’s just sad really. We all know the devs don’t really have the time to work on it, at least not until Xen is out, and who knows when that will be. So I’m proposing we restart the fan trailer project. Now that the majority of the mod is out, we have access to the resources to make a legitimate trailer, and not just something full of moving screenshots or crazy CG elements. This way (assuming the devs actually accept this) we can update the video and stop leaving people scratching their heads.

So, submit your trailers below!
