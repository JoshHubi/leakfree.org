Ok guys, this is a first of many chapters in my 5.1 musical experiment.
So as you know you need a 5.1 speaker setup for this music to play to it’s full extent, if you don’t you will only hear 2 of the 6 audio tracks in each of the songs.

Each song is an uncompressed wav as there is no other ready medium for 5.1 to be stored and played by a normal media player.

(Please note, I have only played this song in WMP as a test, I do not know if iTunes or VLC support 5.1 audio).

But without further talk, I present ‘Daydreams’ a series of songs produced using Acid Pro 7 and mixed in real Dolby Pro 5.1 at 24bit (Rendered to 16bit for listening purposes) at 48K.

The first song Chapter I or also can be called ‘Impulse’ as was the code name I gave the song in production is my first real attempt at a 5.1 mixed song, each song in this series I hope to try something different genre wise while still keeping my signature style.

So please enjoy.
