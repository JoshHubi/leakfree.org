I do.

Barney and Gordon are friends. They would challenge each other to vent races whenever Kleiner lost his keys (or whatever). They were, evidently, also beer buddies (Gordon: The alcoholic?) and everyone knew it. Later, on Gordon’s way to work (he’s late!), he sees Barney also having some problems, being locked out of the security section. Then the RC happens.

Xenians begin teleporting in all over the facility (maybe even outside the facility but that hasn’t been confirmed) and Barney begins to battle the Xenians. Word begins to spread about Gordon’s love of the crowbar (and who doesn’t?) and Barney finds a crowbar which he keeps. He knows that Gordon was battling Xenians as well and headed to Xen to stop the incursion, but hasn’t heard from him since.

Later, Barney sees Gordon about to board a train to Nova Prospekt and stops him, commenting about that beer that he owes him (perhaps Gordon won a vent race the day before the RC and owes him a beer) and then giving Gordon the crowbar that he thinks Gordon dropped in Black Mesa.
