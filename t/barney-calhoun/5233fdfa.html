A: Barney saw Gordon through the window, and Gordon’s crowbar slipped through the bars. It was caught in his teleportation field and teleported out with him.
B: There was another crowbar in that same closet
C: Barney’s crowbar was kept from Blue Shift, and learning of Gordon’s use of the crowbar, his “back at Black Mesa” line is just a sentiment.
