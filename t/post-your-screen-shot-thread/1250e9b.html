Ahhh yes, the ol’ invisible-face bug. From certain angles it will go unrendered. Wedge-like areas are sometimes stuck with more surfaces than they’re made to handle, so when you’re within that particular wedge, a surface will turn invisible.

This happens in maps where you have a lot of faces in one area, a lot more common in custom maps. They didn’t catch that particular example, so it’s one of the few instances in an official level.
