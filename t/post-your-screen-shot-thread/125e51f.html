Hrmm, both games have good and bad qualities. TA was easily the best RTS of it’s time, no question, and such deserved all the hype it got. Supreme Commander (made by the same people as TA) increases on TA’s land/sea/air tactical combat make-up by adding new strategies, such as the Ferry option on transports (:rabies , the Co-Ordinated attack option, and the introduction of the super-units, AKA Experimentals, which can swing the tide of war every which way.
