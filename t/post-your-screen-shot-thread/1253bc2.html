Grief_Reaper November 24, 2018, 11:59pm #3858

Im using eXtreme 3.0 and the res is set at 2,720 x 1,536 and on average I get 40 fps.

Im not entirely sure how to control the dof/blur as I changed both the visual settings the excel file and the ini never changed anything in game. I did try using both eXtreme and the shaders from the ultimate GT but I lost the hi res and the colours were off.

As for the shadows its mostly the high res Im using but you can tweek it in the ini.

my settings:

[SSAO_SSIL]
ApplyAntialiasing=true
SamplingQuality=1
SamplingRange=0.5
SizeScale=0.8
SourceTexturesScale=0.8
FilterQuality=1
AOAmount=1.0
ILAmount=1.0
EdgeLighteningAmount=1.0

[SHADOW]
FilterQuality=2
CharacterShadowMultiplier=1.0
CarShadowMultiplier=1.0
