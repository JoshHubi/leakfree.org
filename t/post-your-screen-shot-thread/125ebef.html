FireTime August 12, 2020, 2:35am #4977

The circuit is a 7 segment display, a 7 segment display driver(7447), and a BCD to decimal(7490) all created in redpower. The end result is 4bit binary -> numerical display.
Also, managed to compact it even further.

Red power is a mod for mine craft that adds a few very awesome tools for people who build and for people who like redstone. For redstone it adds insulated redstone wire what you can parrell to other wires without them connecting unless they are the same color. Also adds wire bundles.You can run a bunch of wires into them and then out again at any point and the same color will stay synced with any color wire attached. Also the signal can travel up to 255 now. As for builders it adds colored light blocks and a bunch of partial blocks. You can place 1/2, 1/4, 1/8, 1/16 , and some other strange shaped blocks. Here I made a silly pumpkin textured dining set. Also using panels you can run wires “inside” of walls.
