MaxTheLimit November 24, 2018, 7:44pm #3

LCD monitors cannot display MORE pixels than the native resolution. You cannot actually display 1050 horizontal lines of resolution on a monitor that has 900 horizontal lines. It’s possible that you can have the GPU software scale the image down to fit your screen, but there will be graphical issues. This will be exaggerated by the fact that that 1050 resolution isn’t even the 16:10 aspect ratio of the monitor.

I think it’s funny that you say the widescreen resolutions make you puke because things are stretched, when you are looking at stretching with that resolution as there is no way to fit the res. on your monitor without scaling.

Use the native resolution. ALWAYS. Turning off scaling will also give better performance in games.
