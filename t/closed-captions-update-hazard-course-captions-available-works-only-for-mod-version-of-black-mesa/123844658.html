Sometime ago, Dadster once told me that people would be less likely to download every update due to small updates with big file downloads. With this in mind, I came up with a solution to prevent having to download the same patched maps over and over again for each update.

Two of the versions of my mod (Stock and Surface Tension Uncut versions) are now the bases of the captions update mod. You need to download that and one of the caption updates for one of the versions of the bases. If you any mods installed (or more than one if you’re using the STU Base version) however, don’t worry. You will have the option to download a captions updates specific to the mods you have installed.

You may have noticed that 4 versions of the caption updates that have Hazard Course mentioned don’t have any links to them. The reason is because the Hazard Course mod is not out yet. When that mod is out, I’ll release the other updates.
