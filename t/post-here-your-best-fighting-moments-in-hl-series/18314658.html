In ep2, during the ambush at the house where two hunters attack you. Was getting owned by them thinking “wtf they aren’t dying!” Then one of them ram me, sending me flying into a corner. I was about to give up but then noticed the dead cop beside me, he had the ar2 alt fire ammo. I picked it up and shot it at blank range. It hit the first hunter in the head that was running towards me and it went right down, hard. then the energy ball rebounded off it, against the wall, then against the second hunter that was firing flechettes at me. It hit the hunter in the face and sent it crashing into another room. I was like in awe for a few seconds, then regretted not being able to record that on video.
