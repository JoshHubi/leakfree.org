Now I’m thinking the first Portal must have taken place just hours/days after the resonance cascade from HL1. Here’s why:

  1. No aliens in the Aperture labs. Didn’t the portal storms flood the entire planet with Xen aliens prior to the combine showing up?

  2. Could explain why GLaDOS didn’t know what was “going on up there”. GLaDOS is pretty damn smart, and she knows way more about portals than the guys at Black Mesa did. Maybe she just hadn’t had time to piece together what happened.

  3. Could explain why Aperture is deserted. All the scientists decided to get the hell out of dodge, or maybe evacuate to the borealis.

  4. Convenient for Valve. The GI article said they were hesitant to link up the two games too much because of the different tones–light-hearted vs. serious. Having the Portal games take place completely outside the HL2 storyline would be a clean way to separate them, while still having them take place in the same universe.
