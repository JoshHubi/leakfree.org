To all people with this issue:

It may be ATI related, and could have to do with the way how ATI drivers handle memory or physics.

My FIX:

Completely pummel the boxes and crates within wind tunnel using the crowbar (small pieces can be broken down further) and then go up and down the ladders to make sure the pieces fade away. I chose to run up both ladders without looking back, saved it and then looked back and it worked.
