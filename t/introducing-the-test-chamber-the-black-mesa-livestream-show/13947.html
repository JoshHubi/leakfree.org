Introducing The Test Chamber, The Black Mesa Livestream Show

Black Mesa Archive News & Developer Updates
Fr0z3n August 11, 2020, 11:19am #1

Hey guys!

I’m Joe ‘Fr0z3nR’ Radak, Multiplayer Level designer for Black Mesa. Launch has been totally wicked, and thats all because of you guys, so thank you so much. Some of you have noticed that since launch we’ve been really open and active across community fronts: this forum, reddit, steam forums, ingame, facepunch and more. This is something that we at Crowbar have wanted to do for a while: be more active within the community and break that iron veil we had during pre-steam development. We have a lot more community events planned for the future but, I’m happy to announce one of our recurring events now, The Test Chamber.

What is The Test Chamber?
The Test Chamber is a regular livestream that I will be hosting every-other week. For each episode, the first half of the stream will have either community mod spotlights or artwork, chats about development updates, preview of content patches, and more. Whatever remaining time we have, myself and whoever else is on the stream will pop onto a server where you can come play some MP with us … if you can find the server we’re on! (MUH HAAA MUUHHA HA HA cough cough). That can also run as a pseudo Q&A session for whatever topic we were talking about during the stream.
What do I need from you?
Suggestions, ideas, topics! Do you think this format works? We want the communities feedback on this stream.

To review:
A livestream show, every-other-week. Time/Day to be determined.
Hour long. First half is topic of the week, second half is community MP play
Community Mod Spotlights or/and Artwork, Development updates, content patch previews, and more.

So this is our current plan. We’d like to start doing this in the next week or so, once we get a good idea of this is a format ya’ll will like. Let us know in this thread.

EDIT: The stream will happen at our official twitch channel, twitch.tv/blackmesagame
