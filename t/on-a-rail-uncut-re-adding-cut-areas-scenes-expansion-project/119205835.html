TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 3:37am #1915

The good news is that A1 and A2 are beta ready, I literally just finished them up to standard. They’re both significantly improved from their alpha versions, both aesthetically and gameplay wise. A1 has a fair few new areas added to it to make it feel like a more complete map, and the crane control room has been improved a bit too.

A2 has seen a pretty serious reduction in the number of enemies present throughout its battles, but I had some leeway here because I worked around the AI bug. The sense of direction in the office complex should be significantly improved, both to and from the security office. The lighting should also draw you towards it a lot better than before, and I’ve added a few new different types of light to the office complex which helps vary up the lighting a bit. The vertical expansion has really helped some of the tunnels feel a bit less boring, so I’m really pleased with how much I’ve managed to do with A1 and A2. They’ve improved a lot more than I thought they would. Look forward to it! One other thing I’ve done to A1 and A2 is de-linearized them even more. Hopefully this helps makes the maps feel more interesting and fresh.

A2 has one single bug which I need to fix and can’t figure out the cause for, but I can deal with it eventually.

B1 and B2 should only take me 2 - 3 days, at most. There’s much less to do on both of those maps. I may have thought of a good solution for the turntable on B1, to make it way less buggy, but we’ll have to wait and see if that works. I’m still on track for a beta release on Friday - Saturday, though I make no promises, as usual.
