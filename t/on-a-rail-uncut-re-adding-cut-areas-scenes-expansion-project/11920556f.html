Thank you everyone who’s played the beta so far. I assure you all, I’ve read and considered all the feedback and have acted on a fair amount of it. Some changes are too big for beta 2 (considering I’m going to release it tomorrow) but may make it into future versions, and some I don’t agree with. But rest assured I have read everything and appreciate everyone sharing their thoughts on how to improve the beta. I actually personally think the beta’s been really successful, I really haven’t had any major complaints, and I was certainly expecting a few of those! So that’s great, it lets me progress forwards even faster.

I apologize that I don’t have the time to reply to every single bit of feedback individually, which I would like to do. I just don’t have the time. I’ve been unemployed for a while now while trying to finish this project and I’d like that to end, so I’ve been devoting a lot more time to working on OaRU, and less on interfacing with the community. While I do regret that slightly, it’s for the best in terms of my life. I’m sure you guys understand. Please don’t be discouraged from posting feedback and thoughts. I appreciate it greatly and make sure to take in everything. The fact that I don’t necessarily reply to everything doesn’t mean I’m not listening. It means I’m working on the maps!

RE: Office Complex track debate

There seems to be a fairly even 50/50 split concerning this track change, actually, which is kinda what I was expecting. As I am quite attached to Dark Skies myself, but also like Office Complex, I think the best course of action for now is to stick with Dark Skies. This just avoids the circumstance where the BM Devs decide to do something with the track and I end up looking like a fool. Plus, I think Dejan and his music is fucking fantastic, and seeing as I already have his Year of Death on A2, doesn’t hurt to complete the set!

Don’t forget, it’s always possible for players to substitute the track for Office Complex simply by renaming it, which many people did to test it. Obviously what track is the default track is still an important issue though, and based on what I hear, I’ll now ask the question:

Should I shorten Dark Skies?

I have a mate who’s skilled at audio editing so it shouldn’t be much trouble. Would it be better a bit shortened? Obviously not to the extent it was on my first video, where it was only 45 seconds long, but I could maybe trim 30 seconds or so of fat from the song. Again, I’m on the fence about this personally, because I really love the build up and the layers which get added as the track progresses. But I don’t want to let my love of the track override good gameplay and scripting, so I’ll see what you guys think and weigh your opinions.
