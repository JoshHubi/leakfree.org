TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 3:43am #2421

Thank you everyone for your kind words. I’m very excited to be a proper part of BMS now, helping, in my own small way, to shape the future of this absolutely fantastic mod. The support of this community throughout the entirety of my time here has been absolutely fantastic, and I really wouldn’t be here today without you guys backing me up, so thank you everyone. It’s been one helluva ride, and though it’s not over yet, and has certainly had its ups and downs, it’s been an absolute pleasure rolling with you guys.

It’s been a busy month for me. I’ve had some serious family issues which have cut into my spare time a lot, and I’ve been looking for employment (a road I’m still walking), and the application process for joining the BMS Team was pretty substantial too! I apologize for keeping everyone in the dark for so long, but I’m sure you understand.

As for what I can say about the future, I’m sure you also realize that my ability to speak here is extremely limited, and with good reason. For now, yes, OaRU is on hold. I will not be developing that any longer, at least for now. In all honesty I don’t know quite how it will turn out, or if I ever will now get around to finishing it. Only time will tell. For now, the beta will have to do. Luckily, I was really happy with it, and though it undoubtedly has its fair share of problems, I still consider it to be the finest piece of work I’ve ever produced, and hopefully you guys were pretty happy with it too.

As for official implementation of my mods with the final game, that’s a huge can of worms which is best left unopened, for now. Please don’t ask about it anymore, it’s not something I can talk about. Assume the answer is a no, that’s definitely the best course of action. It’s complicated. Very complicated. For now, what your-name-here posted above, is the officially stated stance:

your-name-here:

Just because Text is on the team doesn’t mean he can start spilling the beans on our plans. I’ve already stated what the problems are with integrating his maps in are. We might just do the workshop thing and set those up there. Less hassle and more legal . Could change though. We’ll let you guys know.

Feel free to show your support in this area, but for now, it’s probably best to not actually ask about it, as you won’t get an answer.

Alright, that about wraps it up. I’m really excited to see what the future holds for this mod, and hopefully you guys are too!
