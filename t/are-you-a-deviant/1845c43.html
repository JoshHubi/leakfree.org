This is somewhat unrelated but really cool in my opinion.

On the second page is this post

Cup o Joe:

There’s this guy Nick Bertke, his Deviant art is Pogon, he’s got a lot of Half Life photoshops.

I’m sure most of you saw the fake Half Life 3 videos, those people stole the art from Nick in some of those videos.

eg: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BFFVkO6CSpU

The deviant art account is long gone but Nick has a post about this on his blog:

https://www.pogomix.net/half-life-2-renders/

The crazy thing is that Nick Bertke is now known as the DJ Pogo and has been remixing movies and real life (where he remixes interviews and city life) for a few years now. He’s become relatively well known for his work, it all originally starting with this video wherein he remixes Disney’s “Alice in Wonderland”:

https://youtu.be/pAwR6w2TgxY

He’s since been commissioned by Pixar to do remixes of “Up” and “Toy Story”, and by Cinemax to remix “Dexter” among other things. He’s already done a mini tour in America (He’s from Perth, Australia) and visited Tibet (for the purpose of remixing the recordings he’s done there) on just the support of fans through kickstarter.

I never had heard of his Half-life stuff until that blog, so it’s just crazy to me that someone I’ve purchased tickets to see at a sold out show next month, came from that background. This might not be that cool to everyone else, but just seeing that post today was really crazy to me. I know tons of people who are fans of his music and it’s really weird to see him talked about without his identity as a musician present at all, and has shown how far he’s come.
