JeffMOD November 25, 2018, 4:16am #2

So a render machine?

As far as I know, think a gaming computer, but with more RAM and maybe graphics cards.
Also cooling.
You’d want at least a 6-8 core 3gHz processor, maybe an SSD if it would also be used for video editing, and as good a graphics card as you can find.

Disclaimer: I do not own a dedicated render machine, nor have I built one. All my information is based off things I’ve heard in the past.

Why don’t you just look up the requirements for the programs you need to run.
https://knowledge.autodesk.com/support/3ds-max/troubleshooting/caas/sfdcarticles/sfdcarticles/System-requirements-for-Autodesk-3ds-Max-2015.html

AutoDesk 3ds Max 2015 wants a 64bit CPU, 8 GB’s ram, and it uses 5 GB’s of HDD space.
There’s also stuff about acceptable graphics cards, thinking most of Nvidia’s quadro series will work.
