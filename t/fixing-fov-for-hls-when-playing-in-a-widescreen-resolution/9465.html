Fixing FOV for HLS when playing in a widescreen resolution?

Black Mesa Archive Help!
Th3On3Fr33man November 25, 2018, 2:15am #1

I was told by someone on this forum (before the threads were dropped) that the FOV in Half-Life Source gets messed up when you switch to a widescreen resolution.

How do you fix it?

dky.tehkingd.u November 25, 2018, 2:15am #2

I’ve never had any problems playing HL:S in widescreen. But if you do,

Console:

sv_cheats 1 fov ##

Where ## is some number.

Th3On3Fr33man November 25, 2018, 2:15am #3

Ok, I tried doing that and put it in an autoexec.cfg script with an sv_autosave 1 command as well; but while the autosave definitely works now, the FOV doesn’t seem to work.

I say that because if I go into console and type fov 75, the view changes. Which must mean it didn’t work the first time, correct?

Crypt November 25, 2018, 2:15am #4

It might be that you’re not in game when the command is entered, but I could be wrong. I’d just bind a key to switch your FOV ("bind x “fov 75”).

dky.tehkingd.u November 25, 2018, 2:15am #5

Keep in mind that for widescreen your FOV should probably be set to 90 instead of 75.
