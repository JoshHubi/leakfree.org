Chickenprotector November 25, 2018, 2:32am #25

^ recommendations on that, I have my own config that I feel is unnecessary to share but if you want to make them smarter and tougher just open your skill.cfg and plant these values in:

sk_alien_grunt_health				"90"
sk_alien_grunt_hornet_min_range		"0"
sk_alien_grunt_hornet_max_range		"2048"
sk_alien_grunt_melee_range			"94"
sk_alien_grunt_melee_jump_range		"166"
sk_alien_grunt_melee_dmg			"30"
sk_alien_grunt_front_dmg_scale		"0.8"
sk_alien_grunt_turn_speed			"30"
sk_alien_grunt_rage_threshold		"5"
sk_alien_grunt_rage_reset_delay		"10"

What it will do is make them have more health (per hl1) and they’ll fire their hivehand in melee if they feel the need (usually it’s after a few swings with the crowbar)

My .cfg that I used is really based around realism and making the game uber tough. Agrunts have those same values, but the melee damage is 70, and the health is 250 instead.
