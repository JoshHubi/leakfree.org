GuitarJim November 24, 2018, 11:47pm #30

Yeah if you have no plans to overclock there is no point going for the K version, the normal version is about £20 cheaper.

With CPU fans if it says retail then it will come with a CPU HSF, if it states OEM then it won’t.

If it doesn’t specify either retail or OEM then I would assume it’s retail. That’s the way it works in the UK and I wouldn’t think it would differ in the rest of Europe.

Personally I wouldn’t go for an MSI board either as I have had problems with two of them in the past. Maybe an Asus or Gigabyte one would be better.

RAM you can get cheaper (4GB for about £40 in UK) and make sure it’s rated at 1.5v or lower as 1.65v according to intel is out of spec. People are running it now with no problems however it may cause things to wear prematurely.
