My birthday is coming soon, so I was thinking about getting a tattoo. I grabbed a pencil and started scribbling away, and I would appreciate some feedback on the outcomes. Yep, I’m a wannabe artist.

First, here’s an old thing I did when I was at school:

734×408 225 KB

An ice heart. I used a sharpie to draw the lines, scanned it, and then colored it in PhotoImpression. The wings look awful and pixellated because I drew them separately on notebook paper and had to digitally remove the lines later. Besides, they were much smaller than the heart itself. I personally think the design is way too big, and those colors would surely make the price go up. I don’t have much money, and that’s certainly a problem.

Then the most recent designs come up. Here is something small:

A stylized old-style F-clef. I love music, so I thought about getting a bass clef tattoo, but then I noticed it has been done to death. I did some research and found this design. It was love at first sight. Simple, and most certainly, cheap.

And then, the real point of this thread:

A Half-Life themed tattoo for my ankle. This is the one I would LOVE feedback about. I took the flames from Photostock (lazy, I know, I didn’t even remove the watermark completely), and then added he lambda into the mix. I really, really like it, but I think some comments would be nice, since the design itself would be a pain in the neck to cover later on. What do you guys think?
