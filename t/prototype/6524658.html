I’m having a bitch of a time trying to kill what I think is the final boss. Its that thing that looks like a giant uncircumcised dick and shoots homing green fuzz balls at you (reminded me of half life actually). Anyone have any tips?

When damaged lure hunters behind a building out of sight of the monster and consume them to regain health. Use Armour mode to get close and, if possible, use a Devastator move when basically underneath it (I recommend the Spike Graveyard one). If you don’t have enough health to do that, get close and start slashing like hell with the SwordArm. Once the 3 tentacles are gone it falls over, at this point beat the shit out of it’s face until it retreats. Rinse and repeat. Isn’t too hard, all it needs is persistence.
