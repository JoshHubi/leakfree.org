eyvn November 25, 2018, 1:24am #37

What kind of tea I like cannot be summed up by simply saying hot, cold, sweetened or unsweetened. There is a host of variety out there just waiting to be sampled. Cold or iced is very good with lemon and a bit of sweetener but even that flavour can be adjusted by what sweetener you use: sugar, honey, stevia…

Whether or not to sweeten the hot and with what depends on different things. Green tea is best unsweetened, black tea is good with milk and sugar but lemon instead of milk makes a nice change. Earl Grey tea with milk and sugar and vanilla is even better. And then there are the spiced and herbal teas - but I won’t open that box.
