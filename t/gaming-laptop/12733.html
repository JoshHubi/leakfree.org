Orpheon November 25, 2018, 3:46am #4

I have an hp Elitebook 8460p for work, which is equipped with a Radeon 6470m. It’s definitely not designed to be a gaming laptop, yet it is surprisingly competent at it. Mass Effect 3 is quite playable, which I did not expect, and Source games run very well also - no surprise there though since Source is so light weight. My laptop was purchased on ebay though, and I suspect the seller did not understand what he had (there are two versions of this laptop, one with integrated graphics and the kind I have, with AMD graphics), so it ended up being quite cheap, only about $400-500 I think. So look around, scout out craigslist and ebay, you might find a good deal on a used laptop that will suit your needs.
