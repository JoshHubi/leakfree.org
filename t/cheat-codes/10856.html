Jenova19 November 25, 2018, 2:42am #9
warpedfreeman:

Go to Options > Keyboard > Advanced to enable it, then in Options > Keyboard to change whatever key you want to toggle it.

If that doesn’t work, try this:

  1. Close Black Mesa.
  2. Open Steam.
  3. Browse to the Library (All Games).
  4. Right click “Black Mesa”.
  5. Click “Properties” (on the bottom of the menu).
  6. Click “Set Launch Options…”.
  7. Type “-console” (without “”).
  8. Click “OK”.
  9. Close the “Black Mesa - Properties” window.
  10. Start the game.

Note: Adding “-dev” (without “”) to the Launch Options enables
“sv_cheats” (it is set to 1) and some more in-game information (in the top left corner of the screen), as well.

eligitine November 25, 2018, 2:42am #12

Quick summary of the avalible useful everyday commands for black mesa.

NOTE: The vast majorty of these require “sv_cheats 1” to be enabled.

god | Prevents you from taking any damage or knockback what so ever. You can still die by falling into scripted death triggers, like jumping off of bottomless pits.

buddha | Allows you to take damage, but you will never fall below 1 hitpoint. Very rarely you will die with this code. Does not require cheats.

noclip | Enables “fly” mode, where you can clip through walls and other obstacles. Be aware that you can still trigger map triggers, such as tripmines and scripted sequences.

impulse 101 | Gives you all weapons, maxes out reserve and secondary ammunition, and gives you one health kit and battery.

notarget | Prevents AI from targeting you, AI characters will still move around.

ai_disable | Turns off AI completely, this can cause strange things to happen.

give | A very flexible command, able to give a large quantity of items.

ent_create | Similar to give, but will create the object where your cursor is pointing.

Examples
give item_healthkit | Gives the player a health kit worth 25 health.
give item_battery | Gives the player a battery worth 15 armor.
give weapon_shotgun | Gives the player a shotgun.
give item_ammo_357 | Gives the player a pickup of .357 magnum ammo.
give item_longjump | Gives the player the long jump module.

Type dumpentityfactories to show everything you can give/spawn.
NOTE: Not all of these things can or should be spawned. If it doesn’t look familiar, don’t spawn it!
