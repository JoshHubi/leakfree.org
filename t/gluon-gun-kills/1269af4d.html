In my opinion the Gluon Gun, being the most powerful weapon one can acquire in the game, should be extremely visually impressive as far as destroying enemies is concerned. Gibbing is done with all of the other weapons and thus I think that some sort of blue disintegration effect is in order, with an appearance similar to an electrical discharge. However, one thing that bothered me about the original gluon was that it simply exploded the enemies in place. If it were a beam of energy that powerful, the disintegrated bits of enemies would have to be blown AWAY from the player, as opposed to every conceivable direction at once.

As far as the actual effect, it should be similar to the gibbing, but the gibs should be smaller, charred pieces of enemies with small temporary blue particle trails on them. A satisfying piff of blood upon contact with enemies would also be increasingly satisfying as well.

The beam, in my opinion, should be more or less the same; I think that the center beam should be more intense and opaque, bluish white, and the surrounding helix should have a similar look to an arc of electricity. At least, that’s how I see it.
