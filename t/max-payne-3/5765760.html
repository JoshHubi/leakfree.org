Siggs November 25, 2018, 1:44am #295

Yeah just come out of my first session now. I know Max Payne games never skimp on the difficulty, but I’m only playing on medium and I’m dying a fair bit.

A quick summary;

-I love the cinematic death/bullet time segments, really ramped up nicely from MP 1 & 2.

-What I hate is the overdone TV/static/colour effects in…well, ALL the cutscenes. It’s bloody annoying. And the popup snippets of speech on screen, that wore thin very fast…eh I find I prefered the original comic book pages overall.

-But back to things I love, the refined controls and bullet time. Only mildly streamlined, but effective.

-Although things I hate, that refinement has kinda removed a real jump button, and diving/rolling without slowmo.

-BUT back to things I love, damn it’s a pretty game, runs really well on my system, although sadly I don’t have the grunt to run the max settings across three screens. I’d have to drop to say, medium and dx9, so for now, I’m sticking to one screen, but max settings. Gorgeous.

-BUT back to things I hate, I miss the ‘p button’ pause menu where it just orbited around max. Best feature in MP2 for screenies. It shall be missed.

-BUT I love that they kept the messages on exit. As always the music is excellent, I love the injoke at the start with max playing the piano, and the cut-off cello chords during gameplay. Very tense.

So yeah…I’m torn a bit. The return of max payne gameplay and that gritty James McCaffery voice have gotten me sitting in my chair going EEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE!!! like a fangirl…but some of the stylistic choices and presentation of the game has me going eeeeh

We shall see. I’m only on chapter five.
