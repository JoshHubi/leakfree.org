The Race X aliens were incredibly well-designed and I really love them, except for the Voltigore (is that how you write it?), I hated those big annoying horse-hippo-antlion hybrids. Dias November 25, 2018, 1:26am #19
ironballz:

I sort of figured Valve ditched all those enemies to try to create a more “mature” sort of feel for HL2.

I think what they really wanted to do was reuse as little HL1 content as they could get away with. The headcrab & zombie are too iconic to ditch. They would have brought back the Ichthyosaur, but there wasn’t any place to put him that would have been worthwhile. The Vortigaunts were tied to the Combine in terms of plot & could serve as walking armor dispensers. Barnacles were still useful for restricting player movement & could interact with the physics props. And the leeches restricted player movement in the water. Nothing else really ‘needed’ to be brought back. Most developers don’t really like bringing back old things unless they’re somehow now able to do something with them that they never could have before. Not to say we don’t all get nostalgic at times.
