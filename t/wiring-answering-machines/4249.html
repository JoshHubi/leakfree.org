Anynody know how to wire a soundboard or audio clip to use it as an answering machine message? I’ve been trying to use this (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ykucr0prMl4) one but can’t seem to figure out how.

higgsboson November 24, 2018, 9:18pm #2

It depends on your answering machine! I would say you should take your answering machine apart, find where the microphone is, and wire that to your computer’s audio output. Then just record the message the way you normally would, only start playing audio from your computer. You’ll have to figure out the correct sound levels to get it right. Or if you don’t care about sound quality much, just play it through speakers and record it with the answering machine’s microphone.
