Skaevola November 24, 2018, 6:06pm #176

Back in the days when men were men, before the website was animated, and when we were bored we played Picture Pwnage in the off-topic section while we waited for what was then called Black Mesa: Source (that would put us at what, 2005 or early 2006? Sounds right) I downloaded Half Life: Source because I thought that Half Life deserved a remastering, and honestly believed that HL: S was that that remastering. I was sorely disappointed, and went on an hour-long quest to find some group, somewhere, which was giving Half Life the reimagining it deserved. It had to exist!

I came up with nothing.

So, beaten and broken, I returned to my corner in the Steam forums, where I came across a thread with this title:

BM: S IZ DED!!!11 (Paraphrased… don’t remember the exact title, but it was to this effect…)

Intrigued, I read the poorly written and spellchecked thread and, using what little information I could extract from it, googled Black Mesa: Source. They had yet to have a real media release and the forums were nearly empty (except for a few awesome veterans like BlackOp, and how I miss that guy and his beautiful purist nazism; only now do I see the truth to his ideals), but they weren’t dead.

I’ve been here ever since.
