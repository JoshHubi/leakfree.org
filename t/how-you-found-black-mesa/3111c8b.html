Geronimous August 11, 2020, 1:27am #205

Found out about it?

I was part of the initial team! I was the first leveldesigner of Lambda Core before Jean-Paul took over ( I faced other important life stuff that took up all my time ).

Kalash was our Leader, together with PR guy Denzil.
I remember before announcing the MOD was Work in Progress, we decided that (it was October) we would announce the mod Jan. 1st., and give ourselves time to get familiar with the Source SDK and hire peeps.

I also made the template for Media releases. Still used today.
A nice little trivia about that:

  • in the upper lefthand corner, in the ‘grunge bar’ that has the logo and ‘BLACK MESA’ text, if you look closely on screenshots with a light background, you can read “LIRE CENTO” (good example).

I used brushes I downloaded to get the grunge effect, and one of the brushes was of Italian banknotes. the Lire used to be the Italian currency.

I did not discover it myself until we were already using it at large

PS: Can someone (the webguy) add my real name to my alter ego on the team page as past contributor? > Jeroen “Geronimous” Hendrix.
