professoraire November 24, 2018, 10:41pm #18

If you use an NVidia card then under “Adjust desktop size and position” try setting the option “When using a resolution lower than my display’s native resolution…” to “Use NVIDIA scaling with fixed-aspect ratio.”
This should do pretty much nothing when you’re on the desktop at a normal resolution, but when you switch to a widescreen resolution in-game it should automatically scale it full-width, without distorting the aspect ratio.
‘Should’ being the key word. I dumped my last CRT about a year ago now for my second 24" LCD so I honestly can’t test it for you.
