Mangled November 24, 2018, 6:07pm #11

Use the environment to your advantage. When a tank is climbing up something to reach you, unload your autoshotgun point-blank into him, then drop down… the tank has to finish climbing before he can drop back down giving you valuable reloading time. Even better if you can climb back up a ladder when he is dropping down to the ground again.

The tank can also be painfully slow at climbing through certain tight spaces giving you a good opportunity to fill him with lead.
