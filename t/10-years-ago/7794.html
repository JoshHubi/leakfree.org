As all of you should know, 10 years ago today, terrorists hijacked planes and crashed them into the World Trade Center towers in New York, the Pentagon building in Washington DC, and a fourth plane went down in a field in Pennsylvania thanks to the heroic sacrifice made by those on that plane.
