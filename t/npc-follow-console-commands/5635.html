Point your crosshair at the npc you have spawned, then in the console enter:

ent_setname !picker rebel

I haven’t gotten around to figuring out how to make them interact, talk, etc, but they will follow you around, take orders and shoot enemies (assuming you spawn them equipped with a weapon… I haven’t played with the vort yet, just citizens).
