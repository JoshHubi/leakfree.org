Comrade_Suhov November 25, 2018, 2:17am #2

It apperas that your graphic gard is unable to handle the game, since the ‘triangle’ corruption in most cases is comming from GPU itself. Your HD 3400 looks pretty weak (however I’m not an ATI specialist), is released in 2008 and got only passive cooling. I’d suggest you to check GPU temperature while you play the game - most likelly there is an overheat issue.

And if there is an overheat, here is a few tips:

  • Remove dust from graphics card cooler/
  • Reduce graphics settings, especially the Anti Aliasing.
  • If nothing else help, you’d have to replace your GPU for something more powerfull with active cooling and designed for games.
