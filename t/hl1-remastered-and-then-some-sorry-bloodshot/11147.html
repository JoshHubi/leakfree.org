If I’d have known someone was working on this, I’d have just contacted them and set up a merge or something :T

Ah well, this is a sound pack that replaces all the weapon fire sounds, the explosions, and some NPCs (vort, zombie, houndeye) with remastered HL1 sounds. When I say remastered, I mean either mixed with BM, heavily edited valve 98 originals, or from scratch recordings made to emulate the spirit of the 98 sounds. Some stuff is straight up redone conceptually (zombie melee)

I plan on touching up the Agrunt as well in the future. It needs some real serious touching.

Here’s a vid of the pack in action

And here’s the pack!
Just drag the BMS into source mods, and let it merge and replace anything it asks you to merge and replace.
The pack comes with the vanilla BM sounds inside each folder so uninstallation is easy. If this is an issue, someone just hit me up and I’ll take em out.
