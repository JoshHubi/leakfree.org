I dont know that there really is anything new that the community can do at this late stage of the game. Suggestions are always welcome, ideas are always welcome. But it doesnt take a genius to realize that there DOES come a point where even the best of suggestions are less and less able to be implemented. Heck, even if Gabe Newell himself actually applied to join the team, would it really make sense to stop production in order to get a new guy up to speed on how the team works, what is expected, what time frames are used and even how the team communicates their goals, objectives and targets?

It is hard at this late stage in the game (pardon the pun) to try to come up with new community projects, ideas on how to advertise, or ways to support your favorite mod, and even in other cases… accept a new talent on the team.

The general sense I get from the team as a peon outsider is that no one wants to shoot an idea down, but the time crunch and project crunch make a lot of things difficult to take advantage of.

Does any of that make sense?
