Pyroclase November 25, 2018, 4:03am #5

First off, the Engi ship isn’t weaponless. The Ion Blast II and combat drone combo is one of the most effective loadouts in the game. You can kill the rebel flagship with it.

Second, there are two ways to unlock new ships in AE: By either completing the the unlock quest for that particular ship, or beating the boss with the previous ship in the layout you can see in the hangar menu. Once you have a ship, you can unlock an alternate layout B by getting two of that ship’s three achievements, and then unlock layout C by getting to the final sector with the B layout.
