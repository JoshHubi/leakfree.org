RabidMonkey November 24, 2018, 7:06pm #8

You could add some interesting cover to the center of the map by creating some big generators, boilers, or turbines in the center, which is a welcome departure from the standard stacks of crates which fill most CS maps. I would also add some detail to the ceiling - trusses and beams, lights, windows/skylights, etc - adding large detail like this should help to fill in the map greatly. I would also frame the giant wall of glass above the spawn rooms on each side with some trims since you rarely see giant flat planes of glass like that in real-life.
