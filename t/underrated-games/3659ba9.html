InterloperX November 24, 2018, 5:31pm #48

About Republic Commando, while it was decent, it was by no means underrated. To me it largely felt like a lost opportunity.

While there were some cool things (fistshiv and the lightsaber easter egg as mentioned), and it was an entry into the “Prequel” Star Wars universe that was actually geared towards the old school fans, there were many many points against it

It was linear to a fault, whether it was in corridors or the large outdoor arena type areas, there really was only one way to approach an area and succeed in passing it.

The enemy types were incredibly repetitive to the point where playing through the later parts of the game was more arduous than fun.

Aside from the first scenario, (and yes I understand they’re supposed to be a Special Ops team that go on the super secret deep infiltration missions), it failed to convey the epic scope of the initiation of the Clone Wars. Basically, it felt way way too detached from the larger conflict going on in the Star Wars universe at the time, which leads me to the next point.

Aside from the first scenario, there was really no sense of accomplishment in the missions. It wasn’t about tracking down Grievous or Dooku (which I would think a spec ops team should be doing) and there was no real actual goal (and yes, I know the game served only to give a small introduction to Grievous, but really, they should have waited for Ep. 3 to be released and had an actual confrontation with him, perhaps in the sense of distracting him long enough to save some Jedi or something, -anything-).

Definitely not an underrated game.

And nope, don’t remember Sacrifice at all.
