Jokerine:

Digimon world? Hmmm… How about some arguments? Why is it underrated?

People pass it off as something spun out-of the children’s anime, it was actually created before the anime but to market it Bandai America put the anime logo on the front (Despite Digimon shown on the logo not actually being in the game at all)

It had a poor translation, it doesn’t effect gameplay which is what makes it so great, grammar was just weird, the Digimon (To make it suitable for children) had watered-down dialogue, so they said very weird stuff, Greymon wanted to fight the hero for the prize of…An invisible street sign…(Sigh)

It was like was 4Kids did to Naruto and YuGiOh.

The graphics for PS1 were really good, it was at the same level as FFIX.

People just passed it off from the cover. I came across it when my dad told me to pick a game to rent for his PS1 when i was young, there was nothing there he didn’t have so I went with digimon world, as I had a digimon pet (still do). I was shocked to find the game was about the same digimon pets, (which were shadowed by the anime). I didn’t understand the game mechanics back then, but returning as an adult the game works brilliantly, grind is very optional, you are forced to get fresh digimon, however can save battle data to fight your digimon against your friend’s on their memory cards (That was cool), there’s no fluke in the game it’s all play-skill and judgement, you can hang outside a dungeon for ages deciding if your digimon is strong enough to take it, then returning back to the city for supplies and risking it, ah it’s so good.
When I returned to play it I completed it in a day, not because it has short play-time, but because i knew how to raise a good digimon (Otherwise, Myst would have a game-play time of 20 seconds).

If you have it at hand, give it a chance and play it.

EDIT: Wiki says all https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digimon_World
