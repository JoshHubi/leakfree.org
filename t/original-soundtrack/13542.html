Swaggless November 25, 2018, 4:17am #2

You can replace the songs in the sound file with any music you like. The only issue is that not all of the places in BM with music match HL’s music 1:1, so you’ll need toeither deal with that, or re-map certsin levels so music triggers are where you want.

All of that being said, I think BM’s music fits BM better than HL’s will. Just like you wouldn’t want BM’s music in HL. Unless you already played the game and want to try something different, I say just leave the music alone.
