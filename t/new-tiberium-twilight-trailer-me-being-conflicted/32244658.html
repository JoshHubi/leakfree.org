I own and love all the C&C games and have been a player and a fan since the beginning. With the exception of Generals… I never liked that one for some reason.
But I gotta say I’m extremely disappointed with C&C4… C&C3 took things in a different direction… removing traditional aspects and adding new ones… which was okay for the most part, but still made a great game and addition to the series. But I’ve played the C&C4 beta and feel like the series has been ruined.
EA said they are trying to appeal to the new gen of gamers with C&C4… but I still don’t see why that requires them to completely remake the structure of the series. It’s completely possible to enhance the gameplay of C&C and add new content without making the enormous changes they have.
Honestly, I’m not going to purchase this game. I will probably just rip off the copy my brother pre-ordered so I can see the cutscenes and finish the saga.
