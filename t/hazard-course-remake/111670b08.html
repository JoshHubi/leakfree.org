The turrets are just there to prevent/deter people from shooting the guards because the original didn’t confiscate your weapons.
I’m leaving them in as a legacy thing, and I’m going to set them up to activate if a guard dies, but it’ll most likely be just a throwback detail.

Vending machines are also present as in the original t0a0d, though I don’t know how we could use them as an educational thing. Maybe have the security guard comment on how Gordon looks thirsty? “Man, you must be exhausted from all that running around. Help yourself to one of those vending machines over there, get a drink or something if ya want.”

One thing we could do, depending on how Catz’ lines were written, is not have the fourth wall be cracked at all through the holo’s dialog. For instance, instead of it describing how to press a button in the lift section, it could just say “Please activate the lift” and “E - Press Button” could appear onscreen. Also, that way it could also work as a tutorial for first time players, assuming there are any.
