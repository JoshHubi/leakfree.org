A paintball gun, and a paintball fight with some security guards assigned to Hazard Course duty, to train the player for combat with human or human-like opponents.
A narrative mode where something’s happening involving the scientists in the observation windows that introduces you to the starting points of the game’s story (maybe they’re getting distracted by preparations for the big, upcoming test that you’re training for, or maybe something lower-key and less connected to the main game’s events, like they’re really unsubtly hinting at a surprise birthday party for Gordon at the end of the hazard course, or something silly like that).
A competitive mode that automatically records how well you do on everything, displays it on monitors throughout the course, has the overseer scientists comment on it, and records it on a localized high score board.
