player November 24, 2018, 8:42pm #45

Full towers are huge. I have the Antec 1200 and it takes up 3 times the space of my old computer. It has 5 120mm fans and the 200mm on the top, but the only time you hear them running is when they’re all on full blast. When they’re all on a medium setting the stock cooler on the CPU is louder, which isn’t really all that loud itself. The Antec fans are supposed to last forever too. I have yet to find this out for myself though since I’ve only had it for about a year, but they’ve been on the highest settings every day since then and I’ve never had any problems.

Like I said before, the Cooler Master is only $10 cheaper because of shipping, but I guess what it really comes down to is a matter of preference.
