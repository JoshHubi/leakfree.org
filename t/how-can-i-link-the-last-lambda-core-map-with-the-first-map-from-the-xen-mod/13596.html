Wrong section

It’s not a perfectly simple task in your case. You’d have to decompile the last LC map and the first of the Xen mod, probably (almost definitely) clean up a lot of issues brought on by decompiling maps, then connect them via the map change entities in typical Source changelevel fashion. You’d also have to disable the credits and endgame triggers in the LC map, and make appropriate modifications to them to trigger the transition. Then test compiles of both maps to make sure it works, then final compiles once confirmed, rebuild cubemaps, etc. It all might be a bit much for someone with no Source level design experience, though the VDCW would help.
