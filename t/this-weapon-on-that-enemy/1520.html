Grey_Acumen November 24, 2018, 6:28pm #7

It depends on your situation too. I commonly snipe my enemies, before they can even get in range to shoot back at me, using the pistol. I use the revolver when I expect to only have one shot to finish them conveniently with. I often prefer to use these over the crossbow as the shot travels faster. This is only the case if I’m playing with crosshairs turned on.

Hivehand has its purposes too. I’ve found it most effective against the Alien Controllers, as using the hivehand on them allows you to focus on dodging those psychic blasts they throw at you instead of aiming. The blasts are easy to dodge provided that’s all you’re concentrating on. The same is true for dealing with any bullsquid at a distance, but close range on anything pretty much defaults to Shotgun being the best response, unless you’re conserving shotgun ammo. Hivehand is also excellent for headcrabs, houndeyes, and the occasional zombie when you want to conserve your pistol ammo but not have to get close to use your crowbar.
