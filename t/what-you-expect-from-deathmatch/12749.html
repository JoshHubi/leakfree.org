So I was just curious as to what you guys are expecting/hoping for from deathmatch?

More on the levels side. Of course there will be some of the original maps, but I personally am hoping they get creative with the DM level design to make it more than just a classic run-and-gun mode.

Specifically, traps. I’m really hoping for maps that have triggerable traps like classic Quake and UT maps, whether it being putting a plug in a pool to electrify water like the OaR puzzle, or physics based traps like destroying supports to crush players, or something even like being able to trigger the surgery bot from Questionable Ethics on other players. I feel like they could really make creative use of the setting to make for some really fun multiplayer maps.

So what kind of cool shit are you guys hoping to see in DM?
