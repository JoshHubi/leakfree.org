lets make a list of the best of the best.

the top of my list are these 2:

Seppukuties - possibly one of the most disturbing, yet most interesting game concepts I’ve seen. When combined with teh cute aspect, it’s perfect for all players. Those who love blowing cute things up will have fun blowing them up, and those who like cute things will have fun trying to keep them from getting blown up.
Furthermore, once you beat the normal game, you can go on to both play and create custom content. I’ve personally created both fun and insanely challenging levels for this. The only problem is there’s not much of a way to distribute those other than create a thread for it like I did and then hope more people post in it.

Warbears - Awesome puzzle game, I can beat the first one regularly, the 2nd one I HAVE beaten, but still have trouble with, and the 3rd one I haven’t even touched yet.
