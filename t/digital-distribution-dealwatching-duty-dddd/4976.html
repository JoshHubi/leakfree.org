We’ve had those moments like the Steam summer sale, where a bunch of games drop to a low price for a short period of time. You may have known other sites do that on occasion as well. However, the advertising isn’t really out there, so for those people who ARE interested in buying all the games they can account for, I thought I’d make this group.

The basic way it works is that announcements are posted for any time some interesting game goes on sale for a good price somewhere (things like $50->$45 sales and pre-order bonuses are usually excluded)

Some examples of sites to be included:
https://www.steampowered.com
https://www.direct2drive.com
https://www.impulsedriven.com
https://www.gog.com

Feel free to sign up if the idea sounds neat to you.
