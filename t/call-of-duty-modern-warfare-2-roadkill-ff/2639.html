Buying another copy of the game wont fix your problem. Steam does an integrity check of the game files every time you launch the game, so if the game files aren’t 100% exactly the same as the files on the master Steam server, then the game wont load. Even for single player games.

Hence, downloading the game or reinstalling it wont do anything. Your problem lies elsewhere.
