Welcome to the forums.

To fellow pyros: the faster weapon switch for the degreaser is well worth the decrease in damage. Switching to axtinguisher and criting your victim more than makes up for the difference in damage. In close encounters one can easily drop heavies single-handedly, and get away without much damage. I also tend to prefer the shotgun in case of enemy pyro encounters, but flare crits are excellent and especially help with long range encounters. Also, ever since they changed the distance requirement for flare crits the flare gun has become more useful.

Backburner is just a waste IMO. They may have (unnecessarily) added airblast, but it uses too much ammo. Airlbast is invaluable, IMO the most important tool in the pyro’s arsenal. The backburner’s increased damage is only necessary in certain situations, like ambushing LARGE groups of people when it is difficult to take them out one at a time. Generally, depending on classes, one can efficiently ambush and drop a group of 3 enemies with degreaser+axtinguisher combos.

Pyro is one of the most powerful classes in the game when played correctly, one just has to be smart and know when to use which tactic.
