Hi, I had the same problem.
The -fullscreen option will not work, since the game is already launched in full screen, but the borders are cut of.
So you have to go at the video menu of the game. There you will discover (as I did) that it is very stuborn in not accepting your settings. The only thing you can do is toggle the video resolution untill you can set the game into 16:9. It’s obviously a bug, but it worked on my laptop after some minutes trying and trying again…
