Garcian_Smith November 24, 2018, 6:50pm #3

From another board:

I think I’ve got a solution for ya. Open Run(start>all programs>accessories>run) type in RegEdit hit OK. In there hit HK_LOCAL_MACHINE. Then SOFTWARE. Scroll down and click Surreal, then Riot Engine. Delete SettingsDX105. Once you do that, run The Suffering again and the box that pops up when you first run it that asks you to config will come up. Reconfig your video however you had it. Then under the audio tab, uncheck the box next to Use 3D Sound Buffers. That’s what worked for me.

If that doesn’t work you can do it again and try configging it in other ways. Hope this helped.
