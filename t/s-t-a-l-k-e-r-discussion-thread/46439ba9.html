Has anyone noticed that all STALKER player characters seem to represent the actual players upon release?

The Marked One, he’s been around the block, having done some fighting in his time, but his amnesia leaves him disoriented in this “new” world of the Zone. We’d all played a few FPS games before STALKER, but nothing so different before.

Scar, the veteran merc who’s been in the Zone for a while but has found things have changed rapidly in certain areas. I bought CS with confidence, sound in my knowledge that my SoC campaign had me prepared. Of course, CS has very similar gameplay, but some of the areas underwent serious changes from the last game.

Major Degtyarev, he’s also a vet, but he’s been away from the Zone for a while and isn’t sure what awaits him on his return. After the catastrophe that was Clear Sky I didn’t play any STALKER for a while. I didn’t really know what awaited me at Pripyat, but after getting my bearings I was sure pleased with the result.
