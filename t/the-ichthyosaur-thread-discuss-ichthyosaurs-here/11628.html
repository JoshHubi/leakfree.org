Glad I’m not the only one who thought that. I mean, I was shit scared of the stupid thing, but I noticed it after having jumped into the dam like 5 times because I kept dying.

It has the illusion of being “swimming” because you only see it for a short duration. Either you die,or you kill it.
But if you pay attention,the tail doesn’t really move (it does a bit, but not to make it look like its swimming). It kind of just turns it’s whole body, and it’s tail follows as if it’s got some extra momentum on it, but it rarely, if ever, moves independently in order to swim.
In the few instances where you watch it carefully, the tail moves very randomly. Not in a fluid swimming animation like a fish, or shark, but in a very random kind of jerky way, that doesn’t represent water propulsion.
