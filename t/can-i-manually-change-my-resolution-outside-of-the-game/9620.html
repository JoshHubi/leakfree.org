Yes, there is actually a way to open the game in windowed mode.

With Steam open, in your library find the “Black Mesa” entry and right click, find properties and select that. There is a button labeled “SET LAUNCH OPTIONS…” where once you’ve clicked that, a small window with a text field will open. Type exactly the following:

-window

Click OK, and so forth. I’m not sure the resolution commands, but this will at least get you started in the right direction.

Just wanted to post and say I found a solution. Thanks to it being a source engine game, all of the source switches work so I was able to use;

-w 1028 -h 768

in the launch properties in steam to start the game at that low resolution.
