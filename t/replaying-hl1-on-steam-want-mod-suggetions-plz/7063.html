G-Invasion replaces nearly all models with G-Men
Counter-Life introduces Counter-Strike concepts into the original singleplayer such as money and weapons.
DiamonD is a super definition pack. Doesn’t replace Nihilanth though.

Opposing Force contains lots of cool weapons, but you’ll have to cheat to get them. It also has some quirks that weren’t in the original Half-Life.
A helmet comes with the HEV suit if you play Half-Life with Blue Shift. That’s the only difference I noticed.
Half-Life: Zombie Edition let’s you play as a zombie (and headcrab.) It is possible to do without cheating, if you get hit by a headcrab as a human, you will zombify. The game will crash at a few places, and most buttons can only be activated by hitting them. It also really sucks to be a human since you can’t run. But other than that it’s fun.
