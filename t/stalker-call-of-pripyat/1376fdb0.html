baconeggs November 24, 2018, 6:28pm #74

Got Clear Sky the other day, hoping for another exciting foray into the Zone in its early stages.

Man, there are a lot of things that I don’t like about it.

  1. The inventory screen obscures EVERYTHING. What happened to the translucent version in SoC?

  2. They removed the movement toolbar. Now I have to open the inventory screen to check my stamina, which is stupid. Surely the character would know when he is tired or not? Being unable to see my movement noise isn’t fun either.

  3. The damage icons are puny. In SoC it was very easy to see what kind of pain you were in, wether it be bleeding, fire or radiation. Admittingly, the geiger counter is more realistic, but the bleeding icon is almost unseeable. I’d check my health several minutes after a fight to find it nearly empty! What happened to the big damage icons of old? Surely the character would notice copious blood loss quite easily.

  4. This is minor, but no less annoying. Pistol aiming. Why aren’t the iron sights used anymore? Rather than looking down the barrell of the gun the camera simply zooms in next to the pistol, giving a very gimmicky feel. I liked the iron sights!

However, the game was not without its perks. The ability to actually be part of a faction (besides Freedom) is nice, and it is now much easier to compare weapon condition and characteristics. I was also pleased that full length double barrell shotguns have been put in, and that artifacts are no longer so easy to find. But these new features, and even the other problems are made irrelavent by one startling problem.

The lag. My computer may not be high end, but it still had all the RECOMMENDED requirements. Not minimal, recommended. Despite this Clear Sky played like a jittery wreck in the base, and like a slide show out in the swamps. Turning down all the settings to minimum did nothing except make everything ugly as fuck.
However, I conceded that perhaps the huge draw distances in the swamp was putting a lot of stress of the renderer, so I preservered and hoped that things would improve in my old stomping ground near the Cordon. Alas, this was not true; the slide show effect kicked in whenever a firefight broke out.

I’m pretty pissed off by all this. Is Clear Sky really that much harder to run than SoC? I didn’t notice much graphical improvement, so what is causing all this drop in FPS?
