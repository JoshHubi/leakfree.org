Vibez November 25, 2018, 12:08am #12

Well, if you want good sound quality and flac files on board you should definitely avoid Apple iPod’s. These things have a little over 70dB Signal to Noise Ratio. This covers something about 12bits of music bit depth which means if you play any track at 16 bit (which is the lowest you can find and anyone releases), at least 4 of them are usless because of the noise (which you will be able to hear, even without expensive headphones). I know noone really cares about it but as the matter of fact this is quite important. Look for models with highest SNR possible (96dB is good enough). Also, usually that kind of information isn’t in the specification so look for tests on forums and sites.
