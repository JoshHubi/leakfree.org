Malidictus November 25, 2018, 4:22am #54

If we assume that Opposing Force is canon, the soldiers were sent into Black Mesa without actually being told what they were getting into. What this means to me is they wouldn’t have “official” names for the aliens and would instead call them as they see them. L4D2 did that when the survivors meet at first, call thing the Special infected things like “It’s one of those leaping-on-your-back bitches!” and “It’s that spitting thing!” My impression of military personnel is they don’t want to be poets in battle and would usually pick a simple, descriptive, easy-to-say name for things, so usually one word. With that in mind…

*Headcrab: Headcrab - that’s literally what it is. It looks like a crab and it jumps on your head. That’s how I’d describe one if I saw it.
*Headcrab Zombies: Zombie - again, that’s literally what they are. They walk like zombies, sound like zombies and act like zombies.
*Houndeye: Dog - they look and act like small alien dogs. We used to call them “the three-legged dogs” when I was a kid.
*Bullsquid: Spitter - while it’s tempting to go with “squid,” the Black Mesa version of these things has SUCH a trademark and unique spit as to be defined by it.
*Barnacle Creature: Tonge - that doesn’t necessarily describe the Barnacle Creature itself, but it’s what you see to know they’re on the ceiling and what gets you killed.
*Vortagaunt: Freak - of all the aliens in Half-Life, the Vortagaunt is the most difficult to sum up, so it would make sense that the soldiers would just call them “freaks” or “alien freaks.”
*Alien Grunt: Big Guy - not necessarily very descriptive, but I can imagine ever HECU marine would know EXACTLY who the “big guys” are.
*Snark: Bug - that’s what they look like. They’re giant, fast, aggressive “damn bugs.”
*Gargantua - That Thing - the Garg shows up twice in the entirety of Half-Life and not once in Opposing Force or Blue Shif. It’s safe to assume it’s very rare and something that the soldiers it kills haven’t seen before. “What the hell is that thing?!?” “How are we supposed to kill that thing?!?”
