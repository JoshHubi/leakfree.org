MusicallyInspired November 24, 2018, 10:01pm #27

Goldeneye was going to have the same HD remake treatment and 8-man multiplayer online play that the original Perfect Dark got on XBL. But Microsoft and Nintendo couldn’t come to an agreement on Nintendo’s royalties so nothing was done.

So Nintendo and Activision (who holds the Bond game license) decided to screw the original game that Rare made altogether and remake it into a new game for the Wii exclusively thereby forgoing any necessary royalties to Microsoft for the original game. It’s not the original game by any stretch of the imagination. It’s more like a re-imagining. The levels are altered with a similar atmosphere to the originals, but you won’t automatically know which way to go. The story is even altered somewhat from the post-cold war type of medium to a more modern retelling. They used Daniel Craig’s face/voice because they didn’t have the rites to use Brosnan’s likeness anymore. The Klobb (named after one of the original developers Ken Lobb) is also being renamed to the Klebb (after a developer on the new game).

All that said, I’m looking forward to it and am going to give it a chance. I do wish it would have gotten the remake treatment on XBL, though, as well. Or even a virtual console release on Wiiware.
