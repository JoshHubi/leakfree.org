TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:32am #2

You are probably using Norton Antivirus, which has flagged one of our executables as a virus (it is a false positive; I filed a report with Norton and they said this will be fixed in their next virus definitions update).

You will need to disable Norton for the update. A helpful user posted this solution here:

“Open norton and click the performance tab. Look on the timeline for where it was detected. Mouse over it and click on view details on view details. Then click on the instance in your security history listed as vlocalize.exe. Click on it then under recommended Action click “options” then click “restore & Exclude” then tell it okay. After that verify your install then update. If you get stuck in a verification/validation loop then delete the local files and reinstall.”

You may also need to delete Steam’s “downloading” folder, as this is where it stores the vlocalize until Norton quarantines it, if the previous steps don’t work. Otherwise, you may unfortunately have to reinstall Black Mesa (with Norton disabled).
