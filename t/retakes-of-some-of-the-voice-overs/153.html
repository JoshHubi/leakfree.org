Virginityrocks November 24, 2018, 5:21pm #1

I recall a thread in the other forum about retaking some of the lines from the trailer. I have to agree. Barney’s line especially, “We had a system crash about, I don’t know, 20 minutes ago and I’m still trying to find my files. Just one of those days I guess”.

The line wasn’t done horribly, but the line lacks a sense of preoccupation that the original had. The original line felt some anxiety by Barney, fixing the problem with his computer while trying to give Gordon a simple message. However the new line feels as though Barney is not bothered by the system crash, and is fully focused on giving Gordon the message.

It may be semantics, and new players may not care or notice, but the new line feels too much like foreshadow, as if to imply that shit will in fact hit the fan, where as in the original the first 10 minutes of the game are designed to give the player the immediate impression that nothing will go wrong, and nothing is expected to go wrong, and there is no doubt in anyone’s mind that there is a reason to expect anything other than the day before, except of course those directly responsible for the test chamber’s instruments.

By giving any form of foreshadow outside of the test chamber and its related sub-rooms could detriment the plausibility of the emotions conveyed by the same sprites following the resonance cascade, such as Barney’s “What the hell are these things Gordon? And why are they wearing science uniforms?”.

Just my 43 cents and a Canadian twonie.
