Hi, been lurking a couple of weeks now since I found out about this. I won’t gush like a noob, so on topic.

I was given the original Xbox version of HL2 for my 15th back in '05, and having since played the PC version, I can say that the console version differs majorly in a few areas. I will list the differences.

XBox HL2:

Pros: Well thought out inventory system for the controller, big combine walls make huge creaking, grinding and crushing noises, ummmm…?

Cons: Frequent load points, redesigned levels, removed content, lower-res textures, lower poly count models, less effects, looks overall quite rough, no modding, slow and stodgy looking controls, where are the headcrab’s teeth?

PC HL2:

Pros: Modding, looks awesome (textures, models etc), larger levels, less load points, extra content, keyboard and mouse combo, more effects.

Cons: Big combine walls make no noise, still no headcrab teeth, ummmm…?

So yes, basically, PC HL2 > Xbox HL2.
