Crysis 3 is now officially announced:

You play as Prophet and it’s the year 2047. (24 years after the happenings in Crysis 2)
You’ll be able to use a bow and to upgrade it. You are also using a better nano suit.
You’ll be able to use alien technology as well.

Story:

After his return, Prophet notices that the corrupted CELL organisation has created a giant dome at the Big Apple. That Liberty City Dome is a giant urban, tropic forest, with overgrown tree areas, tight swamps and lurid rivers.

The internal has 7 dangerous areas that have become popular as the “7 wonders.”

It’s supposed to come out early next year, for Xbox 360, PS3 and PC.
