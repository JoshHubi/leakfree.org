Unknowncode November 24, 2018, 6:06pm #36

(Note: I’m not including games for the Xbox 360 or PS3 because frankly consoles can go to hell )

You’re right about Sims 3, it’s an alright game, but it’s not worth the money. If you really want it I reccomend torrenting it or something. Either that or play Sims 2 and sprinkle glitter on the screen and thats basically Sims 3.

As far as other games are concerned, it depends on what area of gaming you like. If you’re on a budget then definitely by the Orange Box. That includes the Half Life series from Half Life 2 up till Episode 2, as well as Portal which is just as entertaining and Team Fortress 2 which frequently gets updated with new content. So it’s 5 games, 1 box, for about £25ish. I’ve seen it for cheaper in my local area and I’ve no idea about prices in other countries but needless to say, it’s worth a look if you’re strapped for cash.

Something like Bioshock would be another good game to look at if you haven’t already got that. An art deco style FPS/RPG set in an underwater city where you can genetically mod your body to be a fire wielding, telekenetic power house. What’s not to love? I personally loved the story and I’ve already pre-ordered the sequel, which is being released March 2010.

The important thing here is to think about what your personally enjoy as a gamer, whether that be story or graphics, gameplay or even specific series/developers. I’d recommend that you don’t just go on what people say though, even myself. Take some time out to look at reviews and gameplay footage and see what interests you. Hope you find what you’re looking for
