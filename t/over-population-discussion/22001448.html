We also have a problem with one minority in both countries – the Gypsies (or “Romas”, as some of them want to be called). This formerly travelling nation/tribe was forced to settle down in the 50s and 60s during a somewhat violent communist initiative called “taking away the wheels” (the Communists basically relocated gypsies to the underdeveloped regions of the Czechoslovakia, and then took the wheels of their travelling road wagons away, thus forcing them to stay put).

During the 60s, 70s and 80s, the communist regime wanted to show to the “Evil western capitalists” that communism is indeed the best solution, and one of the tricks to do that was to have “100% employment rate”. Basically everyone HAD to work. The regime found for everyone a job position, of course at the cost of having 5 people do the work 3 could have done as well. So, the effect on Gypsies was that they had to get up every morning and go to some place, where the foreman would say to others “OK, guys, we have to do [this] and [that], there’s 12 of us, but don’t count on the two gypsies to help us, just do what has to be done and don’t make any problems”. It was possible to work like this, because the work required could be done in 10 people easily. But Gypsies learnt that they don’t actually have to work and still get money.

The regime also gave them city flats for free, and right in the centers of cities. Not having to pay for those flats / appartments, gypsies didn’t value them much, and tend to sell most of the pre-placed equipment on black market, then asking for another appartment, which was granted to them. This also lead to their current tendency to “out-commodate” (as in accomodate OUT) any social flats they receive as social help, which basically means that they receive a fully equipped flat and they turn it into a holo-flat quickly, even ripping out of wall sockets any metallic pipes or heaters, which can be sold under price to scrap collectors and junkyards.

People in Czechia and Slovakia hate Gypsies and blame them for the bad state of state finance, but the real culprits are not Gypsies. Never in the history had Gypsies any sort of participation on the government; the communist laws, the communist “taking away the wheels”, the communist decision to give them appartments for free… none of that was done by gypsies or under the threat of gypsy demonstrations. It was all done by white, non-gypsy people, who wanted to control people, who wanted to brag by having full-employment, etc.
