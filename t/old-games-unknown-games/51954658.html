Here’s 2 of my favorites i forgot about -

Space Hulk (AMIGA,DOS) + Space Hulk - Vengeance of the Blood Angels (WINDOWS 95):

These games i played as a kid, both were quite scary, and the basic idea was you’d move your squad of marines, or terminators through an abandoned hulk overrun with Genestealers (warhammer’s xenomorphs if you will) It was very tactical, fun and scary. It had various objectives based off the board game, such as flaming rooms, collecting archive records, placing field generators, etc.
The Second space hulk introduced a network multiplayer mode which made for great fun doing missions cooperatively, and i believe there was a deathmatch mode where you and another player both have a squad of terminators and duke it out.
