So, I’ve played black mesa quite often and I’ve always wanted to just play on my TV from my bed. However there’s one thing that kind of stops that and that’s the main menu system.
There’s some issues like unable to use the gamepad to navigate the load/save menu to save and the new game menu is annoying to use too.
The buttons are also REALLY small when on anything higher than 1080p as they don’t scale with resolution.
If it could be anything like the Portal 2 main menu in functionality that would be great, it could also be a chance to allow another additional menu selection for custom maps! (This means no more using console to play custom sp maps)
Compare Black Mesa to Portal 2 and notice just how easier it is to navigate on any higher resolution or with a gamepad compared to Black Mesa
