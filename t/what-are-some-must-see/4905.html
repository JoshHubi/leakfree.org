Orpheon November 24, 2018, 10:01pm #4

Yeah, there was a anime rating thread, might wanna check it out. Anyways, since I’m here…

I’ll second Trigun, it is an awesome gun-slinging sci-fi western-in-space story that doesn’t have to rely on teenagers as main characters to appeal. Outlaw Star is another good one that has a very similar feel to Trigun. Evangelion I will only recommend to you if you can handle both the (now) old cliche of gun-wielding robots piloted by teenagers and psychobabble. Azumanga Daioh is a very good school-life show. Lucky Star is a good one similar to Azumanga but is a bit more current (and the main character is an otaku and gamer). FLCL is pretty crazy but if you can handle it, it’s very interesting. It’s one of the only pieces of media I’ve ever enjoyed analyzing. Gurren Lagann is like FLCL and Evangelion thrown together (no surprise, all 3 were made by the same company). Full Metal Panic and FMP The Second Raid are good military/mecha animes, and FMP Fumoffu is a comedy mostly centering around school life as well, but has some fighting and stuff in it because of the main character. Black Lagoon basically plays like an R-Rated Hollywood action movie, so take that as you will. It starts getting kinda weird when the vampire kids show up, so I ditched it then lol. Lastly, a controversial one is the Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya. I personally enjoy it, but plenty of people hate it. If you like school life and/or sci-fi, I think you will enjoy it.
