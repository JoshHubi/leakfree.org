Kind of a tricky subject. I kind of think many of the arguments based on “revenge” are kind of going in the wrong direction. One thing I learned back in high school; revenge is met with revenge. One guy hits someone, they hit back. One guy burns down someone’s house, the other rapes his wife. One guy kills the other guy, and his son comes back with a sledgehammer and murders him. Sure, you can go off some assumptions when someone doesn’t have a son (or anyone who would care about their death) and say killing them is okay. You’re still missing what is a much more fitting punishment; finding some way of introducing guilt to them: Making them understand how horrible they are and how much the world hates them. Capital punishment, to me, is sort of a way of saying “Well, we have no idea what on earth could possibly make you fit for society, so we’re going to remove you from the gene pool.”
