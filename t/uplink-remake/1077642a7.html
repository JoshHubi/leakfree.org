My 2 cents on the mod:

Very well done, except…

Being able to open the door of the hangar after breaking out through the vent, and letting Barney continue to follow you, kind of breaks the next scene for me since Barney’s extra firepower really nerfs the challenge of the troops. Old Barn should be stuck in that hangar until you get back, if you lead him into it. Maybe having that switch be broken, and a stray Vort bolt from the fight opens the hangar door?

Also, I miss the challenge of trying to take out the machine gun nest with grenades from above, without getting killed, and then using that same gun on advancing squads from the next area. That was one of the fun parts of the original demo, and without it the whole thing was a bit flat.

Should have been a blowtorch animation on the main door of the antenna room, after you set the antenna and are fighting those troops, which I didn’t see. The door was open later though, I noted. (I went back to get Barney, who was still alive next to the torched-open door.)

More debris falling in the beginning would have been nice, like in the original…
