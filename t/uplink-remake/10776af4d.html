Hezus November 25, 2018, 2:48am #97
Ronster:

Whilst I think you have done a good job updating the dome and the use of props and lighting yeah it looks great I have to say I think you have fallen short of the Black Mesa treatment it’s more a HD treatment on this as the original made no sense and you have kept it that way.

I know some parts in Uplink made no sense. The indoor dish is pretty far fetched and I’ve thought about it for a long time and considered changing it to something which resembled your setup. The dish inside the dome however is a quite iconic landmark in this map, so completely changing it would take the remake too far from the original. At some points I change minor aspects in favour of realism or gameplay but removing an important vista as the dome is a step too far, imho. I’m planning to change the dome a little with more slits and panels that can open, so it makes more sense. I’ll do that in a later version, though.

I know we have different ideas on how to remake Uplink, so I’ll just do it my way. I appreciate the suggestions but I’m using a certain design philosophy for this remake and I’m going to stick with that. Feel free to finish your version though! It’s interesting to see what other people come up with.
