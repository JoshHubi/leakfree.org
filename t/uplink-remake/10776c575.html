Hezus August 11, 2020, 9:50am #119
MirageES:

Forgive me if I am incorrect in this assumption I am about to make, but wouldn’t an indoor dish surrounded by a dome make perfect sense? Look at some weather radar domes such as Doppler Radar, (Just more about doppler), and satellite domes on some tractor-trailers (Examples); those are dishes inside a dome in a real life application. Why wouldn’t the concept scale up, especially if it was made by the Black Mesa Research Facility. In those cases it would be possible, since the domes are just plastic covers, which do not obstruct the signal. In Uplink the satellite receiver is built in a dome of thick concrete. It might get some signal through, but it’s far from ideal. Thats the reason why there is a slit in the dome to aim the dish through. However, probably because of engine limitations, the designers didn’t make slits for all the other directions. I’ve added those slits now.
