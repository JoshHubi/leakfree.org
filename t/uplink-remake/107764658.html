@neuroboy: It is slightly dark in those corridors in the origional, but I still want people to see enough of the environment. The current light setup are the primary lights I’m also going to use in a final art pass. They’ll look a lot better after I’ve added extra effects and other small light sources. A lot of prettiness also comes from using better compiling parameters. Now I use basic settings, so the compiling goes faster and I can hop ingame directly to see if stuff is working the way I wanted it to.

Hezus November 25, 2018, 2:34am #30

Day 5 of development. I’ve been working on the warehouse. I still have to finish the mechanic with the crate on the rail to get out of that warehouse and add some detail, lighting, etc, but most of the area is done.

As you can see I’ve been a bit more creative here. I felt that the design of the warehouse in Uplink was a bit sloppy. First off, the only way to get large crates into the hallways was by carrying them up to stairs apparently. I added a freight elevator to fix this. Also, the transition from the hallway to the warehouse now has a door, which seemed a lot more plausible.
