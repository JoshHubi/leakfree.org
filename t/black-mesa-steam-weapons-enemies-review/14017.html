Olaf_the_Space_Vikin November 25, 2018, 4:31am #3

-Is it just me or did the magnum take a damage nerf from the mod version? I don’t recall the Vorts being able to survive a body shot on Normal difficulty before.

-The shotgun makes me far happier than it really should. Nice to see a decent spread-pattern for once rather than a weapon that’s absolutely useless outside of point-blank range.

-The MP5 is…Well, maybe a little too good at what it does. Maybe up the recoil a little bit? Also I miss the old GL range. Took a while to get used to the new distance.

-Packs of houndeyes are now a royal pain in my ass. Which is as it should be!

-The grunts are fun to fight at times…Sometimes, though they come off as rather suicidal. Pretty much every WGH and Power Up encounter consisted of me staying put around a corner and waiting for them to charge blindly into my gunfire. Also, while the voices are much improved over the mod release, the grunts don’t seem to be as talkative as before. There’s almost no chatter whatsoever until combat starts, and even then it’s not much. I also miss the radio filter over the voices, but that’s probably easy enough to mod in.
