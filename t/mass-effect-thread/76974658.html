i was really happy with what they did with the classes in ME2 - rather than just make them different combinations of combat/tech/biotic, they fleshed them out to really differentiate them, so that infiltrator was a much better assasin-style character, and vangaurd was 10x more badass as a close-combat fighter. but what i didn’t like was that the classes that didn’t have a “combat” focus (adept, sentinel, engineer) were slow and ineffective on hard mode; not that it was super-challenging, but just that you had to grind down enemies’ defenses with your weak weapons/long cooldown powers before you finally could throw them off cliffs (and on easy mode it was too, well, easy - just zap/burn/or toss them and win in a couple seconds). and you couldn’t count on high-power AI buddies to compensate for the lower damage capabilities.

but all of this was still a major improvement over the ME1 mechanics, so I basically just hope that they keep going in an upward direction with the AI and combat balance.

Kaweebo:

Speak for yourself Ramirezoid. I think Vanguard is my best, especially in Mass Effect 2. Just charge and shotgun to death.

I found Vanguard to be pretty frustrating on the harder difficulties. I felt so ineffectual. Charge is useless unless there’s only one enemy because the other enemies will tear you apart by the time you finish chewing through the first one’s defenses. Your weapons are all close range, leaving you with the same problem. It might be an interesting challenge for some, but for me it just wasn’t any fun.

In fact, a lot of the offensive biotic powers kinda annoy me too, since they don’t work on enemies with defenses. Once you’re done stripping away those defenses with your guns, it only requires a few more shots to take down the enemy since they have little actual health, making biotics useless to me. It’d be nice if more of them were like warp and could help take down armor or shields. I usually just play soldier, since the variety of weapons seems much more useful than powers. However, for my extra power I usually do go with a biotic power, Barrier. Cools down quick, casts instantly and really helps keep you alive in tight spots.
