First of all, thank you very much for the new media, it’s absolutely amazing!

Just to join the discussion about the Bullsquid eyes, I do feel that they were much better on the side rather than on the top:

800px-Bullsquid_spit copy.jpg799×421 44.1 KB

I’m not sure if it’s just the angle of the screenshot, but it seems a bit odd to me. The Bullsquid seems to be inspired by reptiles, squid, frogs, and other aquatic creatures, and the eyes on the side tend to reinforce that idea, while the eyes on top make it very cartoon-monster-like. It just looks much less like the Bullsquid we’ve grown used to and more like a new monster, to me, with the eyes on top.

I know Ram said it’s about making it feel more alive (“warm”), and it probably does achieve that, however, it makes it feel less wild and beastly. It is a more primitive “animal” after all, the little black eyes make it feel more mysterious and unpredictable and aquatic (reptile-like), which I think is a good thing in this case.

Obviously this is really not a big deal and everyone will love this game no matter what, but since it’s a forum, might as well put my thoughts down.
