Getting an entire Facebook page to 20,000 likes is pretty challenging. When we started getting to 20,000 we had to stop and think about what kind of input was actually going in to and getting us to 20,000 likes. None of us were very happy with the quality of the likes on the page. So we scrapped most of the likes we had and started almost completely fresh. We removed all deadlines and windows and are looking carefully in to every like that’s put in to that page. A bunch of crappy likes for the page isn’t going to get us anywhere. But a bunch of quality likes will. Everyone at the Black Mesa fan base cares to much about our likes to “rush it along”. The current amount of likes we have (7,909 at the time of posting) is much better than the nearly 18,000 likes we used to have. Main point: We’ll give you 20,000 quality likes when we give you 20,000 quality likes.
