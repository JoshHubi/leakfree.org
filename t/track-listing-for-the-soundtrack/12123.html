For the most part, I have left the in-game order of the tracks alone. I have made a few changes for listening pleasure.
-BM Theme (Mesa Mix) now plays after the Inbound tracks. It is originally played over the first menu screen, which depicts a tram station, and it seems a fitting beginning to the story to include it after you depart the tram. It’s much better than putting it second in my listing.
-There is no music to accent the disaster that begins the entire video game franchise. It works fine in the game, but it is a hole in the story when just listening to the music. Therefore, I have filled the hole by pulling We’ve Got Hostiles from its proper place and putting it after Anomalous Materials instead.
-With the game not yet complete and the Xen soundtrack pieces not yet released, there is no proper end to the soundtrack when simply listening. Lambada core is dissatisfying for this purpose, and I have put Forget About Freeman there instead, which sounds, while scoring an action scene, largely hopeful and not dark, making it a nice replacement until the soundtrack is completed.

Overall, this listing is very nice to listen to and there are no “action” tracks that jump out at you when they shouldn’t.

Here’s my personal listing as I set it on my copy of the album taking into account both the “miscellaneous ambiance” files & the songs from the “uncut” attachments:

  1. Black Mesa Theme (not used in-game)
  2. Opening Short (named “MusicalEx1.wav” in the files, plays during the title card right before Black Mesa Inbound proper starts)
  3. Black Mesa Inbound Part 1
  4. Black Mesa Inbound Part 2
  5. Black Mesa Inbound Part 3
  6. Anomalous Materials
  7. Black Mesa Theme (Mesa Mix)
  8. Unforeseen Consequences 1
  9. Unforeseen Consequences 2 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambiance1.mp3” in the game files, plays during the “box smashing room”)
  10. Office Complex (Mesa Remix)
  11. “We’ve Got Hostiles!” 1
  12. “We’ve Got Hostiles!” 2 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience2.mp3” in the game files, plays during the room where you get the Franchi SPAS-12)
  13. Blast Pit 1 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience6.mp3” in the game files, plays on the lift leading down to the track)
  14. Blast Pit 2 (named “BMS - BP Part 1.mp3” in the game files, plays upon witnessing the silo)
  15. Blast Pit 3 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience7.mp3” in the game files, plays during the shaft/sewers area leading up to the Oxygen/fuel area)
  16. Blast Pit 4 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience5.mp3” in the game files, plays when you get in the broken lift to the generator room)
  17. Blast Pit 5 (Mesa Remix) (named “BMS - BP Part 2-(crazymix).mp3” in the game files, plays when you start up the generator)
  18. Blast Pit 6 (named “BMS - BP Part 3.mp3” in the files, plays when you return to the Tentacle chamber after setting everything up)
  19. Power Up 1 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience4.mp3” in the game files, plays when you first enter the turntable control room & the injured security guard tells you what you need to do)
  20. Power Up 2 (named “BMS- PU1.mp3” in the game files, plays upon killing the Gargantua)
  21. On a Rail 1
  22. The Year of Death (named "yearofdeath.mp3 in the game files, composed by Dejan, exclusive to the On a Rail Uncut attachment, plays when you start exploring the office complex in Map bm_c2a2a1)
  23. Lights Out (named “yoc.mp3” in the game files, composed by Andrew “The Empire” Bastian, exclusive to the On a Rail Uncut attachment, plays during the turntable ambush in map bm_c2a2b1)
  24. Dark Skies (named “OARU - Dark Skies (Full).mp3” in the game files, composed by Dejan, exclusive to the On a Rail Uncut attachment, plays upon the player entering the outdoor area in Map bm_c2a2b2)
  25. On a Rail 2
  26. Apprehension (Mesa Remix) 1 (plays during the piston room)
  27. Apprehension (Mesa Remix) 2 (plays while being dragged by the soldiers)
  28. Residue Processing
  29. Questionable Ethics 1 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience8.mp3” in the game files, plays after getting past the “electrified locked door puzzle”)
  30. Questionable Ethics 2 (named “BMS - QE2.mp3” in the game files, plays upon obtaining the Tau Cannon)
  31. Questionable Ethics 3 (named “BMS - QE1.mp3” in the game files, plays during the lobby ambush)
  32. Surface Tension 1 - Part 1 (named “BMS - ST1-Part1.mp3” in the game files, plays during the dam)
  33. Surface Tension 2 (plays during the cliff fight)
  34. Surface Tension 1 - Part 2 (named “BMS - ST1-Part2.mp3” in the game files, plays during the Apache battle when you first get the rocket launcher)
  35. Surface Tension 3 (named “BMS - ST4.mp3” in the game files, plays during the trip-mine booby trap)
  36. Surface Tension 4 (named “BMS - OAROld.mp3” in the game files, exclusive to the Surface Tension Uncut attachment, plays during the outdoor Marines versus aliens fight right before the “Satchel Charge Furnace Pipe” scene)
  37. Surface Tension 5 - Part 1 (named “BMS - ST3-Front(loop).wav” in the game files, plays during the Snark vent)
  38. Surface Tension 5 - Part 2 (named “BMS - ST3-End.mp3” in the game files, plays during the Gargantua Chase sequence right before the airstrike)
  39. “Forget About Freeman!”
  40. Lambda Core 1 (plays while descending down the elevator at the beginning of Lambda Core)
  41. Lambda Core 2 (named “BMS - Mscl_Ambience3.mp3” in the game files, plays once you get to the supply depot & are waiting for the ceiling turrets to be deactivated so the scientists can let you in)
  42. Office Complex (not used in-game currently, hopefully will be used in the Xen levels)
  43. Blast Pit 5 (named “BMS - BP Part 2.mp3” in the game files, not used in-game currently, hopefully will be used in the Xen levels)
  44. Apprehension (not used in-game currently, hopefully will be used in the Xen levels)
  45. End Credits Part 1
  46. End Credits Part 2

As you can see, I put the soundtrack songs that weren’t used in-game in an order that takes the possibility of them being used in the Xen levels into account.
