danielsangeo November 25, 2018, 1:41am #4

As to the getting lost thing, I’d suggest getting a map or three of the destinations where you want to go and, if that doesn’t help you in getting around and back to the place where you’re staying, ask around. If you have a GPS device, all the better.

I’ve taken the wrong bus even where I know my way around. Just ask the bus driver how to get to where you’re going. They should be able to help; if not, the bussing company should have a way to get you to where you’re going. Again, ask if you don’t have the information beforehand.
