AllYourCakeAreBelong November 24, 2018, 9:34pm #1

I think it would be interesting to see how you could include the pickup and throw into a combat situation. Imagine it, a Headcrab is coming towards you but you pick up a rock or other piece of debris and throw it at it! It would of course not be as powerful as a weapon and the strength of the throw wouldn’t be as good as the Gravity Gun but it would enable combat situations similar to using the Gravity Gun. Plus it would be pretty realistic. (If I were to pick up a large rock and throw it a soldier, I’m pretty sure it will hurt him regardless of any armor that he has that’s made to stop bullets) It would be an interesting way to use the new physics engine.
