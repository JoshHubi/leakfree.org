Here’s some personal experience with “making stuff and showing it to people or not”:

When I create something, I hate showing it to anyone when it’s still not 100% finished. It’s because the first impression is always very important, and I want the first impression to be the best one and the only one. Therefore, I want the first impression to be that of the final product, not something that’s only 97% finished. With a project as huge as Black Mesa, it’s of course impossible to not show any of it for 6 years, however, I’m sure “the developers” only want to show what they’re the most proud of at the moment, and that isn’t just any random screenshot. The question here isn’t whether they have time to press F5, but rather if they are considering showing us unfinished work while knowing that the finished product should be out soon?

Personally, the closer I am to finishing something, the more I say “I won’t show any of it now, since people will get to see the proper, finished version soon anyway”.
