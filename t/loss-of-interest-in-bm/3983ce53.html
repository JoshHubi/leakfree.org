SkyBlue November 24, 2018, 10:18pm #1160

I don’t think that the devs owe us anything, they’re making a free mod in their own time and they deserve lots of praise and respect for the awesome job they’re doing.

However, I do think that their attitude towards the community is poor. Having set a release date of the end of 2009 and failed to reach it (presumably due to the large amount of work still needed), they were then dismissive of the community’s reaction, if they bothered to respond to the forums at all. The lack of media updates is understandable if they’re working on Xen, but even an occasional message from the devs just to say “we’re still working on the mod” would go a long way just to reassuring the less rabid members of the forums that the devs haven’t forgotten about the community. I think it says a lot that by far the most prolific dev on the forums is CatzEyes, who doesn’t even have access to the latest build of the mod.

In short, I think the devs deserve a huge amount of respect from the community, but the community also deserves some acknowledgement from the devs, who don’t seem to care about their huge number of supporters.
