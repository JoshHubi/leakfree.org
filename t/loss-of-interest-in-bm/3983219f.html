[COLOR=‘Red’]WHEN WILL BLACK MESA BE RELEASED / NEW MEDIA / PERCENT DONE?[/SIZE]

[COLOR=‘LemonChiffon’]In the most recent Black Mesa Official Trailer, the developers put a 2009 date at the end of the trailer. Contrary to popular belief, this has been the only date ever hinted at or offered regarding a release timeframe. According to the Black Mesa 2009 Update thread posted December 6th 2009, Project Leader cman2k states “Unfortunately, to our great disappointment, Black Mesa will not make a 2009 release.” While the dev team does have an internal release date, to prevent any further disappointment or dashed expectations the official public “Release date” has reverted back to a “When it is done” mode. Requests for a release date, requests for more media, and requests for what percent done will be answered with a blanket “WHEN IT’S DONE.”

[align=center]

[align=center]Official statement by the Project Leader, cman2k[/align]

[/align]

Okay. This is an explanation from someone who has minimal experience with game development and knows a few people who develop games.

Black mesa was delayed because things changed in a years period during volunteer game development. In a volunteer project, devs quit a lot and they don’t work on the project nearly as much as devs who work for gaming companies as a full time job. Also some of the devs are probably just amateurs who are starting out and don’t have that much experience. Obviously, BM has been delayed for over six months which isn’t too bad considering that most mods like this don’t make it very far at all before they are abandoned. In a project like this it very hard even to make an accurate release date in the first place.

For my estimation on when it will be released:
Black mesa was probably thought to make a 2009 release until about Q3 2009, when they realized that due to changes in pace and what not it just wasn’t going to make it. They probably started to rush towards the end of 2009 and then thought that it would be better to just delay it and make it as good as possible. At the beginning of 2010, I think some devs quit and the whole team was reorganized. Now they trying to work on it as hard as possible to make their private release date. My estimation for release is about Q3 2010 because they aren’t giving us any updates, so it can’t be too long and I saw one dev post it would be released in 2010 a few weeks ago. What will probably happen is during the summer, the devs will say it’ll be out in the fall and they will actually meet the release date this time.
