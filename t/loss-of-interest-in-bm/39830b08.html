I think some of the confusion comes not just from that fateful 2009 release announcement, but that it wasn’t really called off until it was practically here. I think this gave the impression that this was looking like a sure thing until the devs noticed a few more things that needed fixing or tweaking, or there was still a little too much left to QUITE make that release time, little enough that you couldn’t quite make the call until the last minute.

So to a lot of us, it looked like it should at least be out very, very soon, that whatever little work remained that kept the team from making their release announcement would quickly be completed and we’d just have to wait another couple weeks, when in reality that may not have been the case. I’m guessing it was probably more similar to biding time before breaking up with the girlfriend, trying to gather your courage to have “the talk” or think of what to say.
