Omega_Absolute:

God if there were anymore updates/media, we wouldn’t need to play BM because we would all have already played “Black Mesa Slide Screen Edition”

Exactly, I’m fine with not knowing everything about the game. If you know too much, you may build up some sort of false image of what it’s going to be like, and when you finally play it, you may end up feeling weird about the fact that it isn’t what you expected. Yes, it’s like seeing a movie trailer that gives away too much, or tells you what the whole thing is going to be like in advance.

I think that when the mod is in such an advanced state, probably very close to completion, there is no way the developers would give up. If you worked 6 years on something this huge, told the entire world about it, and it’s now almost finished, there’s no way you’re going to say “oh well… let’s just cancel it and do something else”.

Yes it is indeed a bit frustrating to not know anything about how it’s going along because we’re all curious and we can’t wait, but if you think about it for a moment, you’ll realise that even if there are no updates, they’re still working on it. I wouldn’t mind a tiny twitter update like once every 2 weeks saying “hi chaps, all is well”, but other than making us feel a bit better, it wouldn’t serve much of a purpose anyway.

So even like this, no, I’m no losing interest, I’m actually more interested…
