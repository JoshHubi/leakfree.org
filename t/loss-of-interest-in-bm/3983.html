Black Mesa Archive The Cafeteria
Evan November 24, 2018, 9:01pm #1

It is now July and I have noticed that there are a lot less threads or discussions on the website lately. I’ve noticed that no one I know really talks about Black Mesa anymore. It seems that a lot of people are losing interest in BM because of the delay, no release date, less news and threads, and no updates to speak of. I think that if the devs gave us at least a written update saying the mod would be out in 2010, then more people would support it. Which, I can’t see the mod coming out any later than 2010 because the engine would be too dated compared to other games like Portal 2 and HL2: Episode 3. It’s kind of weird going on a website that was once visited and supported by lots of people, that now seems deserted. I know the devs say they don’t care about this, but the mod is losing less and less potential everyday.

I’m just sharing my concern about people losing interest in the mod. I just want to state my opinion and create a thread. I don’t want this to be a hate thread, just a place of discussion.
