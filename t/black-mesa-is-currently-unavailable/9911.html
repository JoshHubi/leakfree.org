I ran into this issue myself a few minutes ago. See if this fixes it:

Go Tools under your Library.

Find Source SDK 2007, right click on it, and then click Properties.

Go to the Local Files tab, and click “Verify Integrity of Tool Cache”.

Let the process complete, and try again. Let me know if that works for ya.
