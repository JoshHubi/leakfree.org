FERGUS November 25, 2018, 3:07am #5

There’s no console command, you have to change the skill.cfg located at “\Steam\steamapps\sourcemods\BMS\cfg” for maximum ammo.
For clip sizes, you’ll have to edit corresponding weapon script file located at “\Steam\steamapps\sourcemods\BMS\scripts”.
