Mah daddy has an awesome gun collection. His motto “The day they take away our right to bare arms is the day criminals will be the only ones owning them.”

While we do have licenses to own a gun, it’s prohibited to actually use them outside of the hunting grounds and shooting ranch. Additionally, people that own firearms have to keep the gun in one locked cabinet, and the bullets in another locked cabinet, at least xx meters apart from each other. When transporting the firearm (to the shooting ranch, for example), the gun has to be in a locked case, with the bullets in a different locked case. All in all, we have the right to own firearms, but not the right to bear them. The crime rate in Belgium is way lower than in the States, and sits around the middle of the crime rate rankings of Europe.

On the other hand, in Switzerland, adults who have served in the Swiss armed forces (amongst others) are allowed to bear arms. Switzerland has some of the lowest crime rate numbers in the world.

In the end I doubt the right to bear arms has anything to do with crime. I for one would rather give my wallet to a crazed gunman than pull out a gun of my own.
