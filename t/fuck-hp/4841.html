Try plugging in an external monitor. Do you get the flashing lines on it too? If not, then the video chipset is fine. Sometimes, the display cable will work a bit loose from the motherboard socket, or the cable gets stressed from running over the hinge. It’s a major disassembly job to check for these issues, so you might want to call around to local computer stores and see if they have a flat rate repair price. If it is the cable, then it shouldn’t be anywhere close to $400 to repair.
