I love this game. I was originally going to wait until it was on sale for like thirty or something, and then while replaying the demo I got my hands on the machete.

Needless to say, I jizzed and then purchased the game on tuesday.

Anyway, I love it. I love the atmosphere, I love the levels, I love that fucking hurricane. I wish the characters were more fun like the last game, but I like Ellis’s rambling, Nick’s sexiness, how gentle Rochelle is and Coach’s obsession with food. It’s not as ~iconic as the characters from the last game, but it’s cute nonetheless.

I hate the bots. I feel like they had a major downgrade. I haven’t played with humans yet, I do not think I am good enough right now. The finale to Dead Center with only one person that can carry/throw gas cans? Brutal. And for some reason the fucking bots wont pick up first aid kits. It pisses me off.

I love the bladed weapons the best, probably the machete by default. The atmosphere and how everything, even the non-diagetic score contributes to it is absolutely gorgeous. The special-normal infected make it interesting, and the new special infected are ridiculous when combined with one another.

Bots hate spitter goo, so if you’re trapped on the other side of the goo by another infected, game over. Sucks. I hate the bots. Oh well, yay machete.

tl;dr: worth the money.
