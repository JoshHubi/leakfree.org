The fix for Windows 10 is to Enable Borderless Fullscreen Mode

Go to Black Mesa’s “Properties” and then “Set launch options.”
Enter “-windowed -noborder” without the quotation marks.

Same thing as above just described a little differently.
Right click the game in your steam games list.
Click properties.
Under the general tab, click “Set Launch options”
Copy paste this:
-windowed -noborder
