The while probably not the easiest solution, it is effective. Go to the store and buy a Simon Gift card. Its a Visa gift card that works like a non-reloadable Visa Credit card. Bolteh November 24, 2018, 8:38pm #20

VISA Gift Cards are limited to the US only, though they can be used in Europe (just can’t purchase them over here).

Anyway, I try to never use my VISA, unless there’s no other option, and the thing I often do for online payments, is use Paypal that -isn’t- tied to my VISA, but instead uses the prepaid principle. I just wire money from my account to the Paypal account (the amount I need to pay) and pay for Steam games (and pretty much everything I buy online) that way.

Sure, it might take 2-3 days for the money to get onto the Paypal account, but it’s not like you purchase really urgent things on the internet anyway.
