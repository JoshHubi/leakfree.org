Maxey November 24, 2018, 9:37pm #35

Things change for a reason. So the old concept had cooler and creepier designs, but it’s possible that overall Valve wasn’t satisfied with how those concepts meshed together. Most of the time, game designers come up with set-pieces that look cool on paper, but that don’t really add up to the general sense of the game, so they get scrapped.

That’s what happened with Half-Life 2 over the years it was in production. It was originally going to be a longer and darker game, but it’s very much possible that Valve decided that it was too long and some of the original concepts would just ruin the overall pace of the game, so they changed it into something more short-ish and not so dark.
