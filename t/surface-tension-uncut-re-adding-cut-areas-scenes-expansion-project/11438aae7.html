Right, video and final version are released.

Latest Version: 2.0 Final - Final Release (Installer Version). Download here.[/SIZE]

I’ve included an installer which I spent nearly all day working on. It installs all the files necessary for ST Uncut, and also backs up your old copies of any files which ST Uncut mess with. There’s also an uninstaller, which when you run it, reverts the files to their original format. Hopefully this should make ST Uncut much more user friendly. The final release of OaR Uncut will have a similar style installer.

You can find the video here:

Video Link - v2.0 w/ Commentary[/SIZE]

To those of you who’ve been following the thread/previous versions closely, the video may not be too interesting, I didn’t say much on there which I haven’t said already on here. I also only ran through the maps up to the gasworks - as the final sections haven’t changed much at all and my Fraps was being quite annoying. Still, there are some fun bits in the video and I was feeling very talkative whilst filming it, so I’m quite pleased with it. Let me know what you think of it, and enjoy it!

Thanks everyone for helping me revisit ST Uncut. Though it’s been a massive chore at the times and there’s been a fair share of excessive drama on these forums, it’s been fun and rewarding. I can now put this project behind me once and for all and start getting OaR Uncut finished to a similar standard. You guys have been great, I look forward to carrying on my journey through the wilderness of Uncut content with you guys by my side.
