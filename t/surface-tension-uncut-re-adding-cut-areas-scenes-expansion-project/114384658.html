TextFAMGUY1:

TL;DR:
This next section differs most strongly from the original, hopefully for the better (I think you’ll agree). Like in the original, you blow up the blast doors in front of you to progress further, encountering several Alien Grunts along the way. It differs strongly from the original here, in a way I feel is much better. Rather than entering a giant empty ugly warehouse for no apparant reason, you’re now following a fortified road, and you come across a helipad in another courtyard, where 2 Ospreys appear to be LOADING UP soldiers, rather than dropping them off (this scene intentionally ambiguous - are the HECU simply mobilizing their troops, or are they already preparing to pull out?). A Manta Ray zooms past overhead, with 2 harrier jets in hot pursuit. As it flies past, it blows up one of the Ospreys with a beam emitted from the bottom (I am pretty sure it does this somewhere in the original too), and this causes the other Osprey to fly off in panic, as Alien Grunts and Slaves start teleporting in around them as well. A battle then breaks out between the HECU and the Aliens (this battle was already happening when you entered the warehouse in the original). Just as it looks like the HECU are about to lose, an LAV bursts in through a door leading outside the facility (this happened in the original). The humans ultimately lose and you’re left battling the aliens.

Bear in mind, the harrier jet sequence might not be doable. The jets aren’t NPCs, they’re animated props, and their flight paths are entirely pre canned. The Manta Rays, however, are NPCs. I don’t know if they still have chest beams. You should probably do a test map to work out what kind of entity work you’re in for. If you can do it, AWESOME. More power to you. But this sounds like a nightmare in the SDK.

Also, loading soldiers into the Ospreys also might not be doable. I guess you could have the soldiers run behind the Osprey on the tarmac, use Kill inputs to get rid of the soldier NPCs, then have the osprey take off as if it had loaded the soldiers?
