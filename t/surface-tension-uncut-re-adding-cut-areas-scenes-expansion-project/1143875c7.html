Corner shadows not being aligned isn’t really something I can fix - the lightmaps are aligned perfectly. It’s just the way Source handles the lighting/shadowing. It doesn’t look that bad.

I positioned Barney to the left for a reason - he doesn’t actually say that. It’s just a sound file being played. Was MUCH easier to implement. Besides, there’s loads of Xen Infestation blocking your view anyway, even if I’d moved him to the right you wouldn’t be able to see him properly.

Yeah, it might be a bit big and empty, but it’s passed the stage where I can do anything major about it now. It’s already got frame issues, adding anything more will tank the framerate. Just assume that the more open areas are used for transportation. One is a walkway, one is the road.

As for the military truck carrying the gas, that isn’t an issue either. Think a bit deeper, I built a mini little “story” into the gasworks. The HECU were clearly doing something in the gasworks. They’ve set up a little emplacement. They have an M35 and an M1075 there, clearly hastily set up to contain some gas. Where they intended on taking it, who knows. But they met resistance. It looks like there was a serious battle between the HECU and the Aliens, and some security guards got muddled up in it somehow too. At some point, perhaps during the battle or after it, an Apache came crashing through the roof. The fact that all the setup looks rushed is because that’s the look I was going for.
