That is c2a5d.

Now, I’m no expert on making skyboxes (because I haven’t had to deal with them - yet), but it sounds to me like you’re missing c2a5d’s corresponding skybox texture file. Either that, or somehow the skybox material list got bonked somehow. Check the appropriate gcf and see if there is anything missing from the list.

JeffMOD November 25, 2018, 3:35am #2650

If the material was missing completely, you’d get pink and black checkerboards.
