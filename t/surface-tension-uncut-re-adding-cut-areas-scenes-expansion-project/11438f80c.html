TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 3:29am #2417

BT actually fixed my line issue, free of charge, quicker than they said they would. I am genuinely shocked, but pleased. I’ve already started up a 9 - 5 routine for mapping, beginning today. I’ve been whizzing along fixing up problems here and there, and am looking for a Wednesday-Thursday release for the beta version of ST Uncut 2.0, of which there will only be 1 testing version for the public before final release. OaR Uncut still needs to be finished, and I’m eager to move onto that with my newly recharged batteries.

Had my first full day of mapping for ST Uncut 2.0, though I’ll probably carry on into the evening as I’m having fun. Over the course of today I’ve tidied up most of the loose ends on C2A5G, and it’s practically (aside from a few minor problems which I know about) ready to ship and show off.

The most significant change which has been made recently is the office block after the helipad is pretty big now. Though not terrifyingly expansive or massively impactful on the overall experience, it does add a neat little 5 minutes of exploration for more inquisitive players. In fact, it’s entirely skippable - the player can still just go through the security doors into H like they could in previous versions. Or they can choose to explore that office block, collect some goodies, find some cool things. It also helps to make the 2 maps (G and H) feel more connected, as well as making the Helipad have more believable surroundings, far moreso than in 1.11. I’m happy with how it’s turned out. I can’t really expand it any further than I already have due to the entity/brush limits, but it’s turned out a decent enough size.

The reason I’m writing this down is because I want to increase my community involvement, which admittedly tapered off a lot towards the end of university, for obvious reasons. I’d like to get myself as involved with the community as I was during the development of ST Uncut/early OaR Uncut. It just works…better I think.

So, here’s my question for everyone. One of the criticisms which I’ve heard levied at ST Uncut quite a few times is that the player spends too much time observing battles between NPCs (car park, outside garage, helipad, Abrams fight, bathroom snarks vs zombies, headcrabs vs HECU in furnace, Garg vs Marines in I), and not enough time actually fighting. While I would argue that I’ve only added 2 fights which weren’t in HL1 (car park and snarks vs zombies), I can see the reasoning behind such a criticism. As a result, I’m going to add some player oriented conflicts to the offices next to the helipad.

My question is, what sorts of encounters would you like to see in the office block? At most I’ll have 3, but it’s more likely that there’ll be 2. My current plans were for a Zombies vs Player fight in the storage room, and a AGrunt/Vort vs Player fight in one of the offices. All HECU in the office block will be already dead, to explain the alien presence and lack of HECU.

If you can think up of interesting but REALISTIC scenarios for combat in the offices, I’d be happy to hear it. I was considering Houndeyes but the problem is the focus of the latter half of Surface Tension is generally on the Xenian military. Otherwise, I’ll just stick with my current plan!
