There aren’t any PC specs in OP, do you even techsupport?

I recommend playing black mesa on a Core 2 Duo (E6600 is the best, but mobile T7600 works too) and radeon x1300 turbocache GPU or one from the same generation, that’s the only setup so far on which I had zero crashes from start to finish in one playthrough(although with no mods).
