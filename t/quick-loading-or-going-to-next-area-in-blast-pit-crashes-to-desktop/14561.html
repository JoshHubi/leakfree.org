Have you researched what others have done to fix problems since the update.

The following fixes 80% of all game problems after the update.

  1. Restart the computer. (shutting down and turning it back on is not the same in W8/10)
  2. Next, Verify the game cache. if you don’t know how, ask.
  3. Disable any and all mods, somes mods are broken after the update.
  4. Restart the chapter/level from the NEW game menu, not a saved game. A save could be corrupt.

Do all 4 and you will probably be back playing the game in less than half an hour.
