Katana November 25, 2018, 2:17am #4

Yosharian, what it needs is the Source SDK Base (2007). It’s essentially the latest version of the source engine as a “game” without any content in it. You can’t play Black Mesa without that installed, and we did try to explain this to people beforehand.

Go to your library, click in the upper left and select Tools, then look in that list.
