We can blow up tanks, but I guess we cannot blow up vehicles or making bulletholes in their windows and metal…

Forgot to answer this one. There are 2 ways to do vehicles in source. You can have a vehicle with the material properties of metal or metalvehicle and it will just show up as metal bullet holes when shot. For the windows to show up with broken glass details when shot you have to compile the glass separately and place it as a separate model. Our old joop was like that, that is why there wasn’t any glass in them the LDs didn’t place them. The other was is how I did it, all compiled together and the bullet holes show as metal hits on the glass. Unless someone knows a work around that is the limitations as far as I know.
