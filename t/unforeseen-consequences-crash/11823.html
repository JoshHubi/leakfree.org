I have also included some screenshots.

A description of what I did to cause the error:
I am in Sector B Coolant Reserve offices.

After passing the dying guard and through the corridor (where it loads the next scene), you go through the door to the right. Obviously not the one with the cooking zombie.

After entering the only door on the right, you normally choose to beat the large wood crates to the right. I, however, went left and was faced with a smaller wood crate and two shelves on either side. So, to explore, I jumped onto the single small crate and then to the shelf to the right of the crate when you entered the room. This is where the game crashed.

An error message did display, I will replay the event to get that and attach it. It is attached but is not descriptive.

I have also attached the scenes leading up to the point where you see the crate that I jumped onto to jump on the shelf to the right of that crate. Note that I did have to press the Shift key and then jump to get where I didn’t fall before I was able to get to the shelf. If the Shift key was not pressed while running and jumping, you simply fell and the error did not display and the game continues on.

Not sure if this helps with the overall game, but hey, it might help you find other things that could be wrong, etc.
