Right off the bat I’d like to say I cannot wait for Black Mesa to come out. Ever since we were treated with the spectacular trailer we saw back in November, I knew I had found a place that shared my enormous passion for Half Life.

So I joined this forum. To talk, chat, speculate, about everything Half Life.

Who is Gman? What’s going to happen in Episode 3? Who would you cast in a Half Life movie? These are just a few things I was gleefully excited to post and read about with fellow fans. It took me far too long to realize one simple but huge detail about this place.

It’s full of morons.

I cannot remember where and when, but I mentioned how, for the life of me, I do dont understand why I keep coming back here every day and read the utter bullshit you guys splatter across my screen time and time again like it’s - I don’t even know. Trolling is one thing, but I gotta say that this is by far the dumbest forum I’ve experienced. I can only assume that the 3DRealms forum can’t hold a cock up to you guys because some of the things read here are stunning beyond belief.

This began when guys were spewing how Gman is Gordon. Everybody knows it’s impossible, but it never died out.

Shepherd. People want him in Episode 3. Fuck off.

Time travel in the Half Life story is sloppy storytelling. If you think Valve would stoop to the depths of hell and pull out time travel, then your bar for good plot is buried under two miles of shit. Someone even mentioned how they want Nihilanth to come back and help the rebels? Really?

A thread about where Gordon’s feet are. Again, a thread about where Gordon’s feet are.

I knew I’d had it when people in the Half Life Movie thread were saying how Gordon Freeman doesn’t need to be in it. Yes, he does.

You know you’re a Half Life fan when this shit truly bothers you. I’m tired of signing, and rolling my eyes after the first thing I read.

Goodbye. And fuck you all.
