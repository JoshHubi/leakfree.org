papernick November 25, 2018, 3:38am #3

long post incoming

Cliffhanger: Great way to follow TV shows. Pretty UI.
Chrome: A bit sluggish, but the best in terms of features.
Krome: Get Android notifications on your PC (on desktop Chrome).
Eye In Sky: Best weather app.
Nova Launcher: Best launcher.
Fabrik: ebook reader with cloud syncing.
Feedly: Best RSS feeds reader.
Poweramp: Music player with lots of options and skins.
Pocket Casts: Podcast app.
Pocket: Cross-platform read-it-later app.
PushBullet: Send files from your pc to your phone wirelessly.
Shush!: Gives option when you mute your phone to restore sound volume after x amount of hours.
Songkick: Lets you know when a band you like is coming to your city or town.
Timely: Best alarm clock.
Tweedle: Clean looking twitter app.

Also, get the Google Keyboard or Swype instead of the god-awful samsung one.

As far as games go, get Kingdom Rush. Best android game by a mile.
