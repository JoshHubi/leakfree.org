Hmm Cata… well…

I am actually not quite sure what to make of it yet. Going over old ground never really seemed like a good idea to me as they did with Naxx at the beggining of WotLK. I can understand why they did that but still seems a bit of a let down. Maybe just becuase i had already seen it, again one reason i can understand why as many didnt. But to go over old ground now seems really silly as im sure atleast 98% have seen Deadmines. Ohh great Heroic Deadmines with 3 Pirates up. Im sure some of you will get that but some wont

Graphics, well i guess i had hoped to see more than just an upgrade to the water, such as a more 3D armour instead of the painted on chest and legs they seem to have atm. Wont stop me enjoying the content though .

The biggest problem though is the talents and the way they are itemising gear this time. My main atm is a warrior tank, and gearing it up/ choosing the right talents was part of the fun for me. The warrior/ any tank i guess, is by far the hardest to keep right especialy while gearing, making sure you still have Enough Health, Hit, Expertise, Dodge, Block, Parry and of course the Defence cap while maintaing enough threat points was fun. Now it seems they have gone for a silly system where you spend talents and… thats it, you can tank. I say tank is the hardest because any other class is pretty easy, just get hit/ expertise if melee and stack whatever you need to make your character hit hard or heal for more. Pretty easy to gear up most melee/ ranged and healing classes.

It cant be easy trying to balance that many classes for PvP and PvE content so i suppose they have to do something like this to make it right in both areas of the game.

I like the fact they are making it good for casual aswell but i wish they had kept atleast some degree of having to think about your gear choices in Cata. As said it was part of the fun of the game for me. Again aslong as the content and the NEW dungeons and raids are not copy paste bosses, have plenty of new boss abilities, i guess ill be ok with it.

Id like to see more guild focus though, such as the guild leveling. More focus on actual guild event type things. Guild housing would be nice, so that everyone has something to do… building it and the up-keep for instance. Guild Quests outdoors instead of just a boss kill would be nice too i guess. Not going to happen but we can hope.

Seems silly to worry about an expansion to a game, but there is just nothing out there at this time to compare to WoW (For me atleast) or keep my interest for very long. Ive played just about every MMO out there i can think of and have seen, but just keep coming back. I cant go play FPS online… im just to old and slow now(damn you kids and your cat like reactions), and of course the cheats in FPS just spoil it.
