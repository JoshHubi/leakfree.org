bigfan421:

Is there a Cruella De Vil Costume? (in all the videos and Pictures I’ve seen of inside Walt Disney World, I’ve never seen Cruella)
Is there really a Pizza Planet Restaurant on the park premises? (I think I saw it on one of the Travel channel programs)
Does Cinderella’s Castle actually have an interior (apart from the main entrance) or is much of what we see in the photos a shell?

There is! She doesn’t come out often. Your best bet would be at Hollywood Studios. There are thousands of costumes, and each park only has about thirty at any given time. They rotate two or three of them every month or so.

There is a Pizza Planet, but it’s not a Pizza Planet like it should be. It’s a counter service pizza and fast food restaurant at Hollywood Studios. There are no big cool robots guarding the entrance, and the arcade is just okay. The pizza’s pretty good, though. Still worth a look.

The castle is indeed a shell… mostly. At the base of the castle, (the part that you walk through), there are massive mosaics showing different parts of the story. At this level, there is also an entrance and reservation area for the “Cinderella’s Royal Table” restaurant. From there, you take an elevator up to about the middle of the structure. That’s the restaurant proper. It books up far in advance, but you get an amazing view of the fireworks.

And then there’s the Royal Suite. It is a magical place that you will never ever see unless you are an exceptionally adorable child or produce and walk around with an exceptionally adorable child. If you meet either of these criteria, a cast member may select you (out of the million or so people at any of the four parks), to stay there for the night. It’s in one of the turrets, I think. If you get this, you get waited on hand and foot, and can skip any line in the park.
