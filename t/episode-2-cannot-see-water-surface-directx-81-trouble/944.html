Mangled November 24, 2018, 6:02pm #10

I believe the problem lies with rendering water reflections, which is expensive. When you are underwater, reflections on the surface aren’t drawn.

Episode’s 2 Engine is extensively modified from that of HL2 and Episode 1 and requires a better system to run. It’s commonly referred to as the Orange Box engine (the real name for it is the Source 2007 engine). Garry’s Mod uses this engine too.

I don’t think there is any real solution to fix it using the card you’ve got. Just use whatever settings work best I suppose.
