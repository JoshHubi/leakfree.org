Choose a few games you have played recently and say whether you like them or not, why, and if you’d recommend them. I’ll start.

Far Cry 2
Fail African fps iron sight free roamer. Many minor irritations such as mortars that are extremely hard to find, gun jams that take around 5 minutes to unjam, laggy multiplayer, and more. It does have great graphics, but not as good as Crysis Warhead which was released before it. Gets a :meh: Do not recommend.

Dead Space
Pretty good third person space horror. Had an intense horror effect, interesting storyline and really good graphics, but it is very lacking in action and I always felt starved for ammo, something that drives me insane in video games. gets a . I do recommend if you really like horror, but I suggest buying a few of the games listed below first.

Call of Juarez Bound in Blood
Amazing wild west style iron sights fps. Wonderful game mechanics, stunning graphics, very fun storyline (a spectacular climax) and wonderful setting. The one downside is a slightly short campaign, it took me 6 hours and I usually take my time with video games. Lots of clever little things packed into this game, such as when using the bow you get a short period of slo mo when zooming and door breaking scenes in which you and your brother break into a building filled with enemies through giant doubles doors and mow them down in classic wild west style. there is some nice periods of free roam, but they were a little short. Gets a ^_^.

F.E.A.R. Project Origin
Best game ever. (fps iron sights horror) Wonderful horror effect that isn’t over the top, has bits of comic relief, intense firefights, great game mechanics, great storyline, nice slo mo feature and reaply value with its awards system. I really have nothing bad to say about this game. Gets :jizz:.

Prototype
Spectacular third person “power fantasy” free roamer. Whether your tearing through Manhatten trashing citizens, or completing a mission Prototype is very fun. Nice upgrades feature, fun game mechanics, very long fun storyline, and great boss fights. Great rewards for exploration with collectible landmarks and hints. Nothing more satisfying than sneaking up on a soldier and snapping his neck only to become him, smashing through crowds of citizens with a massive shield protruding from your arm or drop kicking fucking helicopters. (there’s a comic somewhere on that) Nothing bad to say. Get’s ^_^. Do buy, but get F.E.A.R. Project Origin first.

Do not buy even if you have played and gotten bored of every single video game from today all the way back to 1998. Go online and find some flash games instead.
