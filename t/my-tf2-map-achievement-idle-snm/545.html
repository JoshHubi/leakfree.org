[align=left][/align]Well, I made a TF2 map for a server to replace its stupid, generic achievement_idle map. It’s called achievement_idle_snm (working title, it’s going through an identity crisis and may be changed to a_i_arena, or a_i_orange) it’s a basic TF2 idle map, but with fixes to each of the annoying things from it’s predecessor. It’s not in it’s final yet, but the reason I’m showing the BMS community this, is that I need testing done on it. I can only test it with one person on the map, and everyone I send it to says the same basic response, but I need critique, and I’m entrusting you guys to give it to me by testing and replying here. Of course.

(Oh, and if you’d like to post screenshots on what you find that is wrong and I should change, or that is good and needs to stay the same, it’d be much easier than guessing what you’re talking about.)
