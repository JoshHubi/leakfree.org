You’re in the room with all those hounds right? On top of the shelve in the corner there are 3 grenades. You can get to them by jumping up on the forklift. Pick them up and throw them at the switch on the other side of the fence. That destroys the switch and turns off the power

Madcat1030 November 25, 2018, 2:24am #33

The final one is the Snark room. I missed it the first time through. It’s in the room marked A07 “Minor Xenos” in lab A.

From where you enter the level, after you leave the Garg test chamber, you go up a ramp. The main hallway goes right and then left to the first zappy room. Instead of going right, look right ahead. There’s a small door (you’ll see two locked bulkhead doors to the left). Inside is another super-zapper, as well as a fire button that shoots snarks. Press that until the machine is empty to get credit for the experiment.
