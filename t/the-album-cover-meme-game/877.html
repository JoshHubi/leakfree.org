One of the few forum games that never gets old.

1 – Go to “wikipedia.” Hit “random”
or click https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random
The first random wikipedia article you get is the name of your band.

2 – Go to “Random quotations”… Read More
or click https://www.quotationspage.com/random.php3
The last four or five words of the very last quote of the page is the title of your first album.

3 – Go to flickr and click on “explore the last seven days”
or click https://www.flickr.com/explore/interesting/7days
Third picture, no matter what it is, will be your album cover

Optionally, you can finalize the album cover in Photoshop or whatever, or just post it here.

GO
