I posted these on Steam Discussions, but I figured it would get more attention here, I also added screenshots and position data.

  1. After you kill the Gargantua, drive the train on the turntable and the security guard rotates it for you, if you go back up in the control room and kill him, you get a “failed to preserve critical personnel” game over screen, even though he is not needed anymore (as far as I can tell). By the way I don’t know why he is mission critical at all. I noticed that in other cases (e.g. the end of “We’ve got hostiles” with the scientist in the control room) it’s usually set up so NPCs can be killed and the player can just press whatever buttons the NPC was supposed to press (and the buttons are locked while the NPC is alive). This could be done here as well.
