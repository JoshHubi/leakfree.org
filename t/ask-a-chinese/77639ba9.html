Two questions about food.

Rice or Noodles? Which do you prefer?

Personally I am a rice man.
I hate Dong fen noodles. For some reason they always get stuck in my throat and I end up choking. Yeah, I’m too retarded to eat.

Dao xiao mian is alright. I prefer the thicker noodles.
When I lived in Japan I always chose Udon over Ramen.
My girlfriend loves noodles and soup, so we’d usually argue over where to go for lunch.
She’d always want to go to a noodle place and I’d want to go to a rice place.

Xinjiang and Hunan food broke my stomach, so I can’t eat that stuff anymore.

Do you have a personal favourite regional dish?

I did like Xinjiang until I couldn’t eat the stuff anymore, so I guess Shandong dishes were what I ate most. Hong shao rou is a heart attack waiting to happen, but for some reason I quite liked those giant lumps of fat. Although I couldn’t eat much of it.
