This post is not trying to condone any sort of Witchhunting, but merely to Inform the Community, and Crowbar Collective, of the Issue taking place at this time.

As some of you may be aware, Hunt Down The Freeman released 18 hours ago on Steam.

Upon release, it was noted that the game was very buggy, shipped without the 36GB of Cut-scenes, and made heavy use of Stolen Assets, Including Maps, Weapons, Models, and Enemies.

Steam User Fourmula has been attempting to compile a list of all these stolen assets.

Of note, is that it has been confirmed that Hunt Down The Freeman is using the Vortigaunt Model from Black Mesa. It is unknown if any other assets have been stolen, at this time.

Other Stolen Assets to note:
-Stolen Zombie Panic : Source map
-Multiple Stolen Garrys Mod addons and maps
-Stolen FireArms:Source assets.
-Stolen running animation from Payday 2
-Stolen Killing Floor 2 Weapons
-Several Models stolen from a FUZE Model Pack
-Stolen Car Model from Stalker
-Multiple assets from Half Life 2, L4D2, DoD:S, and CS:GO used Blatantly, Including .VPK’s
