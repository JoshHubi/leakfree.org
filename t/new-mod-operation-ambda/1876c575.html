Well, I go by Alyx’s age to do my calculation for how long gordon was suspended, and the age of the rebels to calculate how long it was between the combine invasion and when gordon woke up.

Alyx seemed to be roughly 2-6 at the time that the resonance cascade hit, judging by the picture that Eli had in his lab in HL2(it might have been an old picture, which would have actually made her older at the time) She appears to be in her early 20s by HL2 (no older than late 20s) this implies that 15-20 years have passed since the resonance cascade.

Then we look at the rebels who have been unable to reproduce due to the suppression field. No one appears to be younger than 20. Heck, most of the rebels look to be mid 30s or older. From this it can be extrapolated that there has been at LEAST 15-20 years of time SINCE the combine invaded and set up the suppression field.

The first point pretty much declares an upper limit on how much time has passed, while the second points to the lower limit. I can’t imagine more than 5 years taking place between the resonance cascade and the combine invasion. Personally, I’d put it in closer to 1-2 years after the resonance cascade that the combine invaded and the 7 hour war took place. Any more than that and Alyx is practically going to be older than Gordon in terms of actual time experienced.

Shaktal November 24, 2018, 7:09pm #107

Just because we don’t see any children or anyone younger than 30 doesn’t mean that there isn’t any. If we consider real war, the youngest people are those most easily swayed by propaganda, and therefore the youngest people would be targets of opportunity for Civil Protection enrolment (bearing in mind that Civil Protection are just Humans). This could account for some of the lack of the young populous.

Furthermore, younger people are more likely to have fled after the combine invaded, which again lowers the number of young people that we have seen, again, this does not mean that we would see no young people, but it would account for some.

We then have to take into consideration that because young people are more likely to be swayed by propaganda, those who had rebuked the Combine invasion would be more likely to be killed sooner than the older populous who resisted, as the older populous would be more cautious and less likely to act rashly.

Also, any newborns would have been hidden or evacuated by their parents as Gordon only sees City17 and a few other places, as we do not see much countryside, that is the most probable place any of the youngest populous would be.

This greatly reduces the probability of seeing a young person, therefore it is possible that the combine invasion took place more recently that 15 years before hl2, maybe even 10 or 15 years. Although this may sound like comparatively little time, in real life technology is increasing at an exponential rate, add to this the combine’s larger empire (and therefore populous) and it is possible for their technology to have increased drastically in only 10-15 years.
