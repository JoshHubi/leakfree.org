don’t know if this helps, from dev TextFAMGUY1
We removed a crapload of old shaders from the game with this update, for optimisation and performance reasons. It’s likely that your Hammer is trying to load at a Direct X level below 9, or at Shader Model 2.0, which causes it to have many display issues as we removed those shaders. I would know as I had this exact same problem when the old stuff was stripped out.

Luckily, there is a fix. When this happened to me, it was because my laptop was making Hammer run from the integrated graphics rather than my graphics card. So I used the Nvidia Control Panel to tell it to use the graphics card, and voila! Fixed! So, try and make sure your computer isn’t forcing Hammer to use integrated graphics, that is likely the issue.

TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:50am #6

Yes, just to be perfectly clear: Hammer does not decide in any way whatsoever what your computer or graphics card does with it. That’s entirely down to the hardware manufacturer and what they do with their drivers, which we obviously have zero influence over. If Hammer is running on integrated graphics, then it ALWAYS was. It just never caused a graphical issue before, because our engine supported SM2.0 and lower Direct X, which is what integrated graphics used. For optimisation, development and performance, we stripped out all the old, mostly deprecated stuff, which will be the reason why you’re getting visual issues now. No other reason!
