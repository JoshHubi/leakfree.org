danielsangeo November 25, 2018, 3:37am #6

Press the tilde key (~) (should be on the top left of your keyboard) to bring up the console.

Type mat_dxlevel 90 there and see if that solves your problem.

Note: if the tilde key does not bring up the console, go to the in-game menu, click Options, click on the Keyboard tab, click on the Advanced button, and then place a checkmark on “Enable developer console”, then try the command in the console again.

Alternatively, get a newer graphics card.
