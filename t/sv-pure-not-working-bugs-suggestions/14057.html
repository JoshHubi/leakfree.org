Hello,

Let’s get straight to the point.

I will list only a very few bugs that bother me right now and make a few suggestions.

Bugs:
Sv_pure is not working.
Game has HUGE performance issues client side when a lot of players are online, low fps, freezing, etc on fairly high end PC; GTX 780 + fx8350 @ 4.7ghz.
Sometimes the rpg dot of other players is visible thru walls.

Suggestions:
Add motd, it’s useful for a whole bunch of reasons including but not limited to; displaying stats, displaying rules, streaming music.
Allow spectate for individual players & display their names, don’t switch the target we spectate when they die, let us watch who we want, we sometimes don’t spectate people for fun.
Add spectators to the scoreboard, we can’t see who’s online & watching us.
Add SPEC tag to chatting spectators, not DEAD.
Spawning takes too long, people are complaining, allow server owners to disable that CS:GO like death screen via cvar so players can spawn faster, we don’t like waiting to spawn more time than we spend playing.
MP3 files don’t work as sounds, only .wav works… .wav is much bigger in size, allow .mp3
Encrypt in game chat.

I may update / reply to this post in the future with new stuff.
