BaTz281 November 24, 2018, 10:21pm #9

Here’s a funny thing. The system I just built (i7-930 with 6GB G.SKiLL RAM) had an issue where Windows 7 would go into standby, then BSOD immediately upon resume. I memtested each stick individually for 10 passes, no issue. Then I tried putting the machine into Standby with each stick one by one, and lo and behold, had a bad stick. Wonder what Windows’ issue was with that stick, since memtest didn’t care. RMA’d the kit, new set works fine

As for the OP, I would agree that the LEDs on the motherboard were probably there to begin with. However, I would double check the one below your graphics card - that may indicate that you need to plug an additional Molex cable into the motherboard to supply additional PCI power. My old Asus A8N-SLI had that feature.
