Hi BMS devs, first i want to thank you for probably one of the best remakes in videogame´s history, the result of you work is pretty much valve quality, and this is something not less than amazing.

I have a computer with nvidia 3dvision and i try to play every game in 3d. _The weapons, they look like errr giant weapons/models in the background of the scenery, it is the first time i have seen something like this, this is another issue that maybe is fixable.(As an option in the menu maybe?)

_Some water affects, a common problem that probably has no solution.

_Some backgrounds, this happens with other valve games like L4D so again it probably has no solution.

I know that all this is not really important, but i wanted to tell my experience. Otherwise, the game is amazing on 3d, maybe someone out there who likes to play on 3d as much as me can mod the game on the future to make it compatible or maybe some of these problems can be so easily solved that the dev team can expend time on it.
