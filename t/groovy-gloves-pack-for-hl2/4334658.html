NOTE For everyone that finds these forums, tries to download this, and doesn’t know what to do, here is what you do:

Step 1: Extract to steam/steamapps/Half-Life2/HL2

                          OR

If you are running a mod: steam/steamapps/sourcemods/The mod you have

Step 2: Open your CS_Enemy_Pack folder up until you reach cs_enemy_models

Step 3: Choose what model you want it for your Combine to be

Step 4: Choose your desired Combine’s model

Step 5: Copy and paste that fine piece of ass to steam/steamapps/half-life2/hl2/models (Or in your mod folder’s models. Either way).

Step 6: Go into your CS Enemy Texture folder and copy and paste the Materials for that same Combine skin you downloaded.

Basically, if all that was too complicated:

Copy and paste all models and textures of what Combine you want.
