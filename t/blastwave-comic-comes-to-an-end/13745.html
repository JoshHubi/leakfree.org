Blastwave comic comes to an end

Tiki August 11, 2020, 11:17am #1

[/size]Last night, one of the finest webcomics of our generation came to a startling but suitable conclusion. Gone With the Blastwave, a post-apocalyptic video-game inspired webcomic by random Finnish artist Morriperkele, made it to ten years old with a cult-following of post-apocalypse fans everywhere. It was religiously updated two, sometimes three, times per year. It will be missed but never forgotten. Be you red, blue, yellow, or even green at heart, your life was enriched by Blastwave’s presence.
For those of you yet to be indoctrinated, I am obliged to provide you with a starting point (don’t worry, it’s quite short and light on words): https://www.blastwave-comic.com/index.php?p=comic&nro=1
