The OP has a point.

During the beginning of We’ve Got Hostiles we see a zombie pull a Barney into an airduct. But later we come out of that same airduct shaft and there’s no sign of it.

The developers missed a big opportunity for creating suspense. Imagine you are inside the ventilation shaft when suddenly your flashlight picks up something red…it’s blood…human blood. You pause and start to scan the rest of the area with your dim light, the blood is smeared all through the top, bottom, and sides…continuing on endlessly.

Suddenly you hear loud bangs coming your way. You realize it’s coming from behind. You flip around 360° and see something white coming toward you fast. You draw your Glock preparing to face a headcrab, then you realize it’s something much bigger- a full sized zombie!

I know most of you are going to say this will suck or take too much time to work out…but think about it.
