Darkhoodness November 25, 2018, 2:31am #6

Sorry to disappoint you guys, but…

Ross Scott on his news feed:

I’ve had people ask me if I plan to switch Freeman’s Mind over to the new game. No, I don’t. I think the graphical difference would be somewhat disruptive / distracting to the series, especially if I get to Xen before the BM team does. I originally considered switching over after Freeman blacked out since that would be a smoother transition, but I passed that point, so I’m just going to keep going on Half-Life: Source.

Personally I agree with him - Part of the reason why Freeman’s Mind is funny is because he mocks the original Half-Life’s unrealistic level design, plus Half-Life: Source has a certain charm to it. I’m only making a (badly done) Black Mesa rendition as a fun challenge I’ve set for myself, while not really taking it seriously.
