The_KillSmith November 25, 2018, 2:54am #149

Uhh… “The infamous Derp Fish”?

That’s the “dopefish”… it references an easter egg from the original Quake, which in turn was a nod to Tom Hall’s creation in one of the Commander Keen games… and then the easter egg continued on through a few more id software games and even other games from other devs, which have always considered id software as the architect of the FPS.

Whenever someone references the Dopefish, it’s basically a nod to the old school FPS players that were deathmatching before a lot of the current gaming community could walk and talk.

Shockwave S08:

The “Temperature Analysis” panel on Blast Pit’s rocket-engine control-console shows some text that, upon searching for said text on Google, reveals that it is the lyrics to the chorus of [‘Daisy Bell (Bicycle Built for Two)’ - Harry Dacre], a song dating all the way back to 1892.
