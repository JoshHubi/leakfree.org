shmileydawg November 25, 2018, 2:35am #104
Pyro:

What does material condition X-ray mean, though?

It means that every closure that is marked with an “X” must remained closed, or must be closed after going through or working in the space. It’s for very low traffic places like manholes into a ships smoke stack or any type of tanks (fuel for example). The three most common MATCONs are X-Ray, Yoke, and Zebra. Each setting gives a ship better damage control ability, X-Ray being the least, Zebra being the most.
