Storyline
Your name is John Provat, you are a scientist working at the Coredo Research Facility Labs ( CRFL ).
Your late on your work and you need to test unknown DNA of a creature that has recently been caught.
When you finally arrive on work, you notice that everyone’s acting strange today.
You get your lab to test the DNA, when you are analyzing it, you notice that the DNA of the Unknown creature includes some human DNA, that is weird, this means it is or was a human.
When you are at the terminal to report that the power went out, you suddenly hear a scream behind you.
You decide to find out what that was…

Description
A survival horror game, fight your way through hordes of zombies, demons and other evil creatures.
The game also contains puzzles, zombies ofcourse, a huge selection of weapons, scary atmosphere that will keep you on the edge of your seat and a story that draws the player in the game.

Please keep in mind, everything what you see here is very early beta and all the media and content that you see, are not finished in any way and will be replaced in the final product.
