Introduction:[/size]

Hello, I am Pac0master, the Project Lead of a fan base mod called Portal 2: Abyss,
You can take a look at the moddb page here: https://www.moddb.com/mods/portal-2-abyss
Or you can check out the steam Greenlight Page here: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=223636848

The mod is focused on giving multiple choices leading into many different endings.
There are 3 planned stories and many possible ways to end the game.

We have created our own timeline of events which you will quickly notice. It is important to understand that the mod is not cannon to the original story and not planned to be.
Let’s just say that Abyss take place in an alternate Universe which things happened slightly differently.

We are recruiting![/size]

We are indeed looking for talented individuals who have some free time and are also willing to help us with this project
It is important to take note that the project is currently being made on a 0$ budget which mean that we have no way to pay for any services.
We are looking for people who are willing to help us for free.

  • No time restriction
  • You keep all credits for your works
  • Your portfolio can be listed in any media featuring your works
  • You are free to leave any time and keep the right of your works

If you are interested to join us, Please follow this LINK and fill the Form
