Charli August 11, 2020, 3:41am #7

I just found the solution to my problem and thought I’d post it just in case someone having the same problem stumbles upon this thread.

The correct translation of the error message would actually be “popcapgame1.exe has stopped working”. After a quick search for this phrase on google I found a link to the Steam Forums. The solution was to deactivate the ingame overlay in the game’s settings
