I bought HL a little while ago when it - along with HL:OF and :BS - were only 99¢ on Steam. I didn’t think about playing it until yesterday while reading this thread. To set things straight, I’m one of those horrible gamers that in a game must at least have decent graphics and a cool storyline. Knowing that the original has pretty terrible graphics by my standards (yes yes I know they were next-gen back in '99) and the storyline is lacking (yes I know there are a few short instances of dialogue, but nothing like HL2), I decided to try it anyway, just so I could compare to Black Mesa.

After 4 or 5 hours of playing straight through yesterday, I’ve reached ‘Chapter 10: Questionable Ethics.’ I enjoyed it, but no where near as much I as I have with HL2 or it’s episodes. It still is much better then some horrible current-gen shooters I’ve played. On A Rail and I think the one after it - still with the stupid cart - was probably the most annoying part of the experience so far.

I’m going to finish the game sometime this week. If you are deciding upon playing HL1 first or not, why are you stuck on the decision? Do you want to play it for the game experience, the story, just to have played it, to compare it to BM, or another reason? Initially I didn’t want to ruin the story, but I knew basically what happened, and wanted to play the game. I did install the Diamond graphics pack mentioned earlier in this thread, which does add some nice models and such.Overall, I’m happy that I played it and that I’m going to complete it before BM is released.

Besides, I finally picked up on some jokes, along with some CSS soundbytes that I now know are from key points in HL. And I can argue on threads now about content from HL!
