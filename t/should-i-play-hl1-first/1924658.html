I’ll offer a different opinion:

HL1 was great. Amazing game, it changed the way first person shooters are made. Almost every FPS since then has just been emulating HL1, or at least paid homage to it in some way.

But in my opinion, don’t play it. Not at this point. I also don’t care about graphics. I still play commodore 64 games. The problem is the AI. If you are a gamer, and play lots of shooters, then i think you will be unimpressed. So there’s a devil’s-advocate viewpoint from someone who loves HL1.
