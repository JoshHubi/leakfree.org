Ibanez November 25, 2018, 3:47am #15

this game is so goddamn fun, being a pirate is what this series has always needed; I absolutely love the open world, this is the only sandbox I have truly enjoyed exploring, maybe because it’s so unique. The naval combat is incredible, too.

but omg ubisoft you need to improve the mechanics of the freerunning system if you’re gonna increase the detail of the world. I can’t run fluidly around on my own pirate ship rigging without falling forever to the deck of the ship because he got hung up on an awkward corner or didn’t climb where I wanted him to. It’s the exact same system as in Ass Creed 1, and they haven’t done anything to change it except that “don’t press A to sprint anymore” change in AC3. This doesn’t change the actual clunkiness of what happens when you run into a shape the engine doesn’t recognize, which happens far too often on your own ship. Since the ship is such a large part of the game, they really should have put time into making a freerunning system designed specifically for the awkward shapes of a ship’s rigging. It would have been worth it, and could easily have been recycled for every other ship. The combat is also incredibly awkward when boarding, since my own allies keep getting in the way and causing animation clipping.

Anyways, that’s my only complaint. In terms of gameplay, the game is wonderful. The pacing is perfect, much better than other AC games. The setting encourages exploration much more than others in the series, and being a murdering prick always manages to be realistic because at the end of the day, you’re a motherfucking pirate. You can do whatever you want. :pirate::pirate::pirate:
