Try right-clicking on Black Mesa in Steam, click on Properties, click on Set Launch Options and add -width x -height y in the dialogue box (where x is your desired horizontal resolution and y is your desired vertical resolution, e.g. -width 1024 -height [noparse]768[/noparse]).
