I understand that the story has been leading to this point and the location of the Temple in New York, but it’s just that we all know what New York looks like, the history of the American Revolution and are familiar with the setting; we’ve all seen it in dozens of films.

What I loved about the AC games is that they were unparalleled in their originality and ingenuity, in taking us to places and time periods that weren’t so obvious or well known, engaging with historic characters that no one had heard of and taking part in events that only a well-read historian would recognise.

I understand why they’re changing direction, I’m just saying that I’ll miss the history of it all. Wandering the streets of a city barely 200 years old just isn’t that appealing to me.
