OP, could you please provide the following details on your problem:

  1. Your hardware and software specs.

  2. More details about the problem. As far as I could see you’ve got a game engine crash. Had you had it only once when been playing for hours? Or, maybe, you can’t even start up the game each time getting this crash. We can’t help you unless you provide us with all the details possible.

  3. I suspect that Terryer in his message above recommends you to move your Steam installation from the “C:\Archivos de programa (x86)…” to another place having no “()” characters in the path. It is true - there’s a possibility to hit some bugs in Steam, in Source engine and in BM due to having non-ASCII alphanum characters in the path to the game installation folder so moving your Steam to something like “C:\Steam” is a good idea in general.
