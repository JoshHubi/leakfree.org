Zombie Panic: Source is also, but it’s team based. Then again, team based is co-op to some extent; it depends on what your definition of “Co-op”.

If you mean all players against computer strictly b[/b], than Synergy is a good place to start. But nevertheless, its community is small as hell.

If you’re open to players vs player co-op b[/b], than the two most notorious mods, to my mind, are Age of Chivalry and Zombie Panic: Source – they both hold excellent structure as a multiplayer game whilst maintaining good chemistry as a cooperative practice.
