Who here played the Natural Selection mod for HL? I remember when I played it, it was during a lull in server action, and then I got a new computer and never got a chance to play it again. What I do remember was that it was pretty damn fun, and that I kept getting eaten. My friend was grossed out by the stomach texture, heh.

Then, a few weeks ago, I found out about overgrowth, in an unimportant way, and followed it. Hey, I had no idea WHAT THE HELL it was, but there was a video with ninja bunny ragdolls being thrown around. Strange? Yes. Kinda cool, yeah. Creepy, a bit. It wasn’t till after that that I found out that they were also working on a commercial project, Natural Selection 2. It was like, out of the blue, “Hey, look! Natural Selection 2!” And I was like “WHAAAT?” And hey, they made it to the ModDB Top 100.

I remember Natural Selection being a great game, so is there any way that a full-blown indie GAME couldn’t be just as good? I doubt it.

I know that I’mma vote for it, and then prolly buy it, WHO’S WITH ME!
