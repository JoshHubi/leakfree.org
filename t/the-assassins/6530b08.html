Also I find that the obvious laser tripmines and turret activation beams in the original half-life were too obvious. At least in hard mode I would have loved to have them be invisible. I’d find it cool to have to make the beams visible first, before I can try to dodge them. Because laser beams usually are not visible. As said before, I KNOW that this is too much realism for a game and I KNOW it’s not gonna be implemented. I’d just find it cool.
