I’m liking the Black Box a lot so far, feels like a pretty good trade to me. The Holy Mackerel is pretty funny. The Eternal Reward I haven’t used but it is super annoying to get killed by. Item trading is easy enough to do, though a lot of people were caught up in it and forgot to play.
