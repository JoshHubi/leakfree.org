It depends. Are you on the central ring that moves up and down? If you are, the central ring rises up to the ceiling which has spokes which are lower than other places which can crush you. You need to crouch on the central ring between the lower parts of the ceiling when the ring is at its highest.

Or maybe you’re on the rotating “magnet” platforms. There are “walls” so that you can’t just ride the ‘magnet platforms’ around indefinitely waiting for a good jump angle. If you smash into these walls, you’ll be crushed (my belief is that your feet get caught by the walls (outer rings) and pipes (inner ring) and it pulls you down against the walls/pipes, essentially filleting you (perhaps the magnetic platforms attract the HEV suit boots at those points so that you can’t just get pushed off), but perhaps I’m BS-rationalizing it).
