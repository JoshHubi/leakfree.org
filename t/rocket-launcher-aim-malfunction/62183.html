Rocket launcher aim malfunction

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
StrangerElite February 21, 2019, 7:06pm #1

System configuration:
OS: Windows 10 Home Edition 64 bit.
Processor: i5-3570, 4 cores, 3,4-3,8 GHz.
Video card: Radeon R9 280 3Gb.
RAM: 16 Gb. 2 sticks of 8.
SSD for OS and HDD for game included.

Steps to reproduce:

  1. Find a Rocket launcher.
  2. Disable laser on your launcher.
  3. Launch a rocket.

Expected result: rocket flies directly to your target.

Actual result: rocket flies higher than your target.

Priority: major.
