BlackAlpha November 25, 2018, 2:20am #25

Know that this is the only part of the game that is so horribly designed. Once you get past the chopper, the game picks up again.

I’m guessing they never had time to balance that part properly. So tell the devs about it and hopefully they will change it.

As for how to get past that horrendous chopper… Luck.

Make a regular save before picking up the RPG. Hide behind the tall rocks on the opposite side of the infinite rocket box. Make a quick save after every successful hit on the chopper; successful as in, you hit the chopper and the chopper doesn’t hit you or does only very little damage. Remember you need to fire 16 rockets to take it down, so do not get hit. If you need more rockets, wait until the cannon stops firing and then make a dash for the infinite rocket crate and run like hell back to the rocks.

Load up the regular save and try again when you find out your quick saves won’t lead to a victory. It can happen because, like I said, there’s lots of luck involved. But the quick saves help a lot to balance the luck into your favor.

EDIT: Or do what the guy above me said. I never used the Gauss during the cliffs (no ammo), but I noticed on the dam that you can very quickly damage the chopper by firing with the regular attack.
