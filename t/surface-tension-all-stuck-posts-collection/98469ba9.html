dky.tehkingd.u November 25, 2018, 2:21am #55

First part, I take the tedious strategy of peeking out of one hole, crossbowing one grunt, then pressing spacebar to jump back down as fast as possible. Then I choose another random hole, crossbow another, repeat, repeat, repeat. It takes a while but that’s the safest way to do it, I’ve found.

Second part, You really need to take down the Osprey ASAP. Launch rockets at it until it dies. Otherwise, you’re screwed. Don’t even try to cross the pad if you haven’t blown it up yet. On the bright side, it doesn’t reinforce grunts until it completes a circle.
