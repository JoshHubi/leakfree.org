Maxey:

It was already optimized for PC. Rage doesn’t look too advanced because it was made that way, although the engine is capable of much more.

The texture cache settings (on release, not sure if it was patched) were optimised for something with a tiny amount of memory - hence the dreadful, constant texture streaming.
A simple config tweak could eliminate almost all of the texture streaming (they’d load once at the start of a level / save load) with no performance or stability issues.

As for how it looked… it would have been a bit better with less texture compression (particularly in the dark areas which make up a large portion of the game) and some dynamic lights and shadows.
