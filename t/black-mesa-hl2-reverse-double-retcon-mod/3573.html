I think this is a really bad idea.

Half-Life 2 is designed around the concept that the combine are draining earth’s resources. Would you have the HECU drain earth’s resources instead? This makes no sense. Xen aliens then maybe? Then why would everything they use have a combine design even though most of their world and buildings are purely organic? Also makes no sense.

Plus, maps in a videogame are made in such a way that combat with the enemies is fun. Let’s take the antlions for example. They cannot reach you by walking in many sections of the game, but luckily they can fly short distances. Bullsquids can’t do this, so in many sections of the game they won’t be able to reach you. This would be a problem.

You have devices which can keep antlions at a safe distance in Half-Life 2, so you can have a little ‘pause’ inbetween battles. But, bullsquids can ‘fire’ at you from a distance, so keeping them at a distance would be quite pointless. Again, a problem.

If you want to do this and actually create an enjoyable game, you have re-think gameplay mechanics of the entire game and re-make entire sections. But this doesn’t really count as ‘retconning Half-Life 2’ I think.
