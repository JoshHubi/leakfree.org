Marb August 11, 2020, 3:23am #12

Coop:
Sven Co-op (Goldsource) - awesome, but not for those who are hardcore HL fans. If you don’t mind new and/or improved weapons, enemies and abilities, then SC is a great coop game.

SP:
(all Source)
Human Error: Not quite a good representation of what it’s like to be a Combine CP imho, but still fun. Three words: remote control manhacks.
Get a Life: pretty cool so far. Has some interesting game play (weird black & white dream sequences), but one thing I don’t like is the manual healing gets kind of annoying pretty quickly (haven’t finished it yet).
Strider Mountain: I keep hearing this one is very good, so I’m about to try it out.
EDIT: First few levels seem pretty good so far - but a few minor things irked me right off the bat: no crowbar; instead, you get an ice ax-looking thingy with “STRIDER MOUNTAIN” written on it. And I can’t quite figure out how or why the pistol holds 33 rounds :what:
Oh, and it has scripted flashback cut-scenes showing you (as Gordon), receiving instructions on what your mission is. Cool, but in one of them, Gordon’s leg kept twitching weirdly as if he was itching to get the hell out of the briefing room :lol:

rorriMAesU November 24, 2018, 8:35pm #20

I can understand disagreeing with how the changes were implemented, but how does drastically improving the textures, modifying the AI, adding optional ironsights, and actually designing the weapons to look and behave realistically remove the soul from HL2…the story and level design weren’t altered.

I am sorry but the weapons and combat in HL2 was unimpressive IMO. HL2 added only two new weapons (gravity gun and the unimpressive pulse rifle) and removed others (loved the tao cannon and gluon gun from HL…but I understand them being excluded from HL2). They could have at least re-skinned and altered the recycled weapons to make them a little fresh, but they didn’t. I couldn’t believe they implemented the EXACT same crossbow from the original HL.

The accuracy and programming for the pistol, SMG, and pulse rifle is horrible…all you can do is spray your target until they die (good luck attempting to pick off headshots, like it even fucking matters). I am sorry, but bullets are a little more accurate than HL2 will have you believe. This, combined with the AI tendencies of the combine and ant lions, the combat got a little stale. Not to mention HL2 had practically no gore…at least it feels like my guns are doing damage with the cinematic mod. They certainly improved the gore without overdoing it.

Basically, weapons and combat in HL2 were lacking and I feel the cinematic mod greatly improves these elements. The MP5 in the cinematic mod is so damn satisfying lol.
