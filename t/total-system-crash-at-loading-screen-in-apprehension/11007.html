I’m afraid to post it but getting reboots like you get is not a BM problem, it is a hardware or a driver problem with your PC instead. Game has no means to render your computer into total hang/crash or a reboot like you’re getting. You should try updating drivers for your GPU and sound card first and then try monitoring the temperature of the CPU and GPU while playing. It could be a result of the overheat. It could be a problem with a power supply failing to provide enough energy to the GPU. It could simply be a faulty motherboard - ASRock is de facto a “low cost version of the ASUStek brand” which they had separated to avoid reputation damage to the ASUS main brand.

TL;DR: A general rule of thumb: if some app causes total system crash - most of the times it is a hardware problem or a faulty driver. There are no possible ways for a userland app to crash the entire system, at the worst case on properly functioning PC you should get an app crash and nothing more.

I apologize for resurrecting such an old thread, but someone has figured out a solution to my problem. As lexa2 suggested the issue was my motherboard. fortunately there is a tweak in Bios which eliminates the crash

killbot3000:

hello, don’t know if you solved your problem. i also have a asrock mb and was crashing at the same exact spot with the horrible screeching noise. i solve my problem by setting the bios setting in “chipset” section and changed the “flexibility option” to “enabled” for the memory timing. don’t know if it’ll work for you but it solved my problem. good luck!

I don’t know if this will help anybody else but I thought the solution should be shared.
