RedEye November 25, 2018, 4:45am #10

Your AI disabled glitch was caused by broken saves that can happen after game patches and updates.

Before you start typing random things into the console it’s a good idea to understand what they are. cl_showfps 1 just displays the frames per second, it would not have fixed your problem but should not have made anything worse.

Here is a good starting point of things to try.

1. Restart your computer. Both Windows 8 and 10 have a hybrid hibernation file that will corrupt over time and the only way to clear it is by restarting. Shutting down the pc and turning it back on does not clear it.

2. A lot of mods are broken with the latest update. Properly unsubscribe, uninstall and remove any mods you have installed.
Additionally, if you are experiencing issues with missing models or textures in-game, you have a legacy error with mods. If you have subscribed to any mods since launch and are experiencing these issues please do the following steps to fix the issue.

  • 1. Unsubscribe from all Black Mesa mods
  • 2. Go to ***\SteamApps\workshop\content\362890 (where *** is the location to where your steam games may be)
  • 3. Delete all folders within this folder
  • 4. Launch Black Mesa, and you should be good to go.

    Once you do this, you should be able to re-subscribe to any and all mods that you want.

3. Verify your Game Cache. here is a link explaining how.https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=2037-QEUH-3335

4. A saved game could be corrupt. Go to New Game and restart an unlocked chapter. Updates may corrupt and break save files. If you’re experience any issues with old loads, please restart the game at the start of that chapter. You can do that by clicking “New Game” on the main menu, and picking the latest chapter you were on.
