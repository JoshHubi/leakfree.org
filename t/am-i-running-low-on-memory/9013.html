MRPolo November 25, 2018, 1:52am #17

Don’t listen to them all. At least most. For a start, you’re not running low on RAM (Random Available Memory) which is what is considered as “memory” for a computer.
The issue discussed here is not memory but Hard Drive space. You have 81 gigabytes and that is seriously more than enough for now. However, if you want to, look for games that you no longer play. Whenever I do such sweep I get around 20 gigabytes more, and that’s me being conservative.
81 gig is enough to, as an example, install 4 Team Fortress 2s. As I said, it’ll keep you going for weeks if you don’t install every day. You don’t even need an upgrade of any sort since your overall Hard Drive space is 481. As I said, you may want to do a quick sweep through your PC if you’re not happy with 81 gig. However, that is not necessary at all.
