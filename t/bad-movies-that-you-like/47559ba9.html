I think there is sort of an “awkward valley” for entertainment. Enjoyability goes down as quality goes down and then bottoms out at the awkward valley, but spikes up again as the quality continues to fall. Once you get past the valley, the quality becomes so terrible you can enjoy things again because they are just incredibly awful. However, things like Uwe Boll movies fall into the valley because they are not quite bad enough to be enjoyed. You can tell that they were trying hard but just failed.
