your_evil_twin November 25, 2018, 2:37am #11

I found that the problem is caused by having a box or barrel or something fall INTO the fan. It may even happen if you smash boxes and some pieces fall into the fan. To get past the fan bit I had to load a save (I think there was an autosave when entering the fan area) and then make sure to get down the controls without hitting anything or smashing anything. Then it worked OK.

It doesn’t matter if there are any boxes/barrels/pallets around once the fan is on - they will float up. The problem is caused by objects falling underneath the fan, I think the game doesn’t know what to do with things that are underneath the fan that is supposed to send them upwards.

If you are able to activate the fan and fly upwards you shouldn’t have any problem with “save file corruption”. If no objects fall into the fan, you won’t get crashes.
