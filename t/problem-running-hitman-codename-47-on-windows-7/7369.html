Maxey November 25, 2018, 12:27am #4

Open Task Manager by right-clicking on the taskbar, open the Processes tab, find the game’s exe, right click on it, open “Set affinity” and then uncheck all cpus except cpu 0.

That should do it.

Finally, problem solved.

Thanks for the help, Maxey. My laptop uses a default Intel chip instead of a nVidia or a ATI one for graphic issues, but it was solved with a console command, which I am going to share here:

IP_TIMEMULTIPLIER .(enter desired number here)

*to enable console, add “enableconsole 1” on the hitman.ini file, located on the Hitman directory.
