TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:57am #86

  1. We do indeed have the Boids, but they’re mostly used as scenery NPCs. We view them as being similar to the Mantas - they’re mostly scenery rather than actual NPCs.

  2. I’d rather not spoil it.

  3. No new creatures, no.

  4. New MP maps based on Xen? Most likely, yes.

  5. The maps have been designed with optimisation in mind. They’re going to be taxing - the Source engine simply isn’t designed for what we want to do, but they should run about the same as the STU maps do - we hope.

  6. Probably no more content updates before Xen, no. I won’t rule it out entirely, but probably not.
