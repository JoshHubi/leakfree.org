Admiral_Sakai November 25, 2018, 4:57am #40

It’s difficult to say what these might be without knowing more about how far along the map is in development and/or what is currently being hidden from view.

At the moment, we can see no skybox or other obvious enclosing elements, so either the map is a series of tunnels defined by relatively thin walls, or the actual main enclosing elements are hidden and it is a series of floating islands or ledges. If the former is true, the ‘strands’ are probably portions of the wall geometry; if the latter then they are probably rock faces or cliffs.
