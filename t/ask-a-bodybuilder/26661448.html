1 meal a day is sufficient to sustain life. Really laid back life.

If you actually plan to do strenuous activity you will most likely pass out after a week of malnutrition.

Really, I don’t know where this fad came to eat as little meals as possible, but it doesn’t work. If you want to get big, you need to eat big over the course of a day. Spycrabz November 24, 2018, 8:50pm #199

What home workouts would you recommend for a fatty such as myself?

Winged_One November 24, 2018, 8:50pm #200

Fatty? How much do you weigh, if you don’t mind me asking.

You won’t get much done at home unless you have free weights or dumbbells on call. What you really need is a gym. There’s no workaround.

Unless you are going for weight loss. In that case, you wanna diet and walk. Don’t run, just walk. You get just as much calories burned by walking the same distance you might run, and there’s less of a strain on your legs. Besides, you can sustain it longer.

Walk a mile or two a day, combined with diet, for weight loss. But you need the GYM for muscle gain (and, be advised, the more muscle you gain, the more calories you burn in a day…so in the long run, gaining muscle might be your best option).
