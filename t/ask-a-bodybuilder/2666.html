Imonfire:

What do you think about creatine? And supplements of protein?

Creatine works for some people, doesn’t for others. A lot of people take it and expect miracles like it’s some sort of androgenic steroid. If you do use it, you have to drink a lot of water for it to even be slightly effective, and some people cycle it to make it more effective. Bottom line is it can help slightly, but it isn’t necessary and it will not make or break you.

Protein is something you need in general, supplements such as whey and casein protein help you get in your necessary protein each day, as well as providing other benefits such as being loaded with BCAAs. I could spend years talking about every different kind of protein supplement, like how whey is fast digesting and best for postworkout, while casein is slow digesting and best before bed, and all of that. Really, supplements are not necessary for anything, but if you’re going to take a supplement, make it a multivitamin, fish oil, and whey protein. Everything else is either a hit or miss on effectiveness from person to person.
