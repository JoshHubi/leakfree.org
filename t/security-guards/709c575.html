In “We’ve got Hostiles!” there is an opening scene where a security officer gets attacked by a zombie, striking out of an air vent. The officer fights back with his bare fists, later uses his handgun, before he is ultimately ripped to shreds. So the idea of security guards in scripted melee combat scenes is already implemented in Half Life 1. It would just be taking it to another level if there were additional scenes showing guards fending off alien attacks with their fists and boots.

In Blue Shift there was the “Otis” security guard, wearing no helmet, being considerably overweight and utilizing a desert eagle instead of a 9mm handgun.
