TBH, in case the game is crashing a lot on your rig - most probably you’ve got something wrong with your PC. Yeah, it’s possible that the bug is in BMS, but it’s not that probable: thousands of people all around the world are able to play this game without frequent crashes (for example I’ve got crashes only in case I mess up a log with advanced cvar settings in console - which is OK, as HL2 and Co would also crash if you frequently do something like several changes to mat_dxlevel or mat_parralax/mat_bumpmap). Thus chances are that something with either your software or hardware making the game to crash. It could be faulty memory, faulty GPU, faulty motherboard with VRM not able to feed enough power to CPU/DIMM, faulty power supply which is not able to provide enough power for system under huge load and so on. It could be that your system is towards lower end of minimal system requirements for BMS and, for example, it simply hits the exhaustion of the heap pool (out of free memory condition) and starts swapping. It could be as simple as outdated/buggy drivers for your GPU or soundcard. I.e. it’s good that you posted the minidump here and it’d be cool if you would keep on posting them here after each crash you have in the game, but be warned that first of all you should check your PC to be OK as most of the BMS players don’t get a lot of frequent unexpected crashes on their rigs.
