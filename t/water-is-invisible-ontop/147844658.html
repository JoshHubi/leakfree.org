LordDz August 11, 2020, 11:34am #23

It’s 1/16 actually, so scale everything by 0.0625.
But don’t bother with the 3d skybox until you’ve got the basic mappings in good shape.

What I meant with xy is this:

  1. Open your map, press Shift + S (Selection Tool), Drag a selection circle over your whole map in the top view:

    1800×1022

  2. Press Enter, press Shift + A (Texture tool)

  3. Reset the texture settings, press apply. You now no longer have any stretched textures that look awful.

The map in the pictures takes about 11 seconds for the vvis and 50 seconds for the lighting. So that’s why optimization of your map is so important.
