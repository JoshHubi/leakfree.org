JohnWorfin:

Big portals

He never says anything about portals in regards to the Borealis.

λdam:

But another Resonance Cascade with super portals seems likely, maybe even with different species of enemies.

That seems unlikely because it just sounds lazy and redundant. I sincerely doubt that they’d use such a cheap cop-out. Time travel might also seem unoriginal, but then again very little in Half-Life is really that original, it’s more about combining these ideas seamlessly in a well executed manner.

Time alteration is important in Half-Life’s plot & themes.

  • Gordon disappearing for 10-20 years.
  • The one-week slow teleport.
  • The Gman’s sudden mysterious appearance & disappearances.
  • Gman freezing time at the end of HL2 & the vortigaunts interference.
  • HL is full of conspiracy theory references & The Borealis’ story mimics the Philadelphia Experiment.
  • Ignoring safety/standards of risk & tampering with/abusing powerful forces.

The general story & personal motivations.

  • Alyx could see it as a means of being with Eli again.
  • The resonance cascade it’s self could be undone.
  • The Combine could undo the uprising, and guarantee victory in any war.
  • Could guarantee Humanity victory in any war. Even overthrowing the Combine.
  • Is flexible & would allow for dozens of possible ways to continue the story, not writing themselves into a corner.

If anyone’s got a better idea with better explanations I’d love to hear it.
