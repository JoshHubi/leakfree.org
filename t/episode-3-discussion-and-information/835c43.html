My suggestion

Note: This was in no way taken from Valve, although they might read this thread and get some inspiration.

Gordon wakes up in the helicopter with Alyx and DOG, who stored his weapons for later in the back. She is very sad, and they fly over other cities in northern Europe. The helicopter suddenly crashes and they are met by Combine soldiers in a city who are immediately kept at bay by Resistance forces of that city, including Barney.

They journey out of the city and communicate with Mossman, then take a teleporter to Kraken Base, but Gordon is taken aside by the G-Man, who tells him that he had recruited another, who would come to his aid. Gordon is returned, and they are met by Mossman, along with Gina Cross, Colette Green, and a deaf worker.

They are in a protected area from where she was when she made her transmission, but the Combine slowly break through, and they battle and escape into a sub to the Borealis.

They arrive at the Borealis and are attacked by an advisor, which is attacked and killed from behind by Adrian Shephard. He leads them into an area where a few vortigaunts are helping kill advisors.

They journey into the ship’s portal’s core, with some advisors along the way. At one point, the gravity gun is infused with dark energy, allowing for easy advisor killing.

Before they enter, they see a few old videos where Cave Johnson’s wife’s (based on Ellen McLain) brain is being put into GLaDOS, and the coordinator of the Borealis watches the GLaDOS activation; the GLaDOS transmission goes out first, then the first portal storms appear and they are forced to test the portal to escape the phenomena.

They enter the core, and are met by the lead advisor, who traps everyone except Gordon and throws his weapons to the side. Instead, Gordon finds the gluon gun and battles with it. The advisor eventually dies and, as a result, crashes into the core and destroys it. The G-Man saves everyone and reveals some truths to the backstory of the Combine invasion and the Black Mesa Incident to Gordon and Shephard, and leaves them in stasis once again.

end credits
