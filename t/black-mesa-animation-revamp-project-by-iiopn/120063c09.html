sourcegamer101 November 25, 2018, 3:55am #427
Ronster:

How do you change the field of view on the v_hands?

I want to change the fov on the magnum and the glock

or perhaps it is the camera settings for the animation?

Navigate into the Black Mesa folder, then open up the ‘scripts’ folder. Scroll down until you see the ‘weapon_[x].txt’ text files. Open the text file of the weapon you want to configure and locate a line that says ‘fov’. Change the FOV from 54 to any FOV you want. If you are in-game and change this while it’s minimized, then you’ll have to relaunch the game.
