You can change the gun fov, just go to:

Steam\SteamApps\sourcemods\BMS\scripts

Open for example weapon_glock.txt, find this:

fov “54”

Then change the fov to 70 or something, you can do this for every weapon.

Though not every weapon looks good at 70 so you’ll have to experiment a bit to find the right fov for every weapon
