kingcars November 24, 2018, 10:12pm #71

I love the Mafia II demo. As a huge fan of the first, I was really worried that all the detail and realism would be dumbed down, as is the case with many of the older hardcore PC games whose sequels go to consoles. But just about everything is still there, and then some.

If you rob a store, there will be crime scene tape there later, tires deform realistically due to weight transfer of the car, you can fill up for gas, wash your car, tune your car, the damage looks great, shooting is fun, etc. I didn’t even notice the few removed features such as melee weapons and in-car shooting. Of course I’m using simulation driving physics, which feel great, although the input seems to be a little on the sluggish side.

I also got a good laugh from the “demo timer hacks are stealing our game” comment. The irony is that, after I got the demo timer hack and was able to take my time exploring the small details, THAT’s when I was sold on the game. So actually, turning the timer off will probably help the demo sell more copies of the full game.
