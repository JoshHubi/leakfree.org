Lamsey November 24, 2018, 5:44pm #27

Free panorama stitching software: Hugin. Make sure you get autopano-sift and enblend too. Also, take care when you shoot the pics; you need to rotate the camera around the focal point (basically, around the lens), and make sure that your aperture/shutter settings stay the same otherwise you’ll get tidemarks in the finished pano.
