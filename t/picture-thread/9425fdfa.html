pfannkuchen_gesicht November 25, 2018, 3:53am #158

ah well, that one really isn’t that good.

The Nikon D80 you mentioned isn’t that expensive. You can get them for 100-300€ used. Adorama offers a refurbished one for over 400$.
Anyway, you can get good used DSLRs rather cheaply, so maybe look into the used and refurbished DSLR market.
The camera I have cost me about 760€ but I think it’s down to 500€ by now because the K-5 II and K-3 came out meanwhile. Excellent cameras btw and the mount hasn’t fundamentally changed in the last 30-40 years, so you can still get some pretty good primes of the film era for cheap and they work fine, they don’t have autofocus then though, but who cares.
