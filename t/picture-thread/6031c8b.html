Well that all depends on the type of camera you have, because conventional/digital mirror cameras are way different than digital pocket cameras. Both require their own unique set of skills to take pictures with, but here are my tips for both types. As you will see, the skills will probably take quite a few months, if not years, to master, but once you get the hang of it, you’ll be taking pictures like a madman.

For conventional/digital mirror camera’s.

  1. Grab camera
  2. Look through lens
  3. Push button

For digital pocket camera’s.

  1. Grab camera
  2. Look at display
  3. Push button
