Why would it be humping the door? Crocodiles and alligators don’t have sexual intercourse to reproduce, so they have no business humping anything.

And before you ask, they “grow”, much like carrots and potatoes. Basic principle is this: birds eat seeds of a certain type of plant (not sure which, but it’s something that grows in or near swamps). The thing with those seeds is that they have a parasitic relationship with the host (bird that eats it), the seeds slowly kill the bird from the inside. When the bird dies and falls on the ground, the seed tries to find a way to the ground, and digs itself in, so it can start growing.

After a week or 4-5, the seeds are fully grown and burst to reveal a “baby” croc/alligator (they’re already quite big at this point).

So the alligator (that’s what it is in that pic, not a croc) is just trying to get inside the house. Nothing dangerous, really, since they don’t know how doorknobs work, obviously.
