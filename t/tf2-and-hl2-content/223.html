Metritutus August 11, 2020, 12:36am #6

Extract the necessary files using GCF Scape, located here:

https://nemesis.thewavelength.net/index.php?p=25

Then use Pakrat to embed the files into your compiled map BSP file:

https://www.geocities.com/cofrdrbob/pakrat.html

It’s all reasonably self-explanatory. It’s quite easy to test if your stuff is working too, either move your Half-Life 2 gcf files to another location but do not re-download them, or have some kind friend (who you trust) who does not own Half-Life 2 to test your map.

There are other utilities that can do this, but Pakrat is the one I found easiest to use. There is no other way.
