Okay, so the valve is on the ground, next to the feeder pipe. You have to pick it up, and it will automatically connect, when you bring it near the steaming Feeder 2 pipe.
If yours is actually missing, you may have to turn on cheats (temporarily) in the Dev Console with “sv_cheats 1”.
Then type the following: “ent_teleport coolant_pipe_1_wheel_physmdl”
This should cause the valve wheel to teleport to wherever your cursor is pointing. Then turn off cheats with “sv_cheats 0”.
