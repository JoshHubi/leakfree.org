In addition to the standard marine, two other variants are seen in Black Mesa:

Commander Marine, or Squad Leader: A specific separate entity with better stats and a different voice to the standard marine [8]. They will have slightly more health, deal slightly more damage, and direct any other nearby grunts. They have a distinctive red beret [9]. Any change to behaviour of HECU squads when the commander dies has not been confirmed.

Medic: Contrary to Half-life, they do make an appearance in Black Mesa and can be seen healing their teammates or Squad Leader as well as engaging in combat.
