TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:31am #10

Your computer is below the minimum specs required for this game. Integrated graphics chips are not properly supported for Black Mesa, because they do not generally support HDR. What is likely happening is the Steam installer is trying to auto install DirectX 9 and failing because your integrated graphics chip does not support it. See if you can get DX9 to install, if not through the automated Steam, from Microsoft’s drivers. This should resolve your issues, though again, playing below the minimum specs is asking for trouble.
