We’re almost halfway through the year 2010. Now this has me wondering (and I’m sure the rest of you as well) how close Black Mesa is to being released to the public.

So I thought, seeing as we only have half of a year remaining, maybe we could play a little game?

I now present to the BMS community The Unofficial Bitch Don’t Know Bout My Release Date of Black Mesa: Teh Game.
