ShadowNate November 25, 2018, 2:17am #70

For german language, I think you should set the Steam client to be in german.
This is from View->Settings->Interface->Select the language…->German->Ok . It will need restart.

To enable subtitles, you run Black Mesa, and from the main menu, choose Options->Audio->Captioning->Closed Captions or Subtitles (dialogue only)
