amazingcomedy November 25, 2018, 1:50am #4

I can’t think of a weapon modelling tutorial, but if you already have experience with complex modelling, then you won’t need one. Just make a basic model of any weapon you want, make sure there are movable and removable parts of the weapon (ex: trigger, magazine), get textures, smooth out the model so it’s not blocky, make sure the textures fit in good, make animations, etc. Since you want to replace a certain weapon, what you’ll need to do rename the model to EXACTLY whatever model you’re replacing, as well as the animations.
