I got Fallout 3 exactly 7 days ago, and have already beaten it 3 times, I bought the GOTY edition over Steam to replace the torrented version I had and have had a very enjoyable time playing it. You did ask about mods and thus I have to warn you, be careful if you use a custom race. If you do, and apply the race before you begin Broken Steel you will run into a glitch that prevents Broken Steel from loading. There is another problem that you might run into involving Scribe Rothchild and his bloody map, but I have yet to discover if that is caused by mods, or the game itself, (I have also yet to discover a fix for it). It is an amazing game, with quite a few good mods out there. My personal favorite is this one> https://www.fallout3nexus.com/downloads/file.php?id=5666. Excellent retexture of the Chinese Stealth Armor.
Other then those two glitches, it has been an amazing game for me. Have fun.

Edit: I did not see you post information about what distribution method you purchased the game over, as such, if you purchased a physical copy, you should get the FOSE, or Fallout Script Extender. It opens up a plethora of fun mods for you.
