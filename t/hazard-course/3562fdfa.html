There is a list building of volunteers, however to keep this as close to the community as possible at the moment we are in a ‘concept’ stage- anybody can contribute and I / we want as many people as possible to contribute to this stage - and this can be done right here in this thread, no need to ask - just do it!

When the community is happy we will move towards full development where the people who have offered there services in this area can be given specific tasks to do and we we gradually refine and compile their work in to a single product.

Having said all that there are a lot of people who have gotten excited and want to jump the gun, which is fine - but it’ll never come together that way. Let’s slow this down and do it properly as a community. We need enough information for mappers and artists to go forwards and be able to create consistant maps with a clear, and community devised plan, style, themes, layout and progression.

Things that will help in this regard are:

  • Plan views of the Hazard course layouts that are labelled with an idea behind why and how player progression will work
  • Sketches and pictures showing ideas for specific areas and styles/themes
  • Concept maps are fine to show styles, building architecture, obstacle ideas
  • Discussion on wheather all the obstacles are needed? in the same order? more tuition required on features Source may introduce
  • Screenshots of the current hazard course that have been annotated and descriptions to show your ideas for change or to point out features which are important and should remain
  • And more, any way you can think of portraying your ideas are welcome

If we where to go through this properly and at a decent pace, sharing ideas and collaborating then we stand half a chance of making a fucking good map, otherwise we will have lots of maps, assets and models which don’t fit together.
