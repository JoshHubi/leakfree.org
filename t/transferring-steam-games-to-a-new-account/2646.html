CatzEyes93 August 11, 2020, 2:38am #10

You mentioned that the problems you are having happen on 4 different computers. Are all of these computers your computers or are they neighbors, your friends across the street/across the town/across the city? It is rather rare to get the same issues on other people’s computers so I am curious if you tried your games on someone else’s computer.

You are describing several issues so you may need to consider that there are a couple things going on.

The freezing may be:
\ your computer specs (not enough memory/poor graphics card)
\ poor or sloppy installation or corrupt installation
\ your ISP may be capping you? or if you are on cable, there are more folks sucking bandwith?
\ other?

The one hit wonder behavior may be:
\ poor or sloppy installation or corrupt installation
\ config files somewhere are messed up
\ Other?

I am most likely wrong, but in a case like this, if it were me I would suggest completely uninstalling steam and the games. Not just deleting everything in the steam directory except for the steamapps folder, (like some folks suggest) but uninstalling completely. (It means you would loose all your games and will have to re download them, but if they aren’t working in the first place, why would you wanna save them?) Then checking your registry to ensure steam is deleted out of there as well.

Once done, use CCleaner to clean your registry real quick. (I LOVE this program!)

Then install from scratch and see if that changed anything.

OH… and what are your computer specs? Have you run a DXDiag to check that all systems are a go?

EDIT: and before I forget, do you have a “Trainer” installed? A trainer would allow you to have a “One hit” capability.
