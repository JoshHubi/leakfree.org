Glitches I've found

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
coldroll August 11, 2020, 11:20am #1

I decided to create a topic for any glitches or bugs I’ve found.

  1. The Black ops assassin ragdolls sometimes look like they’re cut in half. https://s9.postimg.org/ukz9soe0f/blackops_glitch.jpg

  2. When pressing the alarm on the security desk after guard get’s mad for the last time. The scientist says “Maybe he can fix the computer?” But his mouth doesn’t move. https://s12.postimg.org/fhgbaanml/pissed_off_guard.jpg

  3. The security guards sometimes have jerky animations such as this one. https://s18.postimg.org/m0fykqc5l/jerky_boy.jpg

  4. When a security guard zombie is cut in half it appears as a scientist zombie with a missing gray texture for it’s face. https://s4.postimg.org/sfypz97d9/wtf_face.jpg

  5. The textures on this pipe distort when walking near or by them. https://s8.postimg.org/y8m0cnar9/pipe_glitch.jpg

I’ll post more glitches if I find any.

  1. The head crabs that jump down at you on the elevator in unforeseen consequences take to long to jump at the player and slide down on the elevator before jumping at you. They also start dying about half way down the elevator sequence.
    https://s4.postimg.org/63tm1o019/headcrab_jump.jpg

  2. The security guard corpse in the vent at office complex is a ragdoll in the free version but is static in the early access version. https://s30.postimg.org/bt795dhpt/squished.jpg

  3. The security guard in office complex sometimes get’s stuck on this box. https://s16.postimg.org/q566yulph/stuck_guard.jpg

  4. The player can survive falling down the elevator shaft in office complex and is stuck there until they reload. https://s29.postimg.org/anrx9xto7/elevator_hulk.jpg

  5. This box at we got hostiles is static and can be walked thorough and won’t break. https://s4.postimg.org/a300bz3fx/leet_box.jpg

  6. The scientist that dies before the player encounters the tentacles has jerky animations. https://s18.postimg.org/hihm14t89/jerky_geek.jpg

  7. The bullet proof screen at the power silo on blast pit is not damage proof and will hurt the player if they toss a grenade through the door. https://s3.postimg.org/mlublxng3/window_glitch.jpg

  8. The bullet proof glass at the beginning of on a rail is not damage proof and the blood disappears when you change what direction you’re looking at. https://s15.postimg.org/6nk9r4p6z/on_rail_window_bug.jpg

  9. The dead security guard on the chapter of on a rail is a ragdoll in the free version but appears to be static in the early access version. https://s10.postimg.org/tc8b8aqpl/another_ragdoll_glitch.jpg

  10. This security guard on the chapter We got hostiles is a ragdoll in the free version but is static on the early access version.

That’s all I have for now if I find any others I’ll post them on here.
