An interesting glitch occured. I had just gotten on the train and went through the ramp where a guard told me to find the rocket. I proceeded to the elevator and while moving, I did a quicksave. I exited, came back later and loaded the saved game. Everything was smooth until I accidentally tried to increase the train’s throttle which is when the train appeared on the top of the elevator shaft (the elevator was still further down, descending) and I fell through, recieving a minor fracture.
