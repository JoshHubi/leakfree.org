RossBondReturns November 25, 2018, 2:33am #37

I used a mixture of old familiar tactics and new ones.

I first of all used grenades to clear the wood out of the doors on both exits from as close to the start position as possible.

Then I found that I needed to get the grenade one level above or below the level exit I wanted to use.

I also found that I could hang out on the ladder safely if I made sure I was just under being level with the floor. In other words getting on the ladder and going down until just under the level of the floor.

You can’t toss the grenades as high so when you do toss them make sure you aim higher than you think you need to. Place it as far away from your destination as possible one level up or down. Be ready to move.

The nearest tentacle to the explosion will 9/10 times start banging directly on the spot of the explosion. The next nearest will move over and investigate often. The third will show initial interest- make your move as soon as possible.

Face the tentacles and strafe towards the nearest ladder always ready to toss your next grenade away from your direction of travel.

Hide on the next ladder as before if you have to.

On a door level…toss the grenade away from your eventual direction of travel…start to slowly crouch walk when it’s about to detonate. When you get close to the door do not be afraid to sprint.

Save smart.
