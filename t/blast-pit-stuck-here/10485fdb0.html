subatomic November 25, 2018, 3:45am #67

I beat the tentacles this way:

  1. Throw a grenade down to the bottom floor, wait for them to respond to it.
  2. Run the edge of the 3rd floor ledge and jump down and to the left onto the 2nd floor ledge (skipping the ladder entirely).
  3. Immediately turn around and throw a grenade up to the third floor.
  4. Run left along the 2nd floor, wait for the 3rd floor grenade to go off and the tentacles to go up.
  5. Run to the 2nd floor doorway with the crowbar equipped and beat the crap out of the boards and jump through.

On the way back from turning on the oxygen and fuel, it’s possible to jump through the hole in the 2nd floor and avoid going back into the tentacle chamber to get to the 1st floor.

To get back up the 3rd floor after the oxy/fuel/power is restored takes some serious timing and grenade work. It seems like if the tentacles see you through the first floor doorway, one of them refuses to turn to follow the others, so the key is to not let them see you before you throw the first grenade. When they all turn, you can run in and to the left and up the ladder. Then, throw another grenade down toward the 1st floor doorway, and run to the left around and up the ladder. One more grenade thrown down will keep them occupied and you can run to the 3rd ladder.

danielsangeo November 25, 2018, 4:26am #75

Here’s what to do in Blast Pit, step-by-step (in the spoilers):

[spoiler]1. Upon arriving at the silo area, defeat all the aliens and enter the silo.
2. After the scene where the tentacle drags the scientist away, continue on and into the room with the tentacles.
3. The tentacle room has four levels. The entrance I’ll call Level 1. Level 2 is the first full platform and it has a closed door. Level 3 has the first open door. Level 4 is the floor of the silo room and has an open door.
4. Go to Level 3 and go through the door to exit the tentacle room.
5. There should be a door labeled “PROPLAB 02 - OXY/FUEL”. Open that door.
6. Follow the corridors, defeating any enemies that might get in your way.
7. There will be two elevator shafts without elevators, but water at the bottom.
8. Jump down the elevator shafts into the water.
9. Follow the pipe through the sewer to a ladder. Climb the ladder.
10. You will come to a door marked “PROPLAB 04 - EXHAUST”. Go through this door.
11. You will enter a silo with a giant fan at the bottom. Climb down the ladder to the fan.
12. Go around the fan and there will be another ladder which will take you underneath the fan.
13. Go underneath the fan and to the other side of the fan base. There will be a control panel there.
14. SAVE YOUR GAME. This part might kill you.
15. Flip the switch and quickly run around and climb the ladder before the fan blades can get turning too fast.
16. When the fan blades are turning at top speed, it will allow you to fly up and reach vent shafts in the walls. Take any of the vent shafts; they will all lead to the same place.
17. You will eventually end up in a control room with two red valves. One is broken, so you’ll need to find the valve wheel.
18. Enter the control room and open the door at the far end. The valve wheel will be inside. Attach the valve wheel to the valve outside the control room.
19. Turn both valve wheels to activate the oxygen and fuel lines. PART ONE COMPLETE!

  1. This will open another door in the control room. Enter it and you’ll return to the silo area.

  2. Return to the tentacle room and go down to Level 4.

  3. You will probably have to destroy the boards in front of the door, but exit the tentacle room on Level 4.

  4. You will come to a door labeled “PROPLAB 03 - POWER”. Take this door.

  5. Follow the corridors and you’ll come to a silo with a pool of green glowing toxic waste at the bottom.

  6. Push the button on the wall to bring up a suspended elevator.

  7. Enter the elevator and take it down.

  8. The elevator will stop and dangerous groaning metal sounds will be heard. Get out of the elevator! Jump to the ladder on the wall and take it down. The elevator will crash into the toxic waste below.

  9. You’ll see a scientist tell you about Smithers going to turn on the generator but never coming back.

  10. Follow the corridors and go through the airlock. You will enter another silo.

  11. Go around to the other side of the silo and climb the ladder. There are two control rooms at the top, each with a button inside. You’ll need to activate both buttons to turn on the generator.

  12. With both buttons pressed, the generator should be on, and the fuel/oxygen lines should be running. PART TWO COMPLETE!

  13. Return back the way you came to the tentacle room. Ascend to Level 1 and return to the control room where the tentacle dragged the scientist away in step 2.

  14. The “FIRE” button should now be lit green. The “FUEL” light should be lit red. The “OXYGEN” light should be lit blue and the “POWER” light should be lit green. If that’s all correct:

  15. Push the “FIRE” button and light 'er up!

  16. The way forward will be in the middle of Level 4 where the base of the tentacles were. ENJOY![/spoiler]

Hope that helps!
