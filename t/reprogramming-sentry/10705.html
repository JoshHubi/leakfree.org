How to a reprogramming sentry, when I grab sentry and put to the ground, the sentry shoot to me why ?!

Like fgsfds said, reprogramming it doesn’t make it friendly to you; it just makes it hostile to everything. You can then carry it around (picking it up will automatically orient it so it can’t hit you) and it will shoot anything that moves until it runs out of ammo.
