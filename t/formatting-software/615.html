Formatting is completely erasing, no way to ‘partially format’. If you want to clean out your computer, get a Windows CD, use a serial number and reinstall windows. Sure you have to get your drivers and such again, but it’ll speed up your computer.

Installing and uninstalling can clog up your registry and hard drive if it’s a poorly designed game/installer, which many are. It won’t screw up your computer, but it will slow it down. Try defragmenting your hard drive, that should fix some issues, and get Auslogics registry cleaner, that can help too.
