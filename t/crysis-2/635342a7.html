The aliens in C2 are 100x better, and when you play on the harder difficulties their tactics and aggressiveness change making them even more fun/difficult to fight against. Crysis 2 in many ways differs from the original, but in many ways i think it’s an improvement. It’s very streamlined and polished compared to the original in terms of mechanics, making it slightly easier to utilize the powers of the nanosuit. Crytek also kept the game quite challenging, something that’s missing from this generation of shooters. It does utilize some scripted events, but in no way does it feel forced or linear like call of duty, you still can approach objectives from multiple angles and tactics. i do however feel that the alien enemy design takes a lot of inspiration from the halo franchise(cloaked elites, hunters) , but the artstyle, sound design and the animation gives the aliens a very unique feel to Crysis’s universe. This game is now in my top 10 shooters of all time list, it’s the first unique all platform game I’ve been excited for in a long time, Crytek will have my money on day 1.

I look forward to playing the game in it’s entirety in DX11!
