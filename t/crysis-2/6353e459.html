Solokiller November 25, 2018, 12:00am #554

I got Crysis 2 a few days ago, it’s a really fun game, quite different from Crysis 1, but still has the same basic things.
The vehicle sections weren’t very fun to play, since for some reason the turret rotation sensitivity was turned way down, i could barely aim with it.

The weapons are fun to use, but i don’t like the limited set of attachments for each weapon, and i still haven’t found the 13th attachment, despite not seeing any missing attachments on any weapons.

There are also some obscure features that you might miss, like being able to stabilize your weapon by pressing the sprint button while aiming, or being able to fire pistols while cloaked without draining all your energy.

Finding the collectibles and figuring out what they do isn’t the most obvious, like the speeding tickets which are related to an achievement (does the PC version have achievements?), but at least you can see how many there are in each level in the pause menu.

Once you find all the weapon and suit attachments and start the game over you can play it in different ways, which i like a lot, although the weapon attachments have weird behavior when you use the fire mode toggle, which can cause you to switch from one weapon to another’s attachment, rather than switching to semi auto.

I’d have liked it if they explained things a bit more though, what happened to nomad and psycho, and what those nanosuits are that you find in hargreave’s office.
