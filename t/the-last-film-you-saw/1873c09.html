So, I saw the movie today. Here’s my report:

The Imaginarium of Doctor Parnassus is a disjointed mess of a film. The characters were kind of dull and uninteresting and I didn’t really care if they lived or died (though Verne Troyer was awesome). Mr. Nick was also kind of lame.

The plot seemed to be on a lunch break and only showed up every once in a while to remind us that it’s there. But, when the plot was there, it was stupendous. I loved the message and the metaphor of the Imaginarium.

And, speaking of the land of the Imaginarium, I haven’t seen special effects like this. Unfortunately, those worlds are scattershot and we always end up back in the grimy world of ‘modern?’ London streets where we spend TOO much of the movie (possibly for budgetary reasons).

I give the film 1.5 stars out of 4.

Pros: Verne Troyer, amazing special effects, plot (when it’s there)
Cons: Eveything else
