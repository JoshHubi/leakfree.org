There’s a difference between not being very tech savvy and not even knowing how to copy&paste something :meh:

To copy&paste something, right-click the folders you want to copy and click Copy from the menu, then go the Gmod main directory and right-click again, select Paste.

Alternatively you can select the folders you want to copy and press Ctrl+C and then click Ctrl+V to paste.
