Marb November 24, 2018, 8:34pm #25

Garbage disposals can get really nasty inside no matter what you do (assuming you use it for what it’s intended for). If it’s old, chances are that there’s going to be old food and crap stuck in it. If that’s your problem, the only thing I can suggest is to fill the sink up with water and a strong concentration of either bleach or some other sort of cleaner (DO NOT mix any other cleaners with bleach!!!). Pull the drain plug and turn on the disposal until it sucks all the water from the sink. Repeat if necessary. Make sure you rinse well with plenty of clean water - run a couple of sink fulls through afterwards.
