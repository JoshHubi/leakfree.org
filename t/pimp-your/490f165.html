Bolteh August 12, 2020, 3:39am #2968

Problem with SGs (and Gibson in general) is that for a great SG or LP or whatever model they’re updating these days, you’ll have to look at the higher price ranges. They just can’t seem to do “budget” guitars right, and I really can’t be arsed forking over $2000 or more for a guitar, not even a Gibson.

I had tried a couple of $800-1000 SGs at the store but they all sounded muddy and way too loud at a clean setting. I also tried a $300 Epiphone SG that actually sounded better than the Gibson SG (but didn’t like the feel of the neck on that one).

In the end it was a tie between that $500 Blacktop Jaguar (Mex) and a classic reissue $1000 Jaguar HH (USA), but since I already own a classic Jazzmaster (replaced the stock pickups with vintage spec Curtis Novak’s)

Can’t wait for it to arrive and if for some reason the sound doesn’t match up to the expectations I had after playing one, I’ll simply get a set of new pickups for $90 and still have an insanely good sounding guitar for 2/3 of the price of the cheapest Gibson \o/

Also, I’m a sucker for surf, hence my love for the Jazzmasters and Jaguars.
