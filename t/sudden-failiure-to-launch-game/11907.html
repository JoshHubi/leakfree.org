I seem to recall that the dropouts and “crazy” graphics are the result of a video card that cannot handle certain shaders. Try to play around with the options under “Visual”. First try disabling the noise shader to see if that helps. Otherwise, try different combinations of the three options under “Visual” to see if that helps.
