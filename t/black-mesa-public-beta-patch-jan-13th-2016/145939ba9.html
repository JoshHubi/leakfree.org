KyuuGryphon November 25, 2018, 4:45am #55

In Half-Life 2 speedrunning, that bug’s referred to as Schrodinger’s Crouching, if I recall. If you start holding crouch again in the middle of standing up from a crouch, the player hitbox is still crouching, while the camera gets disconnected at the height you were at when you started crouching again. I don’t know if I’m explaining it very well.

It allows the player to push buttons through walls or ceilings, though I can’t really think of anywhere in Black Mesa that can be exploited that way. Arguably, the more severe effects are 1. the player can see through brushes outside of the map (e.g. getting the camera stuck in the top of a vent) and 2. the disconnect between the player hitbox and camera causes the NPCs’ aiming to get thrown off.
