I actually liked the idea of Fortress Forever when I first heard about the cartoonish direction the actual TF2 was headed in before it came out. I was all like, “Aw well, at least someone’s making a mod that keeps the original TF’s gameplay and more realistic aesthetic.”

Problem was that when both came out (bad idea for the FF team to release the mod in almost the exact same time period as TF2’s release; not a very wise choice indeed) and I played around with TF2, I was kind of won over not so much by the aesthetic, but by the mechanics, gameplay, and general class edits and re-balancing. While I may always have a gripe with the art style and some minor changes, as a whole I think TF2 is very much a worthy sequel. They made changes with thought and insight and made it a genuinely (except for servers full of tards) fun experience.

FF however, I found to be a poorly-done graphics tweak. Granted, I miss stuff like bunnyhopping, grenades, and faster movement of the older game play, but a lot of it really is better left behind. Bunnyhopping breaks balance, grenade spam ruins gameplay in general, and a lot of the classes they can’t seem to balance correctly or define it’s purpose coherently enough. They axe some minor stuff like teleporters (which is terrible for the really large maps, when there are enough people to play them) but still let shit like infinite spy cloaks, EMP grenades, and other totally annoying shit remain.

FF feels shoddy and done with no thoughtful mechanics insight other than just give the old TF a face-lift. Not to mention the player base is pretty much composed of clannish assholes that while blasting the TF2 player base ironically emit from themselves a bad vibe of community envy. Because they are so clannish and anal-retentive they’ve come to isolate themselves from anyone interested in continuing to play with them and it’s part of the reason why nobody plays on any of their servers.

Part of my rant against FF is not so much any kind of superiority complex or game-bias, but instead of my disappointment in what FF became, all because of the dev’s refusal to evolve and keep the whithered and vestigial remains of FPS stagnation while being as anti-social as you can be while trying to recruit for players.

I was really hoping it would be good. I am disappoint
