I am sort of colorblind too. Doctor’s called it “dyschromatopsy” (google says it’s the same as Daltonism). It’s basically close to what TheeGoatPig described.
Can’t see the numbers/letters in most of those tests with the weirdly colored circles.

It’s been a running joke with my sisters, when I was younger. Like “Hey what color is this? A hahaha! And what color is that? A hahahah!” :meh:

What’s most frustrating is when I can’t really tell if a lit led on a device (e.g. a router or a switch) is green or yellow or red. I look at it for a while and if I can’t decide, then I take a guess.
