I was looking this up because my mom in law was telling me that there are now contact lenses that you can get for color blind folks. Her son got these lenses and tried them out. He wound up not using them cuz they brought out the colors so vividly and it was disjointing for him. But I found this whole read very fascinating.

Improving Color Vision with Lenses for the Colorblind

That is where I saw that there was more than one type of color blind.

Protanopia – Red-Green Color Blindness
(protan color blindness are less sensitive to red light)

Deuteranopia – Red-Green Color Blindness
(deuteranomly is related to sensitiveness on green light)

Tritanopia – Blue-Yellow Color Blindness
(People affected by tritan color blindness confuse blue with green and yellow with violet. So the term blue-green color blindness would be more accurate because the colors blue and yellow are usually not mixed up by tritanopes.)

Monochromacy – Complete Color Blindness
(If you are suffering under monochromacy, you perceive everything just in shades of gray.)
