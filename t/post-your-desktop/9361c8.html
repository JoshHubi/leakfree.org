An MMO is inherently a flawed system in my opinion.

The combat’s going to be dull because it’s always the same style click, select action, wait, select other action, wait etc. The only one I’ve seen that doesn’t do this, was Fallen Earth and that was awful.

There’s never going to be proper story, because that just wouldn’t work in an MMO environment. Sure, there’s some kind of plot development, but the fact that the only interaction your likely to get from characters is them asking you to go kill something prevents the player from getting involved. Plus there’s the fact that you’ll frequently see other players going to do the same world-savey quest you just did, making it seem unimportant.

They’re never finished, never polished. Until the time they close down, the developers constantly update and revise the game which is not only frustrating because you feel like you’re stick in a beta, but it also means that any specific character setups get periodically fucked.

I will pose a counter point.

Until I got into EVE I thought the same, point, click, repeat.

At first it was ok, the first time you jump into EVE it jumps you into an ‘Epic Arc’ which in EVE is a series of missions with a story loosely attached, the one it puts you into is a tutorial one, over the course it teaches you bit of most of the components of the EVE universe, a game as large as EVE with a univrse that big it can take years to learn even half of what is there to do.

EVE, as Im sure with most MMO’s, if you can find a group of people in a Clan like style, the experiences you have in the game are so much better, plus you can have loads of fun, luckily when I started playing EVE, I had been with my TF2 clan AEF for some time, but AEF also have an EVE division called Vanguard, which was purely based around enjoying EVE, a non competitive Corp.

Since then EVE has been an excellent game, even with the learning curve that almost resembles that of climbing Mt Everest.

As to the updates side of thing, I’m not sure about other MMO’s, I have heard WoW these days is just a clusterfuck of nerfs and buffs and completely unbalanced races, EVE is quite balanced, with each expansion based on adding new content, and unlike other MMO’s, EVE has a dedicated test server, as EVE has only one single ‘realm’ unlike WoW, so updates are fairly good and unoticable when it comes to core gameplay.

Plus, I’d like to add that while EVE can seem like point and click, if you underestimate your opponents, CPU or another play, you’ll find yourself hitting buttons in order to win.
