CTID November 24, 2018, 10:05pm #2708

The calender? FireTime posted a link to it on the last page. You get it, and install Rainmeter (seperately), extract the FireTime folder to “C:\Users(Name)\My Documents\Rainmeter\Skins” and launch rainmeter. In the tray icon you go to themes, FireTime and open it, Drag it where you want. You can close the other default stuff by right clicking it and going to “Close Config”. You have to have Rainmeter running to make it show up, and you’ll want it to be set to “On Desktop” otherwise it’ll minimize whenever you leave your desktop.
