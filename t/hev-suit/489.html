mattemuse August 11, 2020, 2:58am #4

I wasted 20min drawing a HEV arms concept, because I dislike the current BM arms.
In the reference I was looking at, the suit arms have a raised panel over the outer forearm and vents on the inner forearm. In the BM suit, this raised panel has been inverted and placed on the inner forearm and the vents are on the outer forearm. My concept has the original forearm layout with some embelleshments. Also note that scratches are not regularly distributed over the entire arm, but are rather placed in sections of the armor that might be expected to rub up against other parts of the suit or be scratched by regular use.

740×444 54.2 KB

Feel free to crit my crit, If you flame me hard enough I might be convinced to improve the drawing. If I revisit it, one thing I’d like to do is make it more apparent that the wrist guard rotates separately from the forearm and add some radial scratches to that area,.
