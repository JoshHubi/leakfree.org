I’ve read review upon review as well as looking at gameplay footage and from what I can see, they’re really trying to improve and add on all the parts of the first game that were good in the first place.

The thing that really excites me is the whole concept of going out in the ocean.
The point is that it’s not for combat, so no you won’t get to fight the giant whale from the first game What it does is allow you to take a breather and really think about where you are, admire the world around you and even take a look through the windows at the next area to spot any possible threats.

As far as story is concerned, it’s quite possible that the Big Daddy you play as, the first Big Daddy ever created, may have been protector to the Big Sister. Despite the fact that she stalks you, impales you and generally makes your life hell, there could be some relationship between the two that you find out about during the course of the game. It also seems as though Big Sister may be being controlled by someone else, possibly Orrin Oscar Lutwidge, for those who know who he is. Tennenbaum also has a part to play; having left the city previously in Bioshock she has returned for some unknown reason. Either way, I’m sure any new characters that are brought into the plot line will be of the same quality and detail as those in the first game.
