Oxidious November 24, 2018, 11:31pm #72

I wrote Valve back in Oct. 08 with an idea I had and actually got 2 replies back. Here was my suggestion to them about EP3.

" I had just finished playing Halflife2, Ep1 and Ep2 for the third time. The more I played the series the more little pieces of the story came together. Then this idea hit me. The Borealis, Aperture Labs disappearing ship is much more than what it seems. What if Aperture sent the Borealis away to keep its technology from falling into the hands of the Combine. The Borealis is actually an experimental time portal test bed. Aperture hurriedly sent the Borealis foward in time at the arrival of the Combine’s forces on earth. They planned to retrieve it when it reappeared several years in the future, when humanity had beaten back the Combine…but unfortunately the Combine won. Now it’s a race between Gordon Freeman and the Combine to retrieve the secrets of the Borealis. Judith Mossman spilled the beans about the Borealis once captured and tortured my the Combine. However no one truly knows what the Borealis really does. Gordon battles his way though and discovers that the Borealis is indeed a time portal test bed and better yet, the portal the ship used to come foward in time is still open in the past,lets call it the orange portal (much like the orange and blue portals in the game Portal). All Gordon has to do is “open a Blue portal” in the present and the ship will be transported back to the past “orange” portal and arive the exact same moment it left…at the beginning of the Combine/human war. Here is the catch however. Everyone on the Borealis died in the time travel, they were exposed to energies that were not accounted for, however Gordon’s HEV suit would protect him in the travel back. Another thing is that the scientists in White Forest , Dr. Kleiner and Dr. Magnussen are wary about sending anyone back in time who already exists in that time line, worried that it might cause a cascade and the destruction on earth. The concensus is that Alyx cannot go because she is alive at the time when the Borealis left, but Gordon??.. Gordon has been in some sort of stasis or perhaps in a different dimension (sorry if that sounds corny) the entire time since the end of Halflife 1. What ever the case is, Gordon will not pollute the time line because he was never part of it, he can go back. Armed with information given to him to take back in time, Gordon arrives and is hell bent on changing the outcome of the Combine/human war.

I'd really like to have Gordon and Alyx to be reunited, perhaps the G-man can have something to do with it. If you've read this far I'd like to thank you and the crew for hours and hours of great gaming! I can't wait for Episode 3!!

Thanks!

P.S. Just as an afterthought, I’d be nice to see Gordon and Alyx battle the Aperture combat androids, perhaps Alyx can reprogram them to help Gordon like she did the turrets and mines."
