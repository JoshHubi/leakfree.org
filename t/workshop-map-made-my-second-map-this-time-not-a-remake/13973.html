So I had so much fun making my first map I decided to have another go at making a map, this time a original map idea.

An old decommissioned cargo zone has been used as a weapons testing area for the past year. When the marines moved in a group of scientist set up on one side of the cargo bay in one final stand off.

The map is a roughly mirrored map with two sides. The structure is the same on both sides. I decided to change the props and stuff on each side to match my story. On one side filing cabinets and office supplies are propped up to act as cover while the other uses sand bags.

Again please let me know what you think and how I can improve ?
