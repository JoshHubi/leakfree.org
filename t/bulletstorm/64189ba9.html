Garry:

Ok, so I just played a bit of Bulletstorm. It’s crap.

It makes sense that it’s crap. The only marketing I’ve seen of it is how bloody, how gory, how unsafe for kids it is. It makes me wonder whether the article on the Fox News website about how this game will turn your kids into rapists was actually just marketing. If it was then they knew that such a biased article about a game before it was even released would cause all of the gaming websites to write long editorials about how games are art and should be treated like movies. Playing right into their hands.

I played it on the PC obviously, and it was made for the 360, obviously. Menus that make perfect sense to navigate with the mouse become hard work as you’re forced to navigate with the WSAD and space keys.

The graphics are probably technically good. Maybe I over analyze it, but to me it looks like a quake level with a load of models thrown all over the place. The ‘map’ parts are noticeable by the stretched textures and the obvious low res triangley mesh. To make it look next gen everything you look at has bright blue rim lighting.

The whole USP of the game is that you kill people using awesome skillful moves to earn points. This is fun. The first 10 times. Later on they start adding spikey fences and stuff so you can kick people into them.

There’s no jump.

So in summary: A worse version of Gears Of War that you shouldn’t buy on the PC.
