Pyro: stock Flamethrower, Scorch Shot, Homewrecker

Demoman: stock Grenade Launcher, Scottish Resistance, Ullapool Caper

Heavy: Brass Beast, Sandvich, Gloves of Running Urgently

Engineer: Rescue Ranger, Wrangler, Jag

Medic: Overdose, Kritzkrieg, Vita-Saw/Amputator

Sniper: Machina, Jarate, Tribalman’s Shiv

Spy: Enforcer/Ambassador, Spycicle, Dead Ringer, stock Sapper
