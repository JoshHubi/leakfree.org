note that the sound files in Deus Ex are in the form of Suites (which are a package of multiple musics combined into 1; this is done in halo somtimes)

Differnt sectons of the Suite plays depending on the events as follows:
-Idle theme (which is the music that plays when just roaming the area; this is generally considered the “theme” but of course its not)
-Combat theme(which plays when the player engages combat with NPCs)
-Conversation theme*********************(self explanatory)
-Death/Game over*************************(which play upon death)
-and in certain cases, a chopper departure theme **(that usually plays when the player has a cinematic exit from the area; usually via choppers, but also a submarine in Ocean Labs)

this is done to give Deus Ex dynamic music (and a more rich soundtrack )
