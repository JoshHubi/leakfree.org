Korpiklaani (Finnish culture, lore and general boozing) springs to mind:

  • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PJwo6bMKBaw
  • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MdgLR9RttZQ

  • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y4WR8Wt9-O4
