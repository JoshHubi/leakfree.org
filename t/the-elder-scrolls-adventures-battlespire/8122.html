Battlespire was the first and only game in a spinoff series for the elder scrolls called the elder scrolls legend. It was originally planned to be an expansion pack for daggerfall, but it expanded into its own game (which was probably a bad idea as it isn’t very well known)

Skip to the bottom if you just want to give it a go.

Story:

The plot runs parallel to Arena, and it focuses on how Jagar Tharn from the first game collaborated with Mehrunes Dagon. The Imperial “battlespire”, the training facility for battlemages was taken over by the daedra. The player must travel through the battlespire, and various realms of Oblivion to reach Mehrunes Dagon, defeat him and escape back to Tamriel.

General info:

It was the first game to introduce the oblivion-style dialogue tree to the series, as well as fully voiced NPCs. It is however considered to be one of the most demanding DOS games ever released, so running it even on powerful computers is hard unless you bump down the resolution.

It was also the first and only TES game to feature multiplayer - it had DM, coop, and team games.

IMO the game is pretty fun, but its definitely hardcore, it’s kinda like a more modern dungeon master, or a first person diablo.

Plus the atmosphere of the game is pretty creepy, thanks to the music: Battlespire Soundtrack 02
