What? They’re well written. Persona 4 in particular had a ton of funny writing, and the side story social links managed to even be poignent in some parts. Plus the late game with the Heaven dungeon and who got kidnapped to make it, and it’s immediate aftermath, was incredibly emotional.

GTA4’s main strength for me was the narrative and strong characterization for Niko Bellic. As crazy as the story missions got, and as many terrible decisions the game forces you to make, I stuck with it because dammit, Niko is a great character.

Enslaved- Andy Serkis. Come on, dude. Andy Serkis is the shit. And considering that the story managed to engage me quite well the whole way through, exploiting it’s post-apocalyptic setting and art direction to the fullest, and that twist ending! It’s a good game, if a bit short.
