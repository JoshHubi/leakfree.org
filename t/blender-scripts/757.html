Brother_Shrike November 24, 2018, 5:54pm #7

so just check what directory blender saves your files in by default. If it uses the appdata or whatever directory, your scripts will probably be in there. On XP it’s C:\documents and settings\username\Application Data\Blender Foundation\Blender.blender\scripts

Application Data may be a hidden folder, but if you haven’t already, you should disable hidden folders. Tools->Folder Options->View or display or something.
