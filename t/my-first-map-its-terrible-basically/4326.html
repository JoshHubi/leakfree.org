Ibanez November 24, 2018, 9:23pm #6

I like your potential here. It’s got some hints of Le Corbusier going on, with the stilts and the large horizontal planes and glass walls. Keep some of these things in mind, though: Those stairs are enormous, and don’t have any railings. You should add more, but smaller, steps, and cut down on the diagonal risers and add some railings. Also, a house built on stilts wouldn’t be made out of bricks, it’d be made out of concrete or something similar and would have metal, not wood, stilts. Also, if you add columns to the outside walls, perhaps some pilasters, then it’d not only break up the outside walls but add realistic support to the whole structure. I like your direction, though.
