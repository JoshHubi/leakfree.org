Kasey the Kinderbot was a toy designed by Dr. Freeman which was targeted at kids aged 3–7. Instead of tapes as with the previous versions, this toy used small cartridges that were not linear - the user was able to jump around to various sections at will. It was purely an educational toy with cartridges focusing on such subjects as Math, Science and languages. The cartridges were also color coded to let kids know what group of subject it belonged to.
