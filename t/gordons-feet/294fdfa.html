What “added challenge” do you keep going on about. I don’t see any real difference other then a few bad HL1 monsters placed on the maps.

-NPCs (including Grigori, Alyx, etc) have the same HP at Gordon, and don’t regenerate, meaning there’s an added emphasis on protecting them.

-Most of the sequences where you face a gunship now have about 2 or 3 of them attacking you at once, and they have a different attack sequence meaning you have to use a bit more thinking to take them down.

-Enemies can kill you instantly with a headshot, which generally gives a reason to not run headlong into a firefight, and to use cover.

-Added enemies include houndeyes, the female Combine assassin (who I will admit is poorly animated), invisible combine soldiers, city scanners equipped with machine guns, and stalkers that will attack the player. (A bullsquid and a new type of zombie are being added in Redux 7)

-the ‘bullet time’ effect, as stupid as you may think it is, can actually be extremely useful when the situation warrants its use.

Even if you don’t like it, I’d say give Redux 6 a shot before passing judgment on it based on screenshots and a different variation of the mod
