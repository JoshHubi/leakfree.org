Hello there,

I thought I’d make my first post count, and suggest something. No matter how late I am into the mods completion. :awesome:

To the point:

I’ve always found the Gonarch a very hard enemy to take seriously, with that massive testicle dangling around; giving birth without warning. I’m sure this is how VALVe originally intended for the Gonarch to be portrayed, but I comtemplated the idea of something a little more ‘awesome’ (without drastically changing the silhouette).

I envision something resembling a blastocyst (early foundations for embrygenesis in mammals), hovering between the Gonarch, and the ground.

https://img.photobucket.com/albums/v668/maryscottoconnor/MLW%20Pics/BLASTOCYST.gif An image for reference.

It’d make perfect sense for the Gonarch to have a setup like this, as it’s primary function appeared to be pro-creation.

As well as this, it would add considerably to the general horror of the fight, in that your perception of fear isn’t ruined by the fact that there’s a massive testicle dancing about (although, some may find ball-thrusting scary, as it is ).

I wouldn’t say it’s physically impossible for something non-human either.
Par example: The Nihilanth’s head opened up. This isn’t physically possible to do with the mere strength of muscles in your own cranium.

Just my two cents.

P.S. If I’ve made any mistakes: spelling or otherwise, do tell me.
