Guys, the most straight forward temporary fix would be to go to the console, activate the cheats and then use no-clip.
But try unninstalling Black mesa and installing again.
To activate the console, go to Options -> Keyboard -> Search for the key that’s used to open the console (change it if you have to) and bring up the console, in it type “sv_cheats 1” hit enter and then “noclip”.
Get off the train and go directly into the airlock. Then write “noclip” again to get back to normal.
