Madcat1030 November 24, 2018, 8:28pm #3

This might be stupid, and might not work, but try (in the console) buildcubemaps.

That should reload reflectivity maps, and might fix it. Unless for some reason the map is just not compiled correctly. Again, I use words like “should” “might” and “stupid” because I am not the leading source of knowledge on such things.

But again, it absolutely CANNOT hurt your computer or game to TRY the ‘buildcubemaps’ command, I promise that. It will require a reload of the map, which will happen automatically. Probably shouldn’t try it while connected to a server.
