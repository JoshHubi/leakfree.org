Duke3D remake put on indefinite hold for DNF DLC

Technology and Gaming
Bloodshot August 11, 2020, 7:42am #1

https://www.dukenukemreloaded.com/

If anyone has ever heard of Duke Nukem 3D : Reloaded, it was a remake of Duke3D in UE3 that was in the works after the devs received a non-commercial license from Gearbox. At it’s current state, the single player was largely unfinished but the MP was pretty much ready for some sort of Public beta.

Now, here’s the interesting part: The devs have put it on hold because Gearbox aren’t allowing them to release anything until the next DLC for Duke Nukem Forever that has no announced release date is released.

Are they really serious? I had respect for GBX, but DNF was total shit, and they are still trying to milk that game more when they haven’t even fixed anything that was wrong with it? They really have to let DNF die. I don’t even see how letting the team release stuff would hurt them - unless that teams shit is so much more professional than their stuff that it would put GBX to shame maybe?
