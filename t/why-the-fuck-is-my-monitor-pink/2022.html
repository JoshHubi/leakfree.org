Specifically, in whichever connector your monitor uses, the pin for the Green signal was not connected, thus leaving only Red and Blue, thus pink.
The only other likely cause would have been someone messing with the monitors’ colour temperature custom settings, tho that usually applies a tint rather than selectively removing one whole channel.

In other words, knowing how stuff works comes in handy when it stops working right.
