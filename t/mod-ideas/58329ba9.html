shadowscrawl November 24, 2018, 10:52pm #49

DAYLIGHT: a dystopian cyberpunk fps-rpg originally conceptualized as a total conversion mod for unreal 3

Story:

  "It's in man's DNA to expand, his nature to destroy. For too many years we have been pillaging the earths natural resources en mass, the rain forests of the world came crashing down like piles of tomorrows garbage. We expanded too fast and with our lust for consumption we have robbed the world of its most precious natural resource. The ozone was weakening, and we were the cause. Plant-life began to fade, and what animal life had survived the mass deforestation was left with almost no viable food source and under the relentless rays of the sun began to change, to lash out, against man... and ultimately in their desperation, against each other.


  The ground soon began to burn with a ghostly fire. The radiation set on us by the Sun spread upon the distant horizon. When at last we were backed into a corner, industry, The very villain that had brought us so close to the edge of extinction became our only hope.


  A small group of scientists had foreseen this fate and had been working in secret on a plan, a plan to fill the sky with artificial clouds that would block out the Sun and its deadly rays while pouring down a healing rain to cool our sweltering climate. The barren lands that once held mighty trees was now to be the home of gleaming metal foundations, wires, tubes, and coolant. Sparkling towers that reached over the heights of the trees that were once their fore-bearers.

  It was not by any means perfect. The sun was gone and with it went any form of what was once called "nature" but we were alive, and for the time being there was peace.


  But after many years the once gleaming towers began to crumble and decay, The harmless rain they had once poured down turned acidic and became as a pestilence upon man. Over time the burning rains began to spread and once again we were afraid. But this time we, the masses were not joined by the wealthy aristocracy. Those who owned the towers, and should have cared for them were overcome with another lust... one for power and control. For with the towers damaged a new advantage was revealed to the ruling class. That they could control us like cattle, force upon us whatever way of life that they saw fit. They began to use our own fear as a weapon against us.


  Terror now reigns around the world. Uni-chem, The principle corporation in charge of maintaining the towers has positioned itself as the ruling world power. They create the law and enforce it with ghastly man made soldiers who stalk from street to street. People are taken in the night. A lucky few are beaten in interrogation, released with broken ribs and noses but most are never seen again.


  Blood flows in the streets of this dark twisted metropolis, and the those in charge of Uni-chem known only as "The Board" are riding in on it.

  Those who managed fled to The Zone. The lawless country between the cities where the towers lie and acidic rain still pours. Zone 00 is ours. It stretches the whole of ruined North America. Dotting the country side between Mega cities lie shanty towns and rebel outposts crawling with villainy, prostitution, and black market sales.

  It is here, Within The Zone that the last hope for freedom lies, a resistance. A rebellion is against those on the top, The men on their gleaming pedestals, The Armored fortresses of UniCHEM, And anyone left in the world who would seek to push down the common man. And time is ticking. "


  Setting:

  A dystopian future in which major conglomerates rule the burned out shell of Chicago.

  The current location is a 1 Mile square section of Chicago near Sears Tower. The area will begin to expand as time allows
