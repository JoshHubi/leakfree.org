Just a quick follow-up… if you have a successful install, you must completely exit Steam and restart before it will show in your library.

This is more than just clicking the X in the upper right corner. You must stop it from running in the background. You can either stop it from the system tray (lower right corner of windows) or just reboot your computer.

It should show up at the top, Black Mesa.
