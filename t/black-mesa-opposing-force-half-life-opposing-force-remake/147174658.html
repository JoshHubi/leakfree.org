TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:49am #34

I’m not exactly sure what’s going on in this thread, or what this is even really meant to be.

But…let’s not get hostile to people who are suggesting ideas. “We don’t take ideas” seems like a totally counterintuitive statement, given that this entire project is literally taking someone else’s idea and remaking it. And there’s nothing wrong with that, Black Mesa is the same. That’s what a remake is. You won’t get far if you don’t build off the community.

Also, I just wanted to add - that brushwork Osprey interior is glorious. One thing we’ve learned the hard way recently with our tribulations on Xen and our recent new earthbound stuff - stay away from using brushwork for basically anything, when you can. There’s about a million reasons why you shouldn’t, but the most important one - it always looks horrible. If you want to create a professional and good looking piece of work, you need modellers. Model your details. You need to get modellers and mappers working in very close proximity, trading things back and forth, if you want to get good results. It’s been a big struggle that we’ve been dealing with for Xen.

To lighten things up a bit in here, here’s a brushwork truck. Literally taken from HL1, from an original Bootcamp VMF.
