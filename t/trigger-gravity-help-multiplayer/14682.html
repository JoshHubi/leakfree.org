I could be wrong, but I think trigger_gravity is, like… a volume trigger, as opposed to a toggle, i.e. the modified gravity applies when the player(s) is inside of it. Either that, or there’s an input that needs to be fired. Not sure.

TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:46am #3

Yeah he’s right. trigger_gravity only affects things that are inside its volume. Once you leave the volume, gravity returns to normal. The way you have it setup is wrong.

You need to those triggers be trigger_multiples instead, set to enable and disable trigger_gravities which encase the area where you want the gravity to change.

trigger_gravity does indeed work, we had them working on early versions of Rapidcore (which we decided to cut because it just wasn’t a good map).
