MaxTheLimit November 24, 2018, 6:41pm #4012

Unfortunately the contrast ratio and response time numbers are fudged, because it depends on HOW the manufacturer measures.
At this point there isn’t much you’d be able to tell in difference between any two panels anyhow.

TN means twisted nematic. It’s the cheapest of the LCD panel types. VA panels have better colour representation. There is also IPS panels. The later two are far more expensive than TN panels though.

The problem with TN panels is that they are 6bit, and only emulate the 8bit colour spectrum. there can be some odd results. Also TN panels don’t hold up so well when viewed at angles. If you look from an up/down or left/right angle you’ll notice darkening and colour shift.

Still I have a TN panel and I view it straight on. Looks fine to me. I don’t move around much. I don’t notice problems with the colour emulation. TN panels have the best response times though, which makes them ideal for gamers.
