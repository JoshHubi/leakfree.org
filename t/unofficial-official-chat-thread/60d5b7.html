I had a hypnagogic/sleep-paralysis/weird moment a while back, which had the nice side effect of curing me of any belief in UFOs (as in little green men; I still happily accept that there are flying objects that cannot be classified on occasions, until they get close enough and you realise it is in fact a bird/plane/ICBM/torso/other random shit… but I digress…).

I woke up from a doze one morning to find that I was paralysed and that there was a Grey (you know, the x-files shit) looming over me. I’m now quite happy in reckoning that about 100% of all close encounters of the 4th kind are, in fact, sleep paralysis/hypnagogia combined with failure to engage brain in any kind of rational and/or informed manner.
