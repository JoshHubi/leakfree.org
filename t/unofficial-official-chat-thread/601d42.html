Winged One:

Is it possible to get a job in the industry that is able to pitch ideas and offer creative influence without actually being technical and artsy?

Designer or production co-ordinator. But if you don’t have the ability to be either technical or “artsy” then there is very little hope that you’ll be able to do those jobs either.

What it comes down to is this: If you want to be a game artist, make art. If you want to be a designer, design a game. If you can’t do either of those things on your own initiative, there’s no reason to expect somebody to pay you to do them.

If you have a great idea for a game, you should plan it out, and then get together a group of people to make it happen.
