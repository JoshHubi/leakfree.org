Torrent sites judging you by ratio are unfair in and of themselves. There’s an easy to understand reason for that.

For every copy of a file you download, you have to supply (most of the time at least) one copy of that file. So for every person that downloads that file, there has to be at least one person who wants to download that file, who then needs another person to upload that file to, et cetera ad infinitum. This is even more true if you get rewards for having a very high ratio.
