I paid $499 for the board, chip, ram and cooler. I got 200 bucks back from crap I had sitting around being sold.

The way you use the OC genie is that you turn off the computer. Press in the button. Power up. You’ll hear the machine power cycle a few times looking for stable clocks. Then it will full boot with a message saying the OC genie is active. Then it’s done.

To stress I use P95 which is currently stressing all cores…have reached 64C now…still well stable. After this I’m doing a memtest. After that I’m running a blend. Then a RTHDRIBL loop to make sure it can do GPU intensive stuff.

If anything fails, then she ain’t stable. So far it’s rock solid. P95 is only a couple hours in though. I like 8 hours minimum to declare stable.
