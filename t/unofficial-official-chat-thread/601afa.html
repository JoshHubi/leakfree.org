I’m not sure what you people are whining about, Crysis, and Crysis warhead run fine for me. I have decent systems, but not the pinnacle by any means.
The problem with Crysis is that is doesn’t scale properly. In a lot of cases you can throw immense amounts of hardware at it, and you won’t see greatly improved results. We see people go from a GTX 260, to a GTX 280 and only gain about 5-7 average fps.
So the expected gain in power doesn’t match with the real world results. In most games a change like that would mean much higher gains. In the range of 10+fps.
