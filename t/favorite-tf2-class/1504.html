Comrade_Tiki August 11, 2020, 1:43am #10

I always choose Pyro. With the incendiary cannon and napalm gre-… …aww, crap.

As far as the “Meet the…” videos go, every class seems to fill an existing archetype or stereotype.
In order of class number,

  • Overconfident American
  • Australian Poacher
  • “All-American” Soldier
  • Captured German Doctor who probably worked at a concentration camp
  • Gigantic Russian
  • French Provocateur
  • Technophile

So, there are two classes that can’t be easily classified. The Demoman seems to be a bizarre collection of minority and misfortune, so he could be classified as being the typical oddity (his role being that he has no role).

But then there’s the Pyro. Anonymous, highly non-verbal, and a man with simple goals who enjoys the finer things in life (like burning people and things). He’s not quite a connoisseur, nor is he a romantic. He simply is. To quote Alfred, “Some men just want to watch the world burn,” and there is not yet a word to describe that exact kind of man.

The beauty is that we’ll never know more about him. That is where the fear propagates. The thought of being burned alive by someone whose flesh you’ll never see, whose identity you’ll never know? It’s terrifying. It’s the same art by which submariners were able to terrify surface captains.

Only one word may describe an aspect of the Pyro, and that word is loner.
As the bulk of single-player shooters have inspired us to do, we admire the loner.

Aside from that aspect, he is unique.
I can respect that combination of traits.
