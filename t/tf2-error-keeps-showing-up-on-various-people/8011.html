That error is caused by a missing model. Since it only happens when the character is wearing hat or medal, this means that your TF2 doesn’t have the models for hats or medals. Since you’ve already verified, I would try reinstalling. I doubt there’s much else you could do without obtaining the models from somewhere else and sticking them in your models directory.
