Rumballs

12 oz. Semi Sweet Chocolate chips
1/3 Cup Light corn syrup
1 C Rum or Burbon
5 C Crushed Vanilla Wafers (about 2 boxes of wafers)
1 C Powdered sugar
2 C Walnuts chopped
Regular sugar to roll the balls in

  1. Melt the chocolate and corn syrup slowly in the microwave. About 1 minute is all that is needed to make it slightly creamy.
  2. Add the Rum or Bourbon to the chocolate and mix as best as possible. (It will be very watery.)
  3. Add chocolate mixture to the rest of the ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Let sit for 1/2 hour to set.
  4. Compact and roll the balls in the palm of your hand about the small spoonful size, then roll around in regular sugar.
  5. Let sit another 1/2 hour before bagging or closing them in a container.
