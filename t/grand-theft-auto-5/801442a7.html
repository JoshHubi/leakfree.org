Tei November 25, 2018, 2:05am #309

i can’t say i’m “worried”, but R* is clearly making a move towards the crazy and creative gameplay that made San Andreas so popular. i’m just hoping they keep things as serious as GTA4 did. i LOVED Niko’s story. The two expansion stories were great too but the gritty realism that Niko showed was spot-on perfect. …and don’t start telling me that he’s a psychotic killer and doesn’t deserve empathy. Nothing YOU, the player, do is canon to the story. i only take into account the things he does in cut scenes and outside player control. He clearly wanted OUT of the tough life, which is why he made the move to America. You don’t have relate to him in any way to empathize with him.

Which brings me to what i’ve been thinking since i saw the trailer :

i think R* is giving us control of multiple characters. in the trailer, they show several characters that fit the role of main protagonist. in the exterminator van scene, you clearly see ALL of them together. i think it would be AWESOME if throughout the game, between saves, you could switch control of different characters. This would give the game lots of replayability by allowing you to choose who takes on what missions and who takes what role(s) within the game’s story.

All the crazy fun stuff aside, Niko MADE Grand Theft Auto 4 awesome. But imagine if in GTA4, you had the opportunity switch between Niko, Johnny and Luis between missions. You could literally watch the story unfold with all characters all at once, instead of one at a time. i don’t remember where i read it, but R* was quoted in saying that GTA4’s multi-protagonist story arc was an experiment to see how well it would mesh and play out in an interactive game setting. Originally GTA4 was MEANT to be played with all characters at once but the idea was scrapped. Then M$ came in with a briefcase full of cash and bought exclusive DLC rights and you know the rest.
