Bedankt voor de suggesties ;D
En ik weet dat het geheugen niet verder kan, maar ik had gehoord dat windows 64bit niet goed samen gaat met de source engine.

Anyway, I finally solved it!
It was hyper threading that was causing the problems.
I tried disabling all cores and hyper threading and it worked!
Then all cores enabled, and only hyper threading disabled, and it still works fine.

It’s not an ideal solution, but apparently the MW engine doesn’t go well with hyper threading.
