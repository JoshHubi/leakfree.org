Hey guys, I recently finished a project for my A-Level media course which was to create a fictional film-trailer based on our own characters and plot. The story and continuity didn’t really matter (which is why it’s shit) but the way in which it was edited, the camera angles and the familiar conventions of a film-trailer did. All content had to be created by us, so we made the soundtrack, sound-effects and everything else.

Filmed on a Canon 550D DSLR HD Camera, edited with Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects CS4.

Just thought I’d throw it out there and get some criticism.
