Let me explain a little better…

I just had my music on random and the song ‘So Long Scarecrow’ by Scarling came on. Into my head popped the memory of running through Zangarmarsh in WoW:TBC.
I bought that album way back when the first WoW expansion came out and I must have played it a few times when grinding Zangar for the first time. So now the album gives me mental images of Zangarmarsh. Whenever I hear songs by that band I immediately think back to playing TBC.

Another example would be Gorillaz. There are a few songs from the first album which make me think of the old Lionhead game Black & White. I used to play B&W with Gorillaz in the background.

Finally, whenever I hear certain Dillinger Escape Plan songs I think of the original Dawn of War.

So, are there any songs which you now associate with particular games?
