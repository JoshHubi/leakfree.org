_rot_fodder June 26, 2019, 5:00am #645

First off, absolutely pleased with Xen, and certain spoiler free locations were astounding and had (in my opinion) some of the best horror atmosphere that I have seen in any game, possibly ever. Kind of along that vein, I think the HEV suit zombies are actually a great addition, and I have a single suggestion regarding them. I was exploring a certain zone when I heard one of their suits attempt to inform them that they needed to “seek medical attention”, which I thought was an excellent touch, save for the fact that it was very broken and garbled, which makes sense in it’s own right, but I think that they would be even more creepy if the HEV voice was clear of static, and spoke with less frequency. Something about having that message that you are so used to hearing in your own head, suddenly coming quietly from the darkest corner of the room has a level of horror implicit to it, that I think is somewhat undermined when it is clearly malfunctioning. Just my two cents.
