DansonDelta40 November 24, 2018, 5:56pm #194

How about Alyx and Gordon running straight into a trap on the Borealis, all set up by the combine overminds who had intentionally placed this manipulated message where they would find it in Episode 1? Both of them are captured, not killed, because they are known as idolized leaders of the rebellion and would become martyrs replaced by zealots fighting for what they died for. Instead, Alyx and Gordon are to be made into combine soldiers themselves. Somehow (by intervention of G-Man or the Vorties) Gordon is displaced before he can be transformed, and ends up on the Borealis again, where the only weapon he finds is the portal gun. Using this gun to navigate the maze of science decks on the ship and evading or even trapping enemies with it, he finds a way to destroy the whole ship, before the combine can use it to create another portal. In the course of this action he manages to rescue Mossman, who has a second gravgun prototype with her. Mossman tells him that Alyx has been sent to some kind of underground laboratories formerly occupied by aperture science, where the combine are headed to regroup and aquire technology that helps them reestablish contact with their homeworld.

While the self destruction sequence is going on, the two scientists manage to get a portal projection unit working, sending Mossman somewhere near a helipad, while Gordon, due to interference between the portal projector technology and the portable portal gun, is miswarped into some kind of strange underground laboratories formerly occupied by aperture science.

Using gravgun and portalgun, he has to solve a whole shitload of crazy physical and portal-projecting puzzles (I love alliterations!) to finally reach the control center, which has not yet been taken by the combine, because a long dormant computer AI known as GLADOS has managed to survive through all these decades. It turns out the AI is corrupt and partially malfunctioning, as if major parts were destroyed in some recent incident. Gordon has to repair the AI, because the defense systems of the underground facility are the only help he has to stop the combine and free Alyx from their grasp - if it is not already too late.
Puzzles involving finding machine parts, hacking computers (like in Fallout 3), using Portals and the gravity gun lead to the repair of GLADOS, who at first pretends to help Gordon - which leads up to him being reunited with Alyx, who is in some kind of stasis storage container that Gordon is only able to lift by using the gravity gun. GLADOS, however, is not willing to let the “Test Subject” go and starts flooding the base with a deadyl neurotoxin again. Okay, no problem… Gordon just has to get out of there before the air is unbreathable. He has to carry Alyx along, puzzle his way out (again mainly by using the gravgun and portal device), evade or trick out the automatic defense system which is at the same time fighting not only him, but also combine forces who are invading the basis on full scale to stop the AI and regain control over it. Gordon shoots and warps and physic-puzzles his way out of this mess, always having to carry Alyx along until in some test chamber, he manages to revive her, using aperture science technology. As Alyx is only wearing very unprotective underwear (hell, yeah!) they have to find some more protective clothing for her. Fortunately, they find the remains of a bunch of aperture scientists who had been trying to get into protection suits. One of those suits is still functional, and the helmet of the other one is compatible with Gordon’s hazard suit. So Gordon finally got himself a helmet, buying a little time for the escape. Just when the two have encounteres a bunch of dead combine soldiers, pillaging them for weapons and having to evade the turrets that killed them off, the GLADOS announcement system announces, that due to unauthorized access to the mainframe section the station is going to be flooded with radioactive waste on all levels, due to a security protocoll installed to prevent that the whole project falls into the wrong hands. Alyx and Gordon, both wearing slightly different hazard suits now, have to get to the surface before the levels they are on will also be flooded.
So they shoot, climb, jump, puzzle, portal and run their way up to the surface, while the areas they are escaping are slowly running full of radioactive, green, glowing slime and the GLADOS - AI is continuously harrassing the “intruders” over intercom, making jokes about their efforts being futile. Out in the open, they encounter the remains of what was the previous body of GLADOS, and a dead woman in a jumpsuit nearby. They are found by Dr Mossman and a helicopter crew from the resistance, who had been managing to track the signature of the portable portal device and thus found out about Gordon’s current location. The whole bunch is transported back to the resistance Headquarters in the newly freed urban area of city 14, which is littered with crashed alien helicopters and busted striders, while resistance members in the streets are burning piles of combine corpses and gathering their equipment.
In the newly setup headquarters, they encounter Dr Kleiner and the whole bunch of survivors, along with Eli Vance, who has been ressurected by the Vortigaunts and is currently in bed, unconscious and recovering from his near death experience. Everybody is happy, and Alyx is about to reveal her true, deep feelings for Gordon, when suddenly, something completely unexpected fucks up reality and freezes the scenery - that’s when the G-man walks in like he owns the place and regretfully announces to have to interrupt this intimate moment for a little heart-to-heart with Freeman… :freeman:
