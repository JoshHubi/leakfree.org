trevjamsey November 25, 2018, 12:30am #18

After Gordon left Eli and Kleiner they waited for a long time but he never came back with help. Eli, out of options left for a brief moment to see the severity of the damage. When he came back he saw Kleiner with Lamarr (who was only a babycrab at the time), he was about to kill it when Kleiner stopped him. Kleiner had befriended the headcrab along with the headcrab species as a whole. They proceeded to ride Lamarr’s Gonarch mother out of the facility. At one point they crossed a battle between HECU and Xenian creatures, they fought their way though but Eli lost his leg when a Bullsquid jumped out behind him and ripped it off. After getting medical attention Eli, Kleiner, Baby Lamarr, and Lamarr’s mom rode off into the sunset. During the events of HL2 the Combine captured Lamarr’s Mother for production of headcrabs as weapons.
