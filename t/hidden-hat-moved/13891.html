Ubertrout November 25, 2018, 4:38am #12

I just got this achievement. I’m not even sure why, but there it is. A few general thoughts…

As people noted, there were a few levels where you can leave the hat in one place early in the level and pick it up at the end of the level…these would be We’ve Got Hostiles, Blast Pit, Power Up, and Questionable Ethics.

On a Rail sucked…really annoying. And the hard part wasn’t even keeping the hat in the tram, although that wasn’t necessarily easy. It was getting up the ladders of the rocket during the part where you climb up 3 tiers and then close the doors and raise the rocket. Maybe I was doing something wrong, but the bottom of the floor I was climbing up a ladder to kept knocking the hat out of my hands. I ended up needing to throw the hat to the next tier and then retrieving it each time.

Another thing that really sucked was the part where the gargantua chases you in Surface Tension. If you’re holding the hat you can’t outrun him, so you keep taking damage (maybe you can put the hat down and go back? I didn’t think so but maybe I should try)

By contrast, I didn’t find the teleporters that hard…it was a pain to have to teleport the hat first and then retrieve it, but it seemed like a manageable thing to do, rather than the others, which felt poorly thought out.

Anyhow, fun achievement overall. I don’t know if it’s easier or harder than the gnome achievment, but it certainly takes longer. I’d advise everyone trying to play on easy, unless you really want a struggle.
