TheDragonMask November 25, 2018, 1:30am #238
Katana:

I gotta be honest - a lot of the media blackout is because of the intense desire for release. I only speak for myself and I think this makes sense, but we were pretty disappointed in ourselves for not making the 2009 release predicted in the trailer, and at least for myself, that level of hype just stresses myself out, and since Black Mesa isn’t my job, it makes development less fun. Since I don’t get paid for it, I’m not going to put time into development if it’s not fun.

As a result, we no longer say anything because - at least on my part - I want people to forget about the mod’s existence for a little while, enjoy everything else life has to offer, and then be pleasantly surprised when we suddenly say out of nowhere “All right, we’re ready!”
Simultaneously, we don’t want to put out something subpar just because we demanded a certain release date of ourselves.

Katana, one of the choreographers if I’m not mistaken, has been posting a lot in the “Ask the developers” thread.
