I like the quote in the signature…great stuff. The drawing was actually something I drew up while bored from scratch…just adding as I went. The pencil used was pretty dull…and I didn’t shade it. I spent under 50 minutes on it…as I still wanted it to be creative and not just random. My hands shake a lot as it is…and no straight edges were used if I can remember. Good starting idea to get me going though…there is a blotch on the crowbar.

I can 3D Model…just not very advanced. I am requiring no files sent to me whatsoever. Just wanted to see who would be interested in modeling it when and if I spend more time on it.

It is Half-Life related because it pertains to my machinima (Half-Life machinima). At first it was only a drawing…then when I listened to Metallica songs more and more…the creature slipped into the scenes that play in my mind when I listen to Metallica. Fight Fire with Fire is where it seems to really come into play as a daunting possibly fear inducing giant building crushing robotic combine machine. Thinking of naming it something as an idea or feeling. Like pain and agony…as it looks very tired and agonizing as it is.
