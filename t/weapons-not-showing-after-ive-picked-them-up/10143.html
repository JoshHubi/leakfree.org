Dr_Tub November 25, 2018, 2:21am #5

I had a similar issue with HL Source back in the day (well a day before BMS release). The way I fixed it was this.

Start a new game, reloading saves where its happened doesn’t work.
As the game starts, on the tram before you get anything type “r_drawviewmodel 1” in the console.
Once you get the crowbar it should show up. Along with the rest of the weapons.

Whether this will work for BMS IDK but its worth a try, I’ll tell you, its really hard to hit headcrab’s without the crowbar model.
