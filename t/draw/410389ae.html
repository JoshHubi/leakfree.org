I got a Wacom bamboo pen and touch. Touch functionality sucks, but sometimes it’s good to have because I can rotate the Photoshop canvas with it and more comfortably draw certain curves.

Disturbulator, I’d recommend the Monoprice tablets. They’re cheap as fuck (sixty dollars for a 10X6 pad!) and they feel better than Wacom’s stuff imo. The downside is the pen lacks pretty much all functionality except pressure and the two buttons. No tilt, rotation, or eraser on it. Also the driver installation is finicky as shit. Read the fine print before you touch anything.
