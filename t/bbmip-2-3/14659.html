TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:45am #16

BBMIP is “Brian’s Black Mesa Improvement Project”. It used to be for showcasing remodels that he made from the mod version --> retail, but recently he’s just been using it to show off models he’s working on, because it’s fun!

CU2 is “Content Update 2,” which was our internal codename for the Crossfire Update. It was released just before Christmas.

CU3 is the next update, and is our internal codename for what will likely be named the Surface Tension Update.

As to where they all are, they’re all right here - at the Black Mesa Research Facility!
