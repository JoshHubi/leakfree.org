Everything was going good up until the pistons. It took me well over 100 attempts to get past the pistons, 12 of them are recorded in the following youtube video.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Byc5SoZZEyk

The first set was bypassed easily, the second set, however, is extremely glitched. Whatever triggers that kill you are extremely out of sync and made it IMPOSSIBLE to pass, but the catch is, it goes out of sync over time. If you pull the level and spend a lot of time wandering around before going into the conveyor and approaching the pistons, the kill triggers go out of sync. After 100 or so failures (yes, literally), I decided to load back to the autosave. I pulled the lever and rushed through both sets, first attempt, with no problem. It only appears to be the second set that does this.
