And no not talking about silly skyrim.

Talking about the only racing game that will come out this year with boosts, jumps, wings, stunts, and tron inspired graphics. A FREE game created by a group of students at DigiPen called Nitronic Rush. It’s like the N64’s racing game rush2049 stuck inside of Jeff Bridges super computer. A fun quick play that feels like a proper remake to an 90’s arcade style racer.

Here is the release trailer. Once you manage to control yourself emotionally download it here. See if you can knock my name off the leaderboards.

No I’m not a part of the sales team, I’m just a HUGE rush fan and this student made homage to it is just fantastic. I Just want to share it with everyone I can. They created everything for this game including the graphics and physics engine and it plays and feels rock solid.
