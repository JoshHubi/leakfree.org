danielsangeo November 24, 2018, 8:34pm #127

Call me an old softie, but I like the story line in Half Life 2, Episode 2. I particularly enjoyed the scene in Our Mutual Friend when Gordon, Alyx and Eli were talking (after Alyx tells her father to “prepare for unforeseen consequences”) and Magnusson walks in and sarcastically complains about everyone sitting around.

For some reason, I really liked the character interaction in that scene. In fact, I liked most of the non-action scenes character interaction in the Half-Life series. The running, shooting and driving over zombie bits of Half-Life are great, don’t get me wrong, but the character bits are better than a hundred zombies getting hit by a buggy.

The Alyx-Gordon interaction at the beginning of Episode 1 was great, too, when you’re on the Stalker train.

The thing that really impressed me the most the first time I played the original Half-Life was the entire Resonance Cascade sequence. The panic I felt as the green apocalypse (to use a Ross Scott-ism) was ushered in. I remember panicking, trying to avoid the falling debris (I didn’t know if it would hurt me) and frantically looking for an exit, when suddenly the screen flashed green and cut to black, replaced only with the sound of breathing and a heart beating.

Sent chills down my spine.

I loved it and that’s what got me into Half Life.
