I’ve got a lot of favorite moments from this series…

The playground during the beginning of Half-Life 2.

When I walked over to that playground, hearing children laughing with no children around, only too see a broken and beaten doll on the ground, it got to me. The sad scene was pretty much telling me that “There are no children in this world anymore.” which is later heard by Dr.Breen, addressing the “Suppression Field.”

Probably one of my all time favorite moments in the series that really made me feel sad about the current world the people must have been living in…

Being brought to the top of The Citadel.

Just seeing the inner workings of The Combine was interesting and horrifying enough, but the view of the city, lit in the night was just beautiful.

I really liked this moment…

Emerging from the subway to find the city in ruins.

The first time I walked out of the subway, I just said to myself “Wow.”

Then looking at the Citadel made me want to leave the city as soon as possible.

The Hospital…

Do I need to say anything?

The ending of Half-Life 2: Episode 2.

It was…bitter sweet.

That’s all I’ll say.
