Zelfase November 24, 2018, 7:42pm #78

After thinking over Half Life, I managed to narrow down my two favorite chapters from it, and why I loved 'em.
I thought we should all try!
It doesn’t need to be two, by the way.

Here were mine, with 1 being the best

  1. Surface Tension
    First off, let me get this out.
    This is the longest damn’ level in the entire game. I don’t care if I’m wrong, to me it is.
    First you start off seemingly still early in the game, finally getting to the surface, the one place you need to be, thankful to be out of the underground hellhole that is Black Mesa, only to meet up with a heli going berserk on a dam.
    After going through soldier after soldier, mine after mine, alien after alien, three tanks, an osprey, three alien grunts, ANOTHER HELI, a tentacle seemingly out of nowhere, a shark, a pit full of trip mines and suicidal headcrabs ready to leap at you straight through the lasers, ready to blow the entire damn Black Mesa facility up, scaling a huge fucking cliff, ANOTHER HELI, and too many soldiers to count, you GO BACK UNDERGROUND (At which point I yelled out quite loudly: “OH YOU HAVE TO BE SHITTING ME”). Not in that order, though.
    It’s so fun, and is the most challenging level to me. (That’s right, Xen. You can’t hold three apaches to this pain bringer.)

  2. Residue Processing
    Hooray! After being nearly crushed by a Star Wars-esque garbage compacter, you only get rewarded for cheating death by getting thrown into Al Gore’s worst nightmare.
    I love this one because it’s really just one huge obstacle course. Hopping from conveyor belt to conveyor belt, narrowly avoiding being squished, melted, crushed, splattered, blown up, irradiated, falling to your death, burning, drowning, and falling to your death. Also in the beginning, the three rooms of toxic waste that almost seem like a carnival ride.
    “ALRIGHT, KIDDIES! DO YOU THINK YOU CAN HOP FROM ROCK TO ROCK TO GET TO THE OTHER SIDE OF THIS WASTE SPILL?”
    “HEY KIDS! DO YOU THINK YOU CAN KEEP YOUR COOL ON THESE HOT WASTE SQUISHERS? GET YOUR TIMING RIGHT, OR YOU MAY BE SPLATTERED YOURSELF!”
    “HEY KIDS WHO ARE NOW MUTATING INTO UNGODLY TYPE THINGS! TRY AND KEEP YOUR BALANCE, OR YOU JUST MIGHT GET ALL WE-MELTED! HYUK!”
    Good times.
