danielsangeo November 25, 2018, 1:45am #5

The scientist outside the transporter to Xen with the shotgun (with the guard) does not have an official name or official story. It’s just “a scientist with a shotgun”. Officially, the only scientist that is named (other than Freeman) post-HL1 and identified as canon is Eli Vance, who is the black scientist who lets you out of the test chamber and wants you to tell people that they are stranded down there. Unofficially, the scientist next to Eli is Kleiner and the guard banging on the security door during “Inbound” is Barney, and that’s how they’ll appear in BM.

All other scientists are unnamed (except for those that are identified ingame such as “Peters” who switched on the surgical unit and Smithers who is hiding on top of the power generator) and they don’t have backstories.

Beyond that, there could be fanfiction written about these characters…
