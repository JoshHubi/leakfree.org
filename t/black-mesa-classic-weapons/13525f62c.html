Hey, getting close to the new decade, let’s just do a big news dump!

First off: we managed to get Gunship Mark II, creator of MMod and tons of other smaller source projects, on-board as the FX artist. All this is just from his first day porting his old effects in and polishing them up, not sure if he’s planning on doing anything beyond these.

Additionally, I suppose with that YouTube video I may as well finally announce what I did in regards to sound. After months of searching and careful editing I’ve managed to find the HD sources to around 70% of the original weapon foley and gunfire sounds used in HL1. I’ve done loads of edits to them from their original 96-192 kilohertz Ultra-HD formats to bring them to the 44.1 khz Source engine standard.


Yes, you read that right, all sounds for the Crowbar, Glock, 357, Shotgun, Crossbow, and RPG, as well as the MP5 fire/reload and Tau Cannon charge loop have been found and subsequently remastered in HD. (I also found tons of ambient and foley effects, all of which I’m going to be documenting how to obtain when the pack is released, but those aren’t as exciting.)

And hey, that’s a big one that most people are wondering: When is the damn thing gonna be available?

I still can’t give a 100% concrete answer, given we learned from the masters themselves and would rather not make a Do-Or-Die deadline, but the current goal is by the end of January, given Game Zombie doesn’t have anything pop up between now and then.

Please keep in mind Game Zombie’s still got a lotta shit to fix, and a LOT of other projects he’s promised at least some time to. He’s focusing on this the heaviest, but he’s only human. After all, he still has shit like this to contend with:

https://i.imgur.com/xxS6kuq.gif(image larger than 4096KB)

Just so I don’t leave on a downer note, as a show of good faith that yes, I did indeed find the sounds and I’m not yanking your chains (and to tide a few of you over just a tad) here’s probably my favorite of all the sounds I touched up. Namely because it barely needed any touching up and was glorious fresh out of the box. Everyone’s favorite real-life mortar fire turned 12-gauge shot, sbarrel1:

https://drive.google.com/open?id=1BdDDseDrphqSLQgjF01lLsSDsAyu9MAh

That’s all for right now. See you all in 2020!
