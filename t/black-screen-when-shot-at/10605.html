lexa2 August 11, 2020, 9:29am #11

Videocard is most likely what causes your problem.
If is basically greatly stripped version of Radeon 9700 with next to none onboard memory available for texture storage (usually it comes with 32Mb VRAM which is hardly enough for framebuffer needs, so textures are stored into into main RAM instead and are accessed using AGP/PCIex GART techniques). You should try to follow the same advice I give here to another person with the videocard from that age:
https://forums.blackmesasource.com/showthread.php?p=496520#post496520

You should also took a look into FAQ Question 6 for details on how to check/switch dxlevels and you should also consider setting texture details and model details to “Low” to conserve as much system bus and memory bus bandwidth as possible.
