Years back, in an interview around the time of the Orange Box, a question came up about the Gearbox expansions, and they laughingly dismissed doing anything with Gina and Colette from Decay, but also said “We haven’t forgotten about Adrian. We might still do something with him at some point.” Pretty noncomittal, but also certainly not “Gearbox stuff is non-canon and we won’t use any of it.”

The guy has been on the boxart of several re-releases of Half-Life (compilations that included HL and Opfor, and then the next compilations that had blue shift, etc), so it would be pretty weird to consider him non-canon.

When I first got Half-Life, it was 2 years after it came out, and it was the first HL Generations Pack in the UK. The boxart was of Gordon and Adrian standing next to each other. And I played Half-Life and then I played Opfor and there was never any thought that Opfor was any less official. I’ve got two mates in the real world that played opfor but never got around to finishing original half-life. And for years Gearbox was an integral part of everything Half-Life - numerous HL patches added opfor stuff like opfor CTF, and they made the Dreamcast port, then they did Blue Shift and the HD pack, then they did the PS2 version, and for a while they were working on Condition Zero. If Condition Zero hadn’t gotten ballsed up Valve and Gearbox might have kept on working together for years.
