Maxey November 25, 2018, 3:57am #6

Sometimes things are just retconned without explanation tho. Either because it didn’t seem an important thing to focus at the time or because of technical limitations.

Valve didn’t focus on Vortigaunt communication very much on HL1, seeing as they were just a mid-tier enemy type in the context of that game but for HL2 they decided to make them allies and as such added more depth to them, which, among other changes/retcons, involved a more complex form of communication.

Going from weird high pitch screeches to a smoother, guttural and more elaborated form of vocalization was so big of a change that I don’t even buy the slave collar explanation.

It was a plain old retcon with no real explanation.

edit: as for how they learned english, that’s easier to explain. Enough time passed between the events of HL1 and HL2 that anyone could learn a new language, specially such intelligent creatures as vortigaunts.
