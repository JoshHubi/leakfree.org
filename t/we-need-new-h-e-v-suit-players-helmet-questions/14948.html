I think this has been said before, but the current H.E.V Suit playermodel is long overdue for a makeover. It’s rigging is kinda crappy and it still uses the old version of the suit.

What was the reason for changing it, and (in the next patch) could it be redone to look like the original?

TextFAMGUY1 November 25, 2018, 4:56am #2

We had intended to use the new HEV suit model as the MP playermodel back when Kevin made the new HEV suit (he even made an awesome helmet for it if I remember correctly), but we never got around to doing it because it was a huge amount of work and MP went pretty low down on our list of priorities as we ramped up work on Xen.

We are aware that the rigging on the suit in MP is kind of crappy and exhibits a lot of weirdness - it was a hack job if I remember correctly but there was no better way of doing it.

I’m imagining this is something we may tackle post-Xen (especially if we manage to get some coop action going). But we may also never get around to it, who knows. Hopefully this information is useful.
