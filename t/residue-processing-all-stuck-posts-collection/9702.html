dky.tehkingd.u November 25, 2018, 2:21am #15

If you backtrack to the first room with the single giant toxic tank, you’ll see that there is a gas fire burning above the tank that is blocking a vent opening. Go to the burning zombie room (you should know what I mean) and turn off the gas valve. Then backtrack and go into the vent that you just uncovered.

When you emerge, drop down to the area with the red light.
