In chapter Unforeseen Consequences (map bm_c1a1a at 2475x 2289y -59z), the area just after the control room and just beyond the door in question, the passage curves to the right and you encounter a flaming zombie crawling on the floor. I tried to reproduce the issue and the second (and third) time, the door closed on me again but did not clip through me. However, I am still stuck and need noclip to get out.
