Well you could try to pull a wire from your PC to the router, to see if it’s the wireless that is causing problems.
If you have another computer or laptop connected on your network, you could try transfering something in the LAN and see if it’s as fast as it should be. A ping to the router probably won’t tell you much.

Also, on most ISP website you can go check your bandwidth usage, if it’s a capping issue, you could see it there. And it won’t necessarily reset at midnight…
