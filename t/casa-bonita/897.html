Casa Bonita

Rossman August 11, 2020, 5:07am #1

Has anyone been to this magnificent and awesome restaurant? If you have please share your experience to it and enlighten others to go to it.

Every year I go to Casa Bonita with my family. The food there is nothing to be so excited about, it’s all about the entertainment. It is a place of Ah’s and wonders. Inside the restaurant they have the best entertainment. Included are flame jugglers, gold and silver mines, cliff divers, caves, strolling mariachis, an arcade, live shows, and a wonderful staff, PLUS an endless supply of their world famous sopaipillas.
In the upcoming years I will be moving to Denver for college and I’m going to apply to be one of the cliff divers.
