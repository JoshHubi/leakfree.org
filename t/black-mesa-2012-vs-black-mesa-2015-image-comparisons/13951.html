Deathmonkey7 August 11, 2020, 11:20am #11

Here are some weapon comparisons. I left out several that didn’t have any obvious changes to them.


The crowbar in Black Mesa 2015 is thinner than the 2012 version, Gordon’s hands are further down.


The Glock in 2015 has a wider sight in the back, a different skin, and is positioned a little closer to the center of the screen.


The MP5 has added side rails, some extra details near the stock, and a shorter barrel.


The rocket launcher got a total overhaul, with the scope now on the side (where it makes sense), much better details such as buttons, and a better looking screen.


The Tau gun also got a major overhaul, with almost nothing resembling the original. The C batteries were replaced with double A’s, the coil is no longer exposed near the front. The tubes in the front now have jigglebones, overall an awesome upgrade.
