Surplus November 24, 2018, 9:58pm #12

The bad controls and camera angles were a problem from limited tech. Just like any other classic game you must give it slack. In its day a full 3D environment wouldn’t have been detailed enough for a very immersive horror experience. The camera angles were due to being a 2D environment. And the tank controls were because playing the game with fixed camera angles without it would have been near impossible. IMO the point is moot after long enough you get used to the controls and its no longer a problem.
