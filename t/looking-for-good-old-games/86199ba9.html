Gregorovich November 25, 2018, 1:35am #43

I’d have to recommend the first two Fallout games. The sense of immersion is leagues beyond the more recent shooter-type Fallouts, and there’s no constant hand-holding. It may take a little while to get used to the combat system but believe me they are the types of games that you will not get bored of quickly. Playing the old Fallouts is like watching an ad for the first iPhone - even though the tech is completely outdated, you are still as blown away as the people were at the time.
