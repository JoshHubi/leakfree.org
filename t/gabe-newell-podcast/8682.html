The nearly hour-long podcast is live now if you’re interesting in hearing Newell talk through all of the details of how DOTA 2 will be free-to-play but different. He also chats quite a bit about their upcoming shooter Counter-Strike:Global Offensive, jokes (we think) about Ricochet 2 development and even answers some Half-Life questions.

Before you get too excited, the questions are more about narrative and character development across the series’ games. Literature geeks like us love this stuff, but if you’re just looking for Half-Life 3 details, don’t get your hopes up.
