Comrade Tiki:

[*]Beta-like tongue-dropping ambush-barnacles for Hard-mode (beta-style here)[/size]

What was the reason for the barnacle tongue-ambush idea not going in? I can’t recall - was it simply because it wasn’t in the original? That would be regrettable if it were true, but I can understand I suppose.

I might have mentioned this a long time ago, but if not - I’d prefer to see barnacles hoist their victims up but only close their mouths around a small part of them, perhaps a third or even half. This will avoid that awkward and ugly clipping that occurs when they try and eat a large enemy. The NPC - bullsquid, houndeye, human, vortigaunt etc. would hang there indefinitely, and all you could hear from them is a creepy sucking sound occasionally. I’m hoping this will play on people’s fears of being eaten alive, making the barnacles a far more haunting enemy, like the zombies.

The only other two alternatives are either:

a) Gib large enemies as Valve did in Episode 2 with the antlions, but this looks horrible in my opinion. It doesn’t seem as though the barnacles are eating anything.
b) Make the mouthparts much wider so that no clipping will occur, but this involves changing the barnacle model itself, perhaps to become larger and bigger - canon etc.

To my mind, leaving them dangling half-way in provides a good solution.

  • It effectively negates the threat of the barnacle, but players never really stay round long enough for the barnacle to finish its meal and to lower its tongue again. They either kill it or leave.
  • It would make them far more creepy in my opinion. Although it’s never shown, I imagine the barnacles to be almost like spiders in the way they feed. They use their mouthparts to kill their prey, but they lift the entire body inside the mouth, suggesting that they either have strong acid or they suck out the bodily juices. Since we can’t see any jaws inside, this makes sense.
  • It won’t affect canon.
  • It doesn’t make the game significantly easier. Since barnacles only eat NPCs, you can’t negate the threat of the barnacle easily. You’d have to bait an NPC towards the barnacle, and if you do that, you deserve to have the threat neutralised.
  • It should be easily modified.

Holy balls, long post for a short suggestion.
