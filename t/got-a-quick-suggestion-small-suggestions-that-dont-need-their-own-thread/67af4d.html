Alcator November 24, 2018, 5:21pm #82
LonePaladin:

Really, though — getting the suit for the first time seemed a little, I dunno, anticlimactic.

It might have something to do with

  1. Putting the HEV suit on is not a climax of the story.
  2. Back in 1998, all FPS games accepted the “walk over to pick” mechanism.
  3. Doing it like this was the only way how to allow Gordon full view freedom and movement freedom at the same time. If he were to actually dress into the suit, then the game would have to freeze him at the location; but if the game froze him in the location, it would have to force him to look down, otherwise the player wouldn’t see what’s going on (imagine looking straight forward and putting on your trousers – you don’t actually see them, right?), so for a pretty anticlimactic moment, you would be violating a fundamental game principle…
