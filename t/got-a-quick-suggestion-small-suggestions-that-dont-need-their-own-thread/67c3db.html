Although the M203 fires a 40mm grenade it is not recommended to try using a 40mm grenade that is designed to be fired from a Mk19(belt fed, automatic 40mm grenade launcher which can only be fired if mounted on a tripod or mounted on a vehicle). The Mk19 grenades fire at a higher velocity than the M203 grenades. If an individual(aka idiot) tries to fire a Mk19 grenade out of an M203 launcher it will create over pressure and turn his/her M203 into shrapnel causing horrendous injuries if not death.
