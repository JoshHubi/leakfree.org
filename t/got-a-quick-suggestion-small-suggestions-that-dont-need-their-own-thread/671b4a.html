Ibanez August 11, 2020, 2:21am #2578
nietzschesaurus:

because getting smashed constantly by the tentacle that’s poking its giant head around in that tiny room would be frustrating, and it adds nothing to gameplay.

I disagree. I think it would add to the feeling of having nowhere safe to be within the silo. The tentacle was practically a joke, and was less of a boss and more of a puzzle. It needs to be made more threatening. I think the area with the explosives should be made accessible by the tentacle, as well as the control room. This would mean the only safe places Gordon can go are in that passage between the control room and the explosives room, and outside the silo. Another cool thing to have would be if you fire a weapon outside the silo, huge dents appear on the wall to simulate the tentacle banging the side of the walls to get at the noise. These things would really make the tentacle feel like more of a threat. And, obviously, the player is safe in the control room, explosives room, and outside the silo to walk around and would only attract the tentacle if they fire their weapon.

[EDIT: Actually, I’m expanding on this. In Mirror’s Edge, if you run into an elevator and guards are shooting at you, then little dents appear on the inside of the door. I think that this is pretty much just like the kind of thing that should appear on the outside of the silo wall when you are walking around these hallways outside the silo, and if you fire your weapon, then you could hear the bang of that tentacle slamming against the wall and dents similar to the ones linked above should appear on the wall. It’d be a really neat touch, and probably scare the shit out of anyone. The tentacle wouldn’t even need to be animated, since the player can’t see it at that point.]
