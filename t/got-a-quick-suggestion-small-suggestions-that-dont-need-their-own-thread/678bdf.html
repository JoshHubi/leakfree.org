The_Javid November 24, 2018, 9:07pm #4603

If you want my semi expert opinion…(which you probably don’t) SMG’s are basically short assualt rifles that usually fire the same ammunition you would find in pistols. Pistols are much harder to be accurate with than shoulder fired weapons because you can’t hold them tightly into your shoulder to reduce muscle twitch, muzzle jump and recoil. Pistols are usually light in comparison and their firing mechanisms tend to increase recoil(due to the fact that they eject spent casings, cock the hammer, and chamber the next round in the magazine using the recoil of the previous round. SMG’s are much more accurate, have higher rates of fire, less recoil, higher magazine capacities and tend to have longer ranges(because they typically have longer barrels). Pistols are usually used as a back up to a long gun(assault rifle, shotgun, SMG, or machinegun). Most pistols have a maximum effective range of 50 yards and you practically have to do everything perfect to hit a target at that range. A target at 25 yards is considered long range for a pistol. Not argueing for or against either party, just putting the info out there.
