I’ve read the countless “too much realism sux” debates. And I know that many people could be pissed, but if it is not too much work (I know it is), some suggestions:

  1. Reloading the Cobra when not all shots were fired should show Gordon’s hand closing the chambers which were not depleted with his thumb/hand and shaking the emptied cartridges out (like I believe is done by many not depleting his/her revolver). OK, that requires a new animation system reading the number of shots fired and “manually” reloading 4-5 chambers. But at least the “shaking out” could be done by not showing the animation fully, but just hinting this reload animation by showing Gordon press his hand against the drum half off screen.

  2. Every collected magazine (MP, pistol) gets an ID, with a attached attribute remembering the bullet count. So say a magazine picked up at some point and not emptied is stored, but when its loaded it has the saved amount of bullets. That would mean you can’t use the pistol magazine ammo and the MP Ammo interchangeabely. Emptied magazines are lost. I dont think gordon is carrying them around anyway. And max Mp magazine size is 30 bullets. Crowssbow as above.

  3. Quicker grenade throw and fall speed (but havoc should solve that).

  4. Rifle grenades reloading animation.

  5. Weaker booby-trap (giggle) ray/not visible ray, maybe under misty/smoky/dark conditions visible . Seems FUBAR every marine just runs through that ray considering they brought them to black mesa/use them.

Hope at least some of these suggestions were new and the post isn’t useless.
