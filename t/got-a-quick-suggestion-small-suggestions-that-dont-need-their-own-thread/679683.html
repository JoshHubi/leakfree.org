The cafeterias:

I hated the fact, that the HL1 cafeterias never contained any kind of food. There was absolutely nothing (except for Magnussons lunch in the microwave)!

I think it would make sense to have at least a little fridge (in the smaller cafeteria in Sector C), which contains things like a banana, a sandwich, a yoghurt pot and tupperware.

The bigger canteens should have some pots with standard canteen food (fries, cutlet, etc.) and a few plates and flatware on tables with some remaining food on it.

It would also make sense to add food lying on the tables and put a overthrown plate on the floor to let it look more like a mess.

Furniture:

  • Security offices should contain gun racks and firearm lockers.

  • I was very confused about the fact, that the offices in HL1 never had copying machines and printers (apart from the fact, that there were absolutely no PCs in Office Complex). They should be in!

  • I also would like to see water coolers in the office complex (because bigger offices simply have them!)

  • Fire Extinguishers, which are shootable would be nice!

That was all!

I hope it isn’t too much work.
