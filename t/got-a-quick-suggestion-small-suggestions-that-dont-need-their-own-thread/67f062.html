Torpede:

Also, I know the FAQ mentioned Magnusson is not going to be in BM, but that room in anomalous materials, the small cafeteria, where Gordon is able to blow up a microwave casserole by overheating it. In Episode 2, we learn THAT scientist who cried out, “My God, what are you doing?” IS Magnusson, fellas. Why isn’t he going to be in the mod for that one scene?

Because nowhere in Episode Two does it specify that the incident in question was the same as the one on the day of the resonance cascade. The player is not required to blow up the casserole, and the player is Gordon, so there is a possibility that Gordon did not meet Magnusson in that room and/or blow up his casserole on that day. Alternatively, let’s assume it was his casserole on the day of the resonance cascade. Just because it was Magnusson’s casserole doesn’t mean that Magnusson was in the room at the time. For all we know he might be the guy in the bathroom. Now if the devs put him into that room they run a risk. What if in Episode Three valve says that at the time of the resonance cascade that Magnusson was somewhere else? That creates a plot hole, where as leaving him out does not. Basically the devs are covering their backs just in case something like that happens.
