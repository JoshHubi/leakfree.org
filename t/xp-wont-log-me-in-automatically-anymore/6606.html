sersoft November 24, 2018, 11:35pm #3

disable fast user switching and welcome screen (in control panel>users>change the way users log on etc) then download tweakUI (google powertoys for win xp) and in logon tab select autologon, don’t forget to type in your password, also you can copy your user settings (window color, mouse, wallpaper) to the login screen account for faster loading times
