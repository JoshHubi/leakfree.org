Escape from Black Mesa - Single Player Mod

General Development Source 1

Note: Everything below is super WIP and subject to change.

Okay, so I’ve been working on this for a few weeks now, certain other people have already seen some of the progress, and I thought I would just go ahead an post it up here so I can post updates for the public to see on a regular basis.

This mod is a single player mod that gives you, the players, a whole new story to play through and a chance to see areas of the Black Mesa Research Facility that have never been seen before.

Story So Far…[/SIZE]
You are Dr. Walter Bennett, a biophysicist in the Advanced Biological Research Lab. You wake to your alarm in your dormitory after having a strange dream about a man in a suit. After exploring the dormitories and greeting your colleagues, you board the tram to the research labs. On your way, however, there is a deep rumble from somewhere in the facility, suddenly strange yet familiar creatures begin appearing out of thin air all around you, the ceiling crumbles taking you inside the tram with it. After miraculously surviving the fall you must now find an HEV suit and escape the Black Mesa Research Facility or die.

Major Obstacles to Overcome[/SIZE]

  • Voices and Choreography - I’m not anywhere near a stage where this is important yet, but it will be a must in the future.

Mediaz[/SIZE]
Screenshots are of a super pre-alpha stage, everything is subject to change.

Now to be realistic, this will probably take me a long time to complete. There’s no guarantees that I will complete this since I’m just doing this in my spare time, and if it is completed I can’t guarantee it will be good. So set sail for disappointment!

As a last note: If anyone has experience prop modelling for Source and you want to help, send me a PM with some examples.
