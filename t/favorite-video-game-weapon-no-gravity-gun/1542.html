Comrade_Tiki August 11, 2020, 1:45am #14

Hornet Tank’s rockets from Battletanx 2
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n5mirNfBMQg
(ignore the fact the player is using an invulnerability cheat)[/size]

Great rate of fire, great damage. Rockets start separate, curve toward oneanother, cross paths, then go their separate ways.

This makes aiming really fun.
They cross at an ideal range for taking out individual tanks before they have a chance to kill you. If you know a target is nearly dead, you can hit its side and let one rocket kill it, while the other rocket pass it up to maybe hit someone else. If a target is far away, you need to compensate intelligently for one of the rockets to hit them.

The video doesn’t show it, but you can sometimes avoid repositioning your turret by using a “hook shot” where the rockets will curve up to 90 degrees through the air. This is also awesome for hitting tanks around corners.
