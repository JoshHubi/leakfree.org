I could just not believe my eyes when nobody talked about this. This mod might not be about gunplay like HL2, but instead is like a happier version of Dear Esther.

You play as a man named Stanley, who worked for a large company where he was employee 427. Others might have found this job soul crushingly boring, however Stanley loved his job, until one day, when the most peculiar thing happened, he had been at work for nearly an hour, until he had noticed, that nobody else had shown up for work.

The rest you will have to find out in this Epic Saga of Stanley
