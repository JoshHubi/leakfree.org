The engineer update definitely improves the offensive and front-line-defensive capabilities. Upgrade SGs and dispensers in front of spawn. Carry upgraded dispenser to safe spot near front line, go back and carry SG to front line, than move dispenser up. Put the dispenser in a spot behind the SG to heal you while you can still repair the two. All you need is a good pyro for SG defense. Once one SG is established, have another engineer repeat the same process and back up your SG with his.

For instance, establishing multiple SGs on the bridge in doublecross, with pyros helping with defense, is a great strategy to win. rorriMAesU November 24, 2018, 9:54pm #89
Orpheon:

Indeed, but what Katana is saying is still true…

I know, I never said he was incorrect. The thing I believe people may not realize is that the wrangler update simply allows engineers to put their SG in spots that used to be suicide. The wrangler does not effect the classic, normal SG spots that have been used for years.

For instance, intel guarding on 2fort and doublecross do not need the wrangler. Those are both small rooms with multiple entrances, definitely best to let the SG sort it out. Now however, consider the 2fort sewers. Before the update it was stupid to put a SG at the foot of the stairs in the sewers, the tunnel is waaay too long and out of SG range. The wrangler however, changes that.

Basically, the wrangler allows an SG to guard choke points over long distances, distances that are normally out of range. When using the wrangler you have to be sure to put your SG in a safer spot with less access. The engineer has to guard all vulnerable positions manually. Like Katana said, engineers are doing the defense, not the sentry.
