Underwater zoom-in bug

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
Dotard March 10, 2020, 5:12pm #1

Whenever I enter water, the game zooms in incredibly far, making it impossible to navigate underwater. It’s like the zoom with Z button multiplied by 5 and the only fix is to leave the water…

Dotard March 16, 2020, 10:51am #2

EDIT: I’ve managed to find the solution! If anyone ever finds themselves having a zoom-in while underwater, disable ‘image sharpening’ in Nvidia control panel, does wonders.
