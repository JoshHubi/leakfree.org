Hey there, Black Mesa forum members. There have been two renditions of the MP5 submachine gun, the first is a MP5A5 with a GP-30 grenade launcher, EOTech Holographic sight and MP5K PDW-style side-folding stock and the second is a MP5A3 with a M203 grenade launcher and flash suppressor. Which do you prefer? Or, alternatively, do you dislike both MP5 models and instead favour the M4 Carbine? Discuss.

Personally, I prefer the latest version of the MP5 because I find it more aesthetically pleasing. The side-folding stock on the MP5A5 looked weird and unnatural, while the EOTech Holographic sight was really just there for eye candy and had no useful function. The only thing I liked about it was the GP-30 grenade launcher, because it seemed to “fit” better than the bulkier M203.
