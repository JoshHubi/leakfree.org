DODcrazy November 25, 2018, 12:06am #92

After sifting through this thread I’ve realized that nobody here knows anything about firearms besides what they saw on call of duty and future weapons, so here’s the deal. There is a HUGE difference between a submachine gun (UMP, mp5, p90) and an assault rifle. Submachine guns use pistol ammunition, whereas rifles use…rifle ammunition. The difference in power and range is immense, and the two just seem to be far to interchanged. The need for submachine guns has been mitigated in recent years but the compactness of modern assault rifles, and the need for a more powerful round as pistol ammunition is usually easy to stop with just a kevlar vest, not to mention a sapi plate.

As far as what the Marines should use, it’s either gonna be an mp5a4, an m16a4, or an m4. There wouldn’t be any silly grenade launcher on the mp5 and assuming these are still at least somewhat like actual marines, they would have a mix of different weaponry in each squad. The shotgun should be changed to a benelli 1014 or remington 870, the pistol should be an m9 or 1911 meu (maybe both).

As far as what the Marines wear (slightly off topic), it was never right in the first place, and still isn’t. I would be really happy if they got rid of the berets (the marines are better than silly french hats). I’m happy that the dev’s at least understand what MOLLE gear is to some extent (even though they don’t even have any pouches on their rigs lmao) but the rest made me facepalm at half life 1 and it makes me facepalm today. The camouflage pattern is also ridiculous and never made any sense; they would be better off putting the marines in sage green nomex flight suits or MARPAT camouflage, since that’s what they actually wear for such an environment.

At the end of the day though this is a sci fi game so there’s no denying that. I suppose it’s just as well to protect the image of real marines because the original game made a tremendous mockery of real marines and played on the ridiculous stereotype of marines being stupid brutes, but such was the trend of 90s movies and games.
