Wrong achievement being displayed in top right hand corner (Nuclear Fishin)

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
AsinoEsel November 25, 2018, 4:51am #1

I can’t really screenshot the issue since I can’t really reproduce it, but it’s easy to explain:
When I unlocked the achievement Nuclear Fishin by killing the ichthyosaurs in the dam section of ST with the tau cannon, I got the Steam achievement, BUT instead in the top right hand corner (on the ingame-achievement-display-thingy) it displayed “Universal Gravitation (1/1)” - the achievement you get by getting killed by one of your own snarks.
This MAY have something to do with the fact that the achievement I unlocked prior to Nuclear Fishin was indeed Universal Gravitation, that a coincidence?!
Anyway, it’s a really small glitch and not that important, but whatever… still counts I suppose.

EDIT: Okay, it’s an issue with Achievements in general. Just minutes later I got the achievement “Unified Coupling Theorem” when a headcrab got eaten by a barnacle (or… coupled… with it I suppose), but it instead displayed “Nuclear Fishin’ (1/1)” in the top right hand corner. So apparently it simply displays an achievement you unlocked previously instead of the correct one. Is that only an issue for me or have others experienced this as well? It’s pretty strange.
