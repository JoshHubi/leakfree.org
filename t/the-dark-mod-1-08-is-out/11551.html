The Dark Mod, a Doom 3 mod that is heavily inspired by the Thief series, released a very impressive patch today!

https://www.moddb.com/mods/the-dark-mod

I’m pretty new to the thief series myself, been playing the first one recently. I was thinking to myself while playing it the other night, “this needs a BM style remake”, and stumbled across this while reading Rock Paper Shotgun. While it’s not a 1:1 remake of Thief due to copyright issues, the gameplay and setting are very, very similar. They’re working on a standalone version that won’t require Doom 3, but if you do own D3 and enjoy stealth games, check this one out!
