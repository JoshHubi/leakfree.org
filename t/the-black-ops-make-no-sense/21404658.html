The job of the HECU was to regain control of the facility, remove critical personnel, and generally contain the alien incursion. They were after control. Enter the Black Ops. The theory was the facility would be erased, any witnesses would easily be written off as missing or never having existed in the first place, and the government or whoever could easily wash their hands of the entire matter.

Black Ops: Covert operatives deployed after the failed military assault. According to Opposing Force they were there to deliver a nuclear weapon to destroy the facility.

Xen Crystal: A sample taken by expeditionary teams during their adventures in the Borderworld. Eventually it was delivered to the Anomolous Materials lab for testing.

Retinal Scanners: Gordon is a low level research associate, it makes sense that a lot of areas are restricted to him.

Snarks: While it is still unclear what exactly they are for it can be theorised that they are just another Xen animal teleported to Earth by accident. Gordon seems to find them useful as weapons however.

7 Hour War: Brief period in which Combine forces arrived on Earth and neutralised all opposition leading to total surrender in 7 hours.

Black Mesa Nuclear testing: There is none. Black Mesa used to be a missile silo, all the rockets you see there are in storage from the 60s and are being disarmed. The only operating launch pad in the facility is for satellite rockets.
