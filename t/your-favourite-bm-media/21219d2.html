Isn’t prohibited to upload pics or vids in the wiki (except in the case of HL1 pics or medias not related to the mod). The problem is, as you pointed, the “Xen pictures (that are secret) or stolen material (hacked)”, that are 95% of the pics that you can find on the internetz. If you upload it without some cautions, the devs will simply see and delete them.

But the real problem here is the frenzy of the community when they see those images… You know, people complaining about a WIP thing, bitching or moaning to change some detail (that probably was already improved), bad press, noobs making stupid guesses about release dates etc. Those images doesn’t reflect the real state of development or the quality of the mod, a thing that the devs are always concerned. So, if you really want to share what you think is “an exclusive media”, try to check the source first.
