Obama_Yo_Momma November 25, 2018, 2:33am #12

Yeah there are a couple reasons why we don’t want to use the assets. The main ones being that if we were to use the black mesa assets then we would basically just be replacing a lot of stuff we already have, taking black mesa’s hard and amazing work as our own is one. Another is that most people on the team are not only making OBM or Guard duty for your enjoyment but also for portfolio pieces. So just using Black Mesa’s stuff would be counter productive to the whole making things on our own deal. But we are doing our best to mimic certain areas and assets used in areas that the player sees in Black Mesa so that there is still a sense of continuity. The dam for example, as of right now the outlying building that is on the left side of the dam in Black Mesa isn’t anything like the one we have so that will most likely be changed soon.
