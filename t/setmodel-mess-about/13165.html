Not really.
The player model framework requires player-specific action markers to be set in the animations, which none of the NPC models have, at least at this time.
Thirdperson hasn’t every been fully supported in Half-Life games, but your best bet if you really want it in Black Mesa is to wait until Deathmatch comes out, and use one of the models from it.
