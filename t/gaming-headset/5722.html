Kil3r August 11, 2020, 5:26am #2

This thingy mabob right here: https://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16826265065
I have been using it for 1 - 4 months and so far it has actually WORKED.

The mic catches background sound fairly easily BUT that may not pose a problem if it does not matter to you. The actual quality of the sound from the mic is good and should not have any hissing\ect.

The sound quality of the speakers are just good. It does not support all those super duper dolby digital surround sound modes, so if your really looking for a step up from a normal stereo headset, this headset may not be for you and you’ll probably have to step up the price too.

It is a closed ear headset, and a comfortable one at that. I’ve played entire days without any pain.(If you do end up buying this one, just feel the cloth that is wrapped around the headphones, you will be amazed)

I’ve been using 15-20$ headsets since I got my newest computer and all of them have only lasted a month or so. This headset was a really nice purchase.

Good luck on finding a headset.
