Maxey November 24, 2018, 10:02pm #25

In my reboot, the setting would move to Europe, more specifically in Switzerland. The main character’s name would be Jacques Villeneu and would be a scientist at CERN, working on the Large Hadron Collider. The experiment goes wrong and a portal from another dimension opens right in front of your eyes. From there, an army of advanced aliens invade earth through the portal.

Your immediate mission is to lockdown the facility and find a way out to the surface. However, right before you reach the surface, a portal opens up on you and you get sucked in into their dimension, where you find out that the aliens planned to invade earth a long time ago but just didn’t have the means to do so, until the LHC experiment occurred. A lot of technobabble is used to explain how they did it.

Now you have to find a way to shut down the portal from their dimension, at the cost of you getting stuck in there.

At the end of the game, right before you close your eyes for the last time, a mysterious figure appears. He tells you did a good job and has more work for you in the future.

In this reboot, a fire axe replaces the crowbar and Jacques sports a mustache (he’s french) and an experimental force field around his body instead of a HEV suit.
