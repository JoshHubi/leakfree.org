Making a mod is a huge task, as you might notice that BMS has been in development for a while.
Before you get to the fun part such as fleshing out the storyline, setting and characters, you need to study, study and then do more study on how to use SDK.
Untill then it is mostly pointless trying to do anything else.
Just write down your creative ideas somewhere and read them again when you have learnt how to re create them in game.

Also you cannot expect everyone to jump in and help you in your mod when you have nothing to show, and little experience.

Good luck
