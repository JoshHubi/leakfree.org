Paddy:

You might be better off making a multiplayer mod, and focusing on the gameplay mechanics. Without a full team, your going to be limiting your self heavily when it comes to single player.

Actually, MP is more difficult to mod for than SP- since you have multiplayer stuff, that means you’d have to balance the game’s weapons and maps and God knows what else. With SP you have more control over the player, where he goes, and what he can see. Plus, NPCs are the primary opposition to the player, so the mapper has a greater degree of control over the flow of combat encounters.
