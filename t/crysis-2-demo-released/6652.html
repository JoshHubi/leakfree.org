+cl_fov=80 +pl_movement.power_sprint_targetFov=80 +r_MotionBlur=0 +g_skipIntro=1 +r_glow=0 +i_mouse_smooth=0 +r_PostMSAA=0

+cl_fov XX (XX = number; Field of View)
+r_glow 0 (deactivates bloomy sky - standard: 1)
+i_mouse_accel 0 (deactivates mouse acceleration - standard: 1)
+g_SkipIntro 1 (skips intro and logos - standard: 0)
+r_motionblur 0 (deactivates motion blur - standard: 1)
+cl_crouchToggle 0 (deactivates crouch toggle (press and hold to crouch) - standard 1)
+cl_zoomToggle 0 (deactivates zoom toogle (press and hold mouse button to zoom) - standard: 1)
+pl_movement.power_sprint_targetFov 65 (deactivates zooming while sprint )
