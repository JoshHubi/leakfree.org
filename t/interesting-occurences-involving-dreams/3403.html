First one. Couple days ago I was eating some chicken late at night, I was pretty worried it might have been undercooked. Ate it anyway, figure if I got sick whatever, it tasted good.

Anyways the next morning my mom called me asking if I was ok, she said she had a dream I came to her in the middle of the night clutching my stomach and feeling ill.

Second one. When I was 8 or 9 I had a dream I went to my church to see a magic show. Saw the whole thing in my dream, all his tricks, it was like I was really there.

The next day I find out I’m going to a magic show at my church that weekend. Get there, it is EXACTLY the same as the dream. Everything he did, the spot I was sitting, everything was the same. I knew what he was going to do before he started. Blew my mind at the time, it’s still fucking crazy to think about.

I also have this weird dream involving a massive grey space, think the white room in The Matrix where they go to get guns. Off to my side a bit is this large cube of particles all swirling around, it looks like TV static. They are all black and white. Then way off in the distance my dad is standing there and a stream of these particles are flowing out of the cube towards my dad.

I get the ‘feeling’ of the number one million during this dream. Also everything feels incredibly detached, it’s one of the hardest feelings to describe. It’s like I’m in my body but out of it at the same time, everything feels incredibly distant and detached. I get this feeling when I’m awake now and then, don’t know what triggers it. It’s very sudden and doesn’t usually last long.

It’s really odd and I’ve been having this dream repeatedly since I was a little kid.

I dreamed that I was dead but when I woke up I was still alive.

Seriously though, when I was a kid, as early as I could remember, I was ALWAYS having that kind of creepy dream, I was in a strange world made of infinite metal beams, like a sky scraper skeleton, with grey clouds everywhere and nothing at all going on, I kept wandering around aimlessly, thinking about life. Sometimes I thought to myself, “where am I going?” I told myself I’m just looking for the elevator, I’ve heard about people who had the same dream twice, I’ve heard about people who had the same dream over and over, but my dreams were all different, just roughly in the same location. You won’t belive it, that shit went on for at least 10 years, but after a while when I was about 15, I felt I was getting close to the elevator, I could smell it. One day found the son of a bitch, it was nothing special, cramped so that only I could fit, also made of metal, I remember pushing a red button and hearing the slight hum of the motorized elevator going down.

After that day, I never had those dreams, ever again.
