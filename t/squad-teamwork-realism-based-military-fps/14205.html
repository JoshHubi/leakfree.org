Squad (teamwork & realism based military FPS)

Technology and Gaming
Wolfguard August 11, 2020, 11:22am #1

Squad is a realistic open sandbox military FPS with heavy focus on teamwork and balanced realism.
It’s the spiritual successor to the hugely popular and acclaimed Battlefield 2 modification: Project Reality.

Squad will feature large scale battles with 100 players if not more, base building, huge and varied levels
(with environments like urban, desert, forests, etc.), several infantry classes, light and heavy armored
vehicles, aircrafts, watercrafts and multiple factions, including (if stretch goals are reached): the Russian
and British Armed Forces.

The game is being built with the Unreal Engine 4 and is still in pre-alpha stage and therefore still has a long
way to go. Despite of this there are already several gameplay videos out there portraying, although WIP,
a beautiful functioning game which has already been successfully Kickstarted.

Now with almost $300.000, over 3.000 backers and a little over a week to go, we just need the help to
reach the rest of the stretch goals
