Frozen Pina Colada \ 4 Quarts Party size!

Mix and freeze in a large container or old ice cream bucket:

  • 1 large 46 oz can of pineapple juice
  • 1 13.5 oz can of coconut milk
  • 1 12 oz frozen concentrate lemonade
  • 3 cups of rum

When it comes time to make the drink, scoop and add a bit of Squirt or Sierra Mist soda

~or~

Put it in the blender with a banana first before adding the soda.

Purple rain Cocktail

Version 1:

  • 1/2 shot grenadine
  • 1/2 shot peach schnapps (archers)
  • 1/2 shot white rum
  • 3 count lemonade
  • 3 count sweet & sour mix
  • 1/2 shot Blue Curacao

Version 2:

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • cranberry juice
  • 1 splash Blue Curacao liqueur
  • 1 splash sweet and sour mix
  • 1 splash soda water

Version 3:

  • 1 ounce Blue Curaçao
  • 1 ounce Coconut Rum
  • 1 ounce Vodka
  • 1 splash Grenadine

Shake blue curacao, coconut rum and vodka with moderate ice. Strain into shot glass and add grenadine. Stir and serve. If correctly mixed, drink should have an electric blue/purplish color.
