Grief_Reaper November 25, 2018, 12:39am #2

Well from what I’ve learned from fo3 and Nv is that the unofficial patches will not work with a majority of newer mods.

A few mods to try:
Stutter remover, any 4gb extender, coloured map, map markers, DarNified UI and Qarls Texture Pack.

If you really want to mod the game get used to the Construction set then you can edit your pc or other npc’s the way you want them to look, hair, clothes the weapons they use blah blah. Its time consuming and requires merged patches with the other mod’s you want to use but it’s worth it.
