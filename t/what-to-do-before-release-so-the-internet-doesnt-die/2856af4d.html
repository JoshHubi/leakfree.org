That’s what superseeding is for. Each piece only gets uploaded once by the superseed, and separate pieces go to as many different peers as possible, so you get a full copy distributed throughout the cluster more quickly, and people are forced to stay around to get the whole thing (thus meaning they have their pieces seeded for longer)

Problem with torrents is its harder to see how many distributed copies there are in the wild…
