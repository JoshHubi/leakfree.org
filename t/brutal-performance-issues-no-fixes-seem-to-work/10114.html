Integrated Intel GMA HD is your problem here. TF2 and LoL are specially optimized to be as low demanding on GPU as possible so more players would be able to play these games. You should really try something like HL2:EP2 or Portal 2 to check if your GPU is a bottleneck (which I bet it is). Also, keep in mind that BMS isn’t as perfectly optimized as are full-blown commercial games from Valve or GearBox. It’s a “at free time”-developed mod after all.
