This is a thread for it. To remember how much this series is awesome.

Because they were sandboxes; You could do what you want, where you want, how you want. They say you should take a shotgun and a car to go take down a dealer from another gang or something, why not take a fuckin’ chopper, do a couple of stuns then go blew the motherfucker with a rocket launcher and a flamethrower?

Because of they had very good stories; You really felt (at least I did) attached to the characters, you believed in them. I was shocked when I had to kill Luigi in GTA III, or when you saw Ryder and Big Smoke tradin’ with Tenpenny in GTA San Andreas. There was a lots of action, suspense, everything a good game, or even a movie needs! I don’t know who is the writer for the series, but if he ever writes a movie in the style of GTA III or IV, I will make him passionate sex.

Because of its simplicity; you only had to take the controller and you could do whatever you want. Drive a car, a boat, an hellicopter, a tank, an ATV, a plane, a dodo, E-V-E-R-Y-T-H-I-N-G without even looking in the Control Options. Even if you never had a controller in your hands before, 10 minutes latter you we’re stealin’ Mercedes and Cessnas.

Unfortunately I only ever played to GTA III, Vice City and San Andreas and a bit of the IV which was far too heavy for my comp, but I only own GTA III for the PS2. The others are pirated copies for PC. I will probably get VC/LC stories, as well a VC/SA for the PS2 off ebay if I get the job.
