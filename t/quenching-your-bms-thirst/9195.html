Like Goldeneye Source, Come to think of it its a little disappointing how many (Total Conversion) mods dont make a release and it upsets me because it’s really the aspect of PC gaming i love to see. I think most people on sites like MODDB run before they can walk, they have the enthusiasm and talk about how amazing their mod/ideas will be, often get started, do some artwork which is sometimes good, then its silent and hear nothing about it for ages, then its status turns to ‘dead’.

It’s a shame, its such a demanding industry the only outcomes are the ones that have investment behind them, the bar is so high for realism and wide platforms it often cuts out a lot of the interesting and fun experimental stuff.

Like that game, The Unfinished Swan its probably gets a little repetitive and boring, but its probably built on abstract concept that managed to get funding and turned into a real game.
