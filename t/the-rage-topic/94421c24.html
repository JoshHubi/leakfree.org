Tiki November 25, 2018, 4:24am #3146

The scrunch may have damaged your alternator (= no more battery charging) or given you a small ground (= slowly discharging your battery).

The steps to check for a ground would be to use an ohmmeter between your vehicle’s terminals, with the battery removed and automobile off (a de-energized condition). See if a replacement battery maintains its voltage over time if you have a voltmeter. Car part places will usually check your alternator for free so they can tell you if you need a new one and you can buy it from them-- but make sure the problem isn’t a loose battery terminal first or they will misdiagnose the issue.
