APB was a tactical third person shooter. SERIOUS emphasiss on tactical. Chokepoints, bluff tactics, flanking (for real, COD and counterstrike do not count) Etc. They had tried headshots in an earlier build, and it just degenerated into a twitchfest. Also, player customization was so high that you could even change your height/weight/muscles/etc. Stuff that either made you a big person or a little person. In APB, that didn’t matter. One hitbox, and a a bright red name above your head meant that no matter your size, you were just as hittable and visible as the next guy. Introducing headshots would mean either changing hitboxes to match size, which would mean no more sane people over four and a half feet tall, or they would have to remove height customization.
