I was messing around in FL Studio one day and made a cool sounding song for once[/SIZE], and I thought “God I wish I had something to use it in!”. So I decide to make a small game; Nothing too small or too big, just something for mindless fun. I had finished a playable version (v0.3a to be exact) and created a Changelog and such. I also made a theme song (Which I believe lacks more than the actual in-game music, for some reason) for it, which plays at the main menu. There are many more features then just THAT, for cryin out loud! Just play the damn thing!

Screenie:

(Yes, I took a snap of the very start of the game. Woo.)

Latest Version: https://dl.dropbox.com/u/21499454/Games/YAZSv033a.zip

Controls:

Up, Down, Left, Right = Movement
Mouse = Aim & Fire

Powerups:

Health = Gives you more health. Limit is 200.

x2 = Doubles damage. Picking up another will extend the time and multiply it again, setting it to x8! Limit is x8.

-1 = Lowers Zombie Power, making newly spawned zombies weaker. Limit varys based on Difficulty.

Weapons:

Shotgun: Fires 5 bullets with a x3 dmg multiplier on each bullet. Slow fire time.

Machine Gun: Fires bullets with no multiplier automatically.

Pistol: Fires individual bullets with a x2 dmg multiplier per-click. I could of sworn I added a rate-of-fire already though. :\

BRO-TIP: Zombies each spawn with Unique parameters. Each one has separate Health, Damage, Speed, and Size.

DEMENTED DIFFICULTY UNLOCK REQUIREMENTS (On Death):

Must be playing on Hard Mode.
Score: 100,000 or higher
Kills: 150 or higher
Zombie Power: 10 or higher

Please post some ideas or criticism! …Well besides Maxey of course. He is more than likely going to post a witty comment about my post and laugh evilly while holding that glass of wine with that evil grin of his. :awesome:
