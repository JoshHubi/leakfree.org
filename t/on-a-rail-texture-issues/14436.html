It appears to me that the water isn’t reflecting the props, and it’s then reflecting the nodraw that’s behind the doors.

In your advanced graphics settings, for Water Detail, try to change from “Reflect World”. Try “Simple Reflections” if your computer isn’t the best, or “Reflect All” if it can handle it, and let us know if the issue is still present.
