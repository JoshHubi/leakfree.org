Summary[/SIZE]
Black Mesa: Dormitories is an add-on for the SourceEngine modification Black Mesa:Source, which aims to recreate the Half-Life mod Half-Life:Induction.
The add-on features:
-Completely new and re-vamped maps.
-New HD models & textures.
-New dialogue.
