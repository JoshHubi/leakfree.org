I have RE5. Played it through, enjoyed it well enough. I still play Mercenaries from time to time. It looks beautiful, has some pretty awesome, but the whole partner thing gets annoying and I still miss zombies. That and a lot of it reminds me too much of RE4. The giant Majinji for instance… was just unnecessary. I’d also liked to have seen some rag-doll physics.

I’ve not gotten the expansion, but I hear it’s pretty good for being only $5 dollars (Lost in Nightmares, that’s not including the Versus stuff and the other characters). It’s only about an hour long, but good for reminiscing about the way RE used to be. Slower paced and spotted with puzzles like the good old days. You also get Barry Burton for Mercenaries, and he’s fuckin’ badass.
