Source SDK can be downloaded through Steam by going to the Library tab and finding the Tools Section.

After its downloaded the files themselves are located somewhere in your Steam folder. Its not necessary to back them up yourself.

BMS is installed in your sourcemods folder, which is located in your Steam folder (Normally around C:\Program Files\Steam\steamapps). (Or Program Files (x86) on 64bit systems.)
