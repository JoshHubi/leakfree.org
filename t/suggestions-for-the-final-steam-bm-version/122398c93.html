Going to bump this thread with a little tidbit of information. I am going to preface this with take everything said here at face value, don’t try to read into it.

We’ve ported the game to Source SDK 2013 with little difficulty. We do have to play through the game and go over it with a fine toothed comb to fix bugs listed on the bug tracker and bugs introduced by the port. If you want to see ‘progress’, best we can really do is say Look at the bug tracker.

Personally, I’d like to work on the AI a bit, taking into account the balance feedback and attempting to make it a bit more realistic. One of the things I changed recently was the AI now investigates combat sounds. Previously, the grunt would only turn towards the source of the noise, not actually moving towards it. We’ll see how this plays out during internal playtests.

As I wrote in a previous post, we’ve been bit in the past by over committing, or being too ambiguous. As we move forward, I’ll try to post some very general updates, but don’t expect them. Due to the NDA with VALVe, we are quite limited in terms of what we can discuss. I can’t disclose timetables for any specific functionality or parts of the game (read: Xen). Just sit back and enjoy the news updates if/when they occur
