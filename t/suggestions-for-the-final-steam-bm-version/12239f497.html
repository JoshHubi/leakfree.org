The goal of Black Mesa was to be up to Half-Life 2’s standards, and provide an experience that would have Half-Life mesh better with Half-Life 2, which IMO is something it has far surpassed.

In order for them to move to CE, to be brief, they would have to remake all of their assets to a higher quality, reprogram everything for the new engine, and learn a totally new engine. THEN they would have to get passed legal hoops, and seeing as they’re already in the works to get Black Mesa sold commercially, it would be a stupid idea to jump ship, especially now.

If you can’t grasp that and why it’s not worth it before even mentioning it, you’re horribly naive. That, or just lack forethought, either of which is forgivable.
