Nobody said Crystalis? I am disappointed.

Think Zelda style, but with more RPG elements. Many kinds of armor, the four elemental swords with powerups, accessories with varying effects, magic, and items to support all of this. Also, leveling up. The game is a bit cheap about it, in that if you’re too low level for a boss, you do absolutely no damage to them, but the max level is 16, so it’s not too bad to grind for 10-20 minutes if you can’t hurt the boss.
