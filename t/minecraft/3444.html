Minecraft is a wonderfully in depth java browser game. It’s a first person-puzzle-shooter-rpg-ctf game. You can build and farm and craft and… Well, lots of things. It has multiplayer. You can play singleplayer-sandbox thing and singleplayer survival.

There is a “Indev” version you must prepurchase for a lot less- when it’s finished, it’ll cost 20 euros. It’s waaay more indepth than the others. Features day night cycles, and all kinds of more enemies and challenges- and I’m just going from what I’ve seen!

Check it out and post what you think! :3
