Insomnautik November 25, 2018, 2:19am #24

Oh guys… Right click on Black Mesa in your Steam Games Library. Click Properties from the drop down menu. Then the Set Launch Options button, and type -console into the box. This should enabled your tilde (~) key for opening console. Then its just a matter of “sv_cheats 1” and “noclip” to toggle on and off.

Binding it to a key should help in case this bug reoccurs later in the game.
Type this in console:

bind n noclip

Or whatever key you want to replace N with.

In the case of AI disabled, you can use the console command ai_resume. Sometimes you will have to type ai_disable, then ai_resume for it to work.

You can instead add
ai_norebuildgraph “1”
ai_disable “0”

to your autexec.cfg, but I’m not quite sure if this will work.
