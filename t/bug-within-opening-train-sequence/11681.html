MadDoctor November 25, 2018, 2:55am #7

Try going into the developer console and noclipping back to your tram. If you don’t know how, just go into “options”, then keyboard, then advanced, and check enable developer console. Then, press the “~” key to open the console if you are using an American keyboard. If you are using a European one, or something like a Dvorak keyboard, then it should be the key under Esc, but it may not be “~”. Anyways, type in “sv_cheats 1” without the quotes then type in “noclip” without the quotes. Close the console. The noclip disables gravity and collision so you could possibly move back to your tram. Just move around until you find your tram, get inside, and type in noclip in the console again, and continue with your game. If you can’t understand what I just typed, I’m sorry, and send me a PM if you need further help.

You must be careful when you use noclip.

I’d do in this way:

1-input “sv_cheats 1” and “noclip” (will appear on the console something like noclip is ON).
2-with slight touches of W move Gordon above the catwalk:
this is important because if the distance between Gordon and the floor is too narrow the ragdoll can get stuck.
3-When you’ve place your character 1 mt above the catwalk open the console and input “noclip” again(you’ll have as output noclip is now OFF).
4- input “sv_cheats 0”

I’d try to do it before Barney starts to talk or after.
