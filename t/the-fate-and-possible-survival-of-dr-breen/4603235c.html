about breen: we know that the combine were beginning to become annoyed with him for not producing results, so that could mean they did not go to any extra lengths to protect him. we have no proof that he did or did not die. there is good evidence supporting both outcomes, it really depends on what valve decides to do. we also think that he may have a host body ready for him, and that we have no idea what that body could be, or if they had resorted to a different tactic. there were also reasons for is face flashing across the screen when the gman mentions that some people (breen) did not approve of keeping alyx alive. about the breencast where he is seen that i believe everyone is referring to, why wouldn’t the combine be playing breencasts that were prerecorded? (i mean, would he seriously show all of them live? and apparently not because after a certain amount of time they begin to loop) it doesn’t mean he is alive or dead.

about the advisors: we really don’t know enough about them to draw any major conclusions. the main theories are that a:they all work together, linked telepathically, so they don’t have a single leader, which could mean they are the combine. b:they are a species conquered by the combine. c:they are either low ranking officials or high ranking warriors of the combine, also saying they are part of the original species. or d:they answer to one king-like figure, similar to the nialanth.

regarding the combine: they either are here for our resources, or they want control of more planets. and some people have said that it is strange that humans (a technologically inferior race) have discovered inter-spatial teleportation before the combine, but the combine probably had access to different resources than we did. this also may support that the advisors are not combine (coming from a different environment and hence not being able to breath or something to that angle). we really don’t know if the combine are under control of something worse.

so in the end, i think you really need to just take a guess and see if that is the way it turns out.
