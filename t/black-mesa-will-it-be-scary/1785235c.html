nietzschesaurus November 24, 2018, 7:07pm #122

The first time I played HL the assassins scared me shitless. It took me forever to get through that part because I was too afraid to fight them head on. The second time I played HL was on hard mode, so they were able to cloak.

Bricks. Shat.

Also, the underground carpark. Terrified. Flashlight kept dying, I was sprinting around and worried about Alyx, almost no ammo. The howling the zombies filled my ears like jello made of scary.

I wasn’t able to beat it the first day of trying. Went to a friend’s house the next day, he had just set up his 42" HD tv and hooked his computer/stereo to it. Played through Ep1 on full setting (no way my box can do that), max volume. He was freaked out just as hard as I was the first time and he wasn’t even playing it.

HL1 was a horror game for me. I can’t tell you how many times I’d stop walking, throw myself against a corner and wait–terrified of whatever it was I knew was around the corner.

I don’t want them to MAKE BM scary, I want BM to create its own mood. That’s one of my favorite things about the HL games; it doesn’t seem like they’re trying to fit into a genre. They’re horror when they are, and sci-fi the rest of the time.

EDIT: I’ve never been able to get more than fifteen minutes into Afraid of Monsters. I’m a puss.
