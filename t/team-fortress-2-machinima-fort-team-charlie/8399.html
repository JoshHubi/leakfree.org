Just giving a shout out to Benny Fortman’s quasi-new Team Fortress 2 machinima - Fort Team Charlie - which is somewhat of a continuation of the brilliant Halo machinima Fire Team Charlie. It is among the best, full fledged machinima work done in the Source engine. Status of the web show can be seen here:

https://www.facebook.com/pages/Fort-Team-Charlie/134496596592711

Part 1 of the first episode can be seen here:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TZkJ9ieeIyQ

Please check it out. Lot of work was clearly put into it, and I think it would be a shame for it not to get the proper amount of attention.
