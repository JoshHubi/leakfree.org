If you major in accounting, you will have a bachelor’s in accounting. The only way to get a B.A. in foreign language is to major in it. You could double major, but if you apply yourself and your only goal is to speak fluently, then a minor and some strict discipline will serve you just fine.

What you don’t want to do is major in foreign language and not major in anything else, because you will end up at the local McDonald’s.
