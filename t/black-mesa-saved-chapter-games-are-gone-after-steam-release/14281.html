orudie:

After a steam release, I purchased black mesa on steam, and game was downloaded again. Was it installed under the same path as the original mod location? i noticed that my saved games are gone, and I am playing black mesa from chapter one now. Is this normal?

Consider the paid Steam version to be a completely new game on a completely new engine. It is so different from the original free Black Mesa mod under the hood, that it’s probably a good idea to think of the two as entirely separate, completely incompatible games. The new version is NOT considered a mod (it’s considered a full retail game), which means it was not installed in the original sourcemods/BMS location. It is now located in common/Black Mesa.

orudie:

With other future releases, on steam, will the saved chapter games remain or will they be gone?

Future releases will be patches to the new game. Sometimes the saves will still be compatible, sometimes they will be broken. There are no guarantees, unfortunately. The saves may still be there, but they might have awful game-breaking glitches. At the very least, you won’t have to start at chapter 1.
