Well, this certainly surprised me and cheered up a rather gloomy set events for me recently! I’m anxiously looking forward to this!

I’m so glad they set the release date during the same month as the announcement, or else I probably would have been uncomfortably annoyed.

I’m not sure how I went so long not realizing this project was in the works, as I had only discovered it back in May of this year. I guess I’m excited that I wasn’t waiting for it as long as I was waiting for Half-Life 3, though.

7 years… Wow. This’ll definitely be… Worth the wait.

I trusted that this project would get done, even when I saw it was in the making for such a long time. The insistence that it was not dead and was currently being worked on was good enough reassurance for me. Most mods and projects end up just dying or fading away.

I think… that in the history of video games… This is probably the first mod / remake of a game created by fans, to actually have been completed, and done well.

It makes me sad that other awesome fanmade works that were close to completion, like Chrono Trigger Resurrection, could never have seen the light of day.

This is why I love VAVLe, they encourage their fans to work and modify their games. Seriously the best gaming company ever, if not the best company ever.
