Yes I am, hence why this isn’t already up on the workshop (since getting updates to people there is easy).

Speaking of, here’s a link. Includes the changes necessary to the gameinfo.txt in the download, just extract to the “Black Mesa/bms” directory. Please let me know how it works, anything you’d like to see, etc. This release tweaks a lot of the named female scientists to use more randomized skins, so you can get the newly working skins mixed in without all of the previous ones vanishing.

As for getting female guards modeled, depends. If it were just stitching the female heads/hands onto the guard bodies, with a few tweaks to look more feminine/fit the female rig, would be easy enough. Making an entirely custom model of the guard uniform for females is another story, though, certainly not impossible. Bigger issue would be the lack of female guard lines and things of that sort. While I wouldn’t mind seeing them myself, like I said, without the support files/likely some programming, would mostly just be a model that’d see more use in SFM/GMod than Black Mesa itself.
