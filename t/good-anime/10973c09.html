I’m reallllllly picky when it comes to anime and, true talk, these following animes are both really good.

Just finished Gurren Lagann, which is a mech show which is WAY MORE than a mech show. I don’t even like mecha and I think it’s one of the most empowering show’s I’ve ever seen. Give it a spin, but watch the English version, since the over-the-top, exaggerated dub is half the charm.

Deadman Wonderland. In the middle of watching this one, and it is fucking twisted. High School kid has to watch his friends/classmates get utterly massacred one day, him being the only one spared. He’s found guilty of killing them all so he’s sentenced to death and sent to this fucked up privatized prison where the inmates operate this amusement park for the general public, and are run around and tortured for entertainment’s sake.
