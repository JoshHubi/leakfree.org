Orpheon November 25, 2018, 1:17am #309

Well, Azumanga Daioh is one of the best high school slice-of-life/comedies, if you haven’t watched it yet. Lucky Star seems to be a bit polarizing for people, but I like it. I think some people have already been burned out on otaku-type characters and jokes, which it does have quite a bit of. His and Her Circumstances is a high school show that focuses mainly on the relationship between the two main characters and how it grows, which it does well. Sayonara Zetsubou Sensei is an interesting show - you could call it ordinary, since it too is just about high school, but in reality it’s rather dark, and its characters are metaphors for the problems in Japanese society. K-ON! is alright too. It’s basically just another high school show, the exception being that they play music. Sometimes. Like I said, it’s alright, but it more or less just retreads old territory, so I wouldn’t really recommend it unless you really are into music or high school shows.
