Malidictus November 25, 2018, 2:18am #4

That’s actually a recurring problem in quite a few places: Physics objects will get stuck under and between moving objects and cause them to jam. I exploded one US Marine turret, causing a blast door in “We’ve Got Hostiles” to self-seal in the process, and the turret fell under the door. This prevented it from closing completely, yet also prevented me from using the emergency button to open it and made it impossible to get through.

I think, if at all possible, that large objects like lifts, blast-resistant gates and so forth should be able to crush physics objects even if they are normally indestructible. Either that, or all physics objects should be destructible. It’s all too easy to bump into a barrel, knock it down an incline and have it roll into wall mines, explode them and cause a blast door to close down on top of it, making the game unwinnable.
