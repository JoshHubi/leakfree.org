I haven’t played a whole lot of games released this year, but the worst was almost certainly Final Fantasy XIII. Combat was oversimplified, so the most you really had to know was when to switch paradigms. Wasn’t totally horrible, but a little more complexity, like the older installments, would have been nice. But that’s nothing compared to the characterization. As this article brings out, the presentation of the characters made it, at best, hard to connect with them. Some of them cough Vanille cough were just downright irritating.

Alos, the upgrading system didn’t feel very satisfying either. You got a better item when you upgraded, but it wasn’t as simple or gratifying as purchasing a new item and equipping it every time you wanted better stats.

Music and visuals were mostly what it had going for it, but it’s not enough to support the entire game.
