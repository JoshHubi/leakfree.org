I have never played half-life. I have waited for this mod for several months now. should I just buy half-life source, play it, then get this mod when it comes out to more thuroghly enjoy what I have already experienced? should I wait, and have this mod be my first half-life experience?

The main reason to download Half-Life: Source is this: It takes the old textures, models, etc and adds the new source engine physics. For example, in the original Half-Life, when you shot someone they would shake in place and then fall backwards. Or if it was a soldier, they would slump and fall to the ground and all of them died similarly. However, with the source version, they all die differently depending on where they are shot and the interaction with the environment. The water, particles, and explosions take their queue from the source engine as well. Everything reacts different because of the source physics as opposed to the old version. I have both, but mainly play the source version. I keep the older one around for nostalgia. However, I purchased Half-Life Source when it was on sale for $5. Now, as of May 9th 2010, it is $9.99.
