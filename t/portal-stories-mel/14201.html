Ah. There’s a reason for what’s going on. I’m okay with the mod now.

For your task, Johnwalter, here’s what you do:

[spoiler]The order of the cubes are important.

  1. Put a portal below the first cube chute and one on the angled platform.
  2. Push the pedestal button to start a loop, with Cube A going through the portal and being flung into the emancipation grille. Image
  3. Stand on the large button to bring down the emancipation grille and have Cube A hit the large button on the wall. Image
  4. This will bring Cube B into the area.
  5. Grab Cube A and put it somewhere so the loop ends. Image
  6. Grab Cube B and place it on the large button to keep the emancipation grille down. Image
  7. Grab Cube A and throw it off the edge. Image
  8. As quickly as you can, run to the cylinder and stand on it. Let physics do the rest. Image
  9. Once the cylinder raises, you can use physics and portals to fling a cube up to the ledge with you so you can grab it (Image) and put it on the button (Image).
    [/spoiler]

Hope that helps!
