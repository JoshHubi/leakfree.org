Harry-UK:

Hey guys, got very bored and decided to make my own ‘Still Alive’ cover with robotic voice and all.

You’ve technically made a really good cover. I don’t think it’s that interesting to listen to though. I mean, basically, you’ve remade the original song, just with a little bit different voice. And even that you’ve tried to mask. Personally I prefer covers where the artist aims to do more than just recreate the original song. Covers with different instruments, different voice, little bit different text… I don’t know, something different.

You’ve done a cracking job in making recreation of the original song though, but with a cover I always hope for something special. Some improvement over the original, or at least something different, something unexpected. Right now I would have no real urge to listen to this rather than the original.

Also, a little feedback on your audio mix. The instruments are too far into the background compared to the voice I think. You should try to create balance between the two. I also think you’re using the wrong kind of reverb in the choruses. The reverb you use made it sound like you’re singing in an empty parking garage, which doesn’t seem to fit. A reverb with less delay would fit better, in my humble opinion.

Either way, like I said, you’ve done a very good job on recreating the original. If I had a hat, it would be off to you.
