My problem with teleportation theory is this: Say you are able to dematerialize, atom by atom in one location, and rematerialize in another with nothing out of place, is it still you? Would the information in your brain make it? Were you not, in effect, destroyed and recreated?

Precisely my problem with it. Surely you’d have to be taken as a whole, otherwise your entire conscious state would be lost ( for however brief a period it may be ) between these theoretical teleporters.

I think I’d much prefer to go by wormhole myself.

It would be interesting to see the problems associated philosophically with the importance of self if we could literally break someone down and rebuild them without a care for the original.
