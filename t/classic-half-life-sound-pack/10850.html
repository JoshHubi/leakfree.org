Black Mesa is great. Quite a few times throughout the game I felt like I was playing the original Half-Life, but renovated. Shooting is enjoyable, music is OK, level design is amazing - it is very much close to Half-Life.
The only problem for me were the sound effects. The developers made a huge effort to mimic the classic sounds and they did okay, but it’s just not the originals we all love. Because of that I decided to create a little modification consisting my favorite sound effects from the original Half-Life. These include:

Weapons:[/SIZE]

  • Tau Cannon
  • Gluon Gun
  • Snark hunt and deploy sounds

UI:[/SIZE]

  • Ammo pickup
  • Weapon pickup
  • Flashlight on/off (Half-Life 2)
  • Health charger
  • Suit charger
  • Medkit pickup

NPC:[/SIZE]

  • Houndeye charging and hurting sounds

Notes:
1.) If you think HL’s Tau Cannon overcharge sounds are lame, you can always replace them with the BM’s (included in the pack)
2.) I didn’t replace the battery pickup sound because it’s a bleak Counter-Strike weapon pickup sound. But in case you are an ultra-purist I included it in the pack

xalener November 25, 2018, 2:31am #8

Added those freckles (they were supposed to be pits, but the texture was so low rez in HL1 it always registered to me as splotches of darker pigment) and some of the hexagonal turtle shell shit to the belly that the original had. I changed the diffuse of the shackles back to the original HL2 ones because the iridescence map kinda took care of the green tint it needed anyway. I could whip up something higher rez though, definitely. Also, simplified the pupil detail greatly to match the flat red that was in HL1. That affected the bullsquid too, but meh. I think it looks cooler on the BS anyway. Though I do wish it had that white eye it did in the original. Maybe I can do something about that, but I doubt it.

I also added a separate lightrap texture for the vorts and the Agrunts for more exaggerated translucency. I’m testing out something else for the bullsquids and houndeyes since an orange endodermis layer doesn’t make much sense for them.

Anyway, I don’t want to derail this thread. I’ll start my own when I get home. I was weary about it because the rules said “NO SOUNDS”. It really sucks, because that’s my specialty.
