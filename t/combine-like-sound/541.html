[COLOR=‘Red’]OK, this is stupid. I don’t know why it didn’t occur to me or anybody else, but it would be more helpful if i just posted the content on that page out here. So here it is…there were only 5 posts.

Okay I made this combine voice of me saying “You there, pick up that can” in Audacity by doing (in this order) a Hard Limiter effect (repeated > 1x), followed by Equalization (also more than one time). I saved it as a mp3/wav, then opened it again to find my settings for each of the effects missing to recreate it. I tried to look at the Audacity effect history, but it was not there because it was not saved as a project. Anyway, here it is. It sounds really nice, so it’s a shame I don’t know what settings I used. It almost sounds like a direct recording from the game… at parts. Also, there are a few horrible, little tests of how well I could do some simple effects of HL2: Episode 2, such as radio-voice, metrocop, antlion/animal growl, tv-echoes, and horrible microphone quality. I only used the advanced effects above on part of the clip, you can hear it cut over to the better edited part about 20 seconds in… At least it sounds better than using the Valve distortion.bcs files…

Yeah parts of it don’t make sense, because I made it in like 5 minutes and couldn’t think of anything else to say. I just started saying random words at one part.
