Dark_Signal November 25, 2018, 12:41am #1

Hello again Black Mesa,

There were two things I would like to know about the HECU vehicles, one about the LAV-25, and another about the abrams. If these features aren’t present, they will pose as suggestions.

I’ve read on the BMS wikia that the M1 Abrams will be capable of moving around. While I think that is an awesome feature, I wish to know if the LAV will do the same thing. The fact that there is enough space for it to move around in surface tension certainly implies it. Plus it will be a cool idea to have an LAV advances alongside the infantry as it provides supressive fire.

As for the M1 Abrams, I saw the video where its turret rotates. Will the tank also be capable of pivoting its barrel vertically? I ask because if it only rotates on a horizontial axis, it will be easy to exploit if the player manages to gain the high ground.

A while ago, I asked if the Humvee was going to be a driveable vehicle (Thanks for the response Raminator). Although I have rigged vehicles to be driveable before, modifying a model that already has some of these features is a different story, because then you’ll have to fix what was broken in the whole reverse engineering procedure (which is usually alot). Plus developers frown upon their work being modified, so making these changes myself in the event the mod’s released seems out of the question.

I’m still confident you guys will finish the mod, so I hope you’ll fill me in on that, then I’ll know what to expect.

(I also read that the LAV can’t transport troops. I don’t know how you’ll pull that off, but I’ll leave that to your creativity).
