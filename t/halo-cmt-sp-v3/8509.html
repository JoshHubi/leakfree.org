CMT an overhaul of the halo campaign. But it’s more than a graphical overhaul.

It re-balances and adds some weapons and enemies/allies.

To get the best effect, you gotta DL Open Sauce, which is a script that allows you to hook more features into the engine and enable it to do shit it could never do (motion blur, normal maps/ advanced shaders, alter and add gameplay features)
