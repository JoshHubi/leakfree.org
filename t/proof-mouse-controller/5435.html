What I like with the mouse is that your hand’s movement translates directly into movement in the game. If you stop moving your hand, you stop moving in the game (by “moving” I mean looking around, obviously).

On a controller, the in-game movement happens in the direction that you hold the controller button, and the speed is controlled by how far you twist the knob. If you tilt the controller button, and stop tilting further, you still keep moving in that direction until you let go. I don’t know if this makes sense, but it annoys the hell out of me so I can only play with a mouse.
