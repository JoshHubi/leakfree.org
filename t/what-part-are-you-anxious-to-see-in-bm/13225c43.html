histeachn November 24, 2018, 6:51pm #325

People still visit this thread, so I’ll share what I’m eager to see.

Like a lot of people, I’m interested to see Inbound. I just remember when HL first came out and that scene in the beginning very simply but effectively introduced a new way of approaching FPS. It actually attempted to give an explainable context rather than to just blow stuff up! Nice to revisit that with a fresh look to it.

I sort of hope they add a few more details to make Gordon’s part in causing the RC a little more plausible. I never quite got how a guy with a PhD from MIT would be limited to do something that’s almost janitorial like “push this cart over there.” They may not add to that, but I can hope. How they do the scene shifts to Xen after the RC will be cool, too.

I sort of hope they make Power Up and On A Rail more enjoyable. For whatever reason, I never quite liked those levels. I know they’d mentioned in other areas of wanting to make certain levels flow better, and I hope that happens with those two.

The scenes between the end of Apprehension and Residue Processing. Simple cinematics, but just very cool and introduced a nice plot shift into the game.

The part towards the end of Surface Tension where you dropped down into the building covered with wall mines. When that’s revealed in the game, there’s always a part of me that thinks of the designers and goes, “You bastards . . .”

Beyond that, just hope the game runs well enough on my system. That reveal scene from the pipe in Surface Tension may murder my computer though.
