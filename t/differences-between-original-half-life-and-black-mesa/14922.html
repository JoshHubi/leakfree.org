This sort of list doesn’t exist because basically everything in the game has been altered to some degree- even in areas where the original level design has been kept basically the same, things like the width of corridors and the borders around doors have been modified, and all the assets (including textures, voices, enemies, decals, props, sound effects, whatever) are completely new.
