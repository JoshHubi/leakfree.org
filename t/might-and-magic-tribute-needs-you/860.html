Hello guys.

Maybe I’ve told you a few times about this project that I’m involved into. It’s a project that runs since quite a few years, but lately there’s more and more people disappearing. What’s more saddening is it once involved a lot of people, but not now.

This project is Might and Magic: Tribute. Basically it’s a Tribute game to the Might and Magic series. Maybe some of you guys knows about this series, which is pretty old. If you don’t know about it, let me explain it to you. It’s a traditional RPG series, involving 4 characters you create, sometimes more. But it’s particularity is it’s completely open. You can go where you want, anytime you want, a bit àla Oblivion, except the series existed before the TES series.

What makes is exceptional is the depth of the game. For example, it is said that Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven which is considered by many like the best game of the series, can take up to 300 hours to complete.

Now, why am I here posting this? It’s because MMT needs help. Bad. As I told you before, the team is in pretty bad shape. And they need developers. they need everything: Modellers, Concept Artists, Sound Engineers, Animators, Programmers, Writers, Mappers, Voice Actors, Janitors, Elfves, name it!

So, I hope you guys will help us! Thank you all.

Edit: Oh I just realized I didn’t posted their website. Here it is: https://www.mmtribute.castlegobs.nl/
