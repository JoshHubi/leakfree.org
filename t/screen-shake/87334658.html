For me the appeal of screen shakes has nothing to do with realism. Gun shots just feel more satisfying when they kick the view around. Same goes for damage feedback. Just take Quake for an example, walk up to an explosive box and shoot it, the screen will go very red and your view gets a mean punch downwards (as far as I can see) for a moment. That explosion would not seem nearly as mean if all you got was Quakeguy doing his pain grunt but the view was completely unaffected.
