Hey hey, this is the drugs discussion thread now?

If you’re 16 I don’t recommend using either. While starting to drink at a younger age could lead to problems with alcohol and dependency later in life, little is still known about marijuana’s interaction with younger people. Some theorize that it may lead to attention and learning problems later in life. I’m not a doctor nor a scientist, so take that with a grain of salt!

It’s obviously your choice and nobody can make it for you. If you’re going to smoke at least educate yourself on the potential risks and situations you could be putting yourself in. I wouldn’t worry about mental health issues; the only times I’ve heard or seen of mental problems caused by marijuana is in people that already have a preexisting condition that they should have been hospitalized or at least treated for in the first place.

In all reality smoking marijuana is probably safer; unlike alcohol you can’t smoke too much and overdose and kill yourself. You’re doing harm to different parts of your body, though, so the term ‘safer’ isn’t quite right. You also run into issues with legality in most parts of the world, that could be reason enough not to smoke at all.
