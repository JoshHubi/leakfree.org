Leijonasisu November 25, 2018, 2:39am #7

Lemmie guess it’s an HP laptop. You might have bloatware called “Touchfreeze” running. This disables the trackpad click for 2 seconds after a key is pressed, to prevent accidental clicks while typing. Unfortunately, this disables mouse clicking too – not ideal for WASD shooters.

Before starting the game, look in the taskbar for an icon shaped like a trackpad. It should be called ‘touchfreeze’ if you click on it. Exit the program.
