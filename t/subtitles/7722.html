Hello,

I ran a search but I am not so sure if subtitles have been discussed for Black Mesa. I wanted to layout a few things regarding the purposes of subtitling.

I’m deaf (currently transitioning from HOH to deaf), and I can’t hear at all. My problem with Half Life 1 was its lack of subtitles. Half Life 2 had subtitles, for which I am so grateful for; but like I said, it’s in Half Life 1, Opposing Force, and Blue Shift that opposed any presence of subtitles.

I know there are not that many deaf people out there but it would be greatly appreciated if there’s a toggle option to turn on/off the subtitles option. The story is very critically important to us, and without it, we may never get to understand what Gordon Freeman was doing on that day and why he must uncover Xen and get rid of the otherworldly creatures from entering Earth. Yes, I had to read this from other sources to understand the story better. Talk about spoilers.

I just hope you have figured out a way to put subtitles into the game, not for many of us deaf players but also for those who can’t hear very well or are having a hard time hearing.

I hope there’s a prompt response from one of the moderators on this forum, or from one of the development teammates that may include any tidbit about whether subtitles are added or not.

Thanks,

Huscurian
