I guess E3 2005’s promises of Alan Wake on PC were finally fulfilled this week. I can recommend it. But I miss, that it’s not open world, although it was possible. Still, a great game and yes, Remedy is underrated.

I can’t wait for Alan Wake 2. I hope the PC versions sells good.
