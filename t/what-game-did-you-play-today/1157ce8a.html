baconeggs November 24, 2018, 6:57pm #791

Half Life 2: Riot Act.

Quite a good mod actually. You play as the coincedentally named Jamil, a prisoner in Nova Prospekt. When Freeman turns up and raids the place it gives you a chance to make a break for it, along with several other captives.

It’s pretty well done, very true to the Half Life style, except for a lack of objects which makes rooms look a bit bland. The upside is that it cuts down on loading; there’s only three loading screens in the entire game.

Nice locations, it was good to run around Nova Prospekt again, and you even check out the Depot too.

Only real gripes apart from the lack of detail was that some scripted sequences could be interrupted by antlions who follow your pheropod, leaving you trapped outside a door. There was also a Combine ventilation shaft that ended in a flimsy tin grate, which isn’t particularly logical.

Even so, it’s a good mod with plenty of intense combat and puzzle solving.
