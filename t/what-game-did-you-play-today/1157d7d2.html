Soup:

Doesn’t it make it more useful if it has jets? Or can’t you control the jets?

The jet boots just give you lift, and you can move towards a direction while boosting with them. If you feather the jets, you can easily gain 50 feet or more in one go, dive and climb once with the wing suit to get back to the same height, and feather the jet boots again.

If you practice with the wing suit, you can gain maybe 5 feet each time you dive and climb, to put things in perspective. And I think the wing suit is slightly faster, but you can’t hover and fire your weapons with it. That’s the only advantage the wing suit has over the jet boots.
