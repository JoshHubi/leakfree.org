Someonerandm November 24, 2018, 11:55pm #4727

Trying out Crysis 2. Its some great fun, and I really like the new integration of the suit powers, although I do miss “maximum ______”. Haven’t gotten too far into it, but I can say its less open than the original, but still open enough to have some space for tactics and maneuvering. Pistols no longer count as secondary weapons, making them next to useless as they’d replace a primary weapon. AI has yet to do something stupid, so thats a plus. Environments aren’t as interactive as their predecessors, and I’m really missing that. There is no equivalent for brining down an entire sheet metal structure with a barrel, which was one of the highlights of Crysis.

In short, so far its a solid game thats really fun, suit powers are better, but ultimately lacks a lot of what made Crysis great for me. Worth a play, but its not spectacular.
