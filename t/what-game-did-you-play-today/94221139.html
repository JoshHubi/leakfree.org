MSKyuuni November 25, 2018, 4:20am #3712

I’m sorta on the fence about whether I like Borderlands Legends or not. There’s no story, but I’m guessing it takes place just after BL1 (which is cool I guess) and it still has Roland, Lilith, Mordecai and Brick as the player characters. Unlike the main BL games though, there’s no real structure to anything and it’s like a sort of RTS wave-based game rather than a shooter of any kind. Also unlike the main series games, you have to control every character at once because if you leave any of these Vault Hunters to fend for themselves for even a couple seconds, they refuse to do anything at all and will be dead before you can get someone to revive them. But then the trouble with that is if you’re facing a tougher enemy/enemies there is almost a guarantee that you’ll end up with characters taking turns of down and revive with no gap at all. All this being said though, it still has the (mostly) unique characteristics and tone of Borderlands, and finishing a quest is quite rewarding if only because the fights can be really punishing, and once every Vault Hunter dies in quest, you’ve failed and you can never do that quest again.
