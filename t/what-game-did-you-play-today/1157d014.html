SkyBlue November 24, 2018, 11:35pm #4597

@BahamutBBob I’m guessing you’re on the 360 or PS3, but in the full game you will be able to toggle it. Just the speed and animations make the combat far more satisfying. I really hope that the interface is toggleable in the full version, because on the PC if you press Tab (to show all the interact-able objects), the enemy health is not displayed, but instead you have to target each individual enemy. The game engine seems to handle multiple enemies much better though, and I like the more functional interface (and health bars that actually correspond to the amount of health!).

The character models all look a lot more impressive (especially Flemeth), except for male Hawke’s beard and the darkspawn. The Hurlocks look as if they should be on the PS2, and male Hawke’s beard is just wrong. How can any hair be that flat, especially facial hair?

I don’t like how ability trees are locked from the start (though that could just be the demo), but having more control over choosing individual abilities is very useful, so you don’t have to buy 2 boring spells to get one that you want. Changing the stats to being class-specific is a bit odd from a D+D perspective, but game-wise I think it’s an excellent decision, to save wasting points.

All in all, I don’t think it’s going to be a contender for GotY, but I think it’s made good improvements on DA:O. Hopefully DA3 will be the masterclass to rival ME2.
