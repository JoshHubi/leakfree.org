Yeah, I’ve been playing this too. I’m up to Chapter 4, and apparently about to go through the least exciting part of the game (something about the ADS?). However, so far I’m finding it reasonably enjoyable; the PC port’s controls aren’t quite as good as they should be, and other third person shooters (such as Splinter Cell) feel much better. Its really not a horror game, though occasionally some of the faster zombie…things can make me a little concerned for my life.

The story is very System Shock 2, and the style is very similar. The whole concept of being alone on a space station, fighting off crowds off mutated crew members, being directed by a person somewhere else in the station, fixing various parts of the ship to ultimately complete a far off objective and being scared the entire time is very SS2/Bioshock, but Dead Space just isn’t as good.

However, that said, I like the whole limb amputation thing, and the combat itself I find quite polished and superior, in its own way, to what you ad to go through in SS2. The slow-down/kinectics thing is pretty good, but pretty much exactly the same as in other games.

tl;dr - Good, but nothing new. Play it, but don’t expect anything ground breaking.
