You can make anything taste good if you put the right chemical sweeteners in it. It’s not the fast food you’re enjoying, it’s the stuff they add into it. For example, McDonalds adds HFCS (high-fructose corn syrup) to a lot of their products. HFCS is much sweeter than sugar, this is what makes it ‘taste good’. McDonalds also add MSG (mono-sodium glutamate) to their products, hell they even put it into their chicken nuggets which is why they’re so tasty.

TLDR; fast food doesn’t taste good because it’s good food it tastes good because of the chemicals added into it.
