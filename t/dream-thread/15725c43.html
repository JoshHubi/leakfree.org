[i]I am in a city.
I stand on a brick road. There is a small stone wall to the left, behind which is an endless ocean. to the right of me are large houses made of bricks. They all have large staircases leading to their entrances. No one is around and the weather is grey.

I enter the first staircase to my right and see that I am entering some sort of news studio. The room is made of bricks and is lit in a yellow light. A lot of people are walking around here and the news reader talks about a shawarma place in the area having really bad food.

I walk up a staircase and down a long hall until I find the shawarma place in question. The place has the same look as the news studio(bricks and yellow light) and is also very busy. What I see though is that the owners of the place are secretly trying to buy the news studio that criticized them. They successfully do so and immediately fire everyone at the studio that openly criticized it.

Then I go back to the news studio, I notice there’s no news reader anymore. See some people in suits, probably fired, walking around. The news studio is now working on a skeleton crew but it’s still busy with the people who don’t work there walking around.

I finally exit the building through a back-door and end up in an alley between two large brick houses. The ground is grassy and there’s quite a few shrubs around. I also notice a large number of kids standing in a group. One kid with a walkie-talkie walks to the exit of the alleyway, to the road where I started the dream. I go there too. I notice him talking into his walkie-talkie in a language I do not understand, but he says my name(Thomas) in multiple instances. I figure the guy who he’s talking to is also named Thomas.

As I exit the alleyway and go onto the street, the kid stops there and talks some more into his walkie-talkie. When I’m quite a distance away from him I hear him shout “WHORES!” in a noticeably terrified way. He and the other children are then overrun by a huge horde of blood-thirsty scarcely clothed women. I don’t seem to care a lot, though, because the last thought in my dream is me wondering what time it is in real life. I then wake up and looking at the clock to see it’s around 11 AM.[/i]
