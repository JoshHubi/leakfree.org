Jeannotvb November 25, 2018, 12:45am #3
Combine:

-CAD and 3D modeling programs, ie. 3ds, Autocad, Unity
-rendering complex 3D models (fast rendering times a must)

Those demands are not easily met, I have a i7 930 @ 3.4 GHz, 6 GB of triple channel memory and a GTX285 and use this build primarily for modeling and rendering. With complex models (5+ million poly’s) the rendering is certainly not fast, especially with V-Ray and such. My 6 GB of memory will easily run out when rendering very high poly models. If you stay within the 3-5 million poly range you should be fine but I doubt you’ll have fast rendering times depending on the complexity of the rest of your scenes. The maximum amount I have managed to get out of it was rendering a model with around 35 million poly’s.
