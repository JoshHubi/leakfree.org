https://soundcloud.com/john_georgvich/adagio-for-black-mesa

I played with the soundtrack a little bit (after PURCHASING it) and made this little thing.

It’s loosely based on the story of the game, separated into two uneven sections:

The first half is to represent the events leading up to the cascade and how the chain of command and rule breakdown caused it. The piano falls apart and begins to play more delayed and erratic as the cascade grows closer.

Finally, the music quiets to nothing and the second half, based on the post-cascade descent of Freeman, begins quickly. It grows louder and louder as Freeman traverses the bowels of the facility, an eerie “reverse-sounding” moan penetrating the orchestral beauty.

In the final moments of the track, Freeman creates a portal to the alien world of Xen, and the story grinds to a slow, but breathtaking halt.
