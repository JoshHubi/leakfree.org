sersoft November 25, 2018, 3:30am #15

Many LG screens have a feature that is meant to address permanent burn-in or “ghost image” effect for their panels which basically consists of an almost invisible noise pattern (like for analog tv channels) always displayed in the background, and it’s usually pretty good at keeping functioning pixels alive too, so any dead pixels you have are probably a permanent issue.
