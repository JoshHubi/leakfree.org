I’d just like to briefly say that I mostly agree with the OP as far as the game itself goes.

dark omen:

Gabe Johnson mentions that Black Mesa stole Aperture Science inventions and passed them off as their own. What technology did Black Mesa steal from Aperture Science?

https://www.thinkwithportals.com/comic/#14

It looks like (at least) the wall-mounted health stations and HEV chargers and maybe something that is, or at least looks at lot like the gravity gun? I don’t really get the gravity gun part, I could have sworn Eli said it was made after the BM incident.

I also think there is some familial relation between Caroline/GLaDOS and Chell. Probably Chell being the daughter of Caroline; the “mia bambina” line in the ending opera seems to more than hint at that. I also think that Cave might be her father, biologically or through adoption. According to the new timeline, Cave seems to have died in the mid 80s-ish, which puts Chell in about the right age group. It also explains why her name is redacted in the files.

Furthermore, I don’t think GLaDOS deleted Caroline. Not because I don’t think she can, I’m not sure if she can either way, but because her compassion for Chell remains after she supposedly deletes Caroline. I think this was a typical GLaDOS lie, but this time used to scare Chell into never coming back to the facility, out of fear that GLaDOS has gone back to her normal self. Maybe Caroline realized that she can’t really control her/GLaDOS’ behavior very well and thought that the simplest solution was to lie to Chell to protect her from her GLaDOS side. GLaDOS’ being ‘married to science’ can also be a dehumanized way of referencing Caroline’s being married to Cave. Science, to GLaDOS is Aperture, and Aperture is Cave.
