I thought I would bring this mod to peoples attention as its a quite niffty little mod. Its status on moddb is TBD but its downloadable…

It has three parts to the game:

The house, my favourite part as its pure psychological horror.

Dreamland, think max payne dreams but less frustrating.

The chapters, Typical FPS with bullet time. Some interesting moments and its quite long for a prologue.

[COLOR=‘YellowGreen’]Underhell is an SMOD based Half Life 2 Modification that will be distributed in Episodic releases.
So far ONLY the prologue has being released…
It requires Half Life 2 and Source SDK Base 2006 to run
