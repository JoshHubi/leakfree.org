I…can’t believe this. As I said before, the WORST games ever?

I once saw Prince of Persia: Warrior Within on a list of the top 10 worst games of all time. Firstly, I think that game was amazing; the Dhaka was shit-scary, and it was challenging, deep, visually pleasing and fun all at the same time. Secondly, even if it had flaws, and even if you ignored all of the above, it is still NOWHERE NEAR THE WORST GAME OF ALL TIME. A game may be bad, awful, disturbing, shit and even ludicrously disgraceful, and yet it STILL wont be as bad as the worst games ever.

In order to be the worst game ever, it must be, in every single aspect of its being, a failure. Why? Because in order to be the worst ever, it must be worse than every single other game ever (or at least in the top 10). Not simply bad, but absolutely terrible, with not a single redeeming feature (not even that its so bad its funny). And there have been games that have failed to that extent. Big time.

Most bad games, see Big Rigs, are, on a technical level, unplayable. It was barely possible to win in Big Rigs, let alone begin to play it. Superman 64 is a little beyond that, since it was technically playable, but no sane person would; it looked and played terribly, much like another ‘worst game’, Daikatana. Ergo, in order to get to the level of worst game ever, you must at the very least satisfy the requirement of virtual unplayability; the looks and feel of the game, putting aside the storyline and subsidiary considerations, must be an affront to nature, and there is just no conceivable way that MW2 does that.

At its very core, MW2 is just a basic FPS with good looking graphics and a basic storyline. Ultimately, its not the worst game ever, just no better than average. Sure, its overpriced, its short, its multiplayer is awful and there’s little enjoyment for some, but its by no means the worst ever. Not even a little bit close. Average? Maybe. Worst ever? No. Not by a long shot.
