Mithrandir November 24, 2018, 7:50pm #5

Definitely go with the first one, it’s a much better deal. You may be disappointed with the screen size, but that’s the best you can get with an inexpensive LCD projector. Going to the next step up, DLP projectors, is significantly more expensive and probably out of your price range.

But LCD’s aren’t that bad. I have one and am pretty happy with it. Just don’t expect to get perfect clarity at 150 inches, they are slightly blurry at their max resolution. I’d check a couple reviews though to make sure that this particular model isn’t a dud though…
