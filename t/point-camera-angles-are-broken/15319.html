point_camera angles are broken

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
JeffMOD November 25, 2018, 5:09am #1

It appears that the angles and possibly zoom level of point_camera have become bugged, as cameras no longer point to the area determined in their angles selection, but towards and area offset from that. I have yet to determine the offset, but this is definitely a bug in the code rather than something that got knocked around in a map, because it’s happening in all maps that use point_camera, including custom maps.

I’ve made a screenshot with a quick test map, with a bullseye, monitor, camera, and a prop_dynamic with the camera’s model and angles - as you can see, the camera should be pointing directly at the bullseye:
