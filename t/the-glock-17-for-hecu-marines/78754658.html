I wonder if perhaps we could do better to simply cut out the middle-man, so to speak, and not require any major coding (and the many hours of testing that it would demand) by just having all the grunts drop more than their main weapon? What the hell is the point of those big vests covered in pouches if nothing is in them???

Effect: Lots of ammo for those who like to shoot things a lot and a minimum of work for our skilled dev-team.
