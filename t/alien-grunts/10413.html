BahamutBBob November 25, 2018, 2:26am #4
tomkeb:

Do you think it is possible that the grunts are basically vortiguants altered and cloned by the Combine, the same way as people are made into overwatch soldiers?

Replace “Combine” with “Nihilanth” and that’s the general consensus.

Also, The Combine are nowhere to be found in Half-Life. They literally do not enter the story until the 7 hour war. The Nihilanth is on the run from The Combine. The only reason he invades Earth when the experiment fails (or succeeds, in Gman’s eyes) is because it can put one more step between himself and The Combine. People generally believe that The Combine are the reason Nihilanth has metal bits sticking out of his backside, as well as the scars he has.

We’ve been talking about this stuff since the mod was announced. We’ve (and others not on this forum) asked Mark Laidlaw (the guy who wrote Half-Life and Half-Life 2) just about everything we could think of. This info might not be the most accurate, but it’s probably as close as you’re going to get without being Mark Laidlaw.
