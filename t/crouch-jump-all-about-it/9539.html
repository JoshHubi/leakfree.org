Shrewsbury November 25, 2018, 2:20am #16

If you‚Äôre having issues with the jump height - falling needessly short of platforms, and waist high boxes eluding you - I have found that turning down the gravity a touch helps a lot.

The default value for gravity in BMS is 600. Turning it down to 500 means I can clear simple obstacles that would normally require a crouchjump (waist high boxes and so on).

Enter the following command into the console. It will last for your entire play session, even if you reload the game from a different save.

sv_cheats 1; sv_gravity 500; sv_cheats 0
