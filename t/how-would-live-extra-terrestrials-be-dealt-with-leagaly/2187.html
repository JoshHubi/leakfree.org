Skilgannon November 24, 2018, 7:09pm #20

Assuming it wasn’t an invasion force, and hadn’t already blasted our capital cities into submission, the first hurdle would be communication. I have absolutely no idea how that would be solved, I imagine any intelligent beings that wished to communicate with us would probably try to learn our languages first, maybe. Second, the government of whatever country the aliens decide to make landfall would immediately surround the area with “non-threatening” military force, 1. because we’re an amazingly aggressive race that only needs the drop of a hat to start squabbling over something and 2. as a subtle, but not-so-subtle show of force in a (probably naive) attempt at dissuading any aggressive act from the aliens.

Then they would send out some form of diplomatic party to start negotiations for peace, information, trading etc. Scientific trade would be made in the form of biological subjects. I.e. they study us, we study them. Another thing that seems to be rarely dealt with, is pathogens. They would be absolutely crawling with bacteria and viruses that humans have never before come into contact with, and vice versa, so some form of quarantine would be needed so a vaccine could be developed to be spread to the general populace in the inevitable event that humans and aliens mingle.

And probably a whole host of things that I haven’t thought of.
