Boss battles. A staple of video games since Mario Bros. on the NES starting with King Koopa. But since then there have been many, many more Boss battles to be had. Good ones are memorable, epic, and just plain awesome. So what’s your favorite boss?

Mine would have to be Metal Gear Rex vs Ray in MGS4. I mean wow. Holy fuck. Everything about this boss battle SCREAMS balls to the wall epic. The story behind it, the setting, the environment, the mechs, and the characters in both of them. This was just orgasmic to play for the first time. Hell, any time really. The sound of metal shredding metal, explosions everywhere, debris flying off of buildings as they are blown apart in the crossfire of an epic battle. All climaxing with one of the best cut-scenes in Metal Gear history.

Katana August 11, 2020, 5:43am #11

Aquaria: the Golem is the second-to-last boss fight of the game (not counting the optional side ones, and the fact that the final boss has 5 different forms). The music is pretty powerful here, and its pace seems to fit so well. The golem pauses between attacks, but each one is pretty sudden, and require you to react very quickly. The game of course has plenty of good boss fights, but this one was the one awesome enough to make me keep a save game just before it.

San Andreas also had a pretty cool ending fight. The last mission was almost unnecessarily long, but it was fun most of the way, and made that latter portions pretty tense. It basically goes ‘shootout to the top of a fortress’ - ‘shootout with a boss who wears body armor’ - ‘shootout down to the bottom of the fortress, while time-limited because it’s exploding’ - ‘chasing a firetruck through the whole city while the entire population tosses molotovs around’ - ‘rail-shooting mission to defend your car (while another guy drives)’
