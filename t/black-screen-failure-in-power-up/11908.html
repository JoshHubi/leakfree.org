ThePsycho November 25, 2018, 3:06am #2

Your Intel onboard graphic card obviously can’t handle the shader used by Black Mesa in the toxic waste part.
You should get a decent GPU for your PC. Even if you can turn down lots of settings for Source Engine games to run on onboard graphic, it’s not recommended for Black Mesa since many bugs will come with it.
