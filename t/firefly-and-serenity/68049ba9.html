Winged One:

What is Firefly about, briefly?

On the surface, it’s about space cowboys 500 years from now who fly around and steal stuff while trying to avoid the law. Obviously the show isn’t quite so cheesy as that sounds, of course

The Western theme comes the storyline history. The Captain of the Firefly-class ship Serenity was once a solider serving with the Independents who fought the centralized government, the Alliance, in the War for Independence. The Independents were most from the border planets, as opposed to the heavily Alliance-influenced core planets. The border planets were colonized but not well-managed, and sort of slipped into lawlessness, a lot like the old West.

But forget the subject or plot- what Firefly is really about and why people love it and connect to it so much is that it’s about what makes us human and about the interpersonal connections and relationships between all of us.

There are no aliens in Firefly- only humans. It’s something I find both unique and refreshing about the series.

Plus, it’s super funny and terribly engrossing. The actor chemistry on the show is really amazing, and you can tell that they all really liked working both with each other professionally and on the show as their characters.

Sorry for rambling
