I just finished CUBE, and I’ve got to say that, for the most part, I really enjoyed it. It reminded me a lot of the original Portal: a (seemingly) abandoned science facility, and evil presence watching over you, and the sinister feeling that something bad happened here. I loved the hallucination sections, which broke up the puzzles nicely and kept the game fresh and interesting. There were a few puzzles which felt convoluted, and I was forced to look online for walkthroughs a few times, but this is only to be expected for a teams first mod, and I think they’ve done a fantastic job with this. I would recommend this highly to any fans of the first Portal, or science fiction, particularly the work of J.J. Abrams, but anyone looking for a fresh gamely experience and a brain workout will enjoy it.
