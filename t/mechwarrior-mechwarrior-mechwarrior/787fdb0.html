There are several MP modes in MW4

  • DM (Team DM) - bscly. 1 kill = 1 frag
  • Attrition (Team Attrition) - similar to deathmatch, but damage counts. So that it wouldn’t be so fucking frustrating when some punk kills a mech you’ve been stripping out armor from for the last 10 minutes.
  • CTF - bscly. CTF, but with mechs. With 30 mechs, it arguably has even more depth than TF2.
  • Capture The Beacon - only one flag, everyone gets in a crow fight for that flag. Taken the flag - run away, killing any motherfucker in your way. Assault mechs are no-no here. Light or medium only (With a possible exception of some moderately fast heavies). Damn dramatic sometimes.
  • Escort - Each team has a VIP (light mech, no more than 35 tonnes) and extraction point. To win get your VIP to the point or kill the enemy VIP.
  • King Of The Hill (Team King Of The Hill) - hold the area. As long as you stay, you get points.

With up to 8 teams possible, no repairs and shitloads of mechs, MW4 MP is a fucking intense thing

xilef:

I cried when the Mech4:Merc servers were shutdown.

Mektek have had a replacement up and running since M$ took the old one down.

If you have installed their MekPak3.02b for Mercenaries expansion, go into your Mercs folder and run “mekmatch.reg”. It changes the address that Mercs looks for when you start multiplayer to point to the replacement ZoneMatch server they put up.

If you’ve got MW4:Mercenaries, the expansion they made has dozens of new Mechs and weapons and is well worth installing. I usually play in the Sunder II server Sunday mornings (Saturday nights for Americans). Still lots of fun after all these years.

Another expansion is currently in closed beta testing with heaps of new electronics.

The mekmatch.reg file works for all Mercenaries versions and major mods (PR1, MP3.02b, NBT Hardcore, NetMechIV), but apparently MW4: Vengeance and MW4: Black Knight, use different netcode and don’t work.
