Medevilae November 24, 2018, 10:18pm #45

Frankly, I loved it. The whole fact that you’re working on it by yourself makes it all the more amazing. It looks really nice. However, I’d take heed to some of what has been said thus far. Specifically for me, I didn’t like:

  1. There are to many unusable doors. Put something behind them, or make a reason you can’t go in them.

  2. There’s not enough of a story being invoked by what you’re doing. It’s basically all run and gun. I’m assuming you have a story. Work on incorporating that into the mod in as many ways as you can. Unless you’re going for a “YOU’RE ALL ALONE!” feeling, add some characters. Put up a thread and get some voice actors to do some custom stuff.

  3. Make houndeyes weaker, and get rid of or change the HECU death sounds.

  4. Make environmental hazards more obvious as to whether or not they will hurt. Steam particle effects look nice, but if one is going to hurt, they should all hurt, or the other way around.

  5. When you punch, there shouldn’t be gun smoke. (or even sparks, really.)

Other than that, I really liked this mod. It’s better quality than most mods out there that have actual teams. Your custom models are really nice, and contrary to what has been said I thought your maps were pretty well optimized. (Well, I didn’t lag, anyway.)

Go for something to make it really stand out. Maybe an in-depth melee system? Maybe complex puzzles? Maybe just a damn good story! I can see this mod going somewhere. Try to pick up some people for a team, if you really need the extra help.

Best of luck!

Abcd November 24, 2018, 10:31pm #58

I just try the demo. I was really impressed.

  1. Texturing work was very detailed. Lighting was perfect. However, there is some places you could increase the ambient light to avoid using the flashlight.

  2. The explosions were impressive. You should move some of the barrels to more visible spot to allow use during a battle. The red barrel is non-explosive, maybe you should pick a different color.

  3. HEV and health station should have a progress bar for feedback.

  4. “gas leak” doesn’t do anything. When I saw this, I expected to trigger an explosion when shooting through it.

  5. Navigation in the map is not always obvious. Often there is a door in front of you, but you really need to go elsewhere. This was said by other, but door should follow a convention to know which one can be used. Also, it fairly easy to get stuck in the props (especially after an explosion).

  6. No theme across the level. For a demo, this is ok (maybe it was even intentional). Since you have done all the work for custom textures/props, you should make some sections longer (a entire chapter could be in the sewer, and a other in the office).

  7. Small FX glitches (flashlight, explosion flash visible through walls). Since you aim to release the mod in 2012, I would not worry about that now. Focus on the gameplay.

Bonne chance et félicitations pour le demo.
