bur November 25, 2018, 2:25am #236

Just finished the game. Lambda Core climax was nerve wrecking, it was great.

Overall, the entire thing was great. Exactly what it should have been, except for a few things.

I feel regular jump mechanics should have been kept the same as in HL2. For example, the part right before the rocket launch (the part in the leaked trailer) was quite annoying because it was a chore to jump over those tracks, making it hard to run around.

Enemy reaction times feel very quick. It’s impossible to get a first shot, and sometimes the enemy can hit you even before you can see them. In the part with the soldiers rappelling down from the osprey for example, when you walk up the garage entrance, they’ll be able to shoot you before you get high enough to even see them.

Surface Tension seems unnecessarily short. Scenes where Xen forces win from HECU are cut, and the reason why the HECU pull out in Forget About Freeman seems under emphasized.

Lambda Core overall could do with more Xen creatures. More surprises.

That’s it. Other than that, it was absolutely fucking great.
