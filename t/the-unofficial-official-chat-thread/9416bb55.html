Bolteh November 25, 2018, 2:54am #2174

Yes. But wouldn’t call it “news” though.

That’s Adamson, created by Oscar Jakobsson in 1920. After Jakobsson’s death in 1945, Dane Viggo Ludvigsen took over the comic until the late 60s (so if that’s from 1947, it was done by Ludvigsen).

It’s widely assumed that Groening did use Adamson as an inspiration for Homer, even going as far as assuming that Homer’s middlename (J) is a reference to Jakobsson’s name.

It’s also assumed that Groening used the Itchy & Scratchy plagiarism storyline in relation to this piece of history.
