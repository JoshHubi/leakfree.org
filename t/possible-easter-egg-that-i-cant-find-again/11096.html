Nope, he is not trolling.

You have to go across the top area of the cliff face when you first go out of the pipe. You will see a small cliff room with a computer terminal, a Health Charger, and an H.E.V Charger in the same spot. You have to press a button on the computer terminal, then go back to where you came out of the sewer pipe, climb down a bit, then you’ll find this (sorry for the bugged out model lol), go into the little cave, and you will automatically teleport into here. It’s part of a ARG involving pizza.
