Jenova19 November 25, 2018, 2:32am #6

Type this in the console and hit ENTER on your keyboard:

cl_drawhud 0

To disable it.

cl_drawhud 1

To enable it.

v - How-To enable the console in Black Mesa - v

  1. Close Black Mesa.
  2. Open Steam.
  3. Browse to the Library (All Games).
  4. Right click “Black Mesa”.
  5. Click “Properties” (on the bottom of the menu).
  6. Click “Set Launch Options…”.
  7. Type “-console” (without “”).
  8. Click “OK”.
  9. Close the “Black Mesa - Properties” window.
  10. Start the game.
