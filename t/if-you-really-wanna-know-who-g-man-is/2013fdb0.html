Pettifogg:

If he’s a Vort, then why can’t he talk properly? The others seem to manage it pretty well.

The other Vorts have been living among humans for 10-20 years now, G-man has been popping in and out and doing who knows what, almost certainly not dealing exclusively with the planet Earth or its native inhabitants. Not to mention that even regular Vorts aren’t perfect. The have a tendency to put sentences in Subject Object Verb form on the odd occasion, as well as use definite articles in front of names (the Alyx Vance, the Freeman etc). They also place some strange tonal shifts on some syllables. G-man doesn’t have the same issues, which is a weakness, but the Vorts are by no means perfect English speakers.
