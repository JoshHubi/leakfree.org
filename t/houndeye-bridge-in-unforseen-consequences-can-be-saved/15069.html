"Houndeye Bridge" in Unforseen Consequences can be saved

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
wayne_acoba August 11, 2020, 11:37am #1

As the title says, the bridge (after the Akria-Elevator) that is supposed to collapse after the houndeye sets of his shockwave apparently can be saved.
I specifically tried to do this, so I rushed towards the bridge and tried to shoot the Houndeye right away. I hit it, but it still managed to set of a shockwave, which did kill the guard, but did not blow up the TNT crates, oddly. So I killed the houndeye with the Glock and the bridge was still intact. But I couldn’t cross it, there was an invisible wall half-way on the bridge. So I climbed the pipes and jumped right down to the bridge which triggered the transition. After the loading screen, I turned around and THEN the bridge started to collapse, but the crates and the forklift just stood there in mid-air. I shot the crates and when they exploded the forklift fell down, too (iirc). I attached some screens. If need be I’ll attach the getpos and status. I only tried this once, so far.
