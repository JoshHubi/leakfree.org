I know I’ve been spamming the chat with this a lot in the past week, so now I’ll give you guys a break and spam this thread instead. [/SIZE]

It’s a little project I’ve been thinking about a lot in the last months, and I thought I could share it to you guys. Basically I want to set up a weekly radio show broadcasting electro, house and trance over the wonders that are called Interwebs.

How is it better than other shows like this? Well I don’t only stick to one genre. A lot of show only play Trance or House music. It’s good, but I think sometimes a little variety doesn’t hurt. I’ve also seen a lot of shows that are only broadcasting (bad)[/SIZE]remixes of Pop songs. Yeah they can also sound right, but I don’t think the word “remix” means "adding a few lines of singing. Yes I’m looking at you MIMS and your Calabria remix.

It is also the opportunity for you guys who are producing music to get your songs on air. I know there are quite a few peeps on this board who are making music, and most of the time it sounds quite good! So if you want one of your songs to be put in, just say it!

Now, I havn’t started to do real episodes yet, 'cause I’m still thinking about how the show would go. So far I only did two demos, and here they are.
