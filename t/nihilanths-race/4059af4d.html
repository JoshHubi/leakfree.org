As for telepathy…when you’re talking to Alyx about Nova Prospekt, she says that “every now and then a vortigaunt gets captured and sends back information”, implying that the vorts have some sort of telepathic connection. EDIT: It is also stated in the commentary for Episode 2 that the vorts’ affinity for Dr. Magnusson caused some of his mannerisms to spread among the vortigaunts due to this low-grade telepathy, as evidenced by the way the Victory Mine Vortigaunt says “What next in this parade of constant interruptions?”, the very same line Magnusson uses later in White Forest.
