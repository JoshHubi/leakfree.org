Hello though this is my first time posting, I have been following Black Mesa Source for two years. I do reviews of freeware games as a hobby so your project interests me immensely.

I would like to point out though that the Atomic MPC quote on the front page (“one massive, jawdropping session”) is quite misleading. IMHO, I don’t think that the Atomic MPC writer was referring to your project; he was referring to the original Half-Life as evidenced by the sentence that preceded the quote in question:

It’s one of those things that many people can remember perfectly, including what they were doing when they first played, where they were - we were writing a preview for PC Authority when we received the first 20 per cent of the finished game from then unknown developer Valve.

After this he writes:

And we finished it one massive, jawdropping session.

I hope this won’t be misconstrued as an attack, just a concerned community member giving his two cents.
