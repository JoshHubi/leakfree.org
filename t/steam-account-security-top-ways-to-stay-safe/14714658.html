  1. “No thank you!”[/SIZE] Be careful who you accept gifts from! Be VERY careful!

[INDENT]Example: Joe Shmoe is a Smuck. He stole his best friends mom’s credit card and went out and purchased a bunch of games with it. He then turned around and either gave them away via the “gift” system or he SOLD the games. The unsuspecting person receives this gift or game and assigns it to his uber leet kewl steam account that has over $200 dollars worth of games on it.
