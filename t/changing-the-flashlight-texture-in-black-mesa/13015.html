A textured flashlight can be added to black mesa by copying a texture to materials/effects

Anyway I made a flashlight texture after much messing around, the interesting thing is it will cast dynamic shadows when the texture is applied so it has a performance cost to it but It is a small thing I discovered, here is what I settled on after about 3 hours of mucking about. It’s softer than black mesa’s default flashlight too.
