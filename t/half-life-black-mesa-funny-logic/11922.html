Attempting to kill an HECU to steal his uniform would have the following problems:

  1. The soldiers usually appear in groups. They work with each other all the time, and if someone isn’t responding to his normal callsign they would know something is wrong. Gordon’s disguise wouldn’t hold up very long, and offers less protection than the HEV suit.

  2. Attempting to take out a soldier in such a manner as to render his uniform unusable would be nigh impossible. About the only method I could think of would be disarming an HECU, disabling his radio, and then choking him to death with a crowbar, which would be difficult for Jason Bourne to pull off, let alone an untrained scientist.

  3. Even if the disguise DID hold up, he would still have to contend with the Black Ops, because in OpFor canon they’re there to cover up the coverup.
