Anyone remember Synergy, before it turned into utter shit?
Orange Box Engine more or less killed the Source Modding scene to me.

Synergy:
-Tons of servers for both the HL campaign and custom maps.
-A server or two with puzzle maps 24/7.
-That one server running the fun as balls fishing map.
-About 200 unique custom maps, or even more. I would start the mod up every day and eventually play through one new map every time. The mapping scene for it outgrew even Zombie Master.
-Fun player models.

Then Synergy OB came out and both mods died within the first month.

Synergy OB:
-Nobody mapped for it. Why would they want to redo everything?
-Buggy as all hell.
-Nobody interested in hosting servers for it. Most servers switched from old Synergy, realized S:OB sucks ass, then stopped hosting for both after a week or so.
-Nothing to do.
-No more fun player models, only 5 shades of Civilians, because hurr realism.
-The cool extra weapons were dropped cause hurr those weren’t in HL2 we changed our minds to be more mature.

I hope the Synergy team regret getting hyped for a shitty new engine. They killed a very fun mod and community with their obsession.
