Example:

In On A Rail, the player learns that a satellite has been equipped to reverse the effects of the resonance cascade by the science team, and is ready to be launched into space. However, before the science team could launch the rocket, the HECU Marines arrived and killed them. It is up to the player to traverse the monorail system and reach the satellite control room to initiate the launch.

or

After disembarking the Tram, Gordon enters the Anomalous Materials lab of Sector C. At the reception area a security guard informs him of a system crash in the computer network. Gordon dons his iconic HEV Mark IV Environmental Hazard Suit and proceeds to the testing chamber. Along the way he observes two scientists (Dr. Kleiner and Dr. Vance) assessing an equipment malfunction. Once in the test chamber, gordon is tasked with activating the anti-mass spectrometer and inserting a Xen crystal sample into the machine. Something goes wrong, and a resonance cascade occurs, resulting in a horde of alien creatures teleporting into the facility.
