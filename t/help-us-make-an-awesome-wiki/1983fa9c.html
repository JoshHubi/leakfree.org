I prefer this because it’s a broader category that can catch more pages. You’ll notice it can hold character, locations, and anything else that is purely story/fiction related but not related to another existing category.

If this category were to get really full of pages, and there were suddenly 6 pages in there that COULD possibly benefit from a locations category, than maybe we could consider.

as it is, locations had 5 pages in it, only 2 of which were actually valid and 3 of which were existing chapters (and as we said…technically all chapters could be included here).
