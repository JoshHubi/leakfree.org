Hey again, I just thought I’d step in real quick to clarify something. When I brought up the icons in question (the icon links on the ‘Chapters’ page), I had no misconceptions of idiotic viewers mistaking the HL1 imagery for BM imagery. Rather, I approached the page from a perspective of design. The quality, effectiveness, and coherence of the wiki is improved through a consistency in style/presentation, and attention to subtle correctness.

So, because those chapters of BM have not been shown to the public, it seemed appropriate to suggest that they are a mystery. It has nothing to do with avoiding confusion, but everything to do with quality. And remember that the purpose of the Wiki is to provide all available and relevant information on the mod to ANYONE who views it, not just forum members and long-time BM-followers.
