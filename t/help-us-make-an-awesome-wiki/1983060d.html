Just for the record:

Overview

A lung disease caused by the inhalation of very fine silica dust, causing inflammation in the lungs. A condition meeting the word’s definition is normally called silicosis.

Behaviour

In Black Mesa, the player will have to look out for and dodge large silica clouds (often by solving a puzzle) that have been released throughout the facility in an attempt to kill the Xenian invaders through silicosis. Should the player carelessly wander into a silica cloud, they will experience realistic symptoms such as: Speed decrease, heart failure, and occasional coughing. All of which hinders the players ability to fight enemies.

Cure

In order to be cured of the silicosis, the player must find a medicine cabinet and take antibiotics and the occasional dose of Xopenex. If the player does not cure themselves, they will die.[1]
