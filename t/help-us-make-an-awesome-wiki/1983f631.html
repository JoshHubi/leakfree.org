Also here is fix for the dropimages. (the boxes that you click on to make the rest of the content appear)

replace this
{{dropimage|width=100%|<gallery style= widths=“110px” heights=“110px”>
with this
{{dropimage|width=100%|hhta=left|hfs=9pt|hfc=#989495|cfs=9pt|cfc=#989495|<gallery style= widths=“110px” heights=“110px”>

this will cause the dropimage galleries match the same formatting as a normal gallery.
