Malidictus November 25, 2018, 2:34am #9

The funny thing is, he bleeds this time. In Half-Life, if you decide to shoot the G-Man, your rounds spark off him like he’s armoured, kind of like what you’d get by shooting the Grunts in their armoured bits. In Black Mesa, if you shoot him, he bleeds big red sprites and leaves blood marks on the wall. The man doesn’t flinch, of course, and he can’t die, but the difference is there.

edit
He bleeds from crossbow shots, at least. Not too sure about regular bullets.
