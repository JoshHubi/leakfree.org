Drew November 24, 2018, 10:12pm #107

Crysis, MW2, and most new FPS have view hand models with more than 6,000 triangles.
You need to worry about texture memory way more than triangles/polygons! Unless you’re in the 20,000+ thousand range with a detailed environment.

Why PC is better.
Higher resolution textures, normal maps, and specular maps.
Greater LOD’s for characters, and world models.
Greater resolution at long draw distances.
More advanced shaders.
More Draw-calls allowed.
Better texture filtering.
Better aliasing.
And best of all you the option to change the quality of most of these settings to get the best experience on your system.
On PC you can even go beyond what the game offers normally by ConVars, texture mods and add-ons.
