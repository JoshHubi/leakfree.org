That depends actually, sure the PC is a powerhouse that can outperform a console without even getting stressed but in the end it all depends on whether or not the developers are willing to include extra features for the PC. If the game is made primarily for the console then that means all the in-game materials used (models, textures, the whole thing) are optimized for consoles. Whether you have a PC or not the graphics would be the same (just look at Dead Space).

The only real advantage would be stuff like ambient occlusion, high resolution, anistropical filtering (and AA depending on what engine) and far more frames per second making the gameplay ‘smoother’.
