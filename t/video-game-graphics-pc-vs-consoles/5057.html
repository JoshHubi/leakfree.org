A lot of people say they prefer console graphics over PC graphics. but if you look at it from this way may think differently.

Graphics on the PC are different for EVERYBODY. Some people like to use graphical mods… some just have bad systems. But trying to say graphics on the PC are bad is a bad sentence it self because everyone has a different look. Some like colors and bloom while others like realism. Now on a lot of games that are available on consoles AND PC have the problem of graphics being too artificial. Such as “FAKE HD” (Brown-ness, Bloom Overload, etc.). All of this done so that consoles can handle good looking graphics at a steady and good frame rate. But PC Gamers are effected by this. A lot of PC gamers have very good systems about 3x of a console. so when they have games like GTAIV come out the PC gamers have to deal with the “Fake HD”.

Now with PC only games you can see a huge graphical difference. Look at ARMAII. If that game came out on the consoles it wouldn’t look as good as it does on the PC, for a FACT. There are plenty of Graphx mods that will change the graphics of games that have “FAKE HD” for PC but are really time consuming for most to tweak and perfect.

Now i have played plenty of PC and Console games in my years and i have noticed all of the above to be very common.

What do you, as the community, think?
