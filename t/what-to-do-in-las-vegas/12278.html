TheeGoatPig November 25, 2018, 3:24am #8
Pyro:

On a more serious note, you might wanna try the rollercoaster at New York, New York. There’s also a restaurant there that serves really, really good pizza.

It is a very fun coaster indeed.

New York New York has a few good places to eat.

The Imperial Plaza has a cheap but good buffet on the second floor (it’s up a nearly hidden escalator and around a corner, but ask directions and you will find it). They are across the street from Caesars.

The Flamingo has back massage girls walking the casino floor. It was $2 a minute last I was there, and fuck me sideways if it wasn’t worth every dollar for 40 minutes.

I was going to suggest a place next to The Flamingo for a great sit-in breakfast, but they apparently aren’t there anymore… Same for the breakfast at Binions that was excellent, but disappeared last I was there.

MGM has a lion cage. I don’t know when the lions are typically awake though.

TheeGoatPig November 25, 2018, 3:24am #10

My brother made out with a woman at The Rock House in front of the Imperial Palace (which I called Imperial Plaza a few minutes ago…)

The Venetian has a large poker room with a lot of different types of poker being played. It is also a really beautiful hotel. There was a nice bakery open late too. I had a tasty doughnut there.

The Mirage has a volcano eruption every hour or so out front, but it’s not that impressive.

Across the street, just north of the Outback Steakhouse, there is a small casino with cheap craps tables.

All the downtown casinos (Freemont Street) have cheap table games.

Stay out of the Golden Gate hotel. I have heard bad things about the sanitary conditions there.
