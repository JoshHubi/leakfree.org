MSKyuuni November 25, 2018, 3:32am #6

The trouble with making firearm sounds for Black Mesa is that you need to get a neutral sort of echo on the shot. While there are large concrete areas, there are also tighter corridors and even vents, not to mention open areas on the surface in areas where naturally the shots would have a longer echo. Since Source can’t have different sounds for weapons outside versus inside, there needs to be a sort of reverberation middle ground, since the game does metallic/whatever effects itself.
