Muji November 25, 2018, 1:22am #24

I remember the Tram ride and the test chamber from when I was like 4 or 5 years old. Half life was the first video game I had everseen, and my god, was the game amazing to me. Video games were an amazing concept for my young mind. And the game looked very realistic.

Ten to Eleven years later, I got to relive those memories when I got the game on Steam. Played it over summer, and God, was there so much nostalgia .

Also, I was playing on Hard, when I got to some Blast doors.I ran up to them, trying to open them with E. An Alien Grunt did, and oh my god, did I scream like a girl. I shot it, and blasted it with some grenades, and just as I thought I was going to kill it, Another freaking Grunt spawned behind me. So, instead of trying to finish off the first alien grunt, I started taking out the other one.

God, did my hands shake after that. Half life has been the only game that has scared me like hell.
