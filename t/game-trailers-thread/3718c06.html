I’m hoping it won’t be just “another zombie game,” Maxey. I’m hoping it will redefine zombie games to be less… arcade-y. But I suppose only time will tell. If the same mindset that was used in designing this trailer is used in designing this game, then it will be a hit.

I want a zombie game that is less about fighting hordes of zombies in a fast-paced manner, and more about individual one-on-one combat (where a few zombies in a room at a time is not as often, but really brings the adrenaline pumping). A game where you don’t focus on shooting a zombie until it “dies,” but rather tactfully delivering damage to a zombie in meaningful ways. I want a zombie game that has emotion; wherein I can look into a zombie’s eyes and know that it was once a person like me, and that every action I take to kill it could easily be done to another human being in the same manner.
