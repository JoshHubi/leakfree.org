PlausibleSarge November 25, 2018, 2:30am #10
Raminator:

We had a tool that analysed all of the content that the game used during runtime, so we could purge the unused stuff to trim the filesize down prior to release. Although it saved us about three gigabytes’ worth of data, it looks like the entity setup that the VOX system used didn’t make it through. We’ll have to address it in future updates.
