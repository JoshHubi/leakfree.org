I have no idea what this means.
Since this happened, I have:

uninstalled and reinstalled IE,
uninstalled all of my adobe programs including Flash and reinstalled them.

This only happens on youtube oddly enough, and even stranger still it only affects videos in standard resolution or high quality resolution. If I load an HD video on youtube it will go full screen no problem.
