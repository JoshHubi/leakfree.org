Hello friends, a long time no see do you? Do not you remember me? Well, then again present myself, I did some reskins for Black Mesa in the past, some soldiers in OP4 style, a box of ammunition of 357 with misspellings, and a Glock 17 with night sights.
He was missing for some time, busier with mods for the game Arkham Origins and also busy with personal life (girlfriend, law school, etc). So much so that I forgot my password and e-mail from my old account.

But I’m almost in vacation then I decided to go back to my beloved reskins, all in WIP stage. Because I’m a perfectionist and annoying. And I want tips to improve before my final release.

I guarantee that when I finish, I will release the mods in Workshop, Mediafire and Gamebanana for access.
