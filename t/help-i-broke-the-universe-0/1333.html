I understand exactly what he means, and what he has written is entirely correct, but it didn’t break the universe, because 0.999… is 1.

Here’s how it works:
0.9, the difference with 1 is 0.1, that’s a small number.
0.99, the difference with 1 is 0.01, that’s a smaller number.
0.999, the difference with 1 is 0.001, that’s an even smaller number.
<…>
0.999…, the difference with 1 is infinitely small as the there is an infinite number of 9’s after the decimal point.
Now what’s another word for ‘infinitely small’? ‘Nothing’, that’s the word.

So the difference between ‘1’ and ‘0.999…’ is nothing, because there is no difference, they are the same.
