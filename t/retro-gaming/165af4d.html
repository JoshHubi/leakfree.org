your_evil_twin November 24, 2018, 5:49pm #92

Gotta mention Terminator: Future Shock and its sequel/expansion Skynet. Made by Bethesda. While I was skeptical about Bethesda being able to make a good Fallout game, I knew that at least they shouldn’t have any problem doing a good post-apocalyptic setting since they already did it so well in their old Terminator games.

Unlike other Terminator games, they weren’t crappy movie tie-ins, they made them years after Terminator 2 was released. While Quake was doing corridors of grey and brown filled with monsters, Bethesda was doing huge ruined cities full of Terminators and flying Hunter-Killers, and Half-Life 2/Halo style drivable cars and aircraft, and FMV mission briefings featuring John Connor giving you orders. Awesome stuff.
