[Questionable ethics] Door signs disappearing

Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues (Mod)
Adambean November 25, 2018, 2:38am #1

Some of the door signs in various maps through the questionable ethics chapter disappear, more likely when using the flash light on them.

In the first two attachments the sign completely disappears depending on the angle you look at the door from, suggesting the sign brush is overlapping the door brush exactly.

The same happens with the last two attachments, though this one is more prone to happen with a flash light.
