MusicallyInspired November 25, 2018, 12:25am #93

I loved what the game had to offer (the 2 weapon limit was frustrating for a little bit but the most it did to nerf my gaming experience was force me to stand still in certain areas and decide which 2 guns would be best to use for the next area before I left). I even think the graphics are ok.

I did not like that you can’t do anything else but what the game wants you to do. I’m not talking about the SP or MP experiences. I’m talking about customizing. They’ve been claiming that this is first and foremost a PC game. That’s crap. It has none of the features you’d expect from a PC game. The game menu is made for consoles, barely any customizing options for the graphics beyond a couple checkboxes (most everything seems consolidated into the “Post Processing Effects” option), no FOV control (which is a big issue for some), no MOD tools, no in-game console (removed because it would make people cheat in multiplayer, official word), and no options for the unsupported dedicated server feature AT ALL (there’s no console window or anything, no way to moderate and administrate your own server. No option other than Exit. And you can’t join your own server because Steam won’t allow multiple instances of the game EXE). As a PC game it’s pathetic. As a console game, it’s also pathetic due to the insane load times, low res textures, and low framerate.

Besides that the game is great. I loved it. I even like the My Digs level where you can view all the trophies and unlockables available from leveling up in MP.
