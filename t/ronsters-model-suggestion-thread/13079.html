I imagined it to work like camera and security screens except the flat image would be wrapped and warped over the dome. remember the magnifying glass in kleiners lab. Or a much larger copy of the crossbow scope just as a static world model.

It doesn’t work anything like that.

The camera and security screens render a static image from the location of a camera entity, this doesn’t work for mirrors because a mirror looks different when viewing it from different angles.

The magnifying glass and crossbow scope are only copies of what’s being rendered on screen with warping being applied to it, won’t work for anything else.
