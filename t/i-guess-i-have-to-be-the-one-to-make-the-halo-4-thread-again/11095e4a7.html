Maxey November 25, 2018, 2:53am #412
Pyro:

Silly question, but I’ve been hearing a lot about how the game managed to get the most out of the Xbox’s hardware and used all sorts of clever tricks to get really good graphics. Does this mean that it’s less likely to be ported, since it’s built for a specific platform?

Console exclusive games are usually programmed directly to the hardware, instead of being written for a high-level abstraction layer like multiplatform games and even exclusive PC games.

That allows devs to squeeze all of the processing power out of the hardware and come up with coding tricks that can only be pulled out of that specific hardware.

That’s why games like Uncharted or Gears of War 3 look a lot better than even the best looking multiplatform games like Resident Evil 6 or Battlefield 3.

So, to answer your question, yes, it would be a lot more difficult to port to PC, since the engine was coded specifically just for that console’s hardware.
