WOW

I just played through the first level of the campaign. So far, this is a trillion times better than Halo 2 or 3 (jury is still out as far as ODST and Reach).

Sound took giant leap forward (those guns, man…those guns…), the Covenant are downright frightening, and Masterchief actually feels like a person, rather than a bland machine. Everything feels much more natural and the controls actually feel like they stick (a feeling I haven’t felt in a Halo game since CE).
