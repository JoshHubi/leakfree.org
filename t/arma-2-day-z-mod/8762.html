For those of you that haven’t heard about this recently, read up here:
https://www.dayzmod.com/

I’m surprised no one has already created a topic about this, since it’s rapidly gaining popularity in the world of PC gaming. So has anyone else here been playing it? I’ve played it several times this week, and it’s just awesome. I haven’t had an experience playing a game like this in a long time. Even in its buggy alpha state, the game has made me feel genuine fear, tension and paranoia. Creeping through a zombie infested town scavenging for supplies was one of the scariest things I’ve played recently. And the zombies aren’t the least of your problems, its the other players that adds the element of unpredictability. You never know if another “friendly” player is going to cap you in the back of the head and steal your stuff. Playing with some friends makes it even more enjoyable, if you can find each other in the enormous map.

But really, there must be a reason Arma 2 has jumped to the top sellers on steam. It’s telling me that people are longing for these kind of emergent games. Ones where they’re just dropped in to the world with some basic supplies and no instructions. With this much attention, I wouldn’t be surprised if a studio picked it up and developed it in to a fully fledged game. It has huge potential. Just as long as they don’t dumb it down, because i think one of the main draws is just how harsh and brutal it is. So, anyone else got any thoughts or stories about Day Z?
