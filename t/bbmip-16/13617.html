bkdale86 November 25, 2018, 4:20am #18

The original was really blurry and shared a lot of UV space. I made this one larger and has a 2nd UV set for all the outer details on top and bottom. I made higher rez because you can get really close and detail needs to stand up. Hard to keep the detail when you have a top, ceiling, floor and bottom that needs detail. I solved that by modeling the details in on top and shrinking the top and bottom. Also this one shpuld light better due to the increased poly count. Might be why you think it pops…
