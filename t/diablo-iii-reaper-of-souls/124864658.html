Paragon 2.0:

ACCOUNT-WIDE:

Paragon Levels are now account-wide, so whichever character you play, you will work towards the same Paragon Level.Paragon Experience can only be earned with level 70 characters.All Paragon Levels you have earned so far in Diablo 3 will be saved and converted into your Account Paragon Level when the changes occur. So you won’t lose any of your hard work. The more you play now, the better prepared you will be in the expansion.Each Account Paragon Level gives you one Paragon Point to spend.
UNLIMITED PARAGON LEVELS:

There is no longer a level cap on Paragon Levels, meaning you will always earn new experience as you hunt for loot.At some point, however, experience gain is likely to get so slow, you will progress very slowly. But you will always progress.
PARAGON POINTS:

Account Paragon Levels will award Paragon Points.Paragon Points are used to increase different stat points.Paragon Points that you’ve earned are accessible to all your level 70 characters.They can be reset and re-distributed in case you want to try different builds.You can place up to 50 Paragon Points in either of the stats described below, but each Paragon Point allocated is worth 5 in-game stat points, meaning 10 Paragon Points invested in Dexterity will reward you with 50 extra Dexterity in-game.
