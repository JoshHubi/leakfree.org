papernick November 25, 2018, 4:09am #2

add

-w X -h Y

to the launch options of the game on steam (X is your horizontal resolution and Y is your vertical resolution).

e.g. -w 1920 -h 1080

papernick November 25, 2018, 4:09am #4

Yep. Right click the game, then properties, then set launch options, and put the desired resolution in there.
