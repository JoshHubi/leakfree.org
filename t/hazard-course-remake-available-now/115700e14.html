JeffMOD November 25, 2018, 4:14am #2528

It’s an add-on for Black Mesa. So while it’s not officially going to be part of the game, you will require BM and the Hazard Course will be run through Black Mesa’s menu via an added menu option.

The course will be about the same as the original in difficulty, but is built to a scale similar to the rest of the remade areas in Black Mesa, meaning that most areas will be slightly larger or longer. We’ve also added the extended intro from the PS2 version of the game.

Crypt November 25, 2018, 4:14am #2529

And there are small general extensions between some areas throughout to help them flow better. Nothing major for the most part, but they certainly add a tad bit of length as well.
