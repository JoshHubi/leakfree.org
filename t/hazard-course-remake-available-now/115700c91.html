Crypt November 25, 2018, 4:41am #2954

Well, to address DKY,

  • The Workshop limit will be removed this month. This isn’t an issue.
  • The broken entities probably won’t be fixed this month. We have no idea if/when they will be, but that’s entirely on Crowbar Collective to fix. This is a big issue, and one out of our control.
  • From my personal experimenting early on, most if not all of our assets should be fine as is. We might need to tweak some to get them to work, but overall the workload shouldn’t be high.

It’s all something we’ll look into more after mod release. Steam version is on When It’s Done status, we’re not going to worry about it until after the free version’s release.

I’ll be fixing up some assets that I think aren’t the highest of quality for the Steam release. I’ve been doing that lately, but I won’t have time for all of them. Additionally, we’ll be fixing up any issues people discover w/ the free version, and probably be taking in feedback and addressing that as we see fit.
