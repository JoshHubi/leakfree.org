masterofthedead November 25, 2018, 2:58am #272

Hi guys, just wanted to say that I am 100% behind you on this project and have been very much impressed with your work so far!

There was one point that I wasn’t going to raise, except that as it has been brought up by ysrael214 above me I thought I would. This is the issue of when the hazard course takes place. There is absolutely no evidence that the main game (inbound onwards) takes place on Gordan’s first day and thus on the same day as his hazard course training. There is of course the fact that in half-life 2 and the episodes it is stated that Gordan had been working at Black Mesa a long time before the resonance cascade. But even ignoring that, everyone you meet in sector c labs in the first half-life knows who you are and they act like you are well versed in the insertion procedures in the anti-mass spectrometer. Also you are traveling from your dormitories to work, clearly you are already moved in. Despite all this for some reason so many people assume that it is Gordan’s first day at work, the only reason I can see for this was that there was a poster at the time that the game came out with slogan something like ‘his first day at works going to be hell’. This however is just marketing, with no relation to the actual game. So while I assume you were going to do this anyway, please do not link the hazard course with Inbound or put the course in the main game, have a separate link to it in the main menu. The main game and the hazard course are separated by quite some time.
