elivance:

Actually I use xvid codecs to shrink the file size of fraps recordings.

You need:

-fraps
-VirtualDub 32 bit(latest stable version)
-latest stable xvid codecs

After recording your video, you use virtualdub and select xvid codecs,
You can choose the quality of the rendering.

In this way you’ll reduce the filesize to a 1/4 or more.
You can also use codes for the audio to shrink the size more.
