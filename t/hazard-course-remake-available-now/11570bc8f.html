dky.tehkingd.u August 12, 2020, 7:30am #1819
Mr. Someguy:

I meant to replace the striped random metal plates. The wood would just be placed ontop of the grate to provide something more sturdy to jump to (as the metal plates do, but they’re unfitting).

Because clearly wood is more sturdy than metal…

Okay but seriously the only reason why the plates are there is because different jumping angles would result in slightly different distances between the platforms if I kept it a cylinder, which is no good because the jump distances are VERY finely tuned, and I can guarantee that some less-skilled players will easily get frustrated if they are no more than a few inches off in their jump.

There is no reason why the Hazard Team couldn’t have just welded the metal plates on. I mean they modified the rest of the area… so surely some metal plates would be easy to do.

As of now, the current rendition of the cylindrical platforms has truss supports to hold up the metal plates, to address concerns about it looking “unstable”.
