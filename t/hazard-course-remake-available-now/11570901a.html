Admiral Sakai:

This looks really good, but I’m not sure if I like the painted-on arrow sign… seems a bit cheap for a place of Black Mesa’s budget, especially considering that the Course is an official part of the facility.

“Quickly retrofitted” is the word I would use. The Hazard Course used to service a missile silo (now the HEV silo), then was converted into a water treatment plant, which was then turned into the Course after Black Mesa built the much fancier RP. The Course uses the fastest and most efficient way of turning the old treatment plant into what they need it to be. For instance, they won’t put up a wall if some heavy crates will do. There’s simply no need for anything else. And they’ll put up hand-painted signs if necessary, since it works well, and if they need to reroute the trainee down old disused hallways, they can easily move the plywood boards around.
