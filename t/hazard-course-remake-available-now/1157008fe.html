Crypt August 11, 2020, 11:27am #3032

Ladies and Gentlemen, boys and girls. It would appear Hell has frozen over.

You’ll be able to read the “official” announcement in all its glory over on ModDB later, but if you’d rather not, here’s the gist: As of now, Black Mesa: Hazard Course is officially available for download for the free version of Black Mesa. It took way too long for a variety of reasons, but it’s ready now!

Installer
ZIP

“But wait, where’s the Steam version?!”
As we’ve said before, there’s a couple obstacles. Most of them are relatively small and shouldn’t be too hard to take care of, but some are show-stopping bugs with Black Mesa that are totally out of our control. We’ve contacted the guys over at CC about it, so let’s hope they can get those sorted eventually. We know it’s been a long time, but we promised we’d release in 2015, so we don’t want to delay the mod any longer while waiting for the Steam version.

In the meantime, we’ll have some celebratory rest, then get back to working on getting the Steam version ready as much as we can. For that, we’ll be implementing some stuff we had to cut due to time constraints, misc tidbits here and there, I’ll be upgrading stuff I didn’t have time to do. Additionally, we’ll be keeping a close eye on feedback, because we’ll be keeping that in mind for Steam as well. This thread ain’t goin’ anywhere.

On another note, we all really enjoyed working together, and we’re so much better at it than we used to be, both teamwork, and general development. So much so that we’ve finally decided to be an “actual” dev team and work together on larger projects in the future. Y’know, instead of teaming up for HC and calling it quits. You’re looking at the Lead Developers of PSR!

Looking at that fantastic logo DKY and I designed, reading the name, seeing an acronym, I know what you’re all gonna ask: What are you gonna work on after HC?

Stuff. That’s not for this thread. Maybe another thread. Later.
