No way. I couldn’t disagree more.

Half-Life was much more revolutionary at the time than Black Mesa will be when it’s released, but in non-relative terms, objectively, Black Mesa is the better game. Better graphics, more complex levels, more fleshed-out, modern, and in-sync with Half-Life 2. If Black Mesa isn’t flat-out better, then what’s the point of making it in the first place?!

Playing HL first would only provide spoilers for and totally de-freshen the experience of playing Black Mesa. You can only enter the world of Black Mesa for the first time ONCE. Why not enter the best version of it? Most people I know would want the best experience, not “so you can say” this or that. That’s silly.

Half-Life is old hat. I loved it and it changed video games forever, but once Black Mesa is released, it’s flat-out obsolete. Sure, I’d recommend playing HL after Black Mesa to see the original, revolutionary game that Black Mesa is based on, but certainly not before.

EDIT: This is obviously assuming BM lives up to its potential and the makers don’t fuck it all up like most movie remakes do. (for example: the Keanu Reeves version of “The Day the Earth Stood Still”)
