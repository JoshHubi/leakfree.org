First bit is Gravimetric Sheer (shear), which is actually technobabble, and commonly used in things like star trek to refer to effects created by gravity or particle interaction. (hey its a science fiction weapon), and is referring to the reaction of the magnetic calibrators on the tau particle beam.

Second bit is the tangential component of an electromagnetic fields vector (see maxwells equations).

The boards are fluff though remember, and arranged to be artistic as well as interesting.
