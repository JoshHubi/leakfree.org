I’m a writer. I focus a lot of my time learning how people convey (or fail to convey) their thoughts through the spoken word. I’d be more than happy to help you with dialogue and voice acting.

One thing I hate to see in a mod is a lack of adequate voice acting, interesting dialogue and character development/motivation. Well, that’s three things, but I also hate not being able to form an attachment to the characters.

IMO Barney should speak, maybe with the assistance of simple hand gestures so the player knows he’s talking. But then there’s the problem of the player running off while Barney’s talking. You may have to work the story so that there’s no where for the player to go while dialogue is going on, and you may even (and i know this is a painful idea for a HL game) take control from the player, though leave it in first-person. Cinematics are pretty common in a lot of mods though.

If you don’t have a coder I don’t see this going very far though.
