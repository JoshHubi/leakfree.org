If you can afford it you can find 6750’s for around $85 (although this is after mail in rebates from what I’ve seen) and they run on 450W PSUs. They would be much better than a 6670 for not much more money.

Also, as far as PSUs go, do not simply get the cheapest you can find. Try to find actual reviews of them and buy name brand. I’ve had my motherboard fried by a cheap PSU in the past and learned this lesson the hard way. Also, make sure that your case takes ATX PSUs. Some Dells use proprietary PSUs and won’t take the standard ATX ones.
