The important thing is the Certified Level printed on the PSU. Look at the PSU and look for anything that reads 90, 95, 99 or 100.

That’s a percentage that the PSU guarantees to deliver no matter what.

If you have a 450W PSU with just 90% rating, then it will only guarantee 405 W. If your graphics card needs 400W and the rest of your rig needs 100, then obviously you’re screwed.

Please first check what power does your motherboard, your hard drive(s?) etc. need.

Let us know how much does your RIG need, and then it will limit what GPU you can get.
