Dias November 24, 2018, 11:08pm #17
  • the guards & scientists would have a lighter blue shirt & the guard(& zombie) vest would have stitches in the same pattern as the original barney model.
  • the zombie security guard wouldn’t be dark & crusty looking hands, but wet & bloody like the scientist zombies.
  • the scientists would wear gray pants instead of brown & the labcoats would be a lot less wrinkled.
  • Kleiner’s hair would be a bit more volumetric & Eli would be clean-shaven.
  • keep the same suit texture gman has in HL2.
  • remake the Robogrunt model for deathmatch & hide a aperture science logo on it somewhere.
  • keep the classic c1a0 lobby look as seen in the gman sequence in ep2_outland_02.
  • keep the gargantua’s hands as hunter-like mandibles instead of 3-fingered claws.
  • make the bullsquid tentacles less claw-like & give it a flamethrower-like acid spray attack for medium range.
  • the houndeye body & eyelid thinner with consistently-sized photoreceptors & brighter skin.
  • reuse the HL2 magnum, crowbar & shotgun models, sounds & animations, changing only the textures. Same thing for the Retinal Scanner from HL2.
  • the baby headcrab consistently whiter & slimier looking.
  • have the MP5 & snark look glossier.
  • the HEV Suit helmet look more like the low-def model’s chemical hood & less like the high-def model with a motorcycle helmet.
  • keep grenades & sentries grabbable with the use key & satchels go back into the inventory when used.
  • I would remove the zoom function from the Magnum.
  • rename all the maps before compiling so they’d be easier to read through when trying to pick them via console.
  • make a zombie soldier, no different than regular zombies except for more health & I’d introduce them somewhere in On A Rail.
  • I wouldn’t make the gargantua invincible unless flagged to be, that way ones in custom maps & spawned via console can die from regular explosives.
  • maintain realistic movement physics in both single & multiplayer.
  • have Dr. Magnusson the scientist doing CPR at the beginning of Unforseen Consequences.
  • I would tone down the camera-tilting caused by strafing, but add some for turning while sprinting, like leaning into the turn.
  • make the grenade, slam & satchel limited to 5. RPG & smg-grenade limited to 3.
  • keep the original alien grunt body shape/proportions, use an ep2 vortigaunt-like texture, make the armor shell-like protrusions in the skin, maybe a touch of iridescence.

I know that’s mostly aesthetic stuff, but I can’t really think of anything else I’d do differently from the dev team, since that’s most of what we’ve seen.
