So. What can I say about the game.[/size]
I LOVE IT![/size]
Just that. The game is so well done that i don’t have words for it.
The amount of effort and detail that[/size] went into this game is extremely impressive.
As a long-term player (played the mod back in 2012 when it was released), I can say that the game has improved drastically over the years.
All I have to say to the developers is: Thank you. Thank you all for making me relive Half-Life 1, the first PC game that I’ve ever played, since the beginning.
Many people complain about the “heavy” price tag of the game, and it being on early access, things get even harder.
But I do not regret buying the game. The game is simply perfect, the dialogue is great, the enemies hard, the detailing amazing. Simply perfect.
Again, thank you developers, thank you for this blessing that is Black Mesa. Continue with the great work. You have my support.
I’m waiting patiently for Xen, and when it’s released, I’ll enjoy it because I know that the developers made a big effort to make it as perfect as possible.
