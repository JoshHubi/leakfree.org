So I watched the video and came here thinking I’d be chiming in on a “OMG, the music is amazing” conversation that would be going on, but from the first few pages of comments I’m surprised that everyone hates it?

I can’t help but scratch my head at other things too. I thought that more people would have been impressed by the graphics and how professional everything is. There’s only so much you can get from screenshots, but actual footage is a different story. From little things like “Chromatic Abberation” when the player gets shot; to the consistancy of the colour pallette, textures/models and lighting that bring the artistic vision together in a very professional way. I guess what I mean by that is that not one thing on screen stands out in an odd way, everything fits together like it’s meant to be.

The biggest thing that surprised me from comments so far is about the voice acting. As I watched the video I had a rush of nostalgia from the cheesy lines from the soldiers. I was given the impression that it was that way on purpose, not for the fact that it’s done by amateurs, but rather for the reasons that; a) it was cheesy in the original game; and b) The concept recordings that were released years ago were fantastic and were themselves hinting at the humour of the original. (ie Security gaurd blowing himself and the scientist up by accident).

All in all, I personally had certain expectations for the game and what the team were going to do and the video exceeded it in ways I didn’t expect.

I would also like to jump on the bandwagon asking for Raminator to release the 1080p version officially, so I can watch that badboy in all its proper glory.
