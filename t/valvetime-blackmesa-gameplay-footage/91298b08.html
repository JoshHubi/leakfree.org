So I just spent hours reading this thread from start to finish. I’ll do a quick recap to help others out. This is pretty informal and I’ll probably forget a few things.

The leaked video was intended to be released among several other gameplay videos within a short timespan leading up to the release. Someone, known to the devs but undisclosed to us, showed someone the video, and that someone gave it to Valvetime (or Valvetime was that someone, not sure on this part). The devs asked Valvetime not to leak the content but Valvetime did it anyway. There was a squabble and some video pulling between Valvetime ad the devs. That appears to have been resolved.

There were quite a few negative responses to the leaked video. Among them were iron sights on the magnum, lack of hgrunt radio static sound, (intentionally) cheesy line reading, disjointed conversation flow, and out of place music. The devs have responded directly to all of these, and the changes have ALREADY been implemented. The irons sights on the magnum were given a toggle option in the game’s menu.

The changes had little or no impact on the release date. The devs DID NOT implement the changes just because some whiny assholes and some respectable forum regulars wanted them to, the devs made the changes because they thought it would improve the mod. Stop bitching about the changes. They may conflict with your personal preferences, but that’ll always happen. Not much can be done about it.

Issues such as too dark, lack of bass to the explosions, soundtrack is too loud, and several other things can all be attributed to youtube quality and audio settings.

The explosives trap leading to the control room has (presumably) been removed. The tracks on OAR are no longer electrified. The sniper has been (presumably) removed.

Ramminator originally planned to release a revamped version of the leaked video, however it appears the devs have changed their minds and will not release such a video to avoid confusing fans with two similar videos.

The leaked video’s soundtrack will be released with the rest of the ost.

The devs are likely not going to release any more media until release.

I am sure I left some things out and may have gotten one or two details wrong, say so and I’ll edit the original post. Hopefully the influx of newfags will read this and spare us the stupid questions.

Oh and spec-fucking-tacular job with the mod. It has exceeded my expectations. I think most of the complaints were moderately well grounded and I’m glad you’ve made the changes you have. However a lot of the people complaining (mostly on yt) were ungrateful bastards who generally didn’t even know shit about the mod and posted out of their asses.
