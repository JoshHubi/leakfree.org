mattemuse August 11, 2020, 3:02am #29

Try https://www.cgtextures.com/ as well.

The point is that if you want for example a photorealistic concrete wall in your game, it’s going to be really tough to paint that kind of surface from scratch and have it look good. Neither can you simple take a photo and map it to a 3d object, because the scale isn’t going to be correct and it is going to have seams when you tile it. What a texture artist does is grab a photo of a concrete wall, select a generic part of the surface and make it tile with itself enough times so it’s the correct scale. Then, grab the more interesting parts from the photo (cracks, stains, etc) and overlay them on the tiled image to make the pattern less obvious. That’s a really basic example.
