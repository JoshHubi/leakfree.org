Coolm4n November 24, 2018, 8:58pm #2

Go to your Steam Games Library, right click on Counter-Strike: Source, go to properties and set launch options to “-w [COLOR=‘Red’]x -h [COLOR=‘Green’]y”, where [COLOR=‘Red’]x is your screen’s width resolution and [COLOR=‘Green’]y your screen’s height resolution.

For example, if your resolution is 1280 x 720, then put: “-w 1280 -h 720”.
