Hazard Team (Decay: Source), so is called the remake of Haf-Life: Decay.

This mod is the Half-Life 2 total conversion of the PS2 game Half-Life: Decay.

Re-live and re-play the experience of Half life: Decay
Step into the HEV suit’s of Dr. Cross and Dr. Green as they to make their way to the surface and help rescue the employees of Black Mesa.

Planned Features

  • Remake of the entire game
  • Enhanced gameplay (better physics, better graphics, better sound quality, and better ass kickin’)
  • New voice acting
  • New AI
  • New Models
  • Fitting the timeline better with half life (just a few things at the beginning nothing gameplay changing serious)
  • Fix few errors the Half life Decay port has
  • Plus more

So, not there remains nothing more for remaking.
We will have Black Mesa, HL: Opposing Force: Source, HL: Blue Shift: Source and HL: Decay: Source

Last update of Hazard team:Nov 20th, 2010
