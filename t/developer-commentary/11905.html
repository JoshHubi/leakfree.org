Artic_Xiongmao November 25, 2018, 3:05am #2

Yes, please! I’d rather have commentaries than Xen even. I know that dichotomy doesn’t make sense, but it shows how important it is to me. Since Black Mesa is a modern re-imagining of a classic, the process of its creation is just as interesting (if not more so, in some ways) as the experience of the finished product itself.

So, yes, I’m happy they’re working on Xen, but I’d be ecstatic If I knew that we would be hearing the dev’s voices on the myriad of decisions they had to make. Think about it. It’s not only the technical sides of level design, but how they tried to give those levels a better sense of “place” by making them more authentic, and how they expanded the script without falling on fanboyism (“ooooh, here’s Breen saying hello”.)

To sum up: I really, really hope that at least some of the devs take some time to give us at least some developer commentaries.
