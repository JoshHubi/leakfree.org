mechaelite November 25, 2018, 2:02am #32

my experience of gameplay:
Playin’ alone: 1st wave defeated me. The soldierbots are problematic if you’re alone
Playin’ with 5 more people:
Co-operation is always needed here, the bots are dumb as hell but they know how to aim.
The Soldierbots and the Demobots are the worst as they spawn often, come in large groups and will shoot thousands of projectiles at you if they manage to get you in range, I highly recommend having some medics or dispensers close by because many of the times you won’t get into a health kit alive. Upgrade your primary weapon a lot, and if you are a spy upgrade your sapper and then upgrade your knife to be armor piercing and be able to stab giants. Also I recommend Dead Ringer and L’etranger as it will save your life more than what you expect. As a heavy Upgrade your primary weapon and your speed, I recommend taking down projectiles upgrade when the soldiers and demos come in big groups.
As a pyro try to teamwork, if you go alone to the frontline you won’t stand much.

Oh and [COLOR=‘Red’]TRY TO GRAB AS MANY CREDITS AS YOU CAN! The more you have the more upgrades you can buy
