FINALLY finished the game…some thoughts:
STORY:utterly predictable from the very beginning…as a matter of fact at one point I was expecting …not…BEGGING them to pull the old cheese “I bet you didn’t see that coming ha” twist that I hate so much just so the actual plot would get somehow interesting but NOPE…also…writing and dialogues are REALLY atrocious at times.
GAMEPLAY:The hacking gimmick is pretty neat and at least to me it actually didn’t get old that fast…for some reason “press button for awesome” is something I just can’t get enough of.What I didn’t lik though is that so many things were contextual(takedowns,climbing,crouching… …)
GRAPHICS:The fact that Ubisoft hid some of the gfx effects is just…UBISOFT.The game actually ran and looked pretty solid(once you tweak it to the hell and back).
MUSIC:The part that seriously disappointed me…there were some interesting musical cues here and there like the fixer contracts theme,but everything else was just really bland.
I give this game an Assassin’s Creed /10 and hope that the sequel (just like for that game)will improve pretty much on everything(I said “pretty much” cause you can’t have EVERYTHING better…that’s just silly).
P.S. No Person of Interest easter eggs…
WHY…JUST…WHY…
