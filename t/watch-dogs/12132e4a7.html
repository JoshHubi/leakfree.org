If you like open world games this one is really fun. I am enjoying the hell out of it.

The driving is a little wonky, it seems like you need an analogue input device for proper acceleration and my keyboard is very I/O. But other than that, I’m loving the game.

Haters are hating, but the game is fun and looks great on my system. I don’t have any real complaints for it.

Okay so after a couple more hours playing the game I have to say… my initial impressions were fairly meh at first, but after getting used to the way the game feels and plays (especially after getting used to how the damn driving mechanics work), I find that it can be very fun and rewarding.
