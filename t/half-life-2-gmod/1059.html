If both the missile origin and target are stationary I recommend parenting the missile model (which must be a prop_dynamic) to a func_tanktrain entity and have it follow a route of path_track entities to its destination.

Then pretty much the rest is just basic trigger usage, when the missile reaches a trigger brush you can trigger explosions and sounds and such.
