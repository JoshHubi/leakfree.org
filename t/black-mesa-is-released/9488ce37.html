Malidictus November 25, 2018, 2:18am #239

I admit: Black Mesa is awesome. To me, this isn’t a mod, it’s an honest-to-God AAA game that just happens to be free. It’s about the size of L4D2 and a damn deal longer than one of the Half-Life 2 episodes and everything about it is custom-made. Far as I’m concerned, you should have charged money for this, because it’s worth a lot.

That said… It’s not exactly like the original. It has a lot more self-referential humour. It was funny when Barney mocked Gordon for plugging in a thing in a thing, but now Freeman seems to have been turned into a useless guy who doesn’t belong on the science team and doesn’t care about his job. A lot of sections also seem to have been cut down. On A Rail is easily half as long, if not a third, and many puzzles have lost their platforming sections. Which is disappointing, since On A Rail was one of my favourite spots, comparable to the Airboat section in Half-Life 2.

That said, quite a few things have been improved, and dramatically. For the first hour or so, I was elbowing a friend of mine going “Look! Look! This used to be just a wall!” So much attention to detail has been paid it’s amazing. Setting the water monsters to die from two tranquilliser darts is also much appreciated. Fighting those things was always more of a pain. That, and not adding clipping to their corpses so they can’t block ladders is pure gold.

Overall, this is a solid product and I heartily endorse it, but it does stray very far away from Half-Life in many, many places.
