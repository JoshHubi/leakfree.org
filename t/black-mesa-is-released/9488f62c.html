Psychrbbt November 25, 2018, 2:28am #396

Wow. Where do I start? To the team behind this astonishing project: Thank you for your generous contribution to the Half-Life mythos.

You’ve not only captured the essence of a decade-old classic, you’ve polished it and gave it new life. All the easter eggs, every minuscule detail, refined into a new iteration.

Having played HL1 in my younger days, I was expecting to storm through Black Mesa easily. I was so wrong. I felt the same terrifying fear as I did when I first played HL1. At every turn, the same feeling of being alone kept on growing. Enemies hit hard and path well, the epic story is retold with greater depth through meticulously scripted NPCs.

Trolls and naysayers have said this isn’t “a real game” because it’s a remake. That’s like saying every Batman game or movie isn’t “real”. If Half-Life was an epic Batman comic, Black Mesa Source is Chris Nolan’s version.

Thank you so much for this release.
