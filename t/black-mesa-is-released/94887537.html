zakurowrath November 25, 2018, 2:26am #366

I’m honestly blown away by the sheer detail and dedication to this mod. Half-Life has always been one of my favorite games of all time and I loved the fact that no other game at the time did what it did allowing you have people help you along the way, wondering who was the strange suit guy that kept popping up everywhere known as G-Man, the difficulty of the solders that had some kind of super armor that pissed you off but once you killed them you yelled in celebration and the long and complex Black Mesa that took hours just to get through because you didn’t want a linear path and wanted to explore.

This mod has brought that back to a modern era, with a ton of extras along the way. I really loved the fact you expanded on the complex and made it seem like a real scientific research facility. The extra labs in Sector C and extra offices in the Office Complex really made it seem like a real place one would wanna visit and be Gordon Freeman. Elaborating on that note, I loved the Biodome Labs, I always thought in the original game that the complex was abit too small and that it really needed alot more than what was there.

The outside was just eye candy everywhere, the detail that went into the hydroelectric dam, the roads, the cliffs, the skybox, the desert that Black Mesa is located in, really made me smile and want to no clip everywhere just to see what was over the hills.

The enemies are just ruthless this time around. They’ve really grown in numbers and are pains in the ass to kill, especially the solders, I honestly had to survive on +5 health and just hope there was a medkit around the bin, but the developers where cruel and kept them just out of reach as much as possible, which made you want to fight more and more just to get to the next area.

The atmosphere, very dark, very secluded, you feel you really are all alone in a huge complex with aliens, solders, scientists, guards all having their own agendas either wanting to help you or kill you. The original has nothing on this since most eras in the original were very well lit and you could see everything. Black Mesa makes areas so dark you really don’t know what could pop up at anytime and the electricity sounds next to power boxes and the air conditioning wind noises had an eerie feeling to them.

And then, there’s the human aspect of the AI. The first guard you meet, after getting off the tram, I honestly stayed there about five minutes laughing about what he’d had said about Barney, pressing E just to see what he’d say next. The next guard with the comment about Gordon’s haircut, the scientist in the hall that wanted a vacation, the guard that was making a bet with the scientist in the server room, I mean the list goes on.

But the thing that actually made myself have an emotional bond with these non existent humans is when you, lead a few guards and scientists to the elevator shaft and they had to stay behind, just having one of the guards while you’re climbing the ladder look up at you and say “Don’t forget about us” really almost made you wanna help them and not leave them behind. That is what makes a damn good game, that is Black Mesa.

I truly thank everyone that is involved with this mod, and was involved with this mod, I saw the dedication at the end of the credits, it honestly put a smile on my face, made me want to see all the hidden details I’d missed and made me want to play it over and over again.

Thank you all so much.
