Hello everyone,
I downloaded Black Mesa: Source a week ago and I have to say the developers did a really goodjob on recreating the original title. I played this game three times I still can’t get enough it, I absolutely love this mod. However, I encountered numerous of bugs and glitches in the game. On the first play I did notice some bugs like crashing, stuttering, low frames, ect… But in the second play through, I did some ‘Nitpicking’ and notice some more bugs. Some are very minor but some are sort of major types and needs fixing. I did some searching through the community forums for any fixes that people created and I searched through the web too. Here is a list of bugs I encountered, they’re categorized in major to minor bugs. I also placed fixes for some of these issues. These fixes may work but aren’t 100% guaranteed. Credits go to whoever created the fixes.

-[COLOR=‘Orange’]Jumping height is too low[/SIZE]
Carlos Montero considered this as a feature so we get used to Crouch-Jumping, story here:
https://www.kotaku.com.au/2012/10/black-mesa-developers-made-some-changes-to-enhance-half-life/
-[COLOR=‘Lime’]Fixes?
Located at: \steamapps\sourcemods\BMS\cfg\skill.cfg
Find and change these to:
bms_normal_jump_vertical_speed “200”
bms_normal_jump_crouch_vertical_speed “200”
