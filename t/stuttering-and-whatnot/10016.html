Well, looking at the budget panel screenshot you had posted it’s either vsync kicking in and clamping FPS down to 30 or a GPU fillrate shortage. Concerning “reload and try again” advice: yeah, it could work sometimes (when playing non-stop for a relatively long time running through demanding areas) but most of the times it won’t help :-(. Dlight-related FPS drops or fillrate shortage won’t be healed by the game engine restart.
