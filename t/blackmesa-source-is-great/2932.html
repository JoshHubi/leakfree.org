There’s already teams making subtitles in different languages for the game, but the forum is down since a week.

And no, they won’t send you the script.

The teams working on the subtitles for the mod started by translating the original Half-Life script, so that when the mod is released, they don’t waste much time making subtitles, knowing that the BM script will be similar to Half-Life one. The devs are making themselves English subtitles, so when the mod is out, the teams working on the subtitles will only have to replace the english text with their translation.
