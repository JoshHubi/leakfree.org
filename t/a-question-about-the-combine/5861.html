Maxey November 24, 2018, 10:53pm #2

The Combine are an intergalactic alien force that conquer worlds and use whatever they find in those world for their own use.

When they conquered Earth, they started using humans for their own cause, either by hiring normal humans for urban security (Metrocops) or actually genetically modify them for the elite squads (Elite soldiers). If you are captured by the combine and resist their brainwashing, you are eventually transformed into a Stalker.

It’s assumed that most of the synth enemies, like the Striders or Hunters are actually based on beings from other worlds they had conquered previously.
