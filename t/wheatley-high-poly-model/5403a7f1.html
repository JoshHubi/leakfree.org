Harry-UK August 11, 2020, 6:24am #273
Jeannotvb:

Any tutorials on how you do your rendering?

I don’t use anything fancy; just the standard 3DS Max Scanline Renderer. I create a Skylight for the ambient-occluded shadows and give the model a standard coloured texture with a bit of self-illumination. This method makes the details stand out whilst making it visually pleasing. It takes about 30 seconds to set up and about a minute to render; with good results. :3

With textured models, I step things up a bit and add HDRI maps and several Omni lights. HDR tutorials can be found all over the internet. I used it for the reflections on the Portal Gun.
