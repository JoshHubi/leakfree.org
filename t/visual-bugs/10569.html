lexa2 November 25, 2018, 2:26am #4
Stixxy:

Another visual glitch in Questionable Ethics - the consoles are missing…

Try restarting up the game and make sure you have no “-dxlevel XX” switch at the BMS command line parameters. Also make sure you play in DirectX 9.x mode and have no “mat_picmip”/“mat_dxlevel” refferences in autoexec.cfg. The game could misbehave like shown on your screenshots when you change some gfx settings “on the fly”, without quitting and restarting the game process right after the change. Yes, it seems to be a bug in BMS, but similar bugs with disappearing entities and crashes after gfx settings change affect HL2 and all other source-based games.
