~The rules of the game:

  1. People (“Requesters”) post things that they want to see in glorious forum-drawing-o-vision
  2. Peope draw (“Drawers”) these things in glorious forum-drawing-o-vision
  3. The first person to reply with an image to the suggestor’s liking, that user gets kudos!
  4. Fun had by all!:retard:

~Specification:

  1. Do not use images that you found in the darker corners of the Internet, or any part of the internet for that matter. Rememeber, a good old google search-with-images will leave you exposed if the requester gets picky.
  2. Be clear about which request you are replying to.
  3. Be clear when you are giving kudos. If you would, please edit your original post to say that the kudos has been awarded, and to who.
  4. Don’t request or submit hentai or anything, because the mods won’t love you for it.
  5. Of course pictures taken in games are ok, especially Garry’s Mod! Scans, digital drawings etc. fine. For bandwidth’s sake, please don’t make things too massive…
  6. Don’t try to assign yourself kudos. That’s silly.
  7. Didn’t there use to be a whole part of the forum for this kind of thing? Have I missed it? Only too happy to move.:meh:

~Advice:

Drawers, don’t go into too much detail, for if you’re submitting a drawing you’ll risk being beaten to the booty (Yarr. :pirate .

(Any rules that don’t make sense? PM me and I’ll fix that when I can)
