Black Mesa Archive In-game Issues
pfannkuchen_gesicht August 11, 2020, 11:27am #1

So I recently bought BM and started playing and found a few issues.

  1. Dr. Kleiner is flat-shaded(probably forgot to export smoothing groups)
  2. Ragdolls glitch out a lot, often times sliding towards one direction of the map. Sometimes just wobbling around on the ground. Especially after reloading from a save, see screenshots below.
  3. The crowbar sometimes hits nowhere near where the crosshair is
  4. Grenades when thrown also start/hit nowhere near where the crosshair is. This makes precise grenade throws almost impossible because they fly somewhere else as expected and of course don’t bounce of where they should’ve.
  5. With the crossbow, if you aim closely above an edge, the dart will hit that edges wall, because for some reason the dart gets fired from a few hundred pixel below the crosshair
  6. crouching and standing up multiple times in a row can lead to a half-crouch(intented?)
  7. soldiers hold their guns the wrong way, causing the front part and the barrel to stick through their hands
  8. A missing monitor screen texture in the lambda labs(sorry, I forgot to get the coordinates, hopefully the screenshot is enough)
  9. Music is too loud, drowning out any dialogue that might be active at the same time
