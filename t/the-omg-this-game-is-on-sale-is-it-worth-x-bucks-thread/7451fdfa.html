Orpheon November 25, 2018, 1:03am #148

I went ahead and bought Bastion on sale today. I think it is very much worth it. I bought the $12.50 soundtrack pack because I’ve heard many good things about the music. I am glad I did. The music is awesome, it sounds sort of like a blend of Age of Empires 2 and Rayman 2, so if you’ve played those games you have kind of an idea of what it’s like. Otherwise I’m sure you can look it up on youtube.

The narrator is hilarious too. He’s got this kind of southern rogue voice going on which he uses to commentate everything you do. I love it, it’s got just the right amount of humor mixed with useful directions. The gameplay is pretty fun too. I’ve only played on KB+M and it worked just fine, but many people suggest that a gamepad is better suited, so I might hook one up later to try it. It’s a dungeon-crawling hack-and-slash game, similar to Spiral Knights or Recettear. You get melee and ranged weapons and different weapons for each type so you can mix things up and try different combinations of weapons to see how they work. When you find materials you can use them to upgrade your weapons. You also get different special abilities to use in a pinch. So although you could probably go through the game just mashing one attack button the whole time, there’s also plenty of other ways you can play it.

All in all, I’d say go ahead and get it if you’re at all interested in it. It would be worth it at its regular price, so it’s a great deal at this price.

EDIT: So I plugged in my 360 controller to play the second time, and I think it probably is a little better. While KB+M allows you to have more precise control over aiming with ranged attacks, with a controller it auto-aims, so a lot of the challenge modes are, incidentally, less challenging. KB+M still works just fine though if you don’t have a controller to use.
