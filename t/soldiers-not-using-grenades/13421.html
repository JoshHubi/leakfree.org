Oh, and atop that, as long as we’re discussing needed AI fixes, I always hoped that the guards would have the same “dynamic interaction” combat abilities as Alyx, but seldom do I see any of these. The few instances I do see of guards struggling with zombies close-quarters, the guard merely shoves the ghoul back instead of doing any fun finishing moves. Even worse, oftentimes when these guards are fighting zombies or headcrabs, they don’t have a huge instinct to run to a safe distance before firing. Their encounters with Marines can be even more amazingly suicidal. I’m not asking our peers at the Black Mesa Security Force to have catlike reflexes, but I would love to see them willing to take cover and hide behind corners instead of just standing in a corridor slowly firing a Glock. I know that weapons are now mapper-defined, but I still think it’d be nice to see some guards choose an MP5, since at least it’s accurate in bursts.

These are just a bunch of nitpicky little details that I’d like to change, nevermind all the convar changes I try to make Black Mesa a little more like the semi-realism of Freeman’s Mind. At least, I think a few glock rounds should be enough to down a soldier, even if the nine-mils just hit him in the arm.
