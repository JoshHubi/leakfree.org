dky.tehkingd.u November 25, 2018, 2:39am #18
Joe Freeman:

Being the AK-47 the most produced weapon in the world

You mean the AKM. Nobody really uses the AK-47 anymore. That myth is popularized by CoD and misinformed movies, all of which use the AKM model but for some reason (erroneously) call it the AK-47.

As for the MP7, I think it was to get the non-traditional look. Technically the MP7 is not quite an SMG by definition; it’s a PDW. It, like most other PDWs, fires specialized ammunition that only a specific company makes (in this case, the proprietary 4.6x30mm armor-piercing round made only by H&K). Because of that, it’s not as widely used as more traditional SMGs like the MP5, which uses a popular pistol round (the 9x19mm Parabellum). I think Valve chose the MP7 because not many people are familiar with it due to its less-widely-used nature, which makes it seem exotic.
