Bad cans? Why are they backward in the first place?

Just read this:
“there was an application (Final Fantasy Eight) which was making the audio reverse. I thought it only did it in-game, but apparently it did a permanent change. I checked off reverse stereo in the FF8 settings, and now everything is fine.”

You might want to check other aps/games you’re running and see if the same thing happened to you.

Also, this:
“If they headphones/speakers aren’t usb it’s easy enough to cross wire it, even without soldering. If you don’t want to solder, just get an 1/8” jack (male) to RCA (male) adapter and an 1/8" jack (female) to RCA (female) adapter. Cross the RCA jacks and that’s it."
