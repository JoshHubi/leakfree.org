To correct people:
There was never a Half-Life2 Alpha made on GoldSrc, what some of you may be thinking of is the “Get Your Free TVs” test, made in 2001.
That was the Source engine, but people commonly mistake it as GoldSrc due to the HUD being the same as the HL1 and the low polygon count (That people forget was common in 2001, polygon counts and texture sizes didn’t start jumping up until 2004)

What you see in Get Your Free TVs are the original “Gas mask” citizens of City 17, back when the chapter Air Exchange was in Half-Life2.
