The programming in Half Life makes the game play music files from a cd during certain parts in Half Life. Once you no longer needed to have the CD to play HL, people started hearing music from whatever CD they did have in the drive. Needless to say, the music is saved on the Half Life CD as a regular music CD.

I was about to throw that in there. Yeah, just use Windows Media Player, or whatever other program you use to rip your CDs to get the songs into an MP3 format.

I know, it’s weird, but you can actually pop the game disc into a CD player, and listen to the Half Life soundtrack straight off the disc. Good thing it’s not anymore complicated than that.
