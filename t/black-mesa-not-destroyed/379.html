Would you be prepared to stand on the other side of a 100 meter thick wall of concrete with a nuke going off the other side? Put your money where your mouth is.

It is not clear where in the facility (canonically speaking) the nuke was detonated. Since opposing force is not canon, so we can only presume.

A nucelar bomb inside, but not far below surface would have the largest impact, all entrances to the facility would potentially be collapsed as well as large portions of underground sectors, there would be surviving structure deep within the ground of course. Even without contact with the blast, any people or xen creatures inside the facility would most likely be killed by the shockwave caused by the blast. There’s no doubt that the facility would be rendered inoperable. Even if the blast didn’t destory parts of the structure, the intensity of the blast would force it down corridors and shafts for miles, scorching everything that can be scorched, and many sections would collapse severing access to deeper parts.

Afterwards the facility would be flooded with radioactive fallout and the ground around the facility would be contaminated for decades. When possible any surviving access points to the facility would most likely be filled with concrete to contain the radiation and prevent access.

Not 100% destruction, but the facility and all its infrastructure would be absolutely decimated, which is just as good as complete destruction.
