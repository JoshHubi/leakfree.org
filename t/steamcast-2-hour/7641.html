SteamCast

Welcome to the official Steamcast website. Steamcast is a fan-made podcast, generally recorded on a weekly basis every Sunday, that hosts a wide range of gaming related topics. Contrary to its name, the podcast does not focus strictly on Valve or Steam related topics but aims to cover as much of the entire gaming industry as possible including gaming digital distribution, major gaming press conferences, and other relevant news. As a community-founded podcast, Steamcast places high importance on community participation. Members of the community are welcome to join the hosts and share their thoughts and opinions on specific topics. This gives the community — everyday gamers — the opportunity to voice their opinions and concerns…
