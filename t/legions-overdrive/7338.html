Legions: Overdrive is an attempt to bring life back into the FE:L project, shortly after development was halted by late company InstantAction. Development has since been outsourced to a community dev team formed mostly of private testers when the game was in alpha/beta years ago. It’s currently available through legionsoverdrive.com as a free game.

The game play involves fast paced CTF with inherited mechanics from the Tribes series to match. Although it’s been criticized in the past for lacking depth, the development team is working to remedy this.

Still, I’ve seen alot of skilled play than in most shooters nowadays. There is a great requirement for coordination of positions in the game as well as strategy, and the Offense vs Defense standoffs are very exciting. In general, I like how it isn’t run and gun.

Brink costs 49.99 on steam, this is a free (better) game. Give it a try.
