OrderOfDagon November 25, 2018, 3:49am #45

My opinion of the HECU: They must have the training of christ because they seem to be able to snapshot me in the face from over 15 meters away without even flinching – even on easy mode.

About the backpacks, and a bit more: Should definitely bring them back, because they look a bit unassuming without them. I always thought the huge-ass testosterone-pumped elite military force was way fucking intimidating; it felt like a play on your concept of yourself, as if you thought you were this one-man army up until the point when they came in, and they made you rethink just how equipped and tough you were by being fucking manly as all hell. It kinds reminded me of the bad-guys from The Rock movie with Nick Cage and Sean Connery… Anyways, I also enjoyed their very specific AI in the original Half-Life, as it provided a much needed change of pace in combat – something that the game heavily relies on to keep it interesting as the whole game is just a shooting spree. That change of pace is something that seems a bit out-of-touch in Black Mesa, where they just seem to be a bit robotic, much like the Combine. It just seemed that everyone was doing something different in the original Half-life. For instance, most soldiers on contact would run to cover, others would flush you out in a direction you weren’t facing, and the rest would be lopping grenades at my nearest cover while screaming scary things in their walkie-talkies making me anxious. All of which the big backpacks full of special who-knows-what gave them an interesting appearance when compared to COD-style basic looking gear.

And NemesisVSJill: I love your post. Too many people ignore the atmosphere that their characters give off, and it’s always important to me. I can’t possibly care about engineers and medics when they wouldn’t feel right to begin with… cool name by the way.
