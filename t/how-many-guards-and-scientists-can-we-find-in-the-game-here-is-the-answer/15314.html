Greetings everyone!

While waiting patiently for the next great update, I decided to make an attempt at determining the number of Black Mesa employees we can find throughout the game. Years ago I did it with Half-Life. There, excluding the Hazard Course and the Xen chapters, we can find:

34 security guards alive, 22 dead.

104 scientists alive and 57 dead.

35 zombies and 3 awesome forklifter guys, called “Gus”.

This puts the total number at 255.

With Black Mesa, this quest took me… slightly longer. First thing first, I made a couple of rules for the job, like:

  • Anomalous Materials and Unforseen Consequences chapters are considered completly separate.

  • Scripted events are ignored. If an npc is alive at the moment we find him, than he is marked as alive.

  • Zombies… alive, dead, half dead or very dead… they are all the same to me.

  • Burnt bodies are counted: usually it’s easy to determine wether they were friends or foes.

  • If only a part of the body remains, I look for a vital part like torso, spine or skull. A lonely severed leg doesn’t have to mean it’s owner is dead (rip Eli Vance)

  • Sometimes only some suspecious blood mark remains on the floor or on the walls. Those are counted as well.

One question remains. What version of the game I used?

  • The up to date Steam version of Black Mesa, extended with the OAR: Uncut and WGH - Vent Mods.

  • 51 security guards alive, 81 dead and 33 zombified.
  • 146 scientists alive, 136 dead and 106 zombified.
  • 34 suspecious blood or body remains.
  • 6 suspecious burned husks.
  • 22 dead engineers (in OAR UNCUT)
  • and 8 dead office clerks (in OAR UNCUT)
