Wait, did PCSX2 FINALLY fix the mipmapping issue!?

Yep
Performance improved a bit with the implementation of mipmapping as well. On most planets, I’ve been able to run R&C at full speed on 60Hz mode.
Ratchet & Clank and R&C2 are almost perfect in hardware rendering now. R&C3 looks fine for the most part, but bloom effects can be a bit iffy at resolutions above native.
Some games that benefit from mipmapping still suffer from other, separate texture cache issues (e.g. Jak 3).
