Kairouseki November 25, 2018, 2:49am #170

I had this same problem. There are a few lines in the config files that were set up wrong when the game was determining what hardware you’re using. At least that’s what was causing it for me. Just gotta remember what lines exactly.

Edit: Go into your folders and open DoomConfig.cfg. Now go through and look for:

seta com_videoRam “2048”

seta image_downSizeLimit “0”
seta image_ignoreHighQuality “0”
seta image_downSizeBumpLimit “0”
seta image_downSizeSpecularLimit “0”
seta image_downSizeBump “0”
seta image_downSizeSpecular “0”

And maybe a few other variables around that area. Some of them are probably wrong. I know that my video ram was set to something negative and most of the second part for me were 1’s.
