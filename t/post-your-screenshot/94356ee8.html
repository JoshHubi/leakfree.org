Bloodshot August 11, 2020, 10:29am #1007

Texture mods make the game look a boatload better. I would suggest wet and cold if you want extra immersion. It makes NPCs cover up with hoods and face covers in snowstorms or rain, you can get covered in snow, you drip water when you come out, etc. It also has an extension for dragonborn so ash storms actually effect the npcs and player, and they use things like dwemer goggles.

Also I like SkyRe (and how it integrates those really cool morrowind weapons released a while back) but I’ve been thinking I might want to replace it with morrowloot - I mean it’s not compatible, but morrowloot takes away the ability to easily craft everything and hand places sets of armor and stuff in dungeons like it was in morrowind, and makes things appropriately rare.

Kinda thought it was stupid how in the original game you could just craft Daedric armor because you killed a dremora at a shrine and got his heart.
