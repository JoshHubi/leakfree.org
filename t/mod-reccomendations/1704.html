DenDrio November 24, 2018, 6:44pm #16

I play only multiplayer mods so… yeah… I has Fortress Forever, Insurgency, Neotokyo, and Battle Grounds 2.

Fortress Forever is a remake of Team Fortress Classic, includes grenades and class differences from Team Fortress 2.

Insurgency is sort of like a war simulation, 2 teams, you try to capture bases.

Neotokyo has only one mode for it, Capture the Ghost, where as there’s a ghost ( DUH ) and the ghost is sort of like a flag and you have to carry it to the capture point. 3 Classes with 3 Models.

Battle Grounds 2 is based in the Revolutionary War. It’s pretty fun, addicting and you have to use teamwork to have fun.

I hoped that helped =]
