Hello folks, not sure if this is the place to post it, so feel free to move it if you think there’s a better place for this contribution (make it a Sticky, maybe?).

So, I don’t know about you but I really like binding weapons to specific keys so I don’t have to be pressing numbers or scrolling in the middle of an encounter.

Problems: weapons names in BMS differ from HL1 and HL2; plus, the names in folder C:/Program Files/Steam/SteamApps/SourceMods/BMS/models/weapons/ also differ from those used in the game (crazy but true).

So here is my little contribution to the community:

Weapons names:

Crowbar: weapon_crowbar
Pistol (9mm): weapon_glock
SMG (9mm): weapon_mp5
Shotgun: weapon_shotgun
Hand grenade: weapon_frag
Missile launcher, RPG or Bazooka: weapon_rpg
Revolver (.357): weapon_357
Gauss, TauCannon, etc: weapon_tau
Hornet Gun, Hive Hand, etc: weapon_hivehand
Gluon Gun, Egon, etc: weapon_gluon
Slam, TripMine, etc: weapon_tripmine
Crossbow, xbow, etc: weapon_crossbow
Satchel Charge, Explosive Bag, etc: weapon_satchel

As you see, many of them differ from HL1, HL2 and BMS/models/weapons/ directory.

How to bind them: bind key “use weapon_…”
