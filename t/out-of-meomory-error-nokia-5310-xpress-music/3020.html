Lord_Grievous November 24, 2018, 8:00pm #2

Well unless you can extend your phone’s memory by using an SD(HD) card or something like that, I’m afraid you’re stuck with whatever memory your phone has.

EDIT: I just checked on the Nokia website and apparently your phone has about 30MB memory and can use up to 4GB with a MicroSD card. Obviously part of this is reserved for the OS and basic programmes, but I find it hard to believe that this would leave you with less than half a MB.

But looking on the interwebz, it would appear this has nothing to do with the physical memory but your phone simply can’t play java games with a heap size over approximately 400-500kB.
