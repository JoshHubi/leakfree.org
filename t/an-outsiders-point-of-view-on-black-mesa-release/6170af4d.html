1] Remove the twitter from the main page. Hubi is not a mod developer IMO, and I can’t imagine it would take too long to remove it. Plus, while he may not get paid for his work on the website, it is Hubicorn’s responsibility as a web administrator to work on these kinds of things IMO.

3] Add on to the main page that while developers won’t be releasing official updates too often, there is often media added into the Wiki, and every so often a post is made by a developer reflecting on some aspect of the mod’s progress in the forums. While these may be well known to the regulars here and can be easily found out by reading any of the countless “updates pl0x” threads here, it can’t hurt to put it in big bold letters on the front page.

Whether or not anybody reads this or cares, I think that the above statements need to be taken into consideration. Also, don’t read this post as hateful or offensive, I have enormous respect for the amount of work and effort put in by all of Black Mesa’s “staff”, but I can’t think of a single reason why some of the above can’t be done.
