Possible solutions

1- Black mesa is darker than HL1&2, so feel free to use always the flashlight.

2- You can adjust the brightness in Options->Video->Adjust brightness level…

3- If you have a CRT monitor (unlikely), it means that is aging and you should change it.

4-You can modify he brightness of your monitor, You should have some buttons that allow you to do it.
