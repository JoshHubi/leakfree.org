DinoThrasher November 24, 2018, 10:30pm #30

I’m enjoying the game so far, but have 2 major complaints:

  1. Combination of sanity meter and bad-guys. I’m having trouble moving around, specifically in the prison, when I have to worry about both of these at the same time. I can’t stay out the dark because the enemies will spot me, but I can’t stay out of the light because then I’ll go insane.

  2. Weaker characterization than Penumbra. I don’t know, but I felt like Phillip was a much more characterized person. The beginning backstory and then some monolouges during the loading screen helped me picture Phillip as a person. David, on the other hand, just has his journals. This more makes me feel like David is no longer here, and I’m the one going through the castle… which is of course not true.

In summary: I like the atmosphere, environment, and overall design of Amnesia better, but David’s a total pussy who’s afraid of the dark and can’t compare to Phillip who just didn’t know when to quit and was overall a more well-rounded character.
