I’m an experienced hl2dm player and I play with 1 key binded per weapon.

For example :

bind a “weapon_357”
bind b “weapon_pistol”

etc…

I dont want 1 key per weapon slot such as : bind a “slot4” because it’s slower to get the weapon you want.

The issue is some weapons of Black Mesa have the same name of the weapons of hl2 when some have not.

For instance : “weapon_357” is still “weapon_357”
But in BM : “weapon_pistol” is now “weapon_glock”

I have found the name “glock” in the /models/weapons but for the other weapons sometimes it does not work.

So if someone has a list of all the weapons in console, I would be grateful to share it. It could be added to the wiki too.

Thx !
