^ I actually got a little bit of a plot going for it in my head, no ending to it yet. But as I said I have no modding skills whatsoever, I probably could learn some but I am about to start college for engineering and I am not a math person so I will probably be studying hard to keep up and wont have time for much else. Even so the Dev’s don’t want us using their stuff and I am more than willing to respect their wishes. So if I ever want to make it I’d have to make it from scratch.

Anyway, it would start with you on a tram ride going in to Black Mesa. I was thinking about having like an Aperture Science guy explaining what you need to do to get around, dropping hints and such via earpiece or text through like a Blackberry or something. He would guide you through the first part of it up to the Resonance Cascade, it would be something like a tutorial showing you all the basics that you would need to know to survive the game, and then he would abandon you once the poop hits the paddles so the player would pretty much be on his own.

To get in the facility a scientist’s profile has already been added to your disguise kit. This particular scientist is home sick thanks to a massive dose of a laxative that was added to his coffee grounds. Your first objective will read something like this: “We have received word that the scientist sent out an email to let his colleagues know he wouldn’t be in today. Prevent the Anomalous materials team from reading that email! Use your sapper to fry their computers." (“Hey Mr. Freeman, I had messages for you but we had a system crash, I don’t know about 20 minutes ago and I am still trying to find my files…”)

Once you get in you can find a computer that you can plug into and download all the Anomalous materials employee profiles into your disguise kit, allowing you to transform into anyone of them without having to see them first. Their personnel profiles contain clues and information about each one of them and their security clearance as well. Your disguise kit has been upgraded to allow you to fake retinal scans so you can gain access to different sections of the facility. As the game progresses you will pretty well need to find and replace certain scientists, track down their corpses, or if they have been disfigured or are not accessible download their personnel files to be able to disguise yourself as them to fake the retinal scanners all over the facility.
