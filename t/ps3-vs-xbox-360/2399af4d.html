1 ) Free online multiplayer (sure, not as well-polished as Live, but its FREE)

9 ) Built much better (no RRODs on the PS3)
10 ) Virtually no piracy (I don’t know of anyone who developed a way to play burnt Bluray games on the PS3, or even games downloaded to its hard drive)
