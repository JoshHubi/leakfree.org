You have a pistol and that’s enaugh for a fresh spawned guy… just aim for the head. Pistol is accurate enaugh for that (click primary fire, secondary fire is completely inaccurate… but good in close firefights).
Making everybody spawn with good weapons wouldn’t make the game any better. You can see it when you play HL1:Sev Mod… No depth in gameplay… No tactics. Just brainless.

You must be one of these players who thinks Deathmatch is only crazy running and shooting… but it’s not that.
It’s about taking an advantage in equipment and arms and using that advantage by aggressive playstyle (frag and frag and frag and frag…) and if you don’t have the weapons, you have to concentrate and play careful and smart.
It’s like in Biathlon… If you are skilled (endurance, concentration and aiming), you get advantaged because you deserve it. If you aren’t skilled… then you get challenged and have to improve your style to something more efficient. It’s just that excitement in DM games to not make any faults. Even if you have the advantage… you can easily lose it again by playing unconcentrated and dumb.
