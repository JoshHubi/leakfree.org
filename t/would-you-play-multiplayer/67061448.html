I may play it for the rest of my life when it’ll be designed for competition and fun… Just like Adrenaline Gamer was. Imo a voting menu with matchmaking options is a must for BMDM.
It will also be very important to have an improved netcode that can handle fast gameplay… because that’s one of the few things that really suck in HLDM… netcode and hit registration.

When I tried out AG2 aka. HL2DM pro I was shocked, because it wasn’t able to reproduce HL1’s gamestyle and gameplay.
For example:
I played Stalkyard and you were able to pull every weapon down the boxes in the storage hall using your gravgun. It didn’t make any sense to climb up all the boxes, because it would make you too slow. You weren’t even able to bunnyhop over them, because you didn’t have the same jumphight like in HL1. In HL2 you don’t jump as high as in HL1 using a normal jump.

I hope BM will faithfully rebuild all essencial gameplay elements HLDM had… including jumphight, bhop, circlejumps, driftjumps, wallgauss, spawnchain, spawn delay, rampslides, footstep sound volume, unmovable items/weapons in the map, crossbow jumps, the ability to steal an enemy rocket using your own RPG laser, HLDM gamespeed (I hope you won’t use max_speed “320” in BM, because that is just too fast for precise aiming… Call me a noob but as soon as it laggs a little bit it gets impossible to foresee that lag and take it into account), climbing, sliding and having the same delay on switching a weapon on every weapon (In HL2DM for example, you need more time to switch to your RPG than to switch to your assault rifle. It shouldn’t be like that.).

One more advice and comment on the hit registration in HL2DM/AG2: Some weapons (shotgun) have a better hit reg. than other weapons (magnum, gauss…)
You should care a lot about making weapons behave less random like they did in AG2, because shotgun is really overpowered in that game. You only have to roughly aim near the enemy model and it will allways hit, while you won’t hit with Gauss or Magnum when you exactly aim ON the enemy model -> blankshots when aiming exact and using gauss/magnum -> hits when aiming only rough using sprayweapons.
I hope you won’t have that aimbot-like effect when using shotgun in BMDM.
