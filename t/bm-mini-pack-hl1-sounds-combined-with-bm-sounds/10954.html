Black Mesa - HL1 combination sound effects

This small pack changes the gluon, hivehand, and tripmine (and adds the HL1 weapon select sound)

Rather then straight up replace these sounds with the HL1 sounds (since I thought they sounded good) I opted to combine them together, so while it sounds more like HL1 you still get to hear the new BM sounds.

I chose the gluon and hivehand specifically because the sounds for both were a simple sound that could easily be overlayed with the old one, and I think especially on the Gluon, the effect is quite nice.
