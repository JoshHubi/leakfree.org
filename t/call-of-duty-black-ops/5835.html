Kinda surprised no one made a thread about this yet.

Very good solo campaign. Plot kinda deviates from the CoD norm, since it’s more character based and not about all-out war. Also, you can blow up the enemies’ limbs Soldier of Fortune style.

Still haven’t beat it, but so far it seems lengthier and a bit more varied gameplay wise than MW2.

As for multiplayer, it’s basically MW2 with dedicated servers and slightly more balanced killstreak bonuses and no tactical nuke (as far as I know, the largest killstreak is 11 kills). However the game needs a patch to fix the lag spikes players are getting during the matches and there also seems to be numerous reports of bad graphical performance on MP only.

The server browser is decent enough, at least it’s far better than BC2’s one, and there’s an option to change the field of view in the settings, which is great for me, since I can’t stand the default field of view from MW2.

Anyone else willing to share their opinion on the game?
