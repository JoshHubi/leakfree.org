I’m one of the guys who knew nothing. A friend of mine, Sn1pe, messaged me when I was playing TF2 saying “lol those images of BM are so bad.”

Some people have asked questions about why we don’t like having this sort of hype. There have been answers from other devs; my answer is that being a mod, we don’t actually NEED publicity as much as anyone else. It takes up a lot of our time making sure what we’re showing is polished, answering questions, etc. We’re only concerned about making a good game; if only 100 people played Black Mesa and loved it, I’d be fine with that. If 1,000,000 people played Black Mesa and loved it, I might be a bit happier, but certainly not any richer.
With no need for any sort of publicity, there’s no reason to show anything, and it is in fact better to NOT show anything so there are no false expectations or theories.
