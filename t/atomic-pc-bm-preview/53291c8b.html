Hello, long time lurker and first time posting here!
About the leaked screenshots, I wanted to post my honest thoughts on them as I have been around this place ever since you guys first started this mod!
So to start off, I did see all the leaks ever made including the recent one and I have all of them saved for archive purposes.
After seeing the recent leak I decided to compare the screens that I could, with some of the official ones. To my surprise, in most of the cases the official ones seemed to have better lighting, while the leaked ones seemed to have some areas more detailed (like the round desk at the beginning) but suffer from lack of shadows, bump maps and show a form of excessive ambient lighting.
I tried to find the logical answer for the difference between official and leaked screenshots in graphical details and I couldn’t find anything other than the possibility that the leaked screens have been taken from an older build or taken on medium settings.
It would be very nice if some of the devs would be able to give an answer to those questions, now that the leak has happened. Some further details on the leaked screens would just clear some of the rumors going on and fix some of the damage already done.

I also wanted to suggest an idea which might be good for the devs and BM’s public image.
From what I read in this topic, I understand some of you devs are pissed off and some of you might even be a bit disoriented after this leak. If I was part of your team, I would suggest that everybody inside would look at the leaked screenshots and see if you have any areas from those leaks that are fully completed and polished!
If you do, then maybe it’s a good idea to post new screenshots of those areas taken by you in a proper way, so that people can see how those areas look when they are fully polished are run on highest settings.
If you do not do this, I fear that it will be a wasted opportunity to fix some of the damage done by Atomic PC!
I will not lie to you, after seeing everything, I do feel a bit disappointed about SOME of the screens as they are IMO showing low res textures, too much ambient lighting, almost no chrome, or bump maps and a big lack of shadows.
To give an example, I find the Inbound tram looking very detailed as a model and design while at the same time I find it’s metal chase texture, looking nothing like metal but more like gray textured paper.
In fact some other textures in those screens seem to look like textured paper because they lack bump maps.
My first thought was that some of the 3d models or walls are covered in textured paper that mimics their materials instead of looking like they are really made out of concrete or metal or what ever the case is for each object. Some of the walls and objects give the sensation that you get when you look at a real wall covered in a poster that has bricks printed on it.
Here’s my brief view on a random screenshot to be more objective.
When I look at the leaked screenshot 73 I see a very well done map with lots of details but I also see too much ambient lighting and no variation in light sources and I definitely see a need for more shadows or at least(though I am not sure it works in the source engine) some ambient occlusion to give the curves of the walls more depth.

I like very much what I’ve seen and I really feel like I am seeing half life 1 being remade, so big congrats to everybody but I also see a few graphical shortcomings in some of the screens which I wish would get fixed.
I hope you agree with my critics and if you don’t it’s the same to me as I will still play this when It comes out with lots of interest.
It would be nice if you would consider my suggestion and take some new screenshots of the already leaked screens in order to fix some of the damage done by ATOMIC. It might not be clear here, but I did see lots of people on other forums talking about the leaked screens and it seems lots have them and also quite a number of them are putting down our work based on this leak with no mercy.

The guy that did this to you deserves to be thrown out of your team for being either incredibly stupid or an incredible bastard!

Ps. I hope you take my critics from an artist’s perspective instead of a whining kid, as I have also done something across the lines of what you guys are doing here with this mod.
For who cares to know I was part of the Classic Doom3 Mod that did have more than a decent reception back when it got out![/SIZE]

Hello 3dwizzard. Welcome to the forums.

I guess the devs always appreciate some constructive oppinions about the mod, but specially in this case of the leaked pictures, let me quote a dev response straight from the BM ModDB page:

Stormseeker 17hours 28mins ago says:

Considering that alot of the shots are of MY work, I can say definitively that these ARE alpha shots, taken of areas not yet finished to beta standards (such as from my chapter Questionable Ethics, the whiteboard etc). The shots were taken by Atomic, not by the dev team. They were supposed to have had a limited access play of the beginning of the game without actual access to the files, instead they got access to the current alpha build and took a bunch of unauthorised shots.

Therefore these are not representative of the quality we hope to have upon release, and should NOT be seen as final.

So… Don’t worry man! A lot will be improved until the release.
