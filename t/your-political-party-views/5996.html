I do not ascribe to any strict political ideologies, nor do I care to label myself as anything other than “politically interested.” I like to have knowledge and have a stake in what happens, but I will not shoehorn myself into “convenient” labels. They just cloud discussion and all too often become the focus of discussion over any real debate or insight into the issues at hand.

I will support the party that makes the most compelling case for my support, and even that’s a bald misstatement. I will support the person that makes the most compelling case for their ability in the position at stake.
I really don’t care if they call themselves Democrat, Republican, Green, Libertarian, or Jedi. As long as they have some fucking values they believe in and stick to them, well…that would be a fucking miracle.
