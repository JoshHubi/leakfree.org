Please post your system specs including the Operating System (CPU, RAM, Graphics Card (model number and total memory))
Also post if you are playing the Regular release or did you Opt-In to the Public Beta.
And list the mods you have, thanks.

Restart your computer and see if it still crashes.

If it still crashes uninstall all mods and verify the integrity of the game files.

Weird crashes can sometimes be fixed by setting all of the graphics settings to low and decreasing your resolution, then try the problem area again.

  1. Disable Cascade Shadow Maps (CSM) same for the Dynamic Lights
  2. Decrease shadows to the lowest setting
  3. Turn off God Rays
  4. Lower or disable Anti-aliasing (AA)
  5. Decrease your resolution
  6. Under the Black Mesa tab turn off everthing DLight related and any video enhancements.
  7. Decrease all settings under the video tab to the lowest setting.

Have you tried restarting that unlocked chapter. Go to the New Game menu and pick that chapter, just in case your saved game is corrupt.

You can use “sv_unlockedchapters 15” in the console without quotes to unlock all the chapters and go to the New Game menu and pick a different chapter to see if it still locks up or to skip that area entirely.
