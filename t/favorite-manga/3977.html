The only mangas I’ve really read are Chobits, Ranma 1/2, Real Bout High School, and Cromartie High School. Chobits is really good, though it gets into some philosophical and existential quandries near the end. Ranma 1/2 has both action and humor, and both are done well. Real Bout…not so much. It has a nice art style, but it becomes hard to understand at times, and fight scenes are often cluttered and hard to follow. Cromartie is more wordy than most mangas but its more than worth it because it is freakin hilarious. Also the FLCL manga is only two volumes, so if you’re looking for a short, insane and incomprehensible read, try it out.
