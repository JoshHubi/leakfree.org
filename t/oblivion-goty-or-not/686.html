Shivering Isles is certainly worth it IMO. Mainly for Sheogorath’s character. His diologue is bloody brilliant most of the time.

Some very nice new quests and items as well, including a very nifty weapon that can be gained quite early on. The disadvantage being that it kind of makes combat very easy. Nice armor as well. It terms of extra play-time, it’s certainly worth it. The ‘main’ quest took me around 5 hours (as far as I can remember, though the last time I played was around a year ago and I’ve since traded it in) with a few extra side-quests to boot.

If you can save often just before encountering these glitches (forgive me for having no knowledge of them) then it is worth it.
